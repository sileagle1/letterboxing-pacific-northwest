Hello! We're new!

From: cheflisbeth (cheflisbeth@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-26 04:01:07 UTC
I found out about letterboxing when I was planning a trip to Sequim
and thought we might go thru Quinalt Rain Forest. Their website for
the rain forest has link regarding Letterboxing. Out of curiosity, I
clicked on it and was pleasantly surprised!
We have had a blast so far and have gotten lots, and lots of excercise
without even realizing it!
And Letterboxing saved my bacon on that road trip to Sequim. We
(myself and 2 dd 8 1/2 & 6) went up there for the lavender festival.
What was supposed to be decent weather turned out to be a down pour
and very cold. The girls were SO not impressed! So I decided to
swing by the Sequim library, hopped on their internet 'puter to copy
down clues while the girls dried out and looked at books. Got some
great clues for one in Brinnon and someothers on the way back. What
was a potentially miserable trip for all of us, turned out to be a
pleasant hike in the woods (turns out we didn't go far enough for
either one, but will be going back with hubby!)
I now have a backpack with all our lb stuff plus will always include
bugspray, gloves and a flashlight!
Thanks to all of you that have hidden such awesome handmade stamps...I
look forward to finding more and hopefully meeting some you great
people!'
Elisabeth "The Buppster's"
