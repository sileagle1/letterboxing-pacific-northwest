From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2008-10-30 03:23:31 UTC
I am already back from my quick visit. We took a lovely stroll in
Stanley Park yesterday morning and were rewarded with a lot of
beautiful photographs and a wonderful memory of sitting on a wet log,
thousands of golden maple leaves at our feet while we stamped into
your box there.

We than headed for Granville Island for lunch. We saved the letterbox
for dessert. Sad to say, Summer Bloom eluded us. We had to leave twice
to wait for folks to leave the hidey hole but when we finally got our
turn there, all we managed to find behind the post was a plethora of
spider webs. I was picking them out of my hair the rest of the afternoon.

Many thanks for sending us to two great spots. I'm only sorry there
wasn't enough time for the other suggestions here. Perhaps next time.
Funhog
