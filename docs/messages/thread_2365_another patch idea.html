Hey, I had another patch idea--but don't have time to create it in
image form.

What about a view East from the mouth of the Columbia?

You cover the Pacific, the river, the mountains North & South and
can incorporate the arid areas east of the Cascades as the river
continues to the horizon toward the top or back of the patch. Or it
could even veer North/left like it does in reality and the terrain
toward the rear left to right behind the river bend could go from
arid to rolling wheat fields--maybe even incorporate the Wallowas,
too? The right/south could incorporate waterfalls. The river could
have a salmon/orca swimming in it? The lettering could be swimming
in the river--above the horizon or beneath the image.

The possibilities are endless...
