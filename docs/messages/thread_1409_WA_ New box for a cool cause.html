WA: New box for a cool cause

From: ccrwalsh (cwalsh@nwlink.com) | Date: 2004-06-29 03:20:40 UTC
Hi all-

Today a good friend of mine set off with an amazing group of folks
who are going to pedal their bikes across the US-Seattle to
Washington DC! She decided to do this for her 57th birthday, to
celebrate quitting smoking, and to rally support for those suffering
from smoking-induced lung disease (like my father).

We waved her on this morning as she passed through Bellevue, and were
so moved by the spirit of these people that we carved a stamp right
there and then and hid it.

Follow the progress of the Big Ride at www.bigride.org!

There, I'll get off my soapbox now...

Celt et al.


Re: WA: New box for a cool cause

From: ccrwalsh (cwalsh@nwlink.com) | Date: 2004-07-02 04:07:10 UTC
OK, so here's the scoop: it's a good cause blah, blah, blah, and yes,
I've already donated, but I'd like to go one better.

For every visit to this letterbox between now and August 14 (the end
of the Big Ride), I'll donate $25 to the cause. What's more, because
of corporate matching, that means the American Lung Association of
Washington will get $50 for each visit!

Combine something you enjoy with a good deed, it's easy!

Celt et al.
