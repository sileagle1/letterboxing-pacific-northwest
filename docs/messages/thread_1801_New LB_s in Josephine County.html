Hi, Just as a heads up to those in Southern heaven, oops I mean
Oregon, :o) There are 3 more LB's hiding, one in Rogue River, Hugo,
And near Canyonville. These won't be listed in our "Region (#6)" on

them in the "whats new" section. The county is under "Dunno" and
after reading the persons website I dont think they knew the county
so I dont think I am blowing any secrets here (which I can be
unfortuantly good at doing). And since they are not listed under
Mystery boxes I figured we should get them while we can, while they
are still under the "whats new" section after that, I dont know where
they will be listed at so if you want these boxes you might need to
act fast, or at least copy down the directions while still find-
able. :o)
