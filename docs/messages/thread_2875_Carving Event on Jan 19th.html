Carving Event on Jan 19th

From: Tyra (tyrarachele@comboard.com) | Date: 2008-01-11 23:45:41 UTC
4 Ch is organizing a Carving Event at Collage on Alberta Street in
Portland, Saturday, January 19th, from 3:30-6:00 pm. Space is limited.

"This is an adults only event. We will be meeting in the workroom of a
wonderful locally owned art store for some carving and chatting.
Newbie letterboxers would sure appreciate some tips. Potluck snacky
food and drinks. The fee for coming to this event is $1 per person,
per hour, payable to the art store. Work space is provided, as are
some tools. Carving tools, materials, ink and other common and unusual
art supplies are avaliable for purchase if you want to shop. AQ Mail
4ch with questions."
