From: catbead1 (libby@twcny.rr.com) | Date: 2003-10-14 02:39:29 UTC
I wanted to reintroduce the subject of exchanging letterboxes with
Dartmoor. I'm sorry I let the ball drop when I brought the subject up
earlier. Some health things were going on and my internet connection
/ modem went on the blink and then finally quit (I'm connected again).

This is a really thrilling exchange opportunity to have some of our
boxes on real Dartmoor land and have boxes from
the "letterbox motherland" here. Here is history and a letterbox
hybrid in the making.

This group is full of creative boxers and what needs to be discussed &
decided on is:
1) a theme.
2) what type clues you want written for the boxes planted in
Dartmoor.

I'll be the gathering point for the boxes being shipped and received.
So all you need to do is get your box to me, and be willing to pay
for postage to receive a Dartmoor box from me. Oh, just no lead balls
in the boxes going to the UK. :-)
