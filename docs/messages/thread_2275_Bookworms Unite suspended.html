From: Kathy (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-10-12 20:47:57 UTC
I go to check on this box today, which I do every time I go to the
library, and I find this weird thing. About a foot from the hiding
spot is a hole about five inches deep, six or seven inches in
diameter, pretty big really, dug with a shovel apparently, with a big
rock weighing down but not covering completely a stack of white papers
with print on them all about psychic readings and how you call this
number or whatever to get one. No plastic around them, just loose
papers in this hole in the ground. Whoever put them there also
grabbed a smaller rock (the one that was hiding my box from view) and
put it on top of the big rock weighing down the papers. So my box is
sitting there in sight if one bends over, maybe not in sight if one is
just walking by - certainly not hidden well enough for a letterbox. I
have no idea if the person saw my box and left it alone on purpose or
just grabbed the rock hiding it to use for their little cache and
didn't even see the box. I brought box home and edited the clues; a
little too much weirdness going on in the vicinity for me. I suppose
this is not too unusual for urban boxes.
