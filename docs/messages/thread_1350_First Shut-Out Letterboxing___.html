Hello Fellow Letterboxers of the Great Pacific Northwest,

Toast Boy, Loud Eyes, Piper and I headed out in search of the Native Plant

expected.

At least we got out on a beautiful day onto some trails we'd never have
found on our own. Thanks for that!
