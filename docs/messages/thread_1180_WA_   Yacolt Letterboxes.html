WA: Yacolt Letterboxes

From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2004-03-06 15:23:20 UTC-08:00
Hi All!

We were heading up to Lake Merwin today and knew we were going into
Yacolt to have breakfast at Grammy's. No better time than the present
to pick up Campfire Lady's letterboxes out that way!

Chelatchie Prairie, Moulton Falls and Lucia Falls Letterboxes are all in
wonderful spots and the stamps totally coincide with the place.

We were unable to get Luv Ya because there were people picnicking at the
falls picnic table where compass bearings must be got and we were
doubtful that we could be discreet, so we will be saving that for
another time.

Thanks to Campfire Lady for placing these terrific letterboxes in
Southwest Washington. We really enjoyed the beautiful scenery and the
artwork!

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
