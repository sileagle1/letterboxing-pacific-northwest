Marshall Park Series and Tryon Creek

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2003-10-06 16:03:15 UTC
4 members of Team Run-A-Muck set out Sunday to find the Marshall Park
Series and Tryon Creek Series. All 5 of Marshall Park Series are
alive and well including clues for a mystery box. Tryon Crekk was
another story. What a hike! I am still trying to recover. Found High
Bridge with no problems, Bird Watcher was a little more tricky but
when it was found there was some hootin and hollerin goin on, Red Fox
Box was a tough climb. The "older" members sat this one out and sent
the kids up the hill only to find that the brush was so thick they
couldn't get to it, Iron Mountain was a no find all together since we
couldn't figure out what Der Mad Stamper meant by a "disk". Any help
on the last two boxes would be greatly appreciated. Took the clues to
the Orphan Box but never made it that far. After hiking for 5 hours
we were wiped out! I did walk away with a nice pair of Ray Ban
sunglasses found off the side of the trail though. Anyway, 7 out of 9
boxes isn't too bad. It will just take a whole week for my knee to
heel.
