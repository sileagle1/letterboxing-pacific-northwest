From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-08-01 19:05:06 UTC
I agree with DVN@RSKR, the Pt Defiance boxes are some of the most
challenging.

I find that you need to relax and think out side of the box for
some. Do not pre-assume anything for others. Some are easy as pie,
and the problem comes when you try to make too much of them.

Have a pen handy and do not hesitate to make notes if it helps you
keep count etc.

Pt Defiance is one of the unsung gems of Puget Sound. The size and
grandure of the trees gets me so distracted.

If you can successfully get all of Trail and Error and Evil Glenns
boxes you can count yourself graduated from LBing 401 and ready for
grad school.

And you can look forwad, even seek out, further T and E / EG boxes
for further fun and frolics.

Seems to me they are a bit behind on their next diabolical
placement, though I failed T and E "1000 words" Owl box up at the
forest. Found the start, never the finish. Tried to do it without
printing out the pictures, perhaps that was my mistake. 8-)
