From: Nail Family (c.nail@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-10-06 00:52:54 UTC
Had a recent report that this contender for worlds smallest
letterbox (it was in a listerine breath strip pack complete with log
book and 1/16" thin hand carved stamp) was missing. Verified that
indeed it has disapeared. The rare rock that it was hidden under was
moved to another location and there was no sign of the tiny box.

I note on the LBNA site in the clues that it is currently gone and
recarve and replace soon. Hopefully I can find a better hiding
place as this was easily found by locals.

Thanks to those few boxers who found it, those other who havent
should stay tuned for updates.
