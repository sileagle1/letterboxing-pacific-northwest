Thought I'd just chime in, for what it's worth. I assist a screenprinter
when he has too much work to handle himself, and thought I'd mention that
colors and styles are not an all-or-nothing deal. We often get orders that
have 5 of this color short sleeve, 10 of that color long, etc, and it makes
no difference in the ultimate price as long as you order the minimum the
printer needs to make it cost-effective. Often folks choose ink colors that
work well for most shirts, with an ink color change if the scheme chosen
won't work for a particular color. Then, you just pay another set-up fee
for those ink colors.

Something to think about.
