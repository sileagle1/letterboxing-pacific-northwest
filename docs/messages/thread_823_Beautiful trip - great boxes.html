Beautiful trip - great boxes

From: Rinda (sleepywhippet@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-10-19 13:53:31 UTC
I was lucky enough to get out and about yesterday. Took Blue Eyed
Monster with me and hit up the Trilium Lake Forest Friends Series.
Thankfully we took a wrong turn and found Box #3 on the ground
behind this giant tree with the lid bearing rodent dentura marks :)
After we stamped in we rehid in the original spot which we found
without problems. What a beautiful place! Mount Hood was glorious
as only she can be! Heard several different languages being spoken
as we circled the lake, Japanese, German, Russian & Spanish.
Interesting people about!

Stopped into the Crater Lake box, lots of people coming from the
lake as we walked in so no one to bother us while looking for the
box. It looked like someone/thing lunched on another thing bearing
lots of bright blue feathers right on top of the spot where the box
was hidden, nothing yukkie there *whew* just feathers. We replaced
exactly like we found without incident.

Camp Creek Campground was closed so we parked off of the main road
and walked in to find the box - clever hiding spot! since it was
close to dark I am glad that mini-maglight found it's way into my
LB'ing stuff. Awesome stamp!

Stopped at Calamity Jane's for giant burgers on the way home. For
those of you who haven't had the pleasure of a Calamity Jane meal
they have about 50 different kinds of hamburgers. Yummy!

Beautiful day and great fun!

Now on to Fright Fest this next weekend!
Happy hunting,
Sleepywhippet
