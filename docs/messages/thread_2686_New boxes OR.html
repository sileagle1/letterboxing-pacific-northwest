From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-11-26 01:18:54 UTC
I made my way outside this chill Central Oregon afternoon. The Three
Sisters, Mt. Jefferson and Black Butte all showed me their snowy grins
beneath an azure sky. A fleet of grey-white clouds slipped by
overhead, hosting a russet-hued hawk on the prowl for an unsuspecting
meal. It was the perfect time to hide two new letterboxes at Crooked
River Ranch.

I've been busy over the years populating the Ranch with boxes. Don't
be shy. C'mon over to the dry side; bundle up and find these newest
offerings. While you're at it, why not hide one of your own? The motel
and one of the RV parks at the Ranch are open year round. There are
three hundred days of sun and few with rain. Enjoy! Funhog
