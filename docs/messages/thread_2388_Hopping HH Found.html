Hopping HH Found

From: Lisa Harkins (lisah@whidbey.com) | Date: 2006-01-30 12:01:21 UTC-08:00
We found a HH in Zen Fruit last weekend. The log book doesn't have an
e-mail, and I can only assume those stamps on the first page are the placers
(Willow, Heartsey, and Daisy, are you out there)? I couldn't locate them
through AQ. I've sent an update to Pete and Wanda, but if you guys are out
there, the Hopping HH (Easter Bunnies 03) is alive and well, with quite a
bit of space left in the log book. I think the placers may be from around
here, because it started its journey around Vancouver, WA.

It's only been in Oregon and Washington so far. Wish I could move it out of
state for you, but we'll see if we can leave it somewhere locally that gets
some traffic going elsewhere.

This HH was found by Cat's Meow and I also while boxing with Willow,
Heartsy, and Daisy back on April, 18, 2004. When we found it in the
Troutdale area there was no logbook with it and no one to contact
about it. We made an attempt to find the owner but were unsuccessful
so whoever owns it is a mystery. A logbook was created for it so it
may keep on traveling. Willow and kids aren't letterboxing all that
much anymore so they are probably not reading this message. I hope
this information helps.
