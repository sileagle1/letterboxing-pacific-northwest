Banks Vernonia boxing

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-07-09 17:39:18 UTC
Good news, fellow boxers! I just got on my PR hat and gave a call to
to the Ranger, Mike Shannon, at the Banks Vernonia Trail SP. Since so
many letterboxes had gone missing out there, several of us surmised
that the staff had pulled them. Well, it seems we were dead wrong.
They do not feel friendly towards geocaches but are perfectly OK with
our treasures.

There are a couple of caveats that Mike wanted to make. First, he
wants us to let him know when we place boxes so he can give them a
check. He'd be looking at all the usual environmental and safety
things. He wants to stress that the trail is pretty narrow and it's
easy to stray onto private property. The park closes at dusk was
another of his points.

So, this is open territory... miles and miles of trail with almost no
boxes along them. Perhaps, Brett will feel inclined to replace his
series out there, too. To contact Mike Shannon, call 503.324.0606 He
was very nice and really wants to have things work for us. No more
Mean Mikey... Now it's Mellow Mikey! Funhog
