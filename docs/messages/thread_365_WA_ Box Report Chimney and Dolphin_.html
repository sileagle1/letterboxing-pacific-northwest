WA: Box Report Chimney and Dolphin.

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-06-29 21:12:53 UTC
Happy to report that "In The Chimney" is still sticking it out at
Luther Burbank. The Cranium HH set loose by the 7 Nails was supposed
to be there (left by DVN2R SKR) but it was not in the box and there
was no stamp showing who might have taken it. They may have
forgotten to stamp into In the Chimney.

Replacing was a challenge as a family rolled in and parked with two
observant kids a short distance from the junction, in full view of
the placement location. Luckily a mother duck with two peeping gawky
stage ducklings chugged by. I waited for them to distract the family
and scuttled in for the replacement.

The fenceposts here are worth the trip for this and the other park
boxes.

I also found the Dolphin Mystery after a minor bit of trouble. I
went right to the spot, but fought the absolute location of the box
until I read between the lines. I could not believe that I was
required to go for it in that fashion but go I did.

Wonderful location with tons of bird song, bug buzzing ( take bug
juice) and butterflies doing their thing. September should present
plenty of opportunity to see Berry Drunk Robins in squabbling action.

Happy sultry summer boxing, next weekend Bellingham!
