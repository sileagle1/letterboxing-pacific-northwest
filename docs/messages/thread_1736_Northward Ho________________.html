Northward Ho!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@lbworld.org) | Date: 2004-10-27 20:47:26 UTC-07:00
I've let my family know, know it's time to let my 'other' family
know...... Very Happy


As of last night (Oct 25th, 2004) I've accepted a full time position
with a company in Galena, AK. Yeppers, that's right, Team Rayvenhaus
will be moving to Alaska. With the recent event (FF2) let me tell you
this decision was hard to make. You guys are some of the greatest people
that we've met and it took a lot for us to decide to do this. The
deciding factor was full time work. I've been working contract jobs for
quite a few months now at a severe pay cut and we've only been falling
farther and farther behind. Having started looking for work elsewhere
when nothing seemed to be coming up in Washington, led me to look North:
Maine, Alaska, Minnesota, etc, etc, etc. This offer came so fast, we
really didn't have much time to think about it, and really didn't need
to since I've got to support of my family first.

Suffice it to say that we shall miss each and everyone one of you guys
here in the Pacific Northwest. I know that some of the recent events may
make others think that there is an animosity between myself and a few
others in this area, but that's not true, not anymore. I only wish each
and everyone person in this hobby to enjoy it in their own way.

Godspeed to one and all. We shall live vicariously through the websites
and the groups as we attempt to introduce letterboxing to a community of
600!

--
Steve of Team Rayvenhaus
