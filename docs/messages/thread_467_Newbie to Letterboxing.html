Hi all,
I have been reading the message boards and would like to add my 50
cents on some of the subjects if I may.
I read the article in the Portland Parents magazine and that is how I
found out about this great pastime. If it is decided to have the
article written in the Columbian there should be an emphasis put on
the rules of etiquette for letterboxing. This pastime is not to be
taken lightly and should be respected so that it may be enjoyed by
everyone wishing to participate. Besides, it might get more
letterboxes hidden for us to find. *wink* My mother was visiting from
So. CA. when I read the article so now she is a mad hunter in
California. I have become so involved in this that I am setting out
today to place a few Hitch Hikers along the way.
I am planning on hunting down a series of Der Mad Stamper boxes in
the Rock Creek area of Portland and I will let you know the status of
them when I get back. I am hoping they are still there. My 8 year old
daughter "Cat's Meow" is as excited as I am!
