From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-08-10 00:16:32 UTC
Just yesterday, I was at Columbia Art Supply on
East Burnside stocking up on slabs o' vinyl for
stamp carving. By the front door there was a table
with a sign on it saying, "buy one get a second
one of equal value free." Well, this table is goldmine
of the kind of boxing goodies I use regularly. There
are tablets of tracing paper, many kinds of art papers,
various sizes of PVA/bookbinding glue, and the best
thing of all, sets of a dozen Marvy LePlume markers.
They have the markers in two different spectrum sets.
For the price of one, $27.50, you can get both...

Thought I'd pass this on in case anyone is suffering
from a shortage of letterboxing supplies. Besides, I
need someone else to collect as much of this stuff as
I have so that I don't feel guilty. Funhog
