Eastern Oregon updates

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-19 16:08:25 UTC-07:00
Mudstumpers had a wonderful time in the Eagle Cap area and did a very little bit of boxing.

First, since finding the Road Trip LB last year, we have verified that it is indeed missing.

Second, we are happy to report that Classic Pooh is alive and well and in great shape. Great Hide by the way and well done stamp!

Third, Obie is just where we thought it would be. I even have a picture of the hidey spot and the water fall. The Hurricane Creek Llama is a wonderful stamp. In great condition too.

Thank you all for making the trip even more fun!

We have placed 2 of our own boxes in the Wallowa Lake area. Clues will be added soon. They are easy and child friendly.

In September we may place one up in the lakes basin of Eagle Cap. It's a good long hike, Ryan.... are you up for it? Or any one else who likes the back country??

Swat those mosquitoes!
