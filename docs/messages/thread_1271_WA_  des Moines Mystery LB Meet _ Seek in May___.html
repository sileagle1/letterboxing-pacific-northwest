Ok, it's official...

We'll play with the official title a bit before we carve it in EZ

~~~***~~~***~~~***~~~***~~~***~~~

WHAT: des Moines wa Mystery letterbox Meet & Seek in May

WHEN: 29 May 04, Saturday

TIME: specific time TBD but plan on formal stuff kicking off around
2-3 pm'ish and finishing up after dinner or whenever you'd like to
leave depending upon when our guest(s) of honor arrive--we'll tweak
the time after we get more specific travel info from our guest(s)

LOCATION: Des Moines Beach Park (whereever the picnic tables are--
park recon to follow)--exactly 3.2 miles West of I-5 Exit 149B!!!

WHO: Anybody who is interested in letterboxing or bring along those
buddies of yours whom you're just dying to introduce to letterboxing

MYSTERY: ??? This event will culminate with honoring some special
guest(s) who will visit the Puget Sound region. I'd tell you who it
is but then that wouldn't be any fun, would it? Kind of like
knowing where the letterbox is before you read the clues... Just
know that you'll be very excited to have a certain signature stamp
or two in your logbook after attending this event!!!

FOOD/FUN: Potluck is the best & easiest way to go. Bring enough
food/drinks/supplies for your group and to share with others.
Previous events have been a gourmet afficionado's dream!!! You
won't be hungry after this event!!!

Other details to come as we go along...

If you're itching to plant a letterbox or two, get 'em planted
before the event. I recommend posting the clues a day or two before
the 29th so folks can print up the clues before they hit the road--
then they can spend Sat morning looking for the boxes before the
formal 'exchange' feeding frenzy begins.

Let me know if I'm forgetting any details.

Hope to see you there,

dvn2r ckr


Directions to Des Moines Beach Park from Interstate-5 follow:

At exit 149B, turn RIGHT onto Ramp (WA-516 / Des Moines) 0.3m
Turn RIGHT (West) onto SR-516 [S Kent des Moines Rd] 2.1m
Bear RIGHT (North-West) onto SR-509 [Marine View Dr S] 0.3m
Turn LEFT (West) onto S 223rd St 0.2m
Road name changes to Cliff Ave S 0.1m
Road name changes to Nebo Blvd 0.1m
Road name changes to Local road(s) < 0.1m
End: Arrive Des Moines Beach Park


We can do a letterbox supply donation/exchange/freebie box if folks
are interested. I've got tons of letterboxing supplies just waiting
to get into the hands of enthusiastic letterboxers. Let me know if
you have any ideas for other events.

As I'm not totally positive about the abundance of picnic
tables/benches at this facility, we may need to bring some
tables/chairs for the event, too--so please keep that in mind.
