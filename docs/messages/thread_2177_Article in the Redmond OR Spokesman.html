Article in the Redmond OR Spokesman

From: 4Dtoes (jfleshman@bendcable.com) | Date: 2005-08-03 20:22:49 UTC
Hi all,
Just wanted you to know that our little hobby just got a boost here in Central
Oregon. Our local paper interviewed our family and just ran the article in
todays paper. The writer is also a letterboxer and she did a great job of
describing Letterboxing. We even planted a box especially for the article,
(Funhogs suggestion), and ran the clues right in the artilce. I was nervous
about how she would present it and I thought she did a great job.

Unfortunately our little local paper isn't online yet so you won't see it, but trust
me it was great! I'll keep you posted on how many newbies we get!

Later,
John of the 4Dtoes
