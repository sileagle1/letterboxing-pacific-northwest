From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2007-04-11 00:09:28 UTC
Additional Details ?
Team Springamajack wanted me to post this here. Sounds like fun!

Break out your loudest Hawaiian shirt or coconut bra, spray on a tan
and come get lei'd with Jess and Team Springamajack!! April 28th
12:30- 6-ish

EVERYONE is welcome! Nothing fancy just a chance to meet other
letterboxers and have some fun.
Prizes for the Loudest, wackiest hawaiian shirt! Grass skirts are
welcome too!
Limbo contest!

Bring a Hawaiian themed dish to share.

Special letterboxes only available that day.

Located at Elijah Bristow State Park which is 15 miles east of Eugene

I-5 to Hwy 58 - follow signs to Elijah Bristow State Park. When
entering the park follow the road ALL the way back to the Picnic Area
"A". There is plenty of parking for everyone. If you happen to have a
portable shelter please bring it. THANKS!

Eugene-Springfield is embarrassingly short on camping facilities. Try
searching east either along Hwy 58 or Hwy 126. On Hwy 58 Dexter
Resevoir, Lowell to Oakridge or on Hwy 126 up Springfield to Blue
River would be most convenient.

Not sure exactly what type of campgrounds you prefer so here are a
couple of links to get you started.
