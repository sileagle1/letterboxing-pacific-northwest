Gnome Series

From: Christine M. (mthemooners@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-11 02:30:41 UTC
Well..we took trip #5 to Pt.Defiance to find some Gnomes. This time
with a strategy...we checked pace counts, coordinates at every
single trail marker. It paid off with us finding Ailill but
Gwendolyn still proved elusive!! We spent another hour backtracking
in search of her after finding Ailill but she must of been out on a
walkabout today. So, all in all, we have found three out of four
Gnomes. We also stopped and checked in on the Gnome Ranger HH who
hasn't moved since the middle of February. We didn't move the Ranger
along today.

We can report that The Forest Gnome has had several visitors! They
must of stumbled upon him. Some of the visitors even visited on Leap
Day..they came from Edgewood. Then twice since then (as early as the
last two weeks) a entire crew of about 12 or more somehow found him
and signed into the logbook.

We once again attempted to find the Narrows Bridge box via clues
from what our miniscule brains could remember. Got the marker count
right but couldn't remember the paces and degrees. Nothing really
popped out as being the right tree! But that's okay.

We checked on the Fantasy box to see if it was still in it's
location and sure enough it is. Didn't have time to check inside it
due to travellers on the trail. Didn't want their dog to find the
box and pee on it you know.

All in all we checked in several boxes and found them in great
shape. Thinking back we probably should of opened several of the
boxes we checked on to see if there were any hitchhikers we could of
helped on our way. Don't recall anyone mentioning that they
deposited any at the LeapDay Event.

Till another time,
MomNoTime and MiniBullyMom
