OR -Grant's Pass Area - updates

From: Pete (jiggs111@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-07-02 23:13:11 UTC
Hi all.

Clan Jiggs spent a few days in the Medford/Grant's Pass area this
past week and managed to get a little boxing in! We had a mixed bag
sort of result for this one, with some great stamps picked up...

APPLEGATE REST STOP - AArrgh! Yet another victim (possibly) of
Ryan's compass headings gone wild!

GRIFFIN - Grants Pass - Box is missing. The clues were very direct
and the hiding spot had been opened and contents removed.

CREATURES OF THE NIGHT - Merlin - WOW! What a spectacular stamp and
location! Road was okay for our car, but careful of the idiots
coming DOWN the mountain at 45 MPH!

RUNNING FREE - Merlin - really enjoyed the trip and short hike for
this one!

B STREET BOX - Grant's Pass - nice spot and stamp!

RUCKMAN #1/2 - Grant's Pass - really enjoyed the walk for these two
boxes! Watch the second set of clues though! Be prepared to "think
outside the box"!

ENDANGERED WILDLIFE - another great little stamp in the same trail
system as the Ruckmans! Took us two tries as we misread the initial
directions!

SCHROEDER - Grants Pass - Toast. If this box was anywhere near where
the clues suggested, it would be buried under 6 or 7 feet of poison
oak, nettles and barbed creeper vines.

So there you have it! We were hoping to ind 1 or 2 others, but, as
per usual, time was all too short!

Pete aka Jiggs
