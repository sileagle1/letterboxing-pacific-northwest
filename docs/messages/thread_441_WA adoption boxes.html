From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-07-19 21:26:20 UTC
I sent e mails to Catbird via her address on her web site but have
not yet heard back. I visited the "Little Happiness" box in Kelsey
Creek and it is gone. I recall now that someone here reported it
missing but the mystery reptile box is still in place.

I suspect that Lizard and Catbird pulled the boxes before moving.

I sent Jay a note saying that I would adopt the lone Bridle Trails SP
box, thus I have 9 in this park. The lone BTSP box has been very
heavily visited, often by family groups.

Tomorrow I will get an early start and do Rattlesnake Ledge to both
get the box and check on status and see if there is a contact address
with the box. If not I will adopt that one as well, perhaps placing
a second box along the way.

After a pause of a few weeks I will be able to check on other boxes
in the area.

Hoopoe
Redmond WA
