This box is part of Matt the Rat's 45th Parallel Series. The Rat is responsible for the
stamp and fold-out while the roving Funhog took care of the logbook and clue.

Find the town represented by zipcode 97057. Make your way to the town's US Postal
Service letterbox. Across the street, you will see a couple of stiff ol' buckaroos
swapping talltales. Take note of the wall behind them because you will find a broken
bit of this same material hiding the letterbox. Look to the east where the town's
moniker is emblazoned on a huge field of red. Walk to the eastern side of this
structure. Counting fifteen bats from the stair, you will find the letterbox at your feet.
