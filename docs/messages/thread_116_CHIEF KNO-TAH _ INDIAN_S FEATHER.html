From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-26 20:48:34 UTC
(Blushing) Thanks for the praise. My head would be in danger of
getting really big but I was fortunate enough to recieve in the mail
today a postcard from a famous east coast letterboxer with some
stamps that really blew me away. So I'm back to my humble self. :)

If you want to know how I carved the chief visit our page at:
http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/PSinst.htm

I used that method plus I had Grace (21 months old) crawling over me
(and "helping") while I worked. I think she was the secret charm!
All I used was the number one speedball blade and x-acto knife. X-
acto just used for the outer shape. For this stamp I made the
printout negative so I could carve away the black - sometimes that
can be easier on a detailed stamp where you need to carve away lots
of little things.

Ditto the wish that Wingnut places more boxes, certainly a great
first box!
