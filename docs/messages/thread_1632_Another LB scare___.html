From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-21 04:00:46 UTC
Hey, we had a slightly different 'scare' the other day while
letterboxing in Seattle. We were following Ryan's sadistic Seattle
Walking Tour clues and along the way 'rnrB' had to use the
facilities. Well, seeing that Seattle recently spent millions of
$$$ on these nifty self-cleaning public toilets throughout the city,
we passed by one and I decided we would 'check it out'.
The 'traffic light' sign on the outside of it said the 'facility'
was empty, so we pushed the button and the door opened. To my
astonishment, out of the 'empty' bathroom came a whole litany of
profanity and two very unkempt people (man & woman) who hurriedly
tried to hide their drug paraphanelia (sp?) from view of passersby.
We hightailed it out of there (2 little kids towed by Mom's
deathgrip!) and thankfully the 'occupants' were too busy trying
to 'clean up' that they couldn't chase after us. So, yes, chalk up
another interesting 'experience' along our letterboxing adventure!!!
