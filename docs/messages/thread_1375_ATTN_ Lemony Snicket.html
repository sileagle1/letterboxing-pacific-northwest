Disappointment was indeed the theme for the day. It seems that the
road is almost entirely overgrown at this point (at least at the
start) though we persevered and found it on the third pass. But

efforts were too slow since Count Olaf escaped our clutches.

she vaguely remembered being in the area having read a clue for it a
number of months back. I rolled my eyes wondering what the chances
were of finding a second letterbox in the area not really having any
idea where to look. Amanda searched half a dozen places and darn nab
