2 New LB at Detroit Lake

From: shebearof3 (thyost@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-05-27 16:36:45 UTC
This year for our family camping trip to Detroit Lake, we planted 2
LBs. We also went on a wonderful hike through old growth forest at
South Brightenbush Gorge trail. If you have time after getting the
LBs I would encourage you to explore it. You can get trail info from
the Detroit Ranger station across the road from Detroit Lake State
Park on Hwy 22.

You can get the clues for the Stahlman Trail series at the
lwtterboxing website. (www.letterboxing.org)

I hope LB friends in Salem will take the trip and let me know when
you find them. I will probably not be back again till next Memorial
Day to check on them.
