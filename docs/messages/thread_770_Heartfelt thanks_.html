I'd like to take a moment to thank each and everyone of you that has
contacted me during the past week concerning RayvenMom's stay in the
hospital. For those of you who didn't know, she went into the hospital for
Laparoscopic Anti-Reflux Surgery, which is fairly easy and common surgery.
During the surgery, and I am to thankful that the doctor was a very good
doctor, there was a bleeder in her abdomen. They were unable to locate it,
and had it not been discovered, she may have very well bled out after the
surgery. As it was, the 4 small incisions were not enough for them to
repair the bleeder and they had to cut her abdomen open to locate, and stop,
the bleeder.

She is resting and will be coming home either today or tomorrow,
depending and again, I would like to thank everyone for their caring words
and support during this.
