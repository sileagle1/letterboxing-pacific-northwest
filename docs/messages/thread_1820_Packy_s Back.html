From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-12-29 01:14:25 UTC

Thanks, Maiden, for the heads up on this box. As of this afternoon, it's back amongst
the living. I knew when I first hid this box last year that it would have a limited
lifespan (unlike the amazing Winking Eagle) This is the third incarnation of Packy's
Place. My fingers are crossed that this one will be there for the next visitor.

As for the long-lived Eagle by Windairen, I gave it a quick check before I left the Zoo
this afternoon. There it was, sitting out in the light of day, for all to see. I place it,
once again, beneath the meager remains of 'grounded timber.' This box has been
moved and rehidden again and again by kindly groundskeepers and has greeted
many visitors in its three years at the Zoo. On my next visit, I think I'll have to stash a
log in my tote bag as first aid for this old timer. How do you smuggle in a log
discreetly? Funhog
