RE: [LbPNW] Inexpensive Carving Tools

From: pandora{I`B} (hispandora@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-10-05 23:11:03 UTC-07:00
Lily the links IB gave you are for exactly where we purchased the tools from
for the Stamp Camp Starter kits... you need to look on Dick Blick for "lino
cutters" and "pen nib holders" the handles should be about $.35 each and the
blades should be relatively cheaper than when you buy then in the store and
come in packs of 12.

As for carving material -- the stamp camp kits came with Mastercarve --
purchased at Columbia Art in Downtown Portland. -- I don't know for sure
what Mitch uses in his kids carving -- it may be a little less advanced then
the age level you are teaching, I know Mitch's instructions were geared for
children that were not old enough to use sharp tools. (I am not sure if that
is what you are looking for with girl scouts.) -- you should be able to
order the tools from the kids relatively easy if you look for what I told
you and spend some time on the site trying to find it.

Pandora
