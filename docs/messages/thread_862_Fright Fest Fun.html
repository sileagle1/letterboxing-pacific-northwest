I had a great time. Met wonderful enthusiastic fun filled people.
Everyone seemed to agree that there the bounty of boxes was
thrilling. I think some of the newcomers got a chance to see the
variety of stamps techniques and got ideas. I talked about my
favorite clues and certainly met with some challenge at finding some
of the hidden boxes at Wapato.

There are some tricks up the sleeve, starting with one reminiscent of
my favorite T&E box..... starting at the correct parking lot!

I took away a postal LB from Pandora and Iron Bear. I was touched by
Iron Bears story inside and I am honored that Pandora choose this one
for me. I know someone who needs this box next, both for healing and
in thanks, so I will send it on Monday.

Now I am off to plant a box carved by Pandora and will be "putting up
some BIG numbers"

Thanks to Steve and the rest of the gang for a great time.
