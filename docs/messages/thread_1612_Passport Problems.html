I would just get a new passport. If you mail your application in, within a
week or two your new passport will arrive. It's pretty easy if you mail in
your expired passport.

Most post offices have the paperwork or one can download it from the
internet thingy. Then there are regional passport offices available if you
need to go in person. Just make sure you send in 2 photos of yourself that
match the required demensions. If it's your first passport, I think you
have to go in person with the required birth certificate. If you don't have
that, it's a bit more complicated. See the passport's webpage for those
details.

Once you have your valid passport, it shows absolute proof of who you are
(which is a good thing in these times).
