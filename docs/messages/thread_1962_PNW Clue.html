The mystery has been solved, the guilty parties brought to justice!

A special thank you to all that attended all 30 of you! We filled the
banquet room at Wall Street Pizza with lots of laughter and plenty of fun.

We were having so much fun we outlasted our reservation, so we moved down
the street two blocks to Main Street Park where we enjoyed not only each
others company, but also the gorgeous weather. Several people left from
there to find some of the many local boxes. I can't wait to hear which ones
were found. I personally went and checked on Mother May I, and yes the first
box is still in place.

I'll be posting pictures of the fun at www.lbworld.org throughout the day
today.

Again, thank you to my family, Pupp and Jenn, Natalie, Cherokee, J Man, Team
Ravynhaus, Bookworm, Camp Fire Lady, David from Beaverton, Der Mad Stamper,
the J Rock Family, Erfellie, Sandy, MiniLes, Bethany and family, Old Vet,
Quilting Noni, Gramma Star, and Touche Turtle for making this so much fun!
