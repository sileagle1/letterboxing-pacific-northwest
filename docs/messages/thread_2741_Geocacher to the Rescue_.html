Geocacher to the Rescue!

From: Danny McMillin (macdanny@mindspring.com) | Date: 2007-03-02 16:38:23 UTC-08:00
I got an email from a Seattle Geocacher. He found my adopted
letterbox, Mee-Kwa-Mooks Lowtide, scattered in the middle of the
trail in the park of the same name.

I have my email address in the letterbox; so he was able to contact
me through that. The good news is he recovered everything; so the
letterbox and its beautiful hand-carved stamp will live again. He was
kind enough to drop it off at my house!!!

This is the second time this letterbox has been vandalized. I will do
a better job finding a new hiding spot. I've temporarily marked this
letterbox as "retired" until it has a new home.

Cheers.
