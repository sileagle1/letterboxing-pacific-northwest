Re: Our 500th find!!!

From: wingnut_lb (wingnut_lb@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-25 22:12:19 UTC
Any suggestions to those of us how falter and smudge along the way?

I have taken to bringing along a few blank index cards. At times
that I do not feel like carrying my log book and my dd's log book,
(both are rather large and heavy) I just stamp a card and tape it in
my book later.... this is nice also when you are doing a series, and
maybe don't find them all in the beginning, (you can put them
together when you get them all) or run into other boxers along the
trail and have a different place you log exchanges in at.

I also kinda scrap book my journal, so it lets me play a bit an add
photos and things I find along the trail into my log book too! :)

A friend of mine new to boxing had a neat idea. They were looking
for their first journal, and ran across one of those inexpensive
photo albums... the little 4x6 ones, they stamp cards and they
slide into the album in what ever order they wish, and can add photos
and tokens in the pages also..... I was thinking I may do this next
time! I am almost in need of a new journal myself...

Wingnut
