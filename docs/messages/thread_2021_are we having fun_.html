sad to say we went to check the status of our Whale of a Time box in
Comox BC and it is missing!!waa waa.
Then we went down the road to attempt to find the Poem Box and alas,
it is missing too. We searched every single hole. I have contacted the
placer of that one. We have had so little success in finding
letterboxes. Lat week we tried to find about 11 different boxes in 3
different series and they were missing!! The kids still have smiles,
though not as big and not nearly as enthusiastic as when we first
started 'hunting'. Planting is seemingly, at this point, so much more
rewarding.
But all is not lost. A fellow boxer who lives about an hour away has
offered to take us out in search of their boxes!
yippee!!
