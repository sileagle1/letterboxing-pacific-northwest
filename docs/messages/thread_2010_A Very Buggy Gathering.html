A big THANKYOU to all the Spokane, WA area letterboxers who came out
for A Very Buggy Gathering. It was so nice to meet the people
behind the stamps and LB journeys that I have enjoyed so much.

force behind us. I don't think we would have gone far without her

big "Thanks!" goes to Blackvelvetray, who was so kind to make a

you.

Check out the "photos" section on this site. I forgot to pull out
the camera until we were pretty much done, but there are a couple to
share.

Thanks again to everyone who made this such a fun day, it just goes
to show that Letterboxers (of any age) are just about the nicest
people you'll ever meet.
