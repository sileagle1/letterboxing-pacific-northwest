FF2: Booooogie in the Park!

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@lbworld.org) | Date: 2004-10-19 19:50:07 UTC-07:00
Just a quick reminder for everyone.......

Come and share in the greatest traditions of All Hallows Eve as we
celebrate a northwest tradition, The second annual Halloween Gathering.
This year, we are holding this event at Millersylvania State Park, just
outside Olympia. From I5, take exit #95 and follow the signs to the
entrance. It looks like we'll be in Kitchen #5, right next to the Camper
check in booth, shouldn't be to hard to find is.

This years event, aptly named FF2: Booooo-gie in the Park - Echoes of
Times Past plans on being much larger than last year's event. Team Chaos
and Team Rayvenhaus are jointly hosting this event and are each planting
a series of boxes for this event.

What we would like to also offer is for anyone who can not make it to
this gathering but would still like to participate in some way, to go
ahead and carve up a themed stamp, make a box and ship it to us and we
would be happy to plant and maintain it, listing you as the creator of
that box. If you'd like to take us up on this, contact RayvenMom at
rayvenmom 'at' lbworld.org and she'll give you the snail mail address to
send it to.

We invite everyone that can make it to come and join us and meet some of
the people behind the posts. There will be a potluck so please bring a
covered dish, a salad, something to eat, enough for your group and share
with your friends. Bring your own sodas or other assorted drinks, (minus
alcohol of course, this is a dry park). Team Chaos, Team Rayvenhaus and
CampFireLady are providing burgers and hot dogs with all the fixings for
40 people.

Although this is a Halloween themed gathering, this is not a costume
party of any sort.

Looking forward to seeing everyone there!

Also, here's the info page for the state park:

http://www.parks.wa.gov/parkpage.asp?selectedpark=Millersylvania&pageno=1


Driving directions:

Millersylvania State Park is located ten miles south of Olympia, Wash.,
on the state's western side.


From I-5:
Take exit #95. Drive east, and follow signs to park (about three miles
northeast of the freeway).

Park opens at 8:00am and closes at 5:00pm (Winter Schedule) We're going
to be there bright and early to secure a spot. The gathering is
scheduled to "begin" at 10:00 am and end at 4:00 pm, however, the park
hours are 8:00 til 5:00 and you're welcome to be there as long as you
want to be there!

Current reports indicate that there are 17 boxes (At the least) planted
for this event plus an even dozen or more in the park and surrounding areas.

We are certainly looking forward to everyone coming and joining us in
the enjoyment of our hobby!
