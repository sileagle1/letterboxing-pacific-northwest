From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-28 23:29:24 UTC
Ensure you have the new clues to the letterbox as I had to modify it
when I found it missing about 5 months ago and replaced it with a
different letterbox in a different location. I am quite diligent
about keeping the status of my boxes/clues accurate (I strive to post
changes within 12 hours of making them/learning about them) so before
you set out on hunts for any of our boxes, please ensure you have
checked for any changes to the clues--unfortunately, I don't get many
comments from folks about visiting my boxes (especially my 'orphan'
boxes out there)--as soon as I get wind that a box may be missing, I
post that info to the board if I am unable to clarify it further with
the folks who reported it as possibly missing or if I'm unable to
physically check on it in a reasonable amount of time. Of course, I
wouldn't rely on a missing report either. Since my experience has
been that for every missing report I receive, I'd guess that 30% of
them are actually missing.

Sorry for your frustration. I hope you enjoyed your visit to the
area despite not finding the box,

dvn2r ckr
