OK I already uploaded the clues to the new national site,
(http://letterboxing.wesgarrison.us/) then copied and pasted here
also, have fun!

Planted by WingNut_LB

Find the Chief Kno-Tah Mystery box, and you are on your way.
(http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/knotah.htm)

Within his stomping grounds you will find a lone standing smoke
signal maker from modern times, different than all the rest. Look
between it's cracking remains, among stones that move, you will find
what you seek.

Note: This can be a very heavily used area, use care for the watchful
eyes of passer bys while searching. It can be extremely difficult to
access during summer months, especially summer weekends as the
location is used frequently for picnics and bbq's. Early Mornings and
off season is best. Also this is a micro-box. So be sure to bring a
pen and stamp pad. Happy Hunting!
Before you set out, please read the waiver of responsibility and
disclaimer.
