So did anyone else hear what I did? The 2 lost hikers on Mt. Hood
found a geocache hanging in a tree, which contained the lat/lon
coordinates. They were able to figure out where they were, and start
working their way off the mtn. Rescued today, happy and healthy. How
would you like to be the owner of a geocache that may have saved a
couple lives? Amazing.
