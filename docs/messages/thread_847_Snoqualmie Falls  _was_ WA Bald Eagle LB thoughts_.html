Snoqualmie Falls #3 was not retrieved before the rains. Because
it's well in the flood plain I tried to make the latest version
extra water tight, and hopfuly with the rocks piled on top it might
pull thru. It'll be fun to find out. #3's hiding place has always
be at risk to high waters, but I really wanted it at the base of the
falls and was willing to take the risk. If it gets washed away,
I'll make another next spring.

Went out to see Snoqualmie Falls last night with Twilight and
Sunshine (safely, from the upper observation level) and it's just
AMAZING.
