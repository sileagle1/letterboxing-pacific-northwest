Hi everyone,

Well, twice during the Silver Falls weekend, I misplaced my signature stamp.
I guess it was so happy in such a beautiful park that it wanted to stay.
The last time I lost it, I'm sure it was at Box 3 in Funhog's series Wood
and Water, because I didn't use it again until I tried to the next morning
at the Calypso Orchid. We were sitting above the log on the trail to avoid
the hornets, and there was much stamping and handing around going on, as
SloMo and Wood aug arrived as we were placing a HH. We did this walk in the
evening of July 4, so I think it's not likely anybody was there after us -
most of you had gone home already. But if anybody should happen to visit
that box and find my sig stamp, I'd be so happy to have it back. It's easy
enough to recarve a replacement, but it's my very first one. I'm kind of
sad about it.

If you didn't happen to exchange with me and know what it looks like, I do
have a picture in my AQ profile.

Thanks so much, and again, so lovely to meet all of you. Thanks Pungent Bob
and Funhog for the great trip!

Lisa

P.S. - did anybody pick up the Pungent after I hid it again? I told several
people who had already found it, where it was, but don't know what happened
after that. We re-hid it in Group Camp C after the rangers came and began
cleaning up A.
