Ahh, so you fell into that trap, too, eh? At least you had a more
pleasant walk than my letterboxing buddy and I did... We were there
WHILE they were doing the backhoe work, and after some rather
illicit behavior had evidently occurred. (If you see magazine
pieces, don't look at them! Baaaaad!)

All three of the Mt. Vernon boxes by alorente/Whizbang/Windairen are
defunct. My friend and I looked for them for two days and employed
(discretely) numerous resources, but to no avail.

Darth Vader has been moved to a new location by park staff during
clearing, and has possibly been removed entirely since by vandals.
The clues as posted will not lead you to it, either way.

The Pride of the City may still be in place, but the path has long
since grown over and is (short of with the aide of a machete) not
accessible. That is, if it's still even there. However,
considering the amount of use of the area by homeless and other
individuals engaging in unhealthy behavior, I doubt the boxes are
still there.

Albert the Elf is more elusive, at best. We followed the clues as
well as we could, including hints and solutions from the placer, but
could not find the "broken up" object that hides the letterbox.

We were really frustrated, as these were our first failures at
finding boxes for which we'd set out to find. I had thought the
clues would be taken down or the boxes improved, as they are
obviously not functional as they are, but I guess I was wrong.

Way to look for the positive in everything, though!
