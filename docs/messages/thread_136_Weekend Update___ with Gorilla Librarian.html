From: payntonm (perky@bung.org) | Date: 2003-04-28 16:47:50 UTC
Trust a native Seattleite not to let a gorgeous day go unappreciated!
I spent the day yesterday collecting Candlelight's St. Edwards Park
trio. I've been to that park several times before, but yesterday it
became one of my all-time favorites. Lots of other folks had the
same idea too! Box #2 in particular was tricky to get and replace
without folks catching on, but I'm pretty sure I managed it. Great
stamps and super great hiding places all!

Oh, and be sure to check out the brand new children's play area
adjacent to the orchard. Volunteers were out in droves yesterday to
finish up this stunning castle for the kiddies. It'll make a nice
resting place for adults who just finished that Grotto Trail climb
too! Whew!
