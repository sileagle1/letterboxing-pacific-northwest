white image on a muted, medium green shirt. This also has a nice, woodsy

shape is so fitting of the PNW (especially lately, huh?. If you guys want
to stay with the lime green and orange - what color would set it off? Hmmm

found that a dark, forest green seems to fight the least with the lime
green. Browns should work with orange but I couldn't find a brown that
complimented the patch colors as well as the dark green. Maybe someone

It's been a long time since I've done a T-shirt design but I imagine this is
all contingent on what color long sleeve shirts are available. So the above
may be all for not. ?

is - I won't buy it because I wouldn't wear it. Long sleeve T-shirt prices
are all over the place, but as of late, I've spotted some as low as $20 . .
. at an airport, no less! Of course that's retail, with a logo - we should
be able to get shirts for less. Some crafts stores carry blank T-shirts for
crafters that are surprisingly low priced. Mostly white though.
