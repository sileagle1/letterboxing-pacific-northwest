Evening.

Yep I think you got it. The cooties are created in a fashion that they are
intended to be passed along from Letterboxer to Letterboxer. You can call it
an infected personal traveler of sorts. But once it is handed to another
Letterboxer it is theirs to then pass on to someone else. The people who
created them - myself included in that as begin the designer -drawer- of
their little DNA's -chuckles- had are of the mind set that Letterboxing is
also a social activity. Which it is - it is enjoyable to talk about -
therefore perhaps a cootie or two will be a good ice breaker - and a small
beginning to having more people meet up together to enjoy this wonderful
hobby.

So infect the North West with cooties - and soon cooties will rule the
world!! -smiles-

Iron`Bear

RE: [LbPNW] Cooties

From: IronBear (ironbear@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-09-09 01:07:09 UTC-07:00
Evening

Actually all I did was draw the cooties - pandora carved them and
CampFireLady made up the log books and boxes that they go in. A cootie is
sort of personal traveler hybrid - which is sort of like an exchange of
personal stamps. You can only get them physically from one Letterboxer to
another. How you receive them is up to the creativity of the one that holds
to cootie. You may find it in your bag - or tent - handed to you directly -
or taped to a card at the foot of your trailer - like what happened at the
Stamp Cam Event in August. However I would care to bet that there will be
cooties floating around at the FF2 event coming up so you may want to
consider coming if at all possible - since they are not an actual letterbox
or anything of that sort I believe that Charlie Trail Duster has images of
some of them on his website. Perhaps we would be so inclined to share that
address with you to take a look at if you are interested.

Iron`Bear
