I had company in town yesterday and I convinced them to try
letterboxing with me. We started off and found the #2 Duck of Duck,
Duck, Goose. We continued and found the Goose. Since we had started
off in the middle of the hunt, we returned to my car. My visitors
decided that they would rather go to the mall to do some after-
Christmas shopping than backtrack to the First Duck :-(

So I can only report on part of this series. It is another quality
job by Green Guillemot.
