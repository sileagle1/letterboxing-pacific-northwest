I'm really sorry to hear that, since that HH has special
meaning for me. The first letterbox I ever found contained
Alfred. It was in Spokane, WA and I brought it back with
me to Texas where I placed it in my very first letterbox
back in September, 2002. There were no letterboxes near
me back then, and only about 10 in the entire state of
Texas, which is why a vacation to Spokane area provided
my first chance to see what letterboxing was all about.
What are the odds my first box would contain a HH
(which I knew nothing about at the time), let alone one
as famous as Hitch. It prompted me to start placing my
own boxes, and the rest is history. Thanks for helping
get me started!

Silver Eagle
