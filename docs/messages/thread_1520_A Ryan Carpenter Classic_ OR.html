After three years and three months in the wild, The Ball Point
Letterbox now is proud to display the Sign of the Hog, it's first
actual signature stamp! Granted, a couple of groups of hikers found
the box and signed the book this last spring but mine is the very
first official visit by a letterboxer.

Yes, this is a remote box but... the hike is wonderful; there are two
places to camp within a dozen miles AND this is an early oevre, not
exactly what the Green Turtle is producing of late. I consider it a
collectible. This box was hidden in May, 2001 B.A.(Before Amanda)So
if you have a hankering to get to a Ryan box before Nancy Drew's
image graces a page, this is your chance. Funhog
