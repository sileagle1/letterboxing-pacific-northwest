Lonely boxes

From: Kris Leibrand (bookwormlb@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-03-20 19:37:04 UTC-08:00
I've finally been able to get out and do some boxing in the last couple of
weeks. I'm finding a lot of VERY lonely boxes; some with no visitors in
more than a year! Just thought I'd give you a run down of what I've found:



Great Outdoors #6 - all 3 in good condition

Great Outdoors #5 - both there and healthy

Great Outdoors #2 - there and fine

Companions - alive and well, but VERY lonely! This is an awesome stamp, so
you should all rush out there and get it.

Robin Hood Festival #1 - no luck finding this one. Anyone seen it recently?

Robin Hood Festival #2 - box is in place with only the stamp, no log book

Jump Dog - alive and well. Also a VERY nice stamp.

To the Bat Cave - no box there. I'm sure I was in the right place, so it
may be missing. Anyone have an update?

Who Loves Lasagna? - alive and well. Watch out for the car key eating
rocks!!!!

Circus Memories #4 - alive and well (didn't check the other boxes in this
series because I already had them)

Three Firs - this box is definitely gone.

Painters Palette - alive and well

Series of the Elements - all alive and well (a nice series, too)

Care Bear Stare - Found 4 out of 5. I imagine the 5th one is there, but I
ran out of time to look for it.

Life Cycle of the Butterfly - Boxes 2 & 3 are there. 1 and 4 are confirmed
missing.

Club Tahlequlah - alive and well (fun clues, too!)

Jerry Garcia Tree- alive and well

Family Forest Letterbox Series - Found 1 & 2 alive and well, but ran out of
time for box 3. This is an awesome place! These boxes have had very few
visitors in their life time. You are all missing out if you don't go find
them!

Letterboox - An AMAZING stamp. You must go get this one too.

First Shoe Tied - alive and well

Colaptus Curatus - alive and well

Favorite Hat - alive and well

Anna's Hummingbirds - #1 is there, couldn't find 2 or 3 (I think 3 is
missing), and ran out of time for the rest.

What's for Dinner - Didn't find #1, found #2, ran out of time for the rest

Who Let the Dogs Out? - Great series, all alive and well

Musician's Series - all alive and well

U-2 - alive and well

Johnny Lang - alive and well

Texas Star - alive and well

The Pearl - alive and well



I have a couple that are on my list to do this week that I wanted to see if
I can get an update on. With all the rain and the flooding we had in
January & February does anyone know the status of Adele, Quackers, Smith &
Bybee Turtles and any others that are close to water?



Anyone else have boxes to report on?



Bookworm
