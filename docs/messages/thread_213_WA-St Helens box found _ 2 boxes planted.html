WA-St Helens box found & 2 boxes planted

From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2003-05-21 01:55:58 UTC-04:00
My family spent last weekend at Mount Saint Helens, our annual trek in honor
of the anniversary of its eruption (May 18, 1980). I'm happy to say we found
Eliza B's Eagle Cliff LB--tho the clues are not as straightforward as they
appear--a LOT has changed in the area since the box was planted. If you are
one of those who delights in the "golden triangle" that was discussed
recently on the main list--meaning you really enjoy figuring out how the
landscape has changed since the box was planted, you should stop reading
because I'm about to talk about that. But if you'd like helpful clues to the
clues, read on....

The main difference is that the picnic area has been extended--so it would
now be more accurate if the clue read "Follow the trail under the bridge and
past the *first picnic table* to a ..." Also, the "clearing" has now become
a little forest of lodgepole pine. But other than THAT :-), the box was
right where Liza said it was. And we were only the second group to stamp in,
so git yourselves up to MSH, people! We planted two more boxes in the
general area to help entice you up there. (I'll include a notice on this
list when their clues are posted.)
