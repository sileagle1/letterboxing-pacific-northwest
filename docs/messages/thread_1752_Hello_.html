My letterboxing pack always has a sheet of plastic in
it for those not-so-dry sitting places. You will also
always find a large, dry cleaner-sized plastic bag in
my kit. You can put the log books, your ink and your
arms all the way inside for dry stamping. A few drops
of rain on a logbook which is then resealed in a baggie
can lead to fuzzy, smelly mold in short order, something
I do my best to avoid. Funhog
