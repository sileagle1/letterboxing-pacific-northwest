I'm sending this to everyone who has contacted me about a troop in the
East Portland/Gresham area.

First, our troop status - in order to be an official troop, we need 3
more people/families to join Dog Scouts of America. Being a member of
the organization gives you access to many membership resources, allows
your dog to gain the Dog Scout and Pack Dog titles, and will be a
requirement for participating in troop activities. You can join
online or print a membership form at
http://www.dogscouts.org/Membership.html

I have started a Yahoo group for the troop, unimaginatively named
dsapdx, you can join at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/dsapdx/join
All events and activities will be announced and discussed there and I
hope you will join even if you aren't sure if you want to join the
troop.

In the meantime, we will be having our first get together Saturday
October 24th at 1pm in Vance Park. We'll be meeting on the north end
(the end next to Park Place Coffee) under / around the big pine tree.

Vance Park is on 181st between SE Stark and SE Division. It has a
Portland address, but is a Gresham city park. There is a map here -
http://tinyurl.com/yhzpp28

We will practice polite dog greetings and go looking for the letterbox
in the park. And yes, we will be meeting rain or shine, this is
Oregon. :-) The meeting is open to everyone, but I recommend that you
only bring one dog per person.

Before the meeting, please read the article "Teaching dogs to greet
each other politely" from the Dog Scouts site:

http://www.dogscouts.org/uploads/Teaching_dogs_to_greet_each_other_politely_1_.doc

OR http://tinyurl.com/politedog

You may also want to read about letterboxing on www.letterboxing.org
or www.atlasquest.com There is a letterboxing badge, but unless your
dog already has and is trained to wear a pack, getting this letterbox
will not count toward the badge (but you can always come back for it
and you will learn what you need to to earn the badge on your own.)

What to bring:
Poop Cleanup Bags (at least 3)
Dog water bowl
Dog treats (LOTS of Dog Treats!!!)

Letterboxing supplies if you have them
Your dogs personal stamp (you can start with any rubber stamp)
Letterboxing log book or paper
Stamp pad
Pen
Compass
Dog Backpack and Backpacking supplies (http://tinyurl.com/packdog)

Hope to see you October 24th!

Julie
