Team Garlic planted two boxes in Seattle today.

Julie's Box
http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=31028

http://www.atlasquest.com/lboxes/showboxinfo.html?gBoxId=31055

prayers will remain, even if the box doesn't.
