Well I can only assume that you are talking about Camp Fire Lady and I and
the Here Kitty Kitty Boxes - So I will step up to the podium and comment. I

distasteful to make a post like this anyways - if you had a real problem
with it perhaps you should have emailed one of us directly and we could have
discussed this privately, but since you have put it out for discussion
amongst the group, let's discussed it a bit.



Using this example - Camp Fire Lady placed her boxes on July 1st and gave me
the clues to beta test them. This has become a pretty common thing in our
little group, I give clues to Maiden and Camp Fire Lady sometimes as well
before posting them on the main board to make sure that there are no
problems with the clues. Iron`Bear, Sim Kitty and I headed out to box with
Camp Fire Lady in the Battleground/Vancouver area on the third. We hit about
18 boxes that day, including boxes by Funhog, Soggy2Pair, Team Nashoba and
Camp Fire Lady herself. (We got the Bookworm Letterbox also after trying to
figure out the clues for several months) - Since I had the clues for her
Here Kitty Kitty in order to beta test them we decided to hit those too. And
since I had Sim Kitty with me (who unlike most people's kids doesn't get to
go with us all the time as we only have her every other weekend or so) and
she has a fierce love of cats we saved the Here Kitty Kitty series until the
last thing we did that day and used it to celebrate her 50th find, letting
her find a box or two, run around the park and so on. So the box obviously
counted as a find for me and for Sim Kitty and the firstie was well deserved
and was split between me and Sim Kitty - a little 7-year-old girl that's
never got a firstie before and who got rewarded with a new letterboxing
journal - a real one instead of the little notepad she was using as an
incentive for her effort and accomplishment in getting 50 boxes.



There are all sorts of things people do with their clues - heck I have clues
to letterboxes you don't even get unless you get them from me personally
meaning unless I want you to have them! I post my clues after some people
have found them. Heck I made my firstie prizes, I made my letterbox, I
planted it, if I want to give someone the clues and let them go get it
before I post the clues to the main board that is totally up to me. I'm the
one that spent the time and money on it.



Many other letter boxers do the same thing Willow - perhaps if you planted a
box or two you would decide to do the same thing also. I know I have heard
Ryan and Amanda talk before about how Ryan plants a box while Amanda is off
somewhere else planting one or something and then Ryan gives her the clues
and she goes for his. There is an entire topic on beta testing going on a
different list right now and what a wonderful idea it is. Heck both Maiden
and Camp Fire Lady found errors in my recent clues.



Personally I like getting the firstie prizes too, just because I think they
are cool and make a neat decoration for my log book, especially Camp Fire
Lady's with her little shrinky dinks - but firsties is not what letterboxing
is about - its another one of those added little bonuses. Most people don't
even put first finder prizes in their letterboxes. Heck I've had firsties on
tons of letterboxes and got nothing in return. Personally I don't throw a
temper tantrum if I don't pick up the firstie prize, for me it's about
finding the box.



You are right about one thing Willow I do pride myself on following the
rules of the hunt and the game, but there is no rule saying my friends can't
test my boxes or find my boxes before you, or that I can't give one group of
people the clues before another, has for my "preaching" on the board - I
don't even post that much anymore since I left the main list, mainly because
I got tired of attitudes just like yours.



Shame on us Willow? Shame on you is more like it - you're childlike temper
tantrum and need to publicly slap at people for doing something you don't
like because you got greedy without having the information before hand is
insulting. I tell you what - so that you are not so offended and don't feel
like you are getting the "wrath" that you are so willingly handing out to
people, the next time I find a box with a firstie prize in it, in your area,
I will write on the back "Please don't take this pandora is leaving it for
Willow" and we can call it even.



Posts like this and you wonder why you are opening cans of worms?

Amen!
I believe the clues and letterboxes are owned by the placer before,
after,during creation, while residing in a location, and during the
period that it is posted for public finding. The placer has complete
discretion at their whim whether to pull the clues or post the clues.
Finders can search for letterboxes only when clues are furnished by
placers in what ever form they choose, whether it be listed on LBNA,
other sites or by word of mouth. I'm sure that there are several
placers that use letterboxes as a gift(see Appology on the main list
for an example), and these letterboxes may or may not reach a public
arena. Gwen and I don't use first Finder certs, and really don't
care to put our letterboxes out just to increase someone's F count.
We are only concerned about the location, and the experience we are
leading you to in our letterboxes. Hopefully you will find the
letterbox , but if you don't then enjoy the moment.
