From: Steve S. (kerjin@myndworx.com) | Date: 2003-09-27 07:52:04 UTC-07:00
*This message was transferred with a trial version of CommuniGate(tm) Pro*


You need to only bring yourself and a covered dish of some kind. Nothing
else is required, though anything else is greatly appreciated. Bring your
current personal stamp for exchanges, but nothing else is needed. If you
want to carve a new traveler, by all means, dive in, the more stamps the
merrier I say.

As far as what we of Team Rayvenhaus are doing, other than the food and the
fellowship, is....

We are carving a new 'Themed' series to be placed for that day, with a
bonus. Find all of the hidden ones and show proof and there's a double
secret probation letterbox that you'll be privy to. We are also taking
boxes that others from around the country in the letterbox community want to
create and send along and placing them in the area as well. We are carving
a new Hitchhiker to release that day AND have carved a new sig stamp for
Rayvenhaus which is, in my opinion, just too funny and fitting for this
family.

Now, if there are any other activities that anyone else thinks might be a
great idea, please don't hesitate to let us know now so we can coordinate
them. We'd be happen to add anything that we can to this gathering to make
it more fun for all.

Please, if you have ideas, drop us a line off list and let us know.

Steve of Rayvenhaus
