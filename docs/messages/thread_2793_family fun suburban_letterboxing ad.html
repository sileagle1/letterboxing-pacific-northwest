family fun suburban/letterboxing ad

From: Jennifer Schramm (schramm.jennifer@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-06-12 18:33:24 UTC
So I just got my new Family Fun magazine and the darn thing opened right away to this heavy
stock page with pull up flaps all about the Chevy Suburban and it is called "Welcome to
Letterbox Camp". I'm all for advertising, but with our "leave no trace" ethic, wouldn't it be
better for us to be sponsored by some fuel efficient car than the gas guzzling Suburban????

The back of the ad is all about letterboxing and even provides an image of the Suburban for
you to xerox and make into a stamp (ugg!)...at least they're encouraging people to make their
own stamps! It actually is pretty well done...Suburbans just bug me.

I tried the familyfun link at the bottom (www.familyfun.com/letterboxcamp), but it doesn't
seem to work (yet?).

Just thought I'd throw out my opinion and let those of you who don't get the magazine know
about the ad.

Team Shroom
