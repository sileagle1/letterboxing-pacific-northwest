Tryon Creek Letterboxes: OR

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-03-29 21:03:31 UTC
This morning's meeting with Ranger Karen went pretty darned well. I think she now
has a good idea of how our pastime differs from geocaching, which has been the
source of ongoing problems in the park. Her concerns are pretty much the same as
those expressed by Kyle Spinks of THPRD: environmental damage and visitor safety.

I got a definite 'maybe' from her, which I consider much better than a definite 'no!'
She is now considering the possibility of incorporating some form of letterboxing into
their Junior Ranger Program, as is Silver Falls SP. I have offered to walk the trails with
a staff member to locate the TC boxes and identify any that might pose a problem.

It also turns out that she is the park manager at Milo McIver SP and is totally aware
that there are boxes hidden in this park as well. I described last summer's Stamp
Camp at McIver to her. Regarding letterboxing here, she felt that this area does not
receive the volume of traffic that Tryon Creek does and is less likely to see the type of
damage that concerns her.

Everyone... cross your fingers, stay on the trails and don't hurt the trilliums! Funhog
