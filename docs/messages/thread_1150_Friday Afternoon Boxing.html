Friday Afternoon Boxing

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-02-20 21:37:45 UTC-08:00
I picked Bindle Babe up from her hotel at 2:30 this afternoon and we hit the
road for some serious letterboxing. We found all four of the boxes at the
zoo in great shape. Then we headed up the hill to the Hoyt Arboretum. We
didn't find the Tres Foil box and fear it's MIA, we did however find
Tribute. The kids LOVED the location. They spent a long time walking on
the fallen tree.

We finished off our trip with Little Bunny Foo Foo, (Pandora, if you're
reading this, the log book is full and the stamp is broken in two pieces).

Overall we found 6 boxes and the weather and company couldn't have been any
better!

Melanie
