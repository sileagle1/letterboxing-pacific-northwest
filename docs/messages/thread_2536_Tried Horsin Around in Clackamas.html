Tried Horsin Around in Clackamas

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2006-06-01 23:23:56 UTC
I tried Horsin Around on my lunch hour in the Clackamas area. I am
temporarily working out here and thought I could take a look.

However, when I went to the park, I ran into trouble.

Seems that some humans wanted to block the troll hideouts with a big
fence that said "Construction, do not enter". This fence blocked off
a huge chunk of the park. I was only able to find one troll hideout
and it didn't seem to be the right one.

But, I got to walk in the rain with a golf umbrella, get my feet a
little wet, get some weird looks from the construction workers on
lunch and very friendly looks from a cute dog. Then I went back to
work.

A lunch hour attempting a LB even in the rain (I AM an Oregonian) is
better than a boring lunch hour doing anything else!

Someday, soon, I hope to find the hideout that I need and then I will
really horse around. . .

KuKu
