"Herman the Worm" Hitch Hiker

From: Tina Schaffer (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2003-07-24 18:01:15 UTC
Placed July 23, 2003 by Camp Fire Lady & Cat's Meow. Designed by
Cat's Meow. Placed in Vancouver, WA. with the very first letterbox
found by us and we were so excited! The title is pronounced "Hoyman
the Woym" and is a song sung by campers on the bus on their way to
Camp Melacoma in Washougal, WA. for Day Camp. If found please contact
tinarie@msn.com so that I may track his whereabouts and keep him
traveling. Hoyman hates to sit in one place for too long.
