Canada: 20 bucks box found

From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2003-09-02 12:28:40 UTC-04:00
Yippee! Our first find outside the US. A letterbox-resistant friend, Wild
Child & I visited Victoria over the weekend--what a lovely time. We went to
the area where 20 bucks is located solely to find the LB--and along the way
enjoyed a charming petting zoo and water-play fountain. Ultimately, we ended up
in a wonderful little cove, where a curious seal bobbed in the water, a dog
named Thumper swam after the sticks we threw (Wild Child retrieved the ones that
Thumper missed), and kayakers glided by. Yet another wonderful experience, in
a place we'd never have found on our own without a friendly LB to guide us.
My LB-resistant friend was wowed and even promised to hit up some Seattle
boxes with us in the future.

MANY thanks to Canada Goose for the site & the fun!

--Wild Woman
