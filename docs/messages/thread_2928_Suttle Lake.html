From: gwendontoo (foxsecurity@earthlink.net) | Date: 2008-08-01 02:57:48 UTC
We left Suttle Lake on a morning with a few sprinkles and it seems
like we had either wind or more wind, or much much more wind. We did
have a beautiful view of the lake and the very best part was our
visitors.

Amanda and Ryan came early and stayed for a few days and on the 27th
Birder showed up about noon after planting a letterbox nearby.

Heron-a-foot straggled in with Peaches, Yiker, Spike , Coondra and
Onyx came next.CaliK was dropped off and Funhog and Keith showed up
sportin a new Trailer. (Seems like the older you get the more you
start leaning toward comfort)
To top things off all the way from Portland came the three Boxers,
Maiden, Marmalade, and Camp Fire Lady.

The chow was pretty eclectic, mostly salads, but no one turned down
freshly caught trout.

We handed out the WOM clues and everyone found as many as they
wanted, but primarily settled in for letterboxing chat.

It was really fun for us to meet new friends and say hello to old
friends.Thanks to all that came. See you in the future.

This is the best part of letterboxing!!!

Don & Gwen
