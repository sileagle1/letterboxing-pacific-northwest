Snoqualmie - Status.

From: soggy2pair (soggy2pair@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-23 03:31:34 UTC
After an early start to my work day I decided that I had to take a
stroll out to Snoqualmie Falls for a 'check-up' hunt. It appears
Mischief's Snoqualmie Falls series is doing just fine, both 1 and 2
were uncovered, but fixed - snug as a bug now. Odd that #2 was all
but sitting out in the open for anybody that happen to be there but
contents and such are great.

As for soggy2pair's Falls in Love LB that is still snug, near
Mischief's #2.

The weather is suppose to be fabulous this week, and if you haven't
been to the Falls it is a great opportunity to nab a total of 4
LB's. The trail is steep but not difficult, at least not until the
rain starts and makes it real slick.

Have a great time, travel safe and don't forget to bring an extra
bag to haul some trash out - - it is about as bad as I've ever seen
it.

Happy Adventures
soggy2pair
