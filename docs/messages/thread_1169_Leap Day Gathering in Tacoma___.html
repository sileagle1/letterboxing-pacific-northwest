From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-28 02:49:30 UTC
Those directions take you to the 'entrance' to the park--actually a
5 way intersection that can be a bit confusing. On Pearl St at
the 'final' intersection in your directions, head straight (middle
lane) as the right lane also sort of goes straight but veers down
the hill to the ferry docks--the left lane heads past the main
entrance to a retirement village.

Once straight, you'll pass a big boulder/monument on the right
(Rhododendron Monument)--continue on that road veering around some
gardens, then get in the left lane past the Japanese Garden
following signs to Five Mile Drive (possibly zoo sign here too) but
don't take the far left lane that will take you back out of
the 'garden' section of the park. Then the road will take you past
the zoo's fence on the left and playground off on the right--at the
next stop, head straight and then follow signs to Never Neverland/Ft
Nisqually. Hope this helps. If you get lost, you'll probably end
up on 5 Mile Drive--it'll still take you to the park--it'll just
take 5 miles longer to get there. ;) It's kind of difficult to
explain this in text format--you can find a 'map' of the park on the
Pierce County Parks & Rec webiste--I recommend studying the map
before you drive there. It may make things a bit clearer for you.
Park Info:
http://www.metroparkstacoma.org/parks/point-defiance.view

Park Map:
http://www.metroparkstacoma.org/parks/trail_map.pdf

See you there.

dvn2r ckr
