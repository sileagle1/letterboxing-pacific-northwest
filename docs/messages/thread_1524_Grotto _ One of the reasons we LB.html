Grotto / One of the reasons we LB

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2004-08-15 22:23:43 UTC
My in-laws were in town from Sacramento and I thought they would like
to see The Grotto (they are Catholic & I know they would like some of
the symbolism.) Besides, it's just a beautiful garden!

I wanted to mention something that has been mentioned before. One of
the reasons we letterbox is to see new wonderful places -- sometimes
gardens, views, little known trails, etc.

Because Grotto LB was the second box my husband & I had done, I had
FINALLY come to the Grotto other than Christmas Lights time.

Every December I would vow to go to the Grotto during the spring or
summer. Because of this wonderful LB, we did last year in June!

I was able to tell my in-laws how beautiful the garden was instead of
just having heard of it.

While we were there, I grabbed the box and my husband & I looked at
the loggings since we had been there. What fun! It was the first
time we had ever visited a box a second time.

Hope any Portland people or visitors catch this box. It's beautiful
& find to find.
