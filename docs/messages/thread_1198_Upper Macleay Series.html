I forgot some important stuff yesterday in my excitement.

First off to anyone that's going to do the Upper Macleay boxes.. PATIENCE is
a HUGE deal. I couldn't believe how busy the trail was. It was hysterically
funny trying to look casual and hide everything every time people went by.
(About every 10 seconds)...

And the more important one.. the third box.. the White Fronted Bee Eater or
something like that.. is in fact there. The clue has it listed as possibly
missing. It is not.. it is in its original spot and happy as a clam. BUT BE
CAREFUL WHEN LOOKING FOR IT. We found several hyperdermic needles on the
ground imbedded in the mud right near it.
