Welcome to the group!! As for 'gatherings' in your area, just keep reading
the list and you'll eventually see them posted. Or, you could plan one on your
own! Just pick a central location in a large park or something, post to the
list the time and date. Invite people to plant letterboxes in that area before
the gathering and just WATCH who comes out of the woodwork. We recently hosted
the GGGathering in Golden Gate Park in San Francisco. There were 18 boxes
planted before the gathering and 37 people showed up!! Amazing response!

Happy Letterboxing!!
