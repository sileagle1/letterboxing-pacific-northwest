Our story

From: nikanderfamily (blancoandyou@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-08 22:44:32 UTC
We started letterboxing in June after reading an article in a family
magazine.We didn't find the first one but were hooked after finding
the second one. Now 22 boxes later we are loving this new sport as a
family. We started out with a store bought stamp but now we carved
our own to represent our family. Our first was in Long beach, WA the
kite letterbox and then some at the oregon coast, but most in the
gorge and Mt. Hood area. We also found our first hitchhiker which was
hitchbiker dude. That was exciting.We try to chose areas that are
good for our 3 small kids and I am 8 months pregnant. Thank you to
those that place boxes in those places. The most difficult box for us
was at Cape Meares which was a mile hike down to the beach and we
couldn't find the box and had to hike up empty handed. We are finding
that happens sometimes. We have learned of this site and have been
able to identify usually first if the box is missing or not. We were
going to go look for the blue lake park one but decided not to when
we read it was missing. Thank you to those for the dedication you put
into places boxes. We hope to some day place some boxes but maybe
when the kids are a little older.Any advice to new people would be
great. Sorry it got so long we just wanted to introduce ourselves and
look forward to getting to know fellow letterboxers. We feel like we
already know some of you by just finding your stamps that you have
carved and placed.Again thanks.
