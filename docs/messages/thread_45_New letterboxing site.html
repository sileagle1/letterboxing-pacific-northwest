PNWLBers:

I want to apologize for the present and future slowness in my posting of
letterboxes in OR, WA, and ID. I am in the middle of transitioning our
letterboxing website from the
'webmaster-cuts-and-pastes-every-clue-into-a-new-webpage' type to the
'user-can-enter-their-own-clues' type. Less administrative work for
webmasters and easier for letterboxers to modify their clues themselves
instead of going through a middleman.

Since we're just about to release this to everyone, I will be spending a lot
of my free time getting it finished up and loaded. Therefore, I will not be
as prompt at posting new boxes to the old website.

I wanted to let you know about this, and hopefully you'll understand. If
you have serious issues, or a box that HAS to be posted right away because
your great-aunt want to be able to see it ON the internet, I'm sure we can
work something out.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask.

Wes, the de-lurker.
