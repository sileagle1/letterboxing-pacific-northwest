April 9th gathering update!

From: cheflisbeth (cheflisbeth@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-12-09 21:16:22 UTC
PNW Spring Flinger!
Come bid farewell to dvn2rckr before she's far "flung" to DC. "Fling"
some letterboxes for others to hunt down. Come meet and greet some
other people that love this crazy hobby as much you do!
We will be meeting in Shelter #2.
Water, Electricity and Barbaque/stove is available in the covered
shelter. This shelter is the closest to the play area. The park
includes a total of 312 acres on both sides of East Bay Drive. Access
to the shoreline, rose garden and lots of nature trails await us.
*************************
CAUTION!!!!!!!! There is access to the water!! Please be mindfull of
children and watch them closely!!
*************************
We will also be having a potluck, but everyone bring their own plates,
napkins and utensils. If you know what you will be bringing, let me
know and I'll post it so everyone knows what's needed!
Hopefully the trails will be dry...but let's face it. This is
Olympia, Washington. We could be having beautiful dry weather or
ankle deep in mud. It's all part of the dvn2r we are ck'ing!
