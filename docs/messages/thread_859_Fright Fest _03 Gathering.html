Fright Fest '03 Gathering

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2003-10-26 04:22:15 UTC
Just walked in the door from driving the 21/2 hours from the
gathering and boy was it worth it! Thank you so much Team Rayvenhaus
for a wonderful time! It was so nice to see the faces (well almost
all the faces ;0)) to put with the names. The stamps were awesome!
Pictures will be posted soon and I would be happy to email copies to
anyone wishing to have them. Just email me off list and I will send
them out. It was so nice to meet everyone and especially you
RayvenDad, RayvenMom and RayvenGranny. You worked so hard to make an
event something to remember and I will remember it always. It was a
wonderful surprise to find out that RayvenMom and I share the same
birthday! Well, I'm off to bed to dream of letterboxes, stamps and
new friends. Thank you again for a wonderful time!
Camp Fire Lady
