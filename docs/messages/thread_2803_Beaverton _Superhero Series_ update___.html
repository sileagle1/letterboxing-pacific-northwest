Greetings,

Over the weekend, I planted boxes 7-9 of my ongoing Superhero Series
in the Beaverton area. Clues are posted in the usual places, so head
on out and let me know what you think.

A couple updates on this series:

#2 (Bruce Wayne): It has been reported that the neighbors have been
alerted to the existance of this box. Therefore, I will be moving it
to a different area of the park this week. Clues will be updated when
the box is in its new home.

#4 (Hal Jordan): As previously reported, this box has gone missing and
has been retired. Clues have been removed but the box is still listed
on LB and AQ as it shares an entry with box #3.

#10 (???): This will be the final box in the series. This stamp is
carved and the box is ready. Look for it to be placed within the next
week.

Thanks to all who have let me know that they have enjoyed this series.
I certainly have had a blast carving and hiding these stamps!
