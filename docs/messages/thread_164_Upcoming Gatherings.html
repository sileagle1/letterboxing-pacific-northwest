We should schedule some fund raising events, so we can bring our hero Wes out
to a gathering, so we can properly thank him for his great efforts. Car washes
after letterboxing, first aid on the trails, personal protection services
for 'boxers, etc.

I, for one, would donate all proceeds from my new profession, Professional
Letterbox Expedition Guide (PLEG). Take various groups and people out into the
wilds to hunt the elusive letterbox. Hopefully won't be too swamped with
business.

Although rumors of my letterbox woes may be running rampant (don't know the
difference between clockwise and counter; Southwest and Southeast confuse me;
as Nailhead so kindly pointed out, went the wrong way on the loop train at
Agate Beach (a very nice, but quite small, park, with a 1/4 +/- mile loop
trail); being 6 for 12 on Funhog's boxes; North is always toward the water,
except in Tacoma; etc.) I hope that the people interested in obtaining my
services will know any better.

See you at the gatherings, with my new brochure :-)
