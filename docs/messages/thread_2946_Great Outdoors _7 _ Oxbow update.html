OK. Things have changed significantly at Oxbow Park. Quite a bit
of trail damage and downed trees from the recent weather. Some
trails are so covered with debris that you can't tell the trail is
there. Some trails are closed. Some are washed away by the Sandy
River.

Including some of the trails in the Great Outdoors #7 series. So
here goes:

Group Camping Area 3 is no more. The shelter was removed some
months ago, I hear, not because of the recent weather. Clue will be
updated shortly, to reflect it's disappearance.

Box 1 missing.

Box 2 happy and healthy.

Box 3 missing. This area looks like it was flooded recently.

Box 4 is fine.

Box 5 is fine. Directions have changed (trail name was changed from
L to J).

Box 6 present and accounted for.

Box 7 I forgot to look for the hollowed out cedar on the right. I
think it's gone. Just watch for the cedar on the left, just off the
trail, and watch for a suspicous clump of moss. You'll find it.
It's not a long section of trail so if you don't find it the first
time, double back and try again. Box is there.

Box 8 missing.

Gimme a few weeks and I'll have these recarved and replaced. Since
some boxes were clearly in better hiding places than others, I'll
probably reduce the boxes placed and just double-up on stamps in
existing boxes. That'll probably reduce "losses" in the future as
well, but the puzzle can still be solved.
