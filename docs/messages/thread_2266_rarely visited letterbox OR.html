From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-10-05 03:48:40 UTC
It was a lovely, crisp Oregon day today as Pungent Bob and I hit the
trail. With the ghosts and goblins holiday on the horizon, it seemed
the perfect time to seek out Ryan's Temple of Terror mystery box. I'm
pleased to report that we were successful in our search. Although the
box was plainly visible when we tracked it down, all the contents were
as good as new.

They were as good as new but not even close to actually being new.
This box has been in place for over four years and we were only the
second visitors! The last ones were there a few days after it was
originally placed and were none other than the long silent Catbird and
Lizard. Where have these folks gone?

This box has a great stamp and is in a wonderful location. Lace on
those boots, pack up some trail mix and be one of the lucky few to
have it in your logbook... You will NOT be disappointed. Funhog
