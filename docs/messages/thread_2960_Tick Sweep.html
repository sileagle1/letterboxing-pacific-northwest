Tick Sweep

From: MJ (KuriousJo@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-04-14 08:09:39 UTC
Okay so this isn't the happiest of subjects but Lyme disease totally dis-railed my life and as outdoorsmen all of us should be concerned about the danger that ticks can bring.

Anyone know of an area in Southern Washington where ticks can be found? The local Lyme community is trying to pull together several areas to do a tick sweep. The idea is to get as many ticks as possible and test them for tick-borne diseases (TBD). Spring is the best time to do it. We're just not sure what locations to target. Appreciate anything you can share: where you've seen or gotten ticks, how many, anything.

Kurious Jo
