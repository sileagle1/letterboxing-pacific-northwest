I would like to recommend a GREAT box- CymruWitch's Halloween 2005 box.

It is in Eugene, and only around for a limited time. It is a cemetary
that is beautiful and unique. The logbook is maybe the most beautiful
I have ever seen, and the hand-carved stamp is cute and creative.

Here is a link to the clues:
http://letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=18487&boxname=Halloween_2005_Box

It fits perfecty for the season. Definatly stop by if you have the
chance. It didn't take too long, and it was well worth it. Happy
Halloween!
