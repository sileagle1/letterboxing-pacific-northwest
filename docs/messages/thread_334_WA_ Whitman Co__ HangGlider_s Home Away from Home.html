From this location, it's 8 degrees (True) to Mt. Spokane and it's 268
degrees (True) to the summit of Mt. Rainier.

While standing on the benchmark, there is only one evergreen tree within a
stone's throw. Don't throw the stone, but imagine that you did. Did you
hit the trunk of the tree? Nice shot! Go retrieve the rock - and
letterbox.
