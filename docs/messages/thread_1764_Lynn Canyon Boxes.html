From: Judy B (sowbiz@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-15 19:38:31 UTC

My trip to Vancouver-Banff-Jasper now over I am pleased to report it
was great fun and had the added bonus of LetterBoxing.

While in Vancouver I caught the SeaBus to North Vancouver and the
city bus to Lynn Canyon. I asked the driver to let me know when we
got to the corner of 'this and that'. The next lady boarding the bus
was wanting the same guidance. So we started chatting and as we left
the Park's environmental center I clued her in on what I was really
doing there. Mimi was on an 8 day visit from Japan and took great
interest in the activity. We found both boxes (Ryan's and Amanda's)
and she took the temporary trail name of "Visiting Sun". It was
great having someone to hike with.

The day was slipping away so I had to hurry back to Stanley Park to
find a box I couldn't find (clues had been emailed to me, not on
LB.org) so I set out to plant two boxes (I still need to post those
clues). I also planted a box in Banff and in Jasper.

It was a great trip and beautiful country.
