Passport To Letterboxing Stamp Camp

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-06-18 19:30:52 UTC
When: August 27-29, 2004
Where: Battle Ground Lake State Park
Battle Ground, WA.

If you have not made your reservations for your campsite for this
event then you would need to hurry! There are only 4 drive-in sites
left and 14 primitive sites. Call 1-888-CAMPOUT or 1-888-226-7688
today!
This event will feature workshops on the basics of Letterboxing,
stamp carving and book binding as well as workshops for the kids
while you parents are attending your workshops.
There will be a passport given to everyone who attends so that you
may collect your event stamps,(and there will be a stamp for every
activity) plus a bonus stamp for collecting them all! There will even
be some surprise events that will be going on in the evenings and
some awesome raffle prizes drawn at each workshop as well as plenty
of letterboxes to find around the park and in the surrounding areas
of Battle Ground and Yacolt.
If you have any questions about this event or have made your
reservations please email Camp Fire Lady at tinarie at msn dot com so
that we can map out where everyone is located in the camp ground and
so that we can provide enough supplies for all that will be attending
the workshops.
If you plan on coming up for the day on Saturday please let me know
so we can plan accordingly. I would need to know how many people are
in your group.
There will be more details to follow about this event so get your
reservations in today. I hope to see you all there!
Camp Fire Lady
tinarie at msn dot com

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-07-25 18:07:07 UTC
PASSPORT TO LETTERBOXING STAMP CAMP

When: August 27-29, 2004
Where: Battle Ground Lake State Park, Battle Ground, WA.

Come out and spend a weekend camping or come for the day on
Saturday. There will be workshops on Letterboxing 101, Stamp Carving,
Book Binding, Jr. Ranger Program and Camp Crafts for the kids as well
as 18 letterboxes in the area to find! Visit http://www.parks.wa.gov
for information on campsites, directions to the park and other things
that the park has to offer while you are there.
Each person attending will be given a Passport that will include
pages for your personal stamp and information, a map of the park,
maps to surrounding areas that have letterboxes, clues to letterboxes
in Battle Ground Lake Park only (you will need to bring the clues for
letterboxes at Lewisville Park and Yacolt if you plan on searching
for them while at the Stamp Camp), a complete schedule of events and
pages for the images of the MANY event stamps and letterboxes that
you will find at the park. This passport will also make a great
keepsake after the event.
There will be some awesome letterbox items raffled off at the
event. Raffle tickets are $1.00 each or 6 for $5.00. Kid's raffle
will be free.
When you arrive, everyone including the kids will need to "check
in" at the Passport to Letterboxing Stamp Camp check in table located
at campsite # 14 to receive your passport and information about the
event.
Please bring your carving tools if you have them, Starter Kits
will be available for $5.00 each if you would like to purchase them
at the event, a hitch hiker if you would like to participate in the
Hitch Hiker Hostel hidden in the park, your own firewood if you will
be camping (the park does not provide firewood), your own meals since
this will not be a potluck event, your letterboxing supplies and
plenty of cash for the raffles and the day use fee of $5.00 if you
are not camping out. Please do not bring extra letterboxes or hitch
hikers to leave on the tables.
Your hosts for this event will be Camp Fire Lady, Iron Bear and
Pandora, Maiden and Bookworm.
Look for the red, blue and green balloons that will direct into
the park from the I-5 freeway and we will see you there at the
Passport To Letterboxing Stamp Camp at Battle Ground Lake State Park,
Battle Ground, WA.
Please R.S.V.P. to Camp Fire Lady at tinarie at msn dot com if you
plan on attending this event so that we cam make sure that we have
enough supplies for everyone.

event.
