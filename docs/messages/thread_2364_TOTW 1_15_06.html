From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-01-19 18:16:16 UTC
I'll have to say that letterboxing taught me something about myself
that I absolutely did not know before I took up the hobby. I had no
idea that I had any ability in the arts of any kind. My drawings look
like a grade schooler to me and I had NEVER carved a rubber stamp in
my life.

When I first took Speedball tool to soft block, I was totally hooked.
I found out that even those elementary drawings can translate into a
stamp image that's fun to look at. My skills have defintely improved
in the carving department and I now prefer to use an Xacto knife and
needle tools but those early stamps represent the beginning of a craft
that I still enjoy immensely and hope will stay with me for many years
to come! I find that totally inspiring. Funhog
