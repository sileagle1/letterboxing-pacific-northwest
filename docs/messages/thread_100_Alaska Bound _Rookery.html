It's so weird that you asked about this box today. Guess where I was
when you sent the post? I was out checking on several of my WA boxes
this afternoon and Alaska Bound was one of them. I hadn't been there
since I first placed it. The box is slated to head north in mid-June
in the care of YeeHaw YaYa. Those who haven't put this stamp into
their logbooks yet need to head out in the next few weeks. This one is
a must-have, in my opinion. She's a beauty...

I also checked on the Rookery since Maronie and the Gimpy Orca
(ouch!!!) failed to find it last weekend. I managed to get wet and
muddy from ankle to thigh and befriend a bunch of bovines in the
process. I also found the box, under a layer of dirt and sans
visitors. It could be that the compass heading in the clue is dead
wrong. Any future searchers should try 35 degrees not 45.

Everything else I looked for was in good shape. I also managed to come
home with two (!) unexpected stamps in my journal, a HH and a bonus
clue that had magically appeared in one the boxes I visited. I was in
(where else?) Hog Heaven... Funhog
