From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-09-06 04:22:56 UTC
I made it out to the Audubon Sanctuary this afternoon to
replace the two boxes that have been missing from the
What's for Dinner series for so danged long. The clue
to the final box has changed somewhat but all four are
now up and running. Enjoy!

I would also like to draw attention to my latest plant, a
letterbox created by LbNA webmaster, Choi. This is a
beautiful production, one which most folks would really
like to see. It's called Quoth the Raven and the clue can
be found in the usual places.

I also checked on that elusive waterfall letterbox of mine
in NE Portland. It seems to have gone AWOL but I will
replace it soon. Hopefully, more folks than the few who
dashed out to get this one as soon as it appeared will
track it down in the future. Funhog
