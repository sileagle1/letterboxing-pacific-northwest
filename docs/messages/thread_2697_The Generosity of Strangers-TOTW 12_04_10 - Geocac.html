I had a similar kindness of strangers story. We had placed two boxes
listed under the Order of the Phoenix. We would check on them
occasionally and sometimes find that the Phoenix was visible from the
path, which had me worried that it would go missing. The other box -
the Greenhouse looked to be safe. But then, one day I got an e-mail
from a geocacher! She had found the greenhouse by the side of the
path, and emailed me that day!! So I quick ran over found it all wet
inside and not closed properly, and pulled them both. The clues are
still listed, as retired on letterboxing.org, just for historical,
sentimental value.

So this is in thanks and praise of our cousins the geocachers!!
