WA Discovery Trail lament

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-08-05 06:49:15 UTC
Recently I received an email from a new letterboxer saying that my Discovery Trail
letterbox was very hard to find because "someone had entirely covered it with leaves."
when I tried to respond to their email pointing out that this was intentional, the email
bounced as undeliverable. It seems that their address was flawed in some way.

I had just been to check on this box a few days earlier because another boxer told me
that they were unable to find it. The box was there but completely exposed, out in the
open. I, of course, rehid it and covered it with leaves. If the person who wrote me
reads this list, I would like them to know that all of my letterboxes are meant to be
entirely out of sight. Please rehide them in the same way.

I am fretting that there is a new letterboxer out there who thinks that our boxes
should be in plain sight. This would be a disaster for all of us. Funhog
