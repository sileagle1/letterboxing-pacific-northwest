Instant Gratification

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-02-20 12:15:29 UTC-08:00
I picked up the vandalized Instant Gratification box today. I found the box
open again and one of the stamps about 20 feet away while the other one was
gone along with the log book. The only thing I found that was encouraging,
it wasn't any fault of anyone that the box got found. The parks people came
through and mowed down all of the grass that had once hidden the pile of
rocks the box was hidden in. They were planting trees in the area and took
out all the grass. This box has been pulled and will be replaced in a new
location on Monday.

When it is replaced it will have a bounty of stamps. The January, February,
and March holiday stamps will all be placed in it until the end of March.
Then those will be pulled and we will return to our regular schedule of
having two stamps per month in it.

Thanks and I hope you are able to get out there before the end of March to
pick up these fun stamps.

Maiden
