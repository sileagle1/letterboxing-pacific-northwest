From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2004-06-28 21:34:20 UTC-04:00
In the past two days, I've had the delight of tracking down some newly
planted boxes. Many thanks to Pupp and Smiley for the fun! Yowza--how can each
of them carve so much better than I do with so much less practice??? Fun
clues, great stamps--check 'em out, folks!

I had the added pleasure of MEETING Smiley and two of her kids, as they were
surreptiously seeking one of Pupp's Lbs. I'd already found it, unaware that
these lbers were hot in pursuit behind me! I'd taken a little walk, and in
wandering back where the box was hidden, I detected in these strangers the
tell-tale signs of fellow boxers (a certain nonchalance, a clutching of certain
downloaded pages...) and couldn't help but greet them with, "I got firsties."
What a fun visit!

Today Heather and I were recalling our concern (when we first started boxing)
that at the voracious, obsessive rate we were seeking the local boxes, we
would soon run out of ones to seek! Man, look at that Portland list now! Who
can keep up? (Besides Funhog, of course...) ;o)

Welcome new boxers, and THANKS for planting!
