Well, everyone, I went on an excursion today at the Washington Park
Zoo in Portland Oregon. Found the 2 remaining boxes of 5 originally
set. The second one was behind a log and between a fern and the most
disgusting fungus stuff I have ever encountered, lol, stuck my hand
right in it. akkkkkkkkkk! but it was a lot of fun. the zoo was
crammed as it was Earth day today. But I think I was successful in
remaining descreet. The stamps were lovely!

Also, we made it up to the Hoyt Aboretum and planted my tribute box
there. Found an awesome place for it, hope it stays hidden and gets
lots of visitors, gotta write the clues and publish them now......
besides the gradual climb, it's an easy find I think.

Hope you are all having a lovely day where ever you are!

Oh ya, and I sat on the end stump the log fell from and got pitch ALL
over my rear, went thru my jeans and onto my skin, so I had sticky
buns all the way back. lol. Anyone have a good remedy for removing
this goo? Wingnut
