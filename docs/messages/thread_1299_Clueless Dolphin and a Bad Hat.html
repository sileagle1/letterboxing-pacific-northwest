Great day of boxing for Twilight, Sunshine and I.

The Clueless Letterbox is alive and well. Great hiding spot. We were
second to log in. Thanks for the hunt Ryan. =)

Bad Hat. What a cute park. The trails have been very recently maintained,

up nothing.
