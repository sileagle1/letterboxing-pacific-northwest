We took a beautiful day and headed to the coast yesterday.

We stopped and picked up the "Spruce Goose" - clever and well done
stamp and what a sweet park, we really enjoyed this park! Some of
the fountains are not on but still delightful!, Your box was totally
exposed - I believe since February, the last visitors were newbies.
We made sure it was well hidden when we left.

We fished out and almost droped the sunfish thru the planks of a
nearby bridge at Under the Deep Blue #3! Very nice location what a
beautiful place! Very nice stamp too.

And finally got the Chief and Indian Feather, I was sweating that
one. Wonderful only begins to describe the craftmanship and detail!

Thank you all for making our outing even more wonderful and opening
up so many more wonderful places we would otherwise neglect to visit!
