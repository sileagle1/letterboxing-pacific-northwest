Public Library Internet access

From: Amanda Arkebauer (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-27 06:11:55 UTC
I am a great fan of Public Libraries all over the country! I can not
afford to have a laptop to travel with and I rely on free internet
service at Public Libraries all over the country. If you find
yourself someplace without letterbox clues. The Library is a great
resource! (the Libraries on the West Coast are especially
accommodating! In Las Vegas you can get up to 2 hours on the
internet...in North Carolina on the other hand, they only let you
have 15 or 30 minutes at the most!) One library I went to on the
East Coast had dozens of computers available, but because I was a
visitor (without a local library card), I could only use one specific
computer. There was a wait for it! Even though half of the other
computers were available!! Weird!

--Amanda from Seattle
