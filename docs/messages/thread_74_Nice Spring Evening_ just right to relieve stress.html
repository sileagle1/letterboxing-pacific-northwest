Nice Spring Evening, just right to relieve stress

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-04-17 21:03:55 UTC
Took a little trip out to McLane Creek yesterday, April 16. Had been
there once before. This time I went alone. Saw only 2 other people
in the park from 5:30-8pm. Found the 3 new letterboxes and took some
time to be alone in the woods. The sound of the creek flowing nearby
put my soul at ease. I found a really nice little spot next to the
creek under some large trees that was actually dry and comfortable
and just took the time to be quiet and listen to God's creation.
Peace like a river...It is well with my soul. Had enough time to
create a little bonus box of my own while I sat there. Anyone wanting
to find my new box will have to visit the other boxes at the park to
find the clues.

I hope that all who follow me there will find as much peace as I did
for my stressed mind.

Nailhead
