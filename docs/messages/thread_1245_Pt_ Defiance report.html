Pt. Defiance report

From: Mariette (dbltall@speakeasy.net) | Date: 2004-03-30 10:40:11 UTC-08:00
Team Garlic spent a sunny Sunday afternoon at Pt. Defiance Park in
Tacoma and found Pt. Pagoda (the Japanese garden was beautiful),
Humbuggy, Sea Otter, and Frontier. We ran out of time and will have to
go back another day to search for Polar Bear and Fantasy.

On one of the less-travelled trails we encountered a family with the
telltale yellow Garmin -
"Geocaching?" we asked.
"Yes, are you?"
"No, we're letterboxing"
"They're letterboxers!" the mom I was talking to told rest of her crew,
so we could all relax in the knowledge that our respective rummagings
around in the bushes would go unremarked.

Mariette
(dbltall of Team Garlic)
