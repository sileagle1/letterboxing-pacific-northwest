From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-10 03:56:32 UTC
It's August and Washington Trails Association Hike-a-thon time again.
I have been out on several hikes already, combining letterboxing with
hike-a-thon hiking. I am hoping to beat my mileage of last year. I am
shooting for at least 40 miles (just over a mile a day).

And I already have more pledges than I did last year! And that is
mainly because of my Flight Attendant and Pilot friends at USAirways.
They have really come through for me this year. I am hoping to get
some more pledges from Letterboxers too. We all use the trails and if
you can't get out and donate your time for trail maintenance, the
next best thing is to support your trails with a little cash
donation!

I have started a blog with all my trail adventures and so everyone
can read about my pledges and mileage totals.

http://www.samanark.blogspot.com

A pledge of $35 gets you membership in the Washington Trails
Association and you get a years subscription to the WTA Magazine (10
issues) And a pledge of $10 or more recieves a movie from my
collection that was filmed in Washington State (unfortunately most of
the DVD's are already gone, some VHS are left) Check out the blog for
the latest updates on what is available. Every little bit helps from
$3.50 to $50!

If you would like to contribute, just email me!
Thanks so much,
Amanda from Seattle
