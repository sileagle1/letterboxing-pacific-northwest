Seattle!

From: Wizbang (wizbang@softhome.net) | Date: 2003-08-14 19:19:18 UTC-07:00
I went down to Seattle with Hatti and Fixing Satsumas-two new letterboxers!
We chased after the Schmitz Park 3-box series, and our results were as
follows:

1. Box was in place and intact, but the stamp was gone :-( It appeared that
the box had been stumbled across several times; my guess is that someone
decided it would be a nice heirloom of their trip to the park.
2. Box, stamp, book all in good shape and well hidden. Cute squirrel!
3. We couldn't find box #3. Time was short, so our search was rather
cursory, but we found the log that looks like it's sliding down the hill,
and a significant portion of it had slid down the hill, possibly taking the
box with it.

Other than the complications, we had a grand day out. The park is beautiful
and the smll children at the community centre, where we ate lunch, were
darling. I'm not sure why, but they weren't allowed to stand up or they'd
be assigned cleanup duty.

I'll be hitting Padilla Bay and Bayview soon-I've gotten every Skagit county
box so far, and I'm not giving up my stranglehold on this county! :-O
