From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-10-14 15:30:15 UTC
Yeah, well, I really put my foot into it. Good thing you can't get
pictures of what I look like with both feet in my mouth at the same
time. =)

First, I talk plenty about people (both good and bad, I might add)
behind their backs, and I'd be surprised if I was the only one.
However, due to the poor user-interface on this talk list, I
accidentally posted it public rather than e-mail, which is what I
intended. (It happens to everyone, eventually.) E-mails of that
content should never, ever be published publicly, and for that I
truly do apologize. I noticed my error immediately and deleted the
post from the talk list within seceonds, but--unfortunately for me--I
couldn't take it back from those that choose to get their posts via e-
mail. Even worse, you were one of them. The stars, obviously, were
not aligned for my well-being.

But you should also understand, I hold ill feelings towards you.
What annoys me is your enthusiam for letterboxing. In your quest to
be helpful--a noble task where your thoughts are certainly in the
right place--you tend to go overboard. Like creating a letterboxing
website as an "alternative" to the original (which I feel ends up
fragmenting the original site if you succeed, but is probably a waste
of your time since other attempts at "alternative" sites have
essentially failed). Rather than that, I wish you'd create a website
that provided more information and how-to tutorials instead of trying
to reinvent the wheel.

You also tend to break 'Miss Janet's guidelines' about posting too
often, although that doesn't really bother me much since I don't read
most posts on this or the main letterboxing board. Rather, it's just
another example of "too much" enthusiam that, at times, gets under my
skin.

But if there's one good thing I can say about newbie enthusiasm, you
guys are great at creating letterboxing gatherings. I'm thrilled to
death Bethany planned out last year's Steilacoom gathering. And Sir
B's SLO gathering. And even your Fright Fest gathering, although I
probably can't make it out to Tacoma in time for that. You should
have seen my pathetic attempt at organizing Seattle's first
letterboxing gathering. Puts me shame just to think about it. Even
worse, I was the last one to arrive for it! What an embarrassment!

And if it's any consolation--probably not, but I'll say it anyhow--
I've also told others behind your back that I'd probably like you far
more in person than I do the persona in your posts. Ironic, huh? Of
course, that one I actually managed to send as e-mail. *rolling eyes*

Anyway.... there's a large following of anti-Ryan supporters out
there--I have little doubt about that--and they'll probably be
sending you an application to join their group soon. If my apology
won't suffice, they'll certainly welcome you with open arms!

-- Ryan, still trying to extract feet from mouth
