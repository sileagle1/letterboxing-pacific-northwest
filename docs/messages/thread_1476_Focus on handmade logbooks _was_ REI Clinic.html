Taking off the other thread there is something I do not understand.

What is up with the focus on cheap memo pads? I am in total agreement that
finding a store bought stamp is a large disappointment and I personally
would never put a store bought stamp in a letterbox, but I've found tons and
tons of letterboxes that did not have handmade log books in them, and have
used all sorts of different memo pads as log books when planting myself and
this has never taken away from the experience for me. Heck the dollar store
has been my best friend. I apologize if I am missing something, but when I
find a letterbox with a handmade logbook, I think I sort of found something
a little special, it's not the norm and not something I expect people to do,
and therefore start feeling disappointed about if they don't. I mean after
all the stamp goes with me in a form of an image, the logbook stays.

I just recently learned how to make handmade logbooks, thanks to Funhog,
when I took her class up in Longview - and my most recent boxes, because
they were particularly special to me, all had handmade log books. However I
won't be putting handmade log books in every letterbox I plant.
