29 May Mystery Meet update

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-27 16:26:14 UTC
YAHOO!!! PEOPLE, PEOPLE, PEOPLE!!! Our numbers for attendance at
Saturday's gathering in Des Moines are now in the mid-40s (and
that's from the folks who've contacted me--I'm sure there are others
out there who plan on attending)!!! Simply amazing for a long
weekend when I presumed most people would be 'out of town'. What a
way to welcome our long distance visitor(s)!!!

LETTERBOXING OPPORTUNITIES IN THE AREA: As for boxes in the area--
there are currently no 'established' letterboxes in the park where
the event is scheduled. There are a couple of postage stamp parks
near the venue that also don't yet have resident letterboxes. There
are a couple of different letterboxes/series hidden in the general
area (within a couple of miles of the park). One series that comes
to mind is The Flight Path Series. Another couple of letterboxes
that are hidden within 10 miles include: Anniversary, Dash Point
collection of boxes, Brown's Pt Lighthouse, You Don't Bring Me
Flowers, Glenn Hansen??? (it really has a different name but I can't
recall at the moment), Deep Sink & Weasel and a bit further out
includes the Turkey Dinner LB to the North and all of the boxes in
the Tacoma area to the South. I may be omitting some letterboxes in
the vicinity as it's been a while since I studied the list--if
anybody knows of any that I've omitted, please add them to the list.

VENUE UPDATE: we haven't officially reserved a pavillion or picnic
tables for this event. My plan is to show up about an hour early
(2pm) to try to 'round up some tables' that will work for a group
this large--anybody who wants to join me then is more than welcome
to come!!! This event will go on rain or shine. If it rains, we'll
just focus on the 'exchanging & eating' part of the event--still
culminating with the arrival of our mystery guest. I still don't
think the renovations of the park will be complete by the time our
event occurs, so we may have to resort to port-a-johns. I plan to
recon the park one more time tomorrow (if I can free up some time)
to scout out possible hiding spots for new letterboxes. Specifics
for the 'location' for the event--we'll meet near the playground in
the park. As you enter the park, continue on the road past the
first grass field/picnic area on the left--the road will wind to the
right and you should see the playground and community activity
center as you come around the bend. There is parking near the
playground and overflow parking in other parts of the park. Not
sure how popular the park will be on a Saturday afternoon of a long
weekend. If it's THE PLACE to hang out, then you may have to plan
on parking a bit outside of the park and walking in to the event.
Hopefully, the park renovations will discourage folks from the spot
so we'll have it to ourselves! ;)

I'll post one final 'update' for the event tomorrow on the big list
& the LbPNW list. Feel free to invite your friends and new
letterboxers with whom you are in contact (those folks who don't
know about the discussion board).

See you then,

dvn2r ckr
