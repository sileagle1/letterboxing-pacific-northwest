I just wanted to say HELLO and thanks to all of you who have placed
letterboxes for us to find. I have started this amazing sport with my 2 daughters in
July and we just love it. I look forward to placing my own someday soon in a
park by my house here in Vancouver. We have found about 53 boxes. We have
gone out for 4-6 hours at a time and are really enjoying our adventures and
seeing many amazing parks that we never knew existed. Today we went to Lewisville
Park in Battleground, WA and did the new Postman Series. It is very
kid-friendly and the stamps are just beautiful. I have a small request though. Please
seal those plastic bags or the books will get wet. I really look forward
to meeting all of you at the next gathering. I am Willow and my kids are Daisy
and Heartsey. We travel with Campfire Lady, Goofy and Critter. Our team
name is Runamuck or D.A.T.T. Thanks again and maybe we will get to exchange
stamps sometime. Willow.
