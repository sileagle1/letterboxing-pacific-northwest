From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-12-13 16:27:50 UTC
I have a box or two that no one has ever found, and it isn't because
the clues are difficult, it's because I plant boxes where we go
camping, and sometimes the boxes are in really out of the way places.
Those are the most rewarding when someone DOES finally find them and
report back. I totally understand your question, because I was there
once, and probably most people reading these posts were there once -
we understand your antipication and what it feels like. We all
planted a "first" box. All I can say is that you have to patient, not
that such advice helps any. While you are waiting you should just
plant some more boxes to keep yourselves busy! What is the trail name
you posted these boxes under, and where are the clues posted - I want
to see. I'm getting that urge to letterbox that comes after a few
weeks, kind of like milk - sometimes you have to get the jug out and
glug down a few swallows after a certain amount of time going without.
