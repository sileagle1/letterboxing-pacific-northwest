A couple of updates on two Millersylvania Boxes

From: Laura B. (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-03 03:28:56 UTC
Well..I didn't have much time to check on all the boxes but did
managed to attempt (by memory) looking for a couple I found during
FF@ and my last trip to the park in Nov 2004.

I did check on "Hiss" for Lily and did some maintenence on it for
her. It has been visited several times this past year by new
letterboxers.

I stopped by the Hogwart's Hitchiker Hostel and noted that it hasn't
been visited since MomNoTime and myself were there in Nov 2004 so I
pulled the three hitchikers out of it and will try to get them moved
to a more active box. The Hostel itself is in great shape for being
where it is and amazingly hadn't been tampered with at all. But I
don't think there are any clues for this Hostel online anywhere
which may be the contributing factor as to why it hasn't been
utilized.

I tried finding "Love at First Byte" but couldn't find the log I
remember sitting on to stamp in that logbook. But I also hadn't
received Maiden's message that it was no longer there until today.

I also looked for the box (can't remember the boxes name now) that
was placed for FF2 and located in the chimney of the fairy mound by
the BathHouse.Couldn't find it anywhere and we checked every stump
and the knocked over rotting stump in the general area. I remember
finding this box at FF2 and thinking gosh this was an easy one.

We will be out there again this coming weekend so I'll try to check
the status of a couple more boxes.
