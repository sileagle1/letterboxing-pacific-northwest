I'm double posting this message because a few regional letterboxers
no longer monitor the main LBNA message board--

Made a few changes to some Tacoma area letterboxes:

o Both Defiant Animals Mystery letterboxes are missing and will
not be replaced. Please throw out your clues to this series so you
don't wander aimlessly looking for non-existent boxes.

o Galloping Gertie LB is alive & well (had several missing reports
over the past year)--I tweaked the clues a tad to reduce the
frustration level--although if you start from the correct spot it
really isn't that difficult at all. A gimme, in fact... ;)

WA: LB updates

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-06 04:54:14 UTC
I know! I know! I've been remiss in maintaining letterboxes. Life
has a habit of throwing major curve balls when you least expect it.
I apologize profusely!

::
::

Since we'll be out of the country for April & May, we have 8 weeks
left on the ground in the area and we're frantically trying to
repair, replace or retire our remaining letterboxes in the region--
thus giving us an excuse to go back and visit some of our favorite
spots in the region one last time. ;) I have this crazy idea to
replace all of the logbooks & bags in the boxes before we drive away
in July knowing that we won't be able to return to the area as
frequently as we'd like once we relocate. I've managed to replace a
handful of logbooks so far--only 100+ or so to go still. :( Until
then, here are some updates for Puget Sound regional letterboxes of
ours:

West Point Mule--alive & well
Secret--alive & well
Dolphin--alive & well despite recent 'missing' reports--it's fine
Calkins Point--alive & well

::
::

Temporarily pulled for repair:
Buried Treasure

::
::

Missing boxes converted to retired (please pull your clues):
Meadow Point
Gun Fight
Bread Batter Hat
Cold Moon Over Puget Sound
Burfoot Park boxes in Boston Harbor
It's Raining Animal Series in Olympia
Pine Cone & Baseball in Lacey
New Market Falls in New Market
Goldfish in Olympia
Battle Point Birdie on Bainbridge (his buddy Fairy Dell Froggie is
still there)

::
::

--retiring soon (June 06):
Bon Sigh Mystery
Little Fishy
Halitaeetus leucocephalus in Des Moines

--potentially returning home to Florida in June 06:
Halitaeetus leucocephalus Exchange Letterbox in Steilacoom

::
::

WA boxes retired after going missing (beyond Puget Sound):
Bald Eagle in Rockport
Grays River Covered Bridge near Wahkiakum
Ocian in View! O the joy! in Wahkiakum
Fallen Rainbow
SamTONEHill'sENGE near Wishram

So--hopefully this will reduce or eliminate any future frustration
folks might have when seeking out our Washington letterboxes.

Enjoy your letterboxing adventures...

dlphinus
