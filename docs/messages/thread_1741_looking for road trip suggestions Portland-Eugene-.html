From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-11-01 19:57:23 UTC

My Bluebird Trail box is a just a short distance off I-5, south of Portland. The Portland
area itself has TONS of good boxes, many of them very short walks. You are unlikely
to find any store bought stamps in this area. I think I've only found three in all the
Oregon boxes I've found. Good luck, Funhog




Re: [LbPNW] looking for road trip suggestions Portland-Eugene-Blue River

From: Colleen (colleendurham@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-11-01 12:35:23 UTC-08:00
If you have a half hour or so in Eugene try The Rhododendron Box. It's not far from the UO campus and it is a delightful park. My son attends Oregon and the only time we could carve out to go letterboxing was 6:00AM Sunday morning, the weekend he moved in. The clues are well written and the stamp is exceptional. Probably one of my favorite finds.
Colls
