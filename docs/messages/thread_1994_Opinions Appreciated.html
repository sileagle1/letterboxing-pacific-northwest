Hey everyone :) Maybe if some of you letterboxers are bored you can
help me out by reading some clues I wrote. I am trying to take that
next step from beginner to experienced letterboxers (even though I
have been at it for almost 2 years) but most of my previously placed
letterboxes arent super creative, mostly a spiral bound pack of index
cards forthe logbook and very direct straightforward clues. So for
the last 2 days I have been in super duper creative mode (rainy days
do
that to me) so i was hoping that if anyone out there is bored enough
to read the stories I made up for my clues and throw out an opinion or
two with mayne constructive critisicm. They are on the LBNA site
under
these adresses below or if the links dont work, Oregon, Region 6, box
names Applegate trail and Myrtle the Turtle. Enjoy them :o) They are
supposed to be funny.
