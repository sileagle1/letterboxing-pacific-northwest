Wenatchee Washington

From: wingnut_lb (wingnut_lb@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-05-13 05:53:58 UTC
I am proud to say we found the Walla Walla Park lb in Washington this
last weekend, and were the first to sign in! What a lovely place, A
bit of a walk..... took us an hour and a half with the little one in
a stroller, but she loved it! NICE! Very easy to follow and well
written clues. Just happend to be some birders come by as I was
stamping up that stood in the exact spot the box was hidden for about
20 minutes..... Figures that we were just making a "quick" pit stop
on thru to the way back home. We still had 6 1/2 hours left to go at
that point! All in all very nice! A great box! Wingnut_LB

Thanks for stopping by. It is a very enjoyable walk, a chance to learn
about and see animals and plants and neat places.

You probably noticed a genuine lack of places to hid a box, and a lack of
'stuff' to put over a box to hide it. A drawback to Eastern Washington in
general, I have noticed.
