Foothills trail in Orting WA

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-06-01 22:56:17 UTC
I paid a visit to box # 2 and 3 of the Foothill Trails series, placed
by Sunseeker and DZ Desi.

I found # 2 fairly easily but did not find # 3 even though I was sure
of my location.

I wrote Sunseeker and DZ to see how they are. They have been fairly
quiet, I hope we did not loose them.

This is a nice trail running over old railbed. It could certainly
use some more boxes. It runs from Orting to the east side of South
Prairie. Did see some good birds, a Red-breasted Sapsucker at nest
and newly fledged Song Sparrow and American Dipper being fed by
adults.

Perhaps next month. I will have more free time then.
