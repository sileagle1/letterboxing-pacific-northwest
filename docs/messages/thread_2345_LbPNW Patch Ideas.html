backpack, inquiring about the average size. He says that the round
ones are about 2" in diameter and the other shapes of similar
dimensions. Do keep this in mind when making your patch. Too much
detail could disappear on such a small canvas. Funhog

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-01-14 06:46:50 UTC
I really love the idea of using a Northwest native style design. It
definitely identifies the region and isn't as literal as a map. The
lettering can easily be altered along the way. In fact, I really like
the lettering ARose created long ago for the PNW Traveling Event Stamp
Letterbox. Maybe something like it could be worked in. I've posted it
to the patch album.

I know we can get patches made in any size. I was just thinking it
would be nice to make ours similar in size to those from the other
regions. I have found out that the other round ones are all 2.5 inches
not 2. That size is also fairly reasonable in price, around $2,
depending on how much of the patch is embroidered. The patch also can
be other shapes than round. Adding the heighth and width and then
dividing by two gives the size.

Keep 'em coming!...y Gracias Seor Bedoya. Funhog
