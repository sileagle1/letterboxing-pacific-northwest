From: Smiley (dwharris@ipns.com) | Date: 2004-07-11 16:29:25 UTC
I was bummed that we missed the Wake Robin event. It sounds like
everyone had a great time.
We were capming at Fort Stevens State Park near Astoria for a few
days and we were having so much fun that we stayed an extra day,
which didn't get us back home in time for the event. We did find a
few boxes while we were at the coast though. The Coffenberry Lake
box was fun. We had our raft all ready to row out to an island, but
the oars were in the roof box on the top of my car and I lost the
keys. It was not a good thing, since a bunch of our stuff neeeded to
go in it when we packed up to leave, and it was very tight squeezing
everything into the car. We were right there and I wasn't going to
let not having any oars stop us from getting out there to find that
box. It was a beautiful day yesterday and there were many people at
the lake. I ended up asking some people that had a smaller raft if
we could borrow their oars. It was a bit of a task rowing with their
oars, they were a bit small for our raft, but we made it and stamped
in. We also found Horsin' Around Seaside and Picea Sitchensis. If
you haven't been to Fort Stevens I highly recommend it, we had a
blast.

Can Miller and Hutch e-mail me off the list please, or can someone
get me their e-mail. We couldn't find one of their boxes.

"Smiley"
