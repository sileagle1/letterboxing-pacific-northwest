From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2007-01-12 22:57:08 UTC
I just couldn't help myself. I just had to put those two letterboxes
into my backpack as I sauntered down into the Deschutes Canyon last
weekend. It was mighty blustery but just as awe inspiring as ever.
Somehow both boxes jumped out of my pack somewhere along the trail.
The clue is called Rock Garden and can be found on LbNA, along with
the clues for the eleven others I've sprinkled about the ranch over
the last five years.

In the process of excavating the pile of papers on my desk, I also
discovered my notes for another box that I hid last September. The
clue isn't doing much good sitting in my room, so I finally posted it,
too. This one's called Drop Me a Line. It may well be under a foot of
snow right now but it'll be waiting patiently for the next lucky
letterboxer who gets to go to Camp Sherman, one of the most beautiful
places on earth.

Finally, the latest shipment of PNW Patches arrived yesterday. If you
need information on how to get one, go here:
www.letterboxpatches.blogspot.com Enjoy! Funhog
