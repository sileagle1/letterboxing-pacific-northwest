The Cold Springs and Juniper Park letterboxes have gone missing.
The Cold Springs box was apparently destroyed be an unneeded forest
service controlled burn. They had burned the area a few years ago and
there wasn't much brush that had grown back, but they burned the
place again anyway. I could find no sign of my box when I checked.
The whole area around the spring is kind of ugly right now.
The Juniper Park boxes were likely vandalized. Someone reported
that they found the boxes, but they were empty. Today when I checked
I couldn't even find the boxes.
I hope to place new boxes in these areas again soon. The Cold
Springs box will probably be placed some where in the campground next
time so it will be less likely to burn again.
