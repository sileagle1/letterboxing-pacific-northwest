Re: On the way to Fright Fest

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-10-11 02:39:55 UTC
One of the nice things about gatherings is that you get to meet and
greet and also SEE peoples personal stamps. There are many media and
many ways to carve and stamp. By seeing the stamp in person (
instead of just the image) you get a feel of what is possible. I
personally use the trace and transfer technique.

I will plan on being at the fest, with a double batch of Krispie
Treats and another samich.

Plus all my signatures stamps each of which was carveed in a
different media. I will bring my carving box, which usually
includeds some bits of idea in progress.
