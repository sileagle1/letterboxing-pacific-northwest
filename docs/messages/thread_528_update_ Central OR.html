From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-08-06 15:49:26 UTC
I headed out to find the mysterious Birder's Phoebe Pond letterbox in
Redmond on Sunday. Even though I left my compass at home and the final
portion of the clue was in headings, the hog prevailed. I had been to
this cool place once before with a group of birders (could one of them
be the new and secretive Birder?) but this clue sent me there from a
different entry point.

Afterwards, I gave the Dry Canyon South box a second try. Once again,
I failed to find a thing. There is just too much "dead wood" and I
refuse to tear up an area looking for a letterbox. Those poor,
innocent rattlesnakes need their homes too.

Anyone coming to Central OR in the forseeable future? We need (read:
gimmee) more boxes in that part of the state. Funhog
