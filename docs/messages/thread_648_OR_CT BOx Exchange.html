OR/CT BOx Exchange

From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-27 20:09:07 UTC
To all PNW Letterboxers:

Since many boxers never get the chance to see stamps from boxers on
the opposite coast, Alafair and myself have exchanged letterboxes
for a few months.

My Oregon box has been planted near Beacon Falls, New Haven
County, CT and Alafair's wonderful box is in Hoyt Arboretum near
Portland, OR. Both letterboxes have hand-made logbooks. The
beautiful stamp in Alafair's box will be a welcome addition to any
logbook.

The respective clues are in "What's New." We'd like to invite as
many people as possible to come out and visit these boxes and share
their stamps and good wishes before we send them back to their
respective coasts for our friends there to enjoy.

Thanks.

BCostley and Alafair
