crappy stamp vs. store-bought stamp

From: Amanda Arkebauer (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-01 04:10:50 UTC
Everyone has preferences. Paper or plastic. Coke or Pepsi. Sean
Connery or Pierce Brosnan.

And I must say that I prefer a crappy hand-carved stamp to a store-
bought stamp any day! No matter how appropriate the store-bought
stamp is to the clues or the hiding place.

And I am guilty of hiding crappy stamps (My Flaming Geyser is almost
legendary among crappy stamps)
http://www.geocities.com/samanark/Clues/WA/flaming
and (horror) once I even planted a store-bought stamp. (Black Angel
Letterbox in Iowa)
http://www.geocities.com/samanark/Clues/iowa

I may hide a crappy stamp again, but I promise never to hide another
store-bought stamp!
--Amanda from Seattle
PS If I ever get back to Iowa, I will have a nicely carved stamp to
replace that store-bought one ready to go. :-)

Flaming Geyser is NOT a crappy stamp. It's cute. And, certainly, the stamp is better than the actual flaming geyser itself. HA! HA!

I must confess, myself. Any CARVED stamp is far superior to something store bought.
