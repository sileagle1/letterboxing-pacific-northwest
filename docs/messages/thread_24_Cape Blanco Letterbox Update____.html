Cape Blanco Letterbox Update....

From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-03-31 17:06:53 UTC
I got an e-mail today from yet another person that failed to find
this elusive box. (That now makes three attempted finds that I know
about--all of which have come to failure.) However, the good news is
that I got some really good information from this person that might
clarify some of the problems people have been having in hunting down
this box.

First, it seems I got the name of the trail wrong in my clue. The
box is on North Cape Trail, not the non-existant North Shore Trail.

In the clue, I also tried to clarify exactly where I started counting
my paces instead of the vague starting point I had before.

However, the person did say that the side trail my clue says to look
for appears to be overgrown and difficult to find, and the "mounds of
dirt" are no longer there. Since I'm not in the area and can't
really verify that, who knows? Maybe with the vague starting point,
she just wasn't looking in the right area for the side trail (it's
not THAT obvious).

In any case, the letterbox is definitely a lot more challenging than
I planned when I wrote the clue, and I hope some of the changes
help. So far, I still don't have any reason to believe that the box
is actually missing!

Carpe diem!

-- Ryan
