Being the sole owner of this board, I was thinking I need a name more
dignified than 'Ryan'. Any suggestions? Perhaps King Ryan? Or, no,
how about Emperor Ryan? Yes, that has a nice ring to it.... ;o)

Just kidding.... I feel like I'm a kid in a playground looking
around at all the interesting 'features' that I've never been able to
see before because I've only been a lowly peon on the Yahoo Groups
until now, though. =)

In other news, I'm working on creating the world's largest AND the
world's smallest letterboxes. It might be awhile before those come
out, but they are in the works. The world's smallest will make a
film cannister look huge in comparison, while the world's largest--
let's just say that if you bring a regular notebook-sized 8 1/2 x 11
piece of paper to log the find, you might find the stamp a little too
large to fit. I'll probably have to provide some LARGE pieces of
paper in the letterbox for people to use to stamp their find.

The two boxes would have to be connected in some way--the
juxtiposition is too great to hide them separately. Hey--I just had
a thought--the world's largest letterbox really is going to be
LARGE. In fact, maybe it would be SO large I could hide the world's
smallest letterbox IN the world's largest. What a hoot that would
be. I'd have to find some clever mechanism to hide the world's
smallest letterbox in the world's largest where unless someone was
actually looking for the world's smallest, they'd miss it.

Ooohh.... Okay, now I'm really getting excited about this project.
What a challenge! There are bonus boxes, hitchhikers, cuckoo boxes,
and probably a dozen other varieties where a clue or letterbox is
hidden in another letterbox, but not really HIDDEN in it, know what I
mean? I've got to think this over some more. Any suggestions would
be greatly appreciated, though! =)

-- General Ryan
