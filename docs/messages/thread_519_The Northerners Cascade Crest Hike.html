The Northerners Cascade Crest Hike

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-08-05 04:13:05 UTC
http://www.trailjournals.com/entry.cfm?id=31980

The Northerner is a Letterboxer who is doing a through hike on the
Pacific Crest trail.

His journal is wonderful. For anyone who enjoys simple but
thoughtful nature observations, ponderings of life and the
appreciation for the little things, this is a must read.

It is up to date through July 20. I check about once a week, he
usually downloads a batch of entries.

He has tried for some boxes along the way and, for the most part come
up empty. He asked for suggested boxes in Oregon and Washington,
along the crest.

If he continues his successful trip, I suggest a boxing get
together. I have a plant in mind that would not be far from the
crest. Stamp already carved.

Hoopoe
Redmond WA
