From: Quillow (quillow@inkandfeather.com) | Date: 2004-07-04 10:07:04 UTC-04:00
I truly can't wait to get there. I grew up in Maryland near the shore and
moved to Ohio 2 years ago. There is hardly any water here in the lower Ohio
Valley, especially when you're used to ocean fronts and lots of moisture. I
thought I would shrivel up and blow away from lack of moisture my first
winter here. And I won't miss the snow & ice, that's for sure! And the
LOUSY drivers! I don't plan to leave the island except to letterbox, shop &
work the gun show circuits.

I can't wait to hit letterboxes, Mt. Rainier & Pike Place Market. I need to
start running again. I ran track in school but have sluffed off since I was
married many years ago and went thru a divorce. I do love chai tea though
but that I like iced. I will be opening up a tea room out there, not sure
if on the island or not but it'll have a nice shop in it. Our online store
has journaling items, teas, antique desk sets & ink bottles, quill pens, etc
and letterboxing supplies. http://www.inkandfeather.com/store/ We're still
adding since we closed our store here in Ohio. I also have a
preparedness/tactical store, Bear Mountain Tactical, which I have been
packing up this weekend. It's new website will be www.bmtactical.com We
sell law enforcement, military, tactical supplies as well as civil defense,
emergency preparedness supplies and LOTS of military surplus. Our surplus
is all GOOD stuff, not the old WWII stuff that you can hardly use anymore.
Everything from tents, (GP Mediums, USMC 2-man tents), the new sleep
systems, light kits, generators, lots of gear that we use for hiking &
camping. And we're the lowest on the internet. We go through and check
everyone's price & then undercut it if we can. If we can't then we don't
sell that product for the most part.

I work for American Tomahawk Company www.americantomahawk.com which is also
located on the island. We don't have a retail store but we're setting up
out shop/production facility. I currently telecommunte which I've done for
4 years. Now I'll actually be there to work. I also write and am working on
a children's series.

We have 3 black labs, one which is going to my boss, we also have 2 cats and
a scorpion.

Suz
