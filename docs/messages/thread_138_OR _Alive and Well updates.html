OR :Alive and Well updates

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-28 17:52:10 UTC
We've been really busy around the house but have managed to do a bit
of boxing over the last couple of weeks.

Spirit of Dartmoor is wonderful! We were the 1st there since last
September to stamp in!

Nutria- very cute we saw 1 duck and lots of raindrops!

Oregon Trail looking good - very nice stamp but the downpoor left us
with a smudged print.

Beacon Rock is alive and well, even in a hail storm it is difficult
to be discreet! We barely got the box put away when a couple of
drenched hikers came past. It was close.

Has anyone found the Council Crest box - Mad Stamper's? We looked in
all the possible places (we think) to no avail, fear it has been
obducted.

Thank you all so much for providing so much fun and beautiful
stamps!!!! So many boxes so little time. Wahhoo!
