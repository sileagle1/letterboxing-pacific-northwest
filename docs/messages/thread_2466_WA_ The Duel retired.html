WA: The Duel retired

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-03-29 22:33:35 UTC
Hey there fellow WA letterboxers--

I went to check on "The Duel" Letterbox in Lewis County, WA the other
day and had to pull it due to long-term construction in the area.
Because we're soon departing the area for the LONG HAUL I've decided to
retire it--at least until we return to the area in 6 years or so...

Please pull your clues to this letterbox. I've annotated in the clues
and all the LB clue websites (I know about) that it is retired--don't
find yourself in the same shoes as other boxers who have travelled long
distances to look for clues to a letterbox that says ***retired*** in
the name/subject/status line. Yep, it's happened...

Have a great April!

daelphinus

I went to look for this box a couple of months ago with my boyfriend and did not manage to find it. We did run out of daylight too soon, though, and it looked like the place where it was hidden (at least, where we thought it was hidden) had been well-prowled through by maintenance people and so forth, so I didn't have much hope that it was still even there.
