From: Nail Family (c.nail@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-10-25 00:14:29 UTC
There is currently only one Chuck's Tacklebox box left that is still
only boat accessable. 2 more remain at the public boat launches, 2
have recently been pulled in prep for placing with the other 7 at
Millers Syl vania.

There is one other I know of that is a 'floater'. It was placed by
Sunseeker at Silver Lake near Castlerock. I have not been to it yet
but it also requires a boat to reach it. It was placed well over a
year ago and I doubt that anyone has found it yet, unless some
curious fisherman looked in a certain stump.....

Oh, I do also recall something on the main list about a year ago
describing an underwater box placed in a stream or lake. It was in a
waterproof container and weighted so it would sink. I believe it was
tethered to a tree, log, or dock perhaps. I have been toying with
that kind of idea for some time. However, underwhelming response to
the Chuck's Tacklebox series has led me to believe that unless a box
is placed in a kayaking area, no one will venture to seek them out.
Just my experience.
