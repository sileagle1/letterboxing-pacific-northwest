Musician Series on Leif Erikson Drive

From: FlyingMonkey (flying.monkey@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-08-14 23:05:22 UTC
Hey all,

Trekkiemom, Flutterby and I made our second trip out to Leif Erikson
Drive this past Sunday to grab the remaining five boxes that we were
missing from the Musician Series. For the few of you who may not know
too much about this series, it is a definite MUST for boxers in the
Portland area. I think the series includes over 20 boxes, all of
which feature fabulous stamps and great clues.

Anyway, Trekkiemom carved two stamps for this series after we made our
first visit last year. Sunday, we were finally able to plant them.
Clues are located in the usual places. I've got a box I'm working for
the series and will hopefully get up to plant it before the end of summer.

Enjoy :)

FlyingMonkey
