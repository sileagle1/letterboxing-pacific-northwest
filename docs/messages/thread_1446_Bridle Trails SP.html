Happy to report that my 8 BTSP boxes are alive and well , as is the
original (9th) box

I tried to adopt this box during the Great Change Over and it was all
for naught, the original clues are gone.

I have updated my clues to include the box and will be revisiting the
Rattlesnake Ledge box to make sure our Original State box is back on
the national list.

For those that tried and failed on the BTSP boxes, I have tweeked the
clues in places to make them a bit more user friendly and account for
some changes in the park. I still consider the clues moderately
challenging for a novice boxer. The big challenge is the lack of
navigation aids, except trail junctions.

For our California visitor, Bridle Trails is just north of the 520 /
405 junction on the city limits of Bellevue / Kirkland. I hope you
pay a visit. Nine boxes waiting for you.

Good Luck to All
