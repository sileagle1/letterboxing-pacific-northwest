Need more weekends like this!

From: kleibrand2002 (bookwormlb@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-11-11 20:24:43 UTC
Sleepy Whippet, See Shore and I had a great time at the Halloween
Fest '03. It was great to see Campfire Lady & Willow & crew again.
See Shore (who is my mother-in-law) hasn't had many chances to
letterbox, so she cleaned up on the exchanges this weekend, and
doubled her F count and then some! Even picked up an event stamp.

After all the fun on Sunday, I decided to forget doing the stupid
errands I had to do on Monday and went letterboxing instead. It
isn't as much fun to go alone, though.

First went and checked out Corps of Discovery at Frenchman's Bar.
Great stamp, nasty tree! Everything was in good shape.

Next headed out to Rookery. Watch out for evidence of cows when you
go do this one! Box was fine...really like the logbook on this
one. No one had been to either of these boxes for a while. Got to
see a huge flock of birds fly around. What an amazing sight. Wish
I knew more about birds!

Finally I snuck in Smith & Bybee Lake. Amazing spot that I had no
idea was there. Again saw some beautiful wildlife and really
enjoyed the peaceful area. Learned my lesson about assuming you
know which log the clues are talking about and wasted a lot of time
before I pulled out my compass. Duh! No one had been there for a
while either. This is a lovely spot right now with the leaves all
turning colors. Nice walk (although I wouldn't call it a "short
stroll").

Thanks for the fun everyone! I'm ready to go out again. Hmmm.
Maybe I can convince hubby (who may become SeaHunter if I can get
him to decide) we should go pick up a few more today!
