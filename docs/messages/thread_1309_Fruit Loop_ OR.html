Ahhh... what a glorious day to be alive! I headed up the Gorge to search out the new
boxes hidden by Kokopelli in the Hood River Valley. I came home with four of the five
fine stamps in my logbook.

This driving tour is absolutely gorgeous. I only wish I hadn't eaten lunch before I
headed out as this trek takes you to a series of places for fine vittles and drink. It's as
much a food tour as a letterboxing trek. As I type, I can smell the apple pie from the
Apple Valley Country Store as it bakes in my oven. Now I need to go up Dog Mountain
to work it off.
