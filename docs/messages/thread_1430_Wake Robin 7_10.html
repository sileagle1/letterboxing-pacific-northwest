Wake Robin 7/10

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-07-07 18:05:17 UTC
Greetings all! I am hoping that some of you will be able to stop by
Saturday afternoon to meet and greet the very first graduating class
from the Kelso-Longview Parks and Recreation Letterboxing class. For
details please refer to Fudrick's post from 6/16/04 #3704.

The venue for this event is called Wake Robin Environmental Learning
Center. To get there, you go west from Longview on Ocean Beach
Highway, route 4, along the Columbia River. Watch for the large Shell
Station on the left. The next light is Coal Creek Road where you will
go right. Two miles up the road, there is a little general store on
the left. Just past the store is the Earl Reed Shelter. A short
distance past the shelter, go left at the Y on Carlon Loop. As soon as
you cross the bridge, Wake Robin is directly in front of you. The gate
should be open for parking.

Let's meet at the Earl Reed Shelter about 2 o'clock to get a map and
say "Hi!" There is a trail, about 1/4 mile long, from the parking area
to the shelter. The clues for the new letterboxes hidden here will be
distributed about 2:30 to all comers. The class participants get first
dibs. Come join the fun... Funhog

From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-08 19:45:35 UTC
Note for folks coming into Longview via the Lewis and Clark Bridge
(no bridge closures this weekend):

If you go left (west) on Industrial Way just after you cross the
bridge, you can follow that road (it changes names a couple of times
but just keep going straight) all the way to route 4 and go straight
across 4 onto Coal Creek Road (another name change!). Follow
Funhog's directions from there.
