From: Kathy Gilbert (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-25 08:40:16 UTC-07:00
What is your definition of cheap - a price range would be helpful. In
the Gresham area several chain hotels that are clean and reasonably
priced come to mind that cater to airport travelers - they are located
on 181st near the entrance to I-84 also near Airport Way. Some people
call this the Troutdale area, although 181st is really just the edge of
Portland/Gresham dividing line. If you stay there you will have plenty
of boxes available to keep you busy in the Troutdale, Gresham, Powell
Butte, and Columbia Gorge areas, just to name a few, and I would
personally drive to your hotel to meet up with you for an exchange - I'm
fairly new to letterboxing and would love to join you for a few hours of
boxing just to meet some new people, or just meet up with you to say
hello and exchange. Where are you coming from, and how are you
traveling? I live near that area and will give you specific names of
hotels that I know to be clean and comfortable if you give me a price
you want to stay under, and if you like the idea of that particular
area. If you don't want one with a pool it will save you money.

E-mail me back privately if you don't want to keep posting to the board.

Kathy aka Calli-K
