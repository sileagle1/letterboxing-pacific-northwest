From: keegan62002 (macdanny@mindspring.com) | Date: 2006-11-19 00:10:41 UTC
The original stamp has gone missing for my Weatherwatch Park letterbox in West Seattle,
Washington.

I have carved a new stamp and replaced the original journal as of November 18, 2006.
Weatherwatch is active and ready for visitors.

This postage stamp sized park has recently had much of its vegetation removed by the Park
Department. The letterbox is a bit more exposed than it was. If you visit, please re-hide
carefully. But then that pretty much applies to each and every letterbox, now doesn't it?
