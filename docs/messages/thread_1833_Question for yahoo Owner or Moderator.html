Actually, I think I know the answer to this. =) (I'm the owner of
the group, but the group doesn't tell me anything when e-mails bounce.
Mostly behind-the-scenes kind of stuff that owners and moderators
don't have to worry about.)

But to get to the point, it's probably because you're using a
Microsoft accout. Their Hotmail and MSN accounts have gotten pretty
strict when it comes to e-mail, and the only permament solution I know
of is to register with a non-Mircosoft account. Microsoft has been
getting VERY strict in order to help eliminate spam, and the
configuration needs to be set *pricisely* for mail to get sent. I
don't think Yahoo Groups has configured their servers quite so
pricisely and it's tripping the Hotmail/MSN accounts into bouncing the
e-mails as spam. Then when Yahoo Groups gets the bounced message, it
inactivates your account.

The only reason I even know this is because virtually everyone with a
Microsoft-based account on Atlas Quest has been getting their e-mails
bounced for that very reason. Took me two months to finally get the
configuration JUST right where e-mail to Hotmail or MSN accounts have
stopped bouncing. (The bounces weren't specific JUST to Microsoft
accounts, but 9 out of 10 bounces probably came from them.) Yahoo and
Gmail accounts don't seem to be quite as picky, if you're looking for
other free alternatives.

Hope this helps!
