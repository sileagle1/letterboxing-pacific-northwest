We were at the Stamp Camp also. Someone there mentioned that Columbia Art
has some things. We went there and was able to buy the carving tips and
handles (similar to what was sold at camp). You may want to ask about them
their hard to find on your own. the store info is:

Columbia Art & Drafting Supply
1515 E. Burnside (So of Lloyd Ctr... from a Vancouverite's perspective)
Portland, OR
503-232-2216

I can't remember the exact price but they seemed reasonable. My carving
hubby, Wood Splinters, sharpened up the tips. You may check the tips for
sharpness to decide which ones to buy. WS does this by lightly running it
across his fingernail to see if it catches. Typical disclaimers apply here.
