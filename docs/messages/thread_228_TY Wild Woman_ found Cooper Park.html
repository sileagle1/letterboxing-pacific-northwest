TY Wild Woman, found Cooper Park

From: wingnut_lb (wingnut_lb@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-05-26 06:33:45 UTC
Well it seems this lovely little box has a gardian angel. A person
living just on the other side of the fence has adopted it and
replaced it on a weekly basis for over a year now. It seems that
local children make a habit of over turning stumps and rummaging thru
the woods there and he sees them and runs them off, then hunts down
the box and replaces it. He didn't even know what letterboxing was!

This was my third time looking thanks to Wild Woman. She emailed me
and said sometimes ya just gotta keep going back, it makes the find
sweeter! That she had found it just before me. Well, she was right,
it really wasn't there the other 2 times I went, probably relocated
in or around the area by hoodlums. (because it was in the tall grass
this time where the girls new it was, and the Man showed us where he
found it under the log like the clues say, and I had looked there
before) Thanks to Mr Angel's sweet little daughter and her friend.
Carie and I found it this evening and stamped in and replaced it. We
explained letterboxing to our Angel and went along our way. BUT.....
there is a better ending. after 3 tries, and finally finding it, we
got a bonus of the Mickey Hitch Hiker all the way from FLA! Way
cool! So I will be replacing that somewhere soon!

I am so glad to see that not everyone who runs into boxes by accident
take them, that some folks even go the extra mile to take special
care of them! Kudos to this box's Angel, and his lovely girl and her
friend. I am so glad I decided to go back and take yet another look!

Wingnut
