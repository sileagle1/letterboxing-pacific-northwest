Just thought I would say that the Leach Botanical Garden is absolutely
wonderful this time of year, even (if not especially) on a rainy day
like today. It is so quiet and peaceful there. I went to replace a
cracked box and baggies with holes for my letterbox there this
afternoon and enjoyed my little hike more than usual. There are at
least two other boxes there, so those of you who haven't gotten them
all should make time to visit. You may even see our PNW mascot there
if you look closely - there is a sign that designates a banana slug
crossing. The garden's web site has been updated and tells all about
the events and tours available. www.leachgardens.org
