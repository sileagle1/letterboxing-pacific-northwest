Lots of unique log books - hats off to you all!

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-06-08 15:40:27 UTC
In the unusual stamp materials department, I've found a stamp carved
from an old wine cork. The pattern on the cork was the image put there
by the winemaker. You rolled it like an ancient Roman seal to make the
impression. I have a good cork sitting on my table right now that I
have been thinking of doing this with. One of my favorite letterbox
containers was an old metal mailbox, out in the middle of nowhere.
Another favorite was a rusted can in a pile of trash. Funhog
