Last time I crossed the border it was for a class geology trip. I had a copy of my birth certificate, and the man who "greeted" us at the border asked our whole van, "Does a copy of a $100 bill work just like a $100 bill?" I hated most of the people in the class, especially after that better-than-thou incident where they all answered as if I was stupid. I refrained from saying something to the effect that it certainly would prove that a $100 bill exists, but the actual bill wouldn't be needed unless I was trying to bribe him. I decided getting the van searched would not go over well with my professor. As it is, the student driver put it in a ditch up on a mountain, anyway, so they all had something else to worry about.

Sorta worried me on getting into Canada anymore...
