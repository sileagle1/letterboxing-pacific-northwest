Homage and a new box esp. for Ryan!

From: Pungent Bob (PungentBob@HotPOP.com) | Date: 2005-05-16 20:58:20 UTC
There's a new box planted in honor of Ryan out at King's Mountain. I
hope someone besides him takes the effort to make the hike. The
wildflowers are beautiful at the top of the mountain now! Clues are in
the usual places.

While out in the area I went to find Brown's Camp which according to
lore is Oregon's first letterbox. (DMS - can you confirm/deny this?
Its placement is listed as Oct 98, but you show some of you gorge
boxes as placed in Aug 98.)

I was quite happy to have finally added this box to my found list but
was somewhat dissapointed that so few have found such a historical
box, I think it was just six finders before me! Head on out to get
this one folks. When, not if, you do, take along a few good baggies,
this box needs a little TLC and my first aid supplies were
unfortunately little low.
