Snaky Lake LB help

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-01 22:36:57 UTC
Hi there,

Just wanted to post a request for help. I'm getting numerous 'could
not find' reports on our letterbox series at Snake Lake in Tacoma--
although somebody reported all 3 alive & well on 31 July 06. If
anybody happens to be headed out there could you please check on
these LBs and see if they're still viable. I'm not sure if it's
because the boxes are indeed missing or if it's because folks new to
the hobby are attempting the series immediately after learning about
the hobby and aren't quite sure what to look for. I appreciate any
help folks can provide with these boxes--they were never intended to
be difficult boxes but rather 'training boxes' for letterboxing
courses sponsored by the nature center there. The
numerous 'missing' reports are starting to make me think that the
boxes have been yanked or vandalized--even though the staff at the
center fully endorse them being in the park. If there are problems
with them, I'll just retire them to preclude folks from the
frustration of not finding boxes in the future.

Thanks,

daelphinus
