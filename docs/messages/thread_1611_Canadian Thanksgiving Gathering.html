From: Secret Garden B&B (secretgardenbnb@shaw.ca) | Date: 2004-09-13 14:26:38 UTC-07:00
Hello All
The date has been set for the Canadian gathering, October 9th, Canadian
Thanksgiving weekend. It will be held at the original site of the Hudson Bay
Fort Langley and near Historic Fort Langley where their annual Cranberry
Festival will also be happening. So there are lots of reason to come on up!
I will be sending out directions, time and other information in the next
week or so, but only to those who respond. So if you can come - let me know
asap! Also, if you have any special ideas or tips to ensure this is a great
event, let me know. Ryan - we will need an event stamp!!!!
Meanwhile - happy hunting
The Secret Gardeners secretgardenbnb@shaw.ca
