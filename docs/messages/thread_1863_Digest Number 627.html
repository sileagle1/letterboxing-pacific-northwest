There are a couple of ways to send a personal e-mail as a reply to a posted
message.

You can always go into the LbPNW groups web page, find the message, click
reply and check at the top box where it says "TO" and click the drop down
arrow to click on the persons e-mail address. It will only go to the address
in the TO box.

If you getting your messages in Daily Digest, Just click on the persons
e-mail address after their name in the "FROM" at the top of their message.

If you get individual messages, click "REPLY ALL" at the top of your Windows
screen and remove the group name so you only leave the address for the
person you are responding to.

Hope this helps,
