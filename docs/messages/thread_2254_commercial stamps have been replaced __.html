From: Kathy (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-09-25 17:06:30 UTC
During my monthly visit to see our grandson yesterday I got out and
put new hand-carved stamps in the Lonely Juniper box. I'm very proud
of them but left the commercial stamps in there because I kind of like
them too. I have a bit of sentimental attachment to them. I took my
grandson with me, and he stamped into his first letterbox at the age
of seven weeks. He slept through the whole thing but made some funny
noises when I wiped the ink off of his little foot with a cold
wet-wipe. I'm just going to use his feet until they won't fit in log
books anymore. I took clues to eight boxes, wanted to get into one of
funhog's and at least one of 4Dtoes and birder's, but we just didn't
have time. Anyway, the new stamps are there now waiting to be used,
thanks to the encouragement of Der Mad Stamper and funhog.
