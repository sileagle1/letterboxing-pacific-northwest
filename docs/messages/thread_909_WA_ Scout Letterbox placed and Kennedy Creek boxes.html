WA: Scout Letterbox placed and Kennedy Creek boxes doing fine

From: Nail Family (the7nails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-10 02:43:33 UTC
Finally got out and placed the special Scout Letterbox made by
Alafair and delivered by Patricius to the Pack 211 Webelos II last
January. The box was placed at Kennedy Falls which is about 1 mile
upstream of the Kennedy Creek Salmon Trail park. The letterbox is
just for the cub scouts of Pack 211 and the clues will not be
posted. This year's Webelos have a day hike planned for next
weekend and plan to find all the letterboxes at Kennedy Creek as
well as the special box we hid today.

I wanted to let you Fudrick, dvn2rckr, T n E, EG, and Hoopoe know
about this so I could say thank you again for placing the
letterboxes at Kennedy Creek. We checked on a few of them and all 4
that we looked at were in fine shape despite the large numbers of
visitors to the park today. The chum salmon are running full force
right now and there are interpreters at several view points around
the park giving information on the spawn. One person is stationed
right next to a letterbox-- I had to distract him with some salmon
questions while EJN and the G-Man grabbed T & E's box that eluded us
the last time we were there. It is the perfect time to get out and
see the salmon and pick up some very nice stamps too!

Incidently, we signed up for the new Postal Scout Letterbox and when
we recieve it, we plan to have it visit the Kennedy Falls Scout
Letterbox. Since this is such a very nice area (with a 4 mile round
trip hike opportunity to get to the falls) we plan to place a box at
the falls for the general letterboxing community. Keep watching for
the new box to be placed ???
