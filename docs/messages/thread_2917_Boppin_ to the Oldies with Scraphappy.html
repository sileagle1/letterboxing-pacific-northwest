Boppin' to the Oldies with Scraphappy

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2008-06-06 15:51:41 UTC
Boppin Bo's Malt Shop And Grill
7809 NE Vancouver Plaza Dr. Ste. 110
Vancouver, WA 98662
7:00 pm to 9:00 pm

Hey Daddy-O and all you groovy hipsters! Come and meet Scraphappy from
So. California at the grooviest hang out in Vancouver, WA. Bring all
the kiddies and Cool Cats and dig the scene at Boppin' Bo's Malt Shop
and Grill for the best 50's style burgers, endless fries, malts,
shakes, and floats and some good ole stamp swappin! Don't be a party
pooper, bring your LTC's! What's your tale nightingale? Don't forget
your personal travelers! This is a very family-friendly mini-meet that
will sure to satisfy your 50's trivia and your sweet tooth with some
tasty ice cream sundaes. Be there or be square! Don't be a drag. See
you there!
Camp Fire Lady
