Yesterday we went to ArtSpeakNW's newly posted mystery box. Great clues. Found number 1, without the lid, looked further, it had just come off. Wandered off to where the soccer playing family might not take as much notice. Opened the log, found that dvn2r ckr had been there that day, just before me. Logged in, went back to the car, took the now-closed box with me, so as not to show them were it was. Sitting in the car, studying the clues, when there is a knock on EskieLover's window. She opens it, a voice says "We are the Letterbox Police, what are you up to!". It was dvn2r and the whole gang! They were stumped by number two, we found the location, trkee found the box.

They said that all the found of the first box was the bottom, they didn't find the top, so they put it back that way.

A nice series, highly recommended!

Fudrick
