From: HangGlider (hangglider@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-06-16 15:31:58 UTC-07:00
Welcome to the group Shell!

There are boxes in North Idaho (not sure about Montana). And since you'll
likely be coming through Spokane, you can pick off a few there too!

The best place to learn about this hobby/sport is to ask questions right
here. There are a lot of letterboxers in the Seattle area. You may be able
to arrange to meet with them - perhaps at one of the local letterbox
gatherings !

And, of course, check out the web site at:

http://www.letterboxing.org

Again, welcome to the Pacific Northwest Letterboxing list!

-HangGlider
