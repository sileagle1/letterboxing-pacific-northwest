Soggy Sunday fun

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-04-13 23:46:52 UTC
I got out for the first time in a very long time for an all day LBing
adventure.

I plotted a trip south. Starting at the wonderful "Book Keeper" in
Longview placed by Lotta and Lillbit. I found the first book but Mon
Ami, I had a bit of trouble finding the second. I was in the right
location but could not find the absolute. It was nice to see a
Georgia Gerber. Osprey were courting over the waterway too. I will
ponder a book to contribute.

I headed north from there and stopped at Jacksons Cabin ( after
waiting for the grounds keeper to leave). Rain splattered me at the
Fallen Rainbow, but with no sun breaks there were no rainbows to
enjoy. Trilliums however are out in force.

Note of Brag For Trillium lovers, now is the time to visit my boxes
at Bridle Trails SP in Kirkland.

Amanda's vaguely vague "Find A" LB was a good challenge. It was then
the rain started in full force.

dvn2r ckr's Buggy Letterbox at Rotary Riverside appears to have left
when the fallen tree was removed. Nothing left but a newly cut
ground level stump.

The Lost Locomotive was easily found.

It must have been a good day because I did not get cheesed off at the
traffic north from Olympia.

I will have to take account of the Count another time.
