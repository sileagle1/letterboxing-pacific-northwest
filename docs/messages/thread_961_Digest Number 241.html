I too got out letterboxing yesterday. I had the distinct pleasure to meet
up with Bookworm and Sleepy Whippet. They are fantastic people. They even
helped my daughter come up with her letterboxing name. But all being said,
I have to say that the 30 minute chat I had with them before heading into
the hills was the best part of the day. After chatting with them and
stamping into Protection, (Where Bookworm found a hitchhiker but let me move
it, Thank you Bookworm, I didn't realize JUST how nice you were being!) we
headed onto the trail of the Jurassic Park series. WELLL, 1.5 hours later
we had found only box 3 and were hopelessly lost. It took us a good long
time to find our way back to the parking lot. We finally did and found the
Clackamas River stamp before calling it a night.

I'm now headed out to plant some more boxes AND to get the Turtle Soup box.
My husband and I spent an hour breaking pandora's clue last night, so I'll
be danged if I'm not getting it today!
