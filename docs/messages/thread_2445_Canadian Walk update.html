Canadian Walk update

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-03-21 01:06:50 UTC
I want to post this because several people have told me that they
thought #4 of Canadian Walk was missing. Judging from the comments in
the log books, none of these five boxes is really all that easy, and
no one has found them all yet. #4 is not missing; in fact Camp Fire
Lady, Cat's Meow, and I were VERY excited to find it and see that no
one had signed into it before us; this series was planted way back in
summer of 2002. It's the oldest box I've ever found "first." We
could not find #3 or #5 and would like to hear if anyone else has
found those two. Canada Goose, if you are out there and reading this
chat list, congratulations - it is a very fun series and is obviously
entertaining lots of people. And yes, I tried actually singing on one
of my searches for this series.

Canadian Walk update

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-26 22:31:29 UTC
Whooee! Went and found #3 of this series today after a friend told me
it is NOT missing like I thought it was - I've been gathering these
five boxes for some time, kind of getting one at a time - today #5
just called my name. Not easy boxes, but very well put together and
well hidden, and I can proudly say I finally found them all. My
thanks to Cat's Meow and Camp Fire Lady, with whom I was boxing when
we found #4, mostly due to Cat's Meow's super powers of deduction. I
don't know if Canada Goose is still out and about, but if anyone sees
him/her, please give my kudos to a clever goose. Sometimes just
knowing a box is there will encourage you to go and try again. I have
the soaked log book for #5 at my house and will put it back as soon as
I can. The stamp is in the box with some fresh paper in the meantime.
Cock-a-doodle-doo.
