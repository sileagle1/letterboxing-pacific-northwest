A good friend of the PNW letterboxing community recently moved to Alaska.
Well he's coming home for a visit, so the Brat Pack is hosting a gathering
and what's a gathering without games?

You are hereby invited to join us at Wall Street Pizza for a letterboxing
version of "Clue". Thanks to Scarab of the Doubtful Guests, we are going to

We already have RSVP's from lots of old friends and new, I hope you see YOU
there as well.

Those that RSVP in time will recieve an email about 3 days before the event
with a list of all local boxes and about how much time to allot for them if
you're interested in combinding some boxing with the gathering!

If you have any questions, please email me at maiden1974@verizon.net
