Pre-Silver Falls

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-06-23 05:28:52 UTC
I understand that it poured buckets in Portland today.
However, I was tramping around hiding boxes out in
the Silver Falls area in preparation for the upcoming
gathering and the weather couldn't have been better.
I toted full raingear but never needed it for a second.
The sky was clear blue with big poofy white clouds
floating by. As dry as the skies were, my waterproof
boots did come in mighty handy, what with the muddy
trails and big puddles.

After leaving three new boxes in my wake, I checked
on my two old boxes out there, Buddah and Frenchie
Falls. Both were doing fine, books nearly full but backs
of pages available for future visitors. I suspect that the
traffic on the Trail of Ten Falls will be thick on the holiday
weekend and these boxes may be difficult to impossible
to collect during the gathering... Too many prying eyes
in the woods. The clues for the new boxes will not be
available until the event. Anybody else hiding boxes for
the big day?

I also had a quick look at the Group Camp which is
located on the right just after you enter the park coming
from Silverton. If you get to the North Falls parking area,
you've gone too far. Tent spaces A, B and C are on the
left as you enter the area, before you get to the recycling
bins. There are fire rings and picnic tables and I plan on
bringing some firewood with me. Gotta have those s'mores
to go with the Krispy Kremes.

I also picked up a brochure for a RV Park in Silverton that
might be an option if there are folks who want to camp but
don't have a spot reserved with Pungent Bob. The place is
called Silver Spur RV Park and the phone number is 503.874.9045
Can you tell I'm excited about a weekend of camping at one
of my favorite places? I'm even scheming on a trail ride while
I'm there. Yee Haw!!! Funhog
