The boxes only show up in the counties under which the planters placed
them when they originally posted the clues. There are several boxes in
incorrect counties in WA--it's up to the planters themselves to correct
this--if that's their intention. Some folks intentionally hide their
letterbox clues in different regions for various regions. Other times
it's generally an oversight or folks just don't know in which counties
the box resides. Not sure what the case is with these and the other
letterboxes that are listed in incorrect counties. Perhaps contact the
planter directly to let them know about the problem? Hope this helps,
