Wotawe

From: Sam (sngngwolf@gmail.com) | Date: 2007-05-02 15:34:01 UTC-07:00
Yesterday morning, May 1st, our dog Wotawe had some sort of seizure and
passed away at the veterinary hospital. We don't understand the reason for
her sudden illness, it was completely unexpected. Many of you met her at
the Spring Flinger event, others met her at the Oregon Luau and were lucky
enough to get her personal stamp. I know she had a wonderful time meeting
all of you and loved all the attention and love you gave her.

We had ten wonderful years with her. We're grateful that her last few days
were spent doing what she loved best, being outside, camping, and making new
friends. Her absence has left a huge hole in our lives that can never be
filled.

Our memories of her are all we have now. She always seemed to have an
impact on those she met, even for a short time. If you have any memories,
impressions, or thoughts of her you'd like to share, send them to us by
email and we will put them up on her personal traveler page.

For now, her stamp will be retired. She carried it herself, and neither of
us are ready to carry it for her. Someday we'll carry it as a memorial to
her, but for now it just hurts too much.

Give your pets a hug tonight and tell them you love them.

Singing Wolf and Storm Wind
