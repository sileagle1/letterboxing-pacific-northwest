New letterboxes in Yacolt, WA!

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2003-11-29 04:22:40 UTC
Since I have reached and exceeded my 100 mark for found letterboxes,
I see it as only fitting that I contribute to the excitement and
place some more boxes besides my Happy Postman Series. There are two
new letterboxes in Yacolt, WA.
Lucia Falls Letterbox and "Luv Ya" Letterbox. Both are located at
Lucia Falls Park with some spectacular views of some waterfalls. When
placing these boxes, Cat's Meow and I witnessed an amazing sight! A
number a very large salmon were trying to make their way up the falls
to spawn. This is something that I have only witness on TV but to see
it in real life was amazing! Be the "Firsties to these boxes for an
added surprise! The clues for these boxes are posted on the
letterboxing.org website. Happy Hunting!
Camp Fire Lady

P.S. Watch for other letterboxes to be placed in the surrounding area
