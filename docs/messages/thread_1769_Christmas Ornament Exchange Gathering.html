Christmas Ornament Exchange Gathering

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-11-20 17:29:46 UTC

Come join Cat's Meow and I for lunch and a Christmas Ornament
Exchange Gathering in Vancouver,WA., Saturday, December 11 at 1:00
pm. The location has yet to be determined and I will let you know
where as soon as I make a reservation. I was so inspired by a recent
PLB that I received and thought it would be a great way to get some
of the many letterboxers in the Vancouver and surrounding areas
together to finally meet each other. In order to participate in the
ornament exchange at 2:00 pm, you must bring an ornament, hand made,
with the theme of letterboxing, and wrapped in holiday wrapping
paper. We will have lunch, exchange signature and gathering stamps,
play some fun games and everyone can then go find some of the
wonderful letterboxes that are planted all over the Vancouver area.
Please R.S.V.P., A.S.A.P. to tinarie at msn dot com if you would like
to come so that I can make the reservation. If you cannot make it at
1:00 for lunch then please join us at 2:00 for the exchange. See you
there!
Camp Fire Lady
