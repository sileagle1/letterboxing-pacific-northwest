From: erfellie (hemenekawan@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-06-09 01:53:19 UTC
I just got back from vacationing in one of the smallest towns in
california (Colfax) and since they dont even have a single stop light
and I thought for sure there were no computers or even electricity, I
didnt think to look to see if there were any nearby letterboxes in this
town. Just on a whim I hopped on my friends computer (which I was
suprised to see she had modern technology) and whaddya know, theres a
couple LB's and I was without my Lbing equipment!! So what did I do? I
went and found 3 of these LB's anyways, grabbed some index cards to
stamp on and a sharpie marker (because I had no ink pad) and this was
my version of a quickie fix-er-up letterboxing trip. I was very happy
to find these LBs even though my stamped images came out really poor
quality, but still its a fun memory :) What a nice suprise. Ill have to
go back there some day and get FunHogs "Empire Mine" LB, I really
wanted to get that one but didnt have the time.
