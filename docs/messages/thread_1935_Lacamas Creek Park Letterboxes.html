Lacamas Creek Park Letterboxes

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2005-02-21 18:05:16 UTC

What a beautiful afternoon it was yesterday! Cat's Meow and I finally
got a chance to venture out and hunt for boxes and of course we had
to go after the 2 new boxes by SloMo and Woodauge. Both are alive and
well and what a beautiful carving Contemplation is! This is one stamp
the you really need to have in your collection.
While there we checked on a few other boxes:

Der Mad Stamper's Lacamas Creek #2 alive and well and not visited
since October 2004.
ISignAsLily's Glub Blub alive and well despite reports of it gone
missing.
My own PNW Wildlife Series *OWL* alive and well and not visited since
September 2004.

Come on people. Lets get out there and box!
Camp Fire Lady

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-02-21 14:33:23 UTC-08:00
We found it very funny, we too headed out to Lacamas Creek Park today. We
noticed we were only a day behind you on those boxes by SloMo and Woodauge
and I have to agree, both are fantastic stamps, I adored Contemplation!!!!
We didn't make it up to Der Mad Stamper's boxes, but did stamp into Glub
Blub and PNW *OWL*, which is another gorgeous stamp you definitely want in
your book. VERY well done!

The kids and I also hit a semi mystery box early this morning and rehid the
Instant Gratification box in it, with six stamps in it. It's a good time to
hit it if you want to join in on the fun!
