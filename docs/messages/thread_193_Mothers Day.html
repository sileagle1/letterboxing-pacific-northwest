Mothers Day

From: Mariette (dbltall@speakeasy.net) | Date: 2003-05-13 17:00:17 UTC-07:00
Team Garlic took me out for a Mothers Day letterboxing in West Seattle.
We had a nice walk on a beautiful sunny day and found Amanda's 3 Schmitz
Park boxes. We took a look at the beginning "stairs" to Mee-Kwa-Mooks
and decided that it was intended for a more athletic audience (as befits
a planter called "Jill the Reckless").
We had a picnic at Camp Long and looked for Kathyh's Camp Long box,
found the exact stump described in the clues, but no box.
We looked around, so I think this one may be missing. It was a fine walk
anyways, and the nicest Mothers Day I could have spent.
