Reading Rocks PLB arrives in Vancouver

4 messages in this thread | Started on 2004-08-30

[LbPNW] Reading Rocks PLB arrives in Vancouver

From: Pupp Naish (rambopupp@doglover.com) | Date: 2004-08-30 20:59:54 UTC-05:00
Hi Everyone -
A new postal letterbox has arrived in Vancouver, Washington. Some of us are getting together on Friday September 3rd at 6 PM at Barnes and Noble. After some stamping at Barnes and Noble, some of us are going to pizza at Round Table. Anyone who wishes may join us. Please bring a book to exchange, in honor of the postal box. The owner of this box said - the more the merrier. There are slots for FIVE books to be placed into the postal letterbox before being sent out. These five books must have a Book Crossing ID Number. Go to http://bookcrossing.org/ for more information about book crossing. Most of the slots for the books that will be placed into the postal are taken. However, feel free to bring a book just to exchange with someone else.
Which Barnes and Noble? WELL....you figure it out - The B&N is in Vancouver, it's by Pupp's place of employment, near the Stop the Stigma letterbox, off 500, near 4th plain, by a Winco, Petsmart, Chuck E Cheese, a movie theatre, Office Max, and a Michaels. Oh, and a Target and Bed, Bath and Beyond. It's also very near 205. If you get stuck, just ask somone, LOL.
Any questions? Just email.
Please let me know if you are planning on coming. If I get enough interest, I'll carve an event stamp. (And, yes, Pandora, you can come do my dishes beforehand, I'm carving the stamp right now! LOLOLOLOL)
Pupp (Team Nashoba / Brat Pack)
