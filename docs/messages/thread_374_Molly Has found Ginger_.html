Molly Has found Ginger!

From: Bobby Eubanks (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-30 21:53:05 UTC-07:00
(This is about the Molly Looking for Ginger Hitchhiker
for those who may be confused)

Today Brett and Ginger met Molly at the Vernonia Lake
Letterbox. (Great stamp Chuck!) She and Ginger are
having lots of fun. Molly plans to attend the Portland
Summer gathering before heading back home.

Interestingly enough, a family of first time
letterboxers had just gotten to the letterbox when we
walked up to the location! These lucky boxers got
their first box, first hitchhiker, and first exchange
all at once! I'll bet that hasn't happened many times
before, if ever. It also made for my first exchange
not at a gathering.

FYI for all you PNW boxers - this new boxing family
has already figured out my Bugle Mystery Box and plan
to get this weekend. As far as I know, only Mark and
Beth have gotten that one. All you other lazy boxers
need to get on the ball! :)

Thanks for the HH and entertainment, Chuck!

PS for those wondering about the status of the Ginger
looking for Molly HH, as of last week it was in New
York somewhere waiting for a ride to Connecticut.
