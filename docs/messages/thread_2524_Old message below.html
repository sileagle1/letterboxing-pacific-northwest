I just looked up old messages from myself on this board. I haven't
posted since August 2004. You see, shortly after I wrote this
message, I found out my husband had been having an affair. I wanted
to work on the marriage, but he didn't and told me to take a hike,
December 2004.

Moved into an apartment January 2005 and bought my own townhouse in
August.

Now it's May 2006 and I want to LB again -- with a new logbook that
will be just my own, though I will still claim all the boxes I did
before!

I have always wanted to meet you and go on LB camping weekend but my
husband wasn't interested.

Soooo, a couple of things:

1)When are any camping events this summer?

2) Do you know of anyone that would want to LB every now and then
with me, as I don't like to go alone?

3) I'm involved with a social group (meetin.org for Portland), a
website for people 20's-60's, single, married, with significant
others. It's free and when people have ideas, they post an event and
other people sign up. I posted a letterbox event for July 15 and
want to take these few people on an easy box or two (boxes that are
hopefully not missing.) Do you have any ideas?

Thanks so much. I actually carved a stamp for my first box to be
placed on Ross Island, so you would have to canoe or kayak to get to
it. My husband and I scoped out the site. I still plan to plant it
but having moved twice and not having unpacked all of my boxes. . .

Thank you for any response.

Email me at susan@kuku.org.

Susan Johnson

And, I'm thriving, not just surviving!
