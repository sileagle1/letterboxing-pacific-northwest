From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-20 17:50:10 UTC
Keeping along the thread of discussion about boxes that don't get many
visitors right away, if ever--I've kept a mental note of the boxes
we've planted over the past few years. One box had been hidden for two
years with nary a visitor until 'poof!' it experienced a flurry of
visitors after an article ran in a local newspaper in that region.
What's worse is that it initially contained a HH that sat idle for a
year until we rescued it on a maintenance trip to ensure it was still
there because we hadn't heard anything about it.

A couple of more recent examples--we hid a box a year ago and it has
only had one group of visitors to date. We also hid two this past Jan
and both have yet to have any visitors some 9.75 months later. Another
case of an idlin' HH involved in one of these. These boxes aren't
in 'far flung' places either, so it's a bit perplexing. ;) The hobby
seems to be 'cyclical' though as it seems to run in spurts around media
attention/WOM.

So, you just never know...
