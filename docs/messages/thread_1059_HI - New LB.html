First I would like to send my thanks to Funhog and Legerdemaine for
the really nice Aloha stamps (Manu Make) on the Valley Isle -
beautiful hike!

And in the spirit of the islands a new green turtle has been born,
albeit not "the" green turtle.

I have left a new LB - "wikiwiki honu" in South of Maui. It is
appropriately named as it resides in a high-traffic area - although
a nice little turtle it may quickly disapear. . The
clues have been posted.

The purpose of the positioning of wikiwiki honu is to allow for a
quick stop-n-find on the way to/from one of Funhogs 'royal' boxes.

If you make it to the the Islands this year be sure to make time for
Funhogs 'Manu Make'. It is a great hike, schedule part of the day
for paragliding on the same mountain, take some layers (the temp had
a 20degree swing from bottom to top the day I was there).

Happy LB'ing!
