Letterboxing Success

From: Jon and Laura (jandljames@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-29 19:12:43 UTC
This weekend marks our most successful LB adventures! Of course, we
still are newbies, and therefore do not have many to boast of yet.

We started the weekend Friday afternoon with the Rood Bridge
collection. Got 4 of 5, but since this collection is growing, we'll
be back.
On Saturdy, we went up to Mt. Hood and snaged the Little Crater Lake
box, which surprised me since I forgot the clues at home! When we
found this box, it was almost out in the open, somewhat behind the
tree. We re-hid it better, however I think that it won't last very
long. I noticed that forest creatures have chewed on the lid. I
suggest the next person who visits this box bring some bark or a rock
to cover it better.
Next we went to Umbrella Falls. This one's hiding spot was perfect,
yet easy to find (with the clues in hand).
Sunday we blew off household chores and captured the entire Upper
MacLeay Series by Paisly Orca, Barry's MacLeay Box, plus one more!
During the weekend adventures, the Multi-tasking HH was found, and HH
George decided to end his ride.

All in all, it was a great weekend with 12 finds. I wouldn't have
gone to several of these locations without being involved in LB.

The Transplanted Cowboys,
Jon, Laura, and Tyler
