Hi :o) Well I finally got my letterbox placed, I carved the stamp
last year, but just never found a good enough place to put it! And I
am most proud of this stamp, its definitly worth the travel and
deciphering I think, but then again I am biased I guess cuz its mine
haha. It can be found on LBNA under "Disney Trivia" in Coos County,
Oregon, Region #6 (Southwestern Oregon). There just arent enough
letterboxes in this county! this would make it the 7th LB out there,
but there a great stamps :o) Coquille River, September Seagull, and
Sandy Creek Covered bridge are some of my favorites. Well since I
must be the only one travels to this town on a regular basis I guess
its up to me to plant more there :o) and maybe, just maybe, someone
that lives there might find out what it letterboxing is, we could use
one more letterboxer, right? So this letterbox might take you a
little work to find it, you must decipher the (easy) clues and the
only way to do this is to watch a certain Disney movie with your
family (and dont forget the popcorn) because this box was made
especially for this reason, to bring families together for a night of
family fun, so enjoy it :o)
