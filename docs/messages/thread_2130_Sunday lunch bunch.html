Sunday lunch bunch

From: wildhairlb (wildhairlb@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-05 19:04:34 UTC
The Silver Falls lunch crowd was so very welcoming! We were feeling a
bit nervous, like most would, approaching a picnic table with a bunch
of people, not knowing a soul and not even sure we're in the right
place! Right away the open log books signaled that we were in the
right place and Wild Woman welcomed us into the fold. I was so happy
to meet some of the people whose beautiful boxes we've found at lovely
and interesting places in the past two years (August.) Seeing all the
stamping kits and stamp carving tech talk was fun.
Thanks to Pungent Bob for organizing the event, sorry we didn't get
to meet you, but loved those Krispy Kremes! Thanks also for the
beautiful event stamp from Der Mad Stamper. That was worth the trip in
itself! And Amanda Briles, the Paisley Orca, (I love your North Head
Lighthouse stamp, by the way, one of our cherished early finds)--
Mandy! It was great to get reaquainted, whoever brief. Your spirit is
as bright as I remember, from 20 years ago! Very happy also to meet
Doublesaj and Old Blue from CA! Did you get your 500? We hiked the
quiet and beautiful Shellburg Trail, found Funhog's great boxes, a HH
and had a rejuvinating afternoon. Then off to the St. John Rodeo (talk
about a contrast.)
We had a really nice time!!
--Wildhair, Ginky, Handyman and Fun Gal
