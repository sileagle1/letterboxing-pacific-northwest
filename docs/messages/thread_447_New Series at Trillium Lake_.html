New Series at Trillium Lake!

From: cyndie626 (cyndiesphoto@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-07-21 03:14:13 UTC
We went camping this weekend and got inspired to place 6 boxes on the
path around the lake! It is absolutely gorgeous here! (and 2 year
old and stroller friendly) And even though it was very crowded, we
didn't "feel" crowded by people! It's next to impossible to find
camping if you don't have reservations, but the fishing was great!
All in all a lovely weekend, I highly recommend a visit! The clues
are posted on the national site, or here if the link works.....
http://letterboxing.wesgarrison.us/BoxView.asp?
boxnum=4877&boxname=Trillium_Lake_Forest_Finds_Series

I hope to hear from some of you soon as to your finds here!

Wingnut
