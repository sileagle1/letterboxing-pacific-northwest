From: Mischief In Wa (Mischief_wa@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-05-26 21:46:21 UTC
Hi Chari! Welcome to letterboxing!

I'm very glad to hear you found my Mukilteo box. I havn't had a chance to
check on it recently and I'm glad to hear it's alive and well.

I got a short list of boxes I'd recommend if you don't mind driving a bit
south.

Honey Pot at St Edwards Park (Kirkland)
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=3933&boxname=Honey_Pot_@_St._Edward's
I visited this one this weekend and it'd alive and well. This great one for
kids as there's this REALLY great playgroud right at the trail head.

BBTC Bridge at St Edwards Park (Kirkland)
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?boxnum=3756&boxname=St._Edward's_BBTC_Bridge_Box
This one is in the same park as the previous box and was doing well when I
found it last weekend. It's a nice walk that little ones can manage. Keep
your eyes open for the bikers. =D

Redmond Watershed Preserve (Redmond)
http://home1.gte.net/res0fpfm/letterboxes/redmond.htm
This one is another of mine. Two boxes on this hike and seems to be very
popular with beginning letterboxers. =D

And if you can manage it at all, you should come join us at our Meet and
Greet this Saturday. It's sure to be loads of fun and I expect there will
be a lot of little ones to play with too. =)

~ Mischief ~
http://www.letterboxingmischief.com
