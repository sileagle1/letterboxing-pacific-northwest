arriving at a LB site

From: BtlDroid (btldroid@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-28 17:59:45 UTC
I can't help but wonder with whom I am crossing paths as I head
toward a letterbox. Yesterday evening, we went to search for one in
a relatively small, old park. As we were arriving, three people were
leaving. I didn't see any sort of pack for carrying LB supplies, so
I figured it was just a coicidence that we were in the same park at
the same time.

When my children and I went to put our stamps in the first logbook,
we noticed that one other set of people had been to the same box the
very same day. Could it have been the people we had just seen?....I
doubt it, but it was fun to wonder.

Rose (with Lucky and Hound)

BTW, If I remember correctly, I think it was Team Chaos who had
visited the same set of boxes.
