From: Kris Leibrand (bookwormlb@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-03-01 22:08:13 UTC

I'm a little late in writing this note, but two weeks ago Seahunter
(whose name will be changed to Grumpy Monkey pretty soon if he
doesn't shape up when letterboxing with me) and I took a trip to
Newport/Lincoln City and visited 3 boxes. Here's an update:

**Freddy at Tadpole's Birthday by the Hoppy Hunters is alive and
well. It was a little damp inside, but the baggies kept everything
nice and dry. This box hasn't been visited for a while (Sept 04).

**Boiler Bay by the Lennen's 3 is alive and well but not recently
vistited either. I just tried to look it up on letterboxing.org and
it isn't on there anymore, but the box is alive and well and ready to
be found!

**Beneath the Blue #2 by Funhog has had a visit more recently and a
new log book added. This box was full of water, but the baggies
protected everything inside. I dried it out and tried to put it back
in its hidey-hole so it wouldn't get more water in it. Hopefully it
worked.


And a box a little closer to home:

**Instant Gratification (new location) by Pupp & Maiden1974. Get out
there and get this box with its new holiday stamps...they are great!
There are SIX new stamps in there that will only be there for a
month. You will be really mad at yourself if you miss these great
stamps.

I've had my nose buried in a book for a while, but I hope to see a
lot of you at the gathering next weekend! I've missed all my
letterboxing buddies!

Bookworm
