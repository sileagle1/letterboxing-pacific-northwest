GREAT NEWS!!! Although not out letterboxing today, I had a moment
to check on the Funny Little Man in D*iscovery Park earlier in the
day. He was very much alive & well!!! It appears the clues to this
letterbox have already been pulled so I am unable to verify if
recent repairs in the park have affected the clues. It appears that
the recent addition of a new bridge/boardwalk may affect the
interpretation of the clues? Not sure since I can't locate a copy
of the old clues to figure out if they have changed any due to the
recent repairs. The placer of this letterbox is not from the area
so I wouldn't know how to contact them at the moment. Anybody know
how to contact the placer and have them repost the clues since it's
still there--very much wanting visitors? I can review them once
posted and see how much/if anything has changed along the trail and
what needs revision.

As for the other boxes in this area, the little trail changes very
much from season to season (as I've learned the past several
months). It also appears that a major windstorm has felled several
trees throughout the area since the series was placed and there has
also been some extreme erosion along various parts of the trail. I
have a feeling this has much to do with the proclivity of missing
boxes in the area.
