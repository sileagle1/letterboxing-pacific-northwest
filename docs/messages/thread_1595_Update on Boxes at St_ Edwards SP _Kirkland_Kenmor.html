Update on Boxes at St. Edwards SP (Kirkland/Kenmore WA)

From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-08 23:06:27 UTC
I was out letterboxing today and found lots of boxes at St. Edwards
State Park. The Honey Pot at St. Edwards, The Skunk Bucket (could use
a ziplock baggie inside the box...unfortunately I didn't have a spare
with me!), and boxes 4-9 of the Savannah Series at Bastyr. (1-3 are
missing and noted as such in the clues)

The Savannah Series has some awesome stamps, so they are well worth
the trip. Be discreet if folks are in the area. I was lucky, there
were no others on the trails here in the middle of the day, middle of
the week.

Also I picked up a Hitchhiker...Let's Play Ball! It looks like it was
just started by Wives and Daughters and left at the Savannah Series
#4. I will be sending it on it's way soon.

-Amanda from Seattle
