I went and got this box today at lunch. What fun clues and a very
nifty box! I love clues that you can't figure out until you are
there. I was the second person to stamp into this box (last one was
Sept 6). It was hidden very well, and I made sure to hide it better
than I found it. I'm very proud to have this wonderful Bookworm stamp
in Bookworm's log book. (Hee hee. Uh, oh, too much sun. I'm getting
silly now!)

Best of all, I used my brand-new signature stamp in this box for the
first time. It seemed fitting that my new stamp should get stamped
into this box first. My buddy Maiden was kind enough to take time out
of her busy schedule and carve it for me. I will be debuting it at the
Joker's Wild Letterboxing Poker Run Gathering on October 1st. So stop
by and see me if you'd like to have it in your book. I'm happy to show
it off. You might want to ask me about any books I've read lately,
too. :-)
