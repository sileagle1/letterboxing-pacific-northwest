Hi! I wish I could have joined you all, it would have been fun to
meet all of you :o) but unfortunately at my job I can't take off a
weekend shift without being fired, lame, huh? but thats life I guess!
Did anyone make and letterbox ornaments? or where they all just
regular xmas ornaments? I was just wondering what ideas other LBers
would have for making ornaments. We arent having a tree this year,
our house is too small and I think it would really confuse my dog
since he has never seen a christmas tree and it would be pretty awful
if he "watered" the presents as a male dog might want to do! Does
anyone have a funny christmas stories they would like to share? Heres
mine if anyone would like to hear it, since its the christmas season
and all :o)
I bought my boyfriend Luke (our dogs name is also Luke) his present 2
months ago. He was desperatly wanting a pretty spendy gun (no he is
not a hunter)and I was worried I wouldnt get it in time for xmas so I
went ahead and ordered it from a local gunshop, well I thought it
would take a few weeks to recieve like everything else you might
order (4-8 weeks, 8-12, 12 to never) and it got there in 2 days. So I
brought it home in the case and hid it in the house and drove to
work, within 10 minutes he found it. (He saw me take the case in the
house i guess and went looking as soon as I was gone.) You would
think that being a letterboxer I would be good at hiding things! well
my strategy was to hide it in such a horribly obvious place that he
would never look there, and of course it was the first place he
looked. duh. well he was so excited he stole the gun and replaced it
(plastic wrapper and safety tags and all) with a plastic toy gun he
spray painted that color and put it back in the too-darn-obvious
hiding spot. Well he teased me later on that he took the gun out of
the case and I told him he was lying and so the first chance I got I
opened the case, saw a gun inside of it, and closed it and rehid it.
Well I am not one who knows the first thing about guns so I didnt
even bother to realize that it was a completely different (Plastic)
gun in the case and figured Luke (the human) was just trying to get a
rise out of me. Well the next night I came home from work and I saw a
gun on the table and at first I was really dissapointed because I
thought he had purchased it himself, the same gun i already bought
him, and was bummed because it was expensive and non-returnable. So I
asked him in a cute innocent voice "Where did you get that?" and he
stuttered a bit and couldnt think of a lie fast enough I guess and
admitted what he had done. Boy did i feel stupid when I opened up
that gun case (for the second time) and picked up the gun inside of
it and realized it didnt weigh anything (cuz its plastic!!). I
couldnt believe it, he was so sneaky he put all the safety tags on
the gun and re-wrapped it in the same plastic bag and everything!
what a sneaky boy :o] So now i have to buy him anoooooother
present.... and his shopping list isnt cheap! I better get a good
present this year for putting up with him :o) Happy holidays
Everyone!!!!!!!! :o)

Moral of the story: Letterboxers should know better than to hide
presents in obvious places, especially if you have super-sneaky loved
ones!!
