OR: Tryon Creek

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-03-26 22:12:59 UTC
Greetings all! I will be donning my diplomat cap on Monday morning and meeting
with the park manager at Tryon Creek SP. Hopefully we'll be able to work something
out similar to what we have done with Kyle Spinks at Tualatin Hills Parks and Rec. If
anyone has anything they would like me to say to the powers that be, please let me
know in advance. All suggestions will be much appreciated.

In the meantime, if you haven't gotten the classic Mad Stamper boxes in this park,
this is probably the perfect weekend to get them. If they have to be pulled it won't be
until after our meeting. I know Mudstumpers have already removed a couple but
perhaps they'd be willing to replace them just for the weekend. Cross your fingers
that they will all be allowed to remain in the future.

Crossing my little pink trotters, Funhog
