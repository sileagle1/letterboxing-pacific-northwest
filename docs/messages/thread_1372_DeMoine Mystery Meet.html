DeMoine Mystery Meet

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-05-31 17:36:31 UTC
Thank you dvn2r ckr for planning this gathering and giving me and
Cat's Meow the opportunity to meet some really neat letterboxers that
we haven't met before and to see some old friends from former
gatherings. We also had the chance to add to our logbooks stamp
images from you, Amanda from Seattle, Trial and Error, Canada Goose
and even Evil Glenn. This weekend also allowed me to mark a huge
milestone of 200 finds with the first of Trial and Error's Gnome
Series at Port Defiance Park. What a beautiful park! Cat's Meow
marked her 200 milestone with your Washington Territorial Insane
Asylum box. Fort Steilacoom is also a very nice park! We picked up a
few hitch hikers and launched a couple as well. It was nice to put
the faces to the names of Wanda and Pete and a pleasure to meet them
and of course it is always a pleasure to spend the entire weekend
boxing with Pandora and Iron Bear. Thanks for the great memories Team
Chaos! We hope to see all of you and others at the Passport to
Letterboxing Stamp Camp at Battle Ground Lake August 27-29. It is
going to be an event that you are not going to want to miss!
Camp Fire Lady
