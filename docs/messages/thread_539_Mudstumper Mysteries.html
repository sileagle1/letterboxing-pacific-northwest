From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-08-07 23:43:52 UTC
Today was the day to add my first two Mudstumper stamps to my log.
Can't tell you how much I enjoy having new carvers on the scene!

What's in a name? Everything...including my cachet...

At the mill bells did ring,
A box was left, waters sing.
The spot is good with sunny skies,
but when it rains the creek will rise.
Letterboxes float away,
Mud and mold come to stay.
The hitcher left with me today,
Where it goes I cannot say.

Funhog
