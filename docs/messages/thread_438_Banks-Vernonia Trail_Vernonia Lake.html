I got a report that the first box in the B-V network (Buxton
Trestle) was missing. I checked and indeed it was but it is now
replaced. It was interesting to carve an eraser again after such a
long time. I first checked the Tophill box to make sure it was there
in case the nefarious Ranger Rick had pulled all the boxes without
notification. It was there so I assume the Buxton Box went missing
via some other reason.

I think it was Heather that told me at the gathering that they were
planning to nab these boxes in August. Two are now accounted for
this week as alive, I suspect the rest are also fine.

Vernonia Lake has gotten a rash of activity lately from some
unfamilar names. I assume the Portland Parent article has something
to do with it. The HH I left there (Ohio Flamingo) was passed up by
one group but taken by the next. Nice to see some letterboxing
activity in the big city of Vernonia. It gives me hope that some
boxes might get planted nearby so we won't have to venture out to
that middle of nowhere Portland place with all the boxes. :)
