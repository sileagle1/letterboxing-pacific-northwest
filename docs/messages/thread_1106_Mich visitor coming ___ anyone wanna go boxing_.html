From: sailing_bindlestiff (sailing_bindlestiff@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-29 14:56:32 UTC
Hello everyone:

I'll be visiting Portland next month and wanted to invite you all to
participate in the opportunity for some boxing-galore! LOL

Bindle Bum isn't making the trip with me, so I'm on my own this
time. This is my first visit to Portland, so I'm not familiar with
the city. I'll be staying at the Embassy Suites/Airport and won't be
getting a rental car. However, not to worry I understand Portland
has a wonderful public transportation system and the very dear,
Maiden1974 (Melanie) has offered to play host and driver for some
boxing!

This is what has been planned have so far:

Wed 2/18 Everyone welcome to join meeting up with Funhog and
myself at the Laurelwood Pub for dinner and stamp exchanges ....
time undecided as yet (Do we need reservations for this place,
Funhog?)

Friday 2/20 Maiden will pick me up from my hotel at 2:15p and
we're going out boxing! Everyone is invited to join us, Maiden will
be in charge of choosing the area to be searched. Then it's on to
the Spaghetti Factory in Vancouver for dinner around 6pm (Thank you
Willow for being willing to make the reservations!) Please contact
Willow with your RSVP so she'll know how many are planning to attend.

Saturday 2/12 I am free the entire day and Maiden has once again
generously offered to host me for another half day of boxing! Of
course, everyone is welcome to join us. We'll be out from about
10am until maybe 3pm with Maiden once again selecting the area for
searching. After that, I'm open to more boxing, another dinner-
time stamp exchange gathering, or whatever!

I had a hitch hiker in my Kent Trails box last time I checked. If
it's still there after Valentines Day I'll go unbury him from under
a couple feet of snow and bring him along with me.

Looking forward to meeting a bunch of you and putting some face to
the names I see on this lists.

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
