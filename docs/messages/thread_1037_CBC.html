The Seattle count is tomorrow. I have lost track when the others in
the area occurred. My partner this weekend did Whidbey Island last
weekend.

I don't keep up on the birding news. In our little territory we have
a hard time because it is not a glamor spot. Hard to get volunteers
to help survey such glamor spots as Boeing Field, Georgetown and
Allentown.

We usually get a very respectable Crow, Starling and house sparrow
count. We get mini surprises like 18 Downey Woodpeckers at one spot
and a Clark's Grebe on the Duwamish.

Often an article will appear in a quarter or so giving discussion of
the regional and national trends.

It will be a long wet day.

Its all good.
