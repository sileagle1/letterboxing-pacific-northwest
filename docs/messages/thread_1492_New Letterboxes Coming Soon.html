excited to get out here and I wanted to go to Pike Place Market and Mt.

pulling teeth. But..........

your while to come over here to the island. I understand there's a geocache
here but since I can barely work a calculator I won't bother with the GPS
thingy.

They'll be going out in September and I'll post the clues ASAP.

Unfortunately it's also becoming the rainy season. Does anyone have an
opinion on maybe waiting until Spring to plant them or do y'all box
year-round? In Ohio I didn't box from probably November thru till Spring
because of the cruddy weather but I don't think winter is as harsh here,
just wetter.

Welcome to the Evergray State. If you plan to box around here better
get a good rain jacket. :) Actually, we do most of our letterboxing
in the more rain prone months, Sept-May, mostly due to work schedules
but also due to the weather....we actually like the rain and the
cooler temps. It is surprising just how empty the parks and other
locations can be in the non-summer months. We like having the peace
and quiet of a park to ourselves.

Glad you made it here, looking forward to a venture to Anderson this
fall!
