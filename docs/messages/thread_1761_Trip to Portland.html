From: erfellie (hemenekawan@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-11-15 04:58:51 UTC

So I just got back from a wonderful 3 day trip to downtown Portland,
which I have never been too, just the outskirts. (The traffic scares
me)its just too easy to get lost in such a big city. But Anyways...I
was a bit anxious during my trip because I was with a small group of
friends who wanted to vacation for a few days and go to a concert,
OMSI, night clubs, dinners, all of it was great fun, however I
couldnt help but wonder at every street corner, "Is there a letterbox
here?" and I went in several book stores knowing 2 of them held
letterboxes but alas found none. I was accompanied by wonderful
friends who have absolutely no interest in letterboxing and and for
the life of them cant understand why I am so enthusiastic about it.
Well Since Sandi and I have acquired evry single letterbox in our
area we now must travel farther for the hunt. Well, unfortunately no
one was interested in finding letterboxes and since I knew that was
how it was going to be I didnt even bother with bringing any clues
but it was a fun expereience nonetheless and educational. In that
huge city, I now know I can take exit 306B and find "The Max" and it
will take me to alot of places for only $3.50 (Unless you lose your
ticket like my drunk friend did haha) So... After I finish unpacking
tonight, I might just have to plan another trip to portland. Oh, and
anyone that hasnt seen in Omnimax movie at OMSI, DO IT!
