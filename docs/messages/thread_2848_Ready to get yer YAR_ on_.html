Got nothing going on this weekend (Sept. 22-23)? Come on down to
Cathedral Park (under the St. Johns Bridge) and revel in the merriment

lookout for me, Trekkiemom and my bud Cap'n Ira Pate. We'll all be
sportin' our kilts and will have pirate-themed travelers to mark the
occasion!

Yar!
