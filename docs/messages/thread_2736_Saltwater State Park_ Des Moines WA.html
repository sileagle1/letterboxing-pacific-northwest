From: cheflisbeth (cheflisbeth@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-02-12 17:20:35 UTC
Hey Ho....
Looks as if SWSP sustained major damage from this winter's storms.
The upper and most of the lower parking lot is open, as is the beach
and the trail above from the upper lot.
However, the majority of the park is completly closed includeing the
trails, picnic shelters on the lower level and the campground.
The bridge that goes over the ravine (Marine View Drive) is blocked
off to all car and foot traffic. The ranger said that the wind
snapped support cables and the water damage eroded the earth under the
south side of the bridge.
This means the larger portion of the boxes are blocked to
access...BUT...SHH's "Flowers For Mom" is perfectly accessable,
provides stunning views of the Puget Sound and is an excellent carve!
Happy Hunting!
