From: minibullymom (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-22 04:12:50 UTC
WooHoo...Got to meet up with SleepyWhippet and Bookworm at the Dog
Show on Monday. Exchanged stamps and I gave them a couple letterboxes
to place for me and a Hitchhiker to launch somewhere down there for
me.

Found "Smith and Bybee" in great shape and after a little logistical
research found the Equus Mystery Box. It does require some
forethought and research but it is actually easy to find/get once you
got that all done!! I was in and out in about 5 minutes flat!!

I forgot the clues to all the letterboxes I wanted to hunt down but
the dog show took up more time than anticpated after all. In doing
all the running around for dog club related activities I have become
familiar with where some of the other local boxes are: Reed College,
Oregon City, Escatada and of course all those out on the Sunset
Highway!

I can't wait til summer's longer days are here! My letterboxing buddy
and our collective kids plan on making a day trip or two down there
for a day's worth of boxing!! WooHoo!!

Thanks for taking the time to meet up with me Kris and Rinda!

Til later,
