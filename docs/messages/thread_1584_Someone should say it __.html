From: Twilight (twilight2001@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-09-06 22:34:50 UTC-07:00
Don't post often -- not much to say... But I wanted to tell you all --
that live or online, you're a great bunch of folks. For all the
occasional blow up we have, this is a group of people who, in general,
support letterboxing in all it's flavors -- from occasional boxer to
avid 5000 count and still counting -- and I LOVE that about this
group...

I just ran into a relatively new boxer who got told he's not welcome on
a list of what I can only assume are very self important jerks who told
him he's not "serious" enough as his PFX count isn't high enough for the
length of time he's been involved. Leaves a bad taste in one's mouth.

I just thought someone ought to tell you (from one who's count is stupid
low for my time in the hobby, but life gets in the way sometimes) that
you're a really great bunch of folks.

Thanks :>

Twilight
