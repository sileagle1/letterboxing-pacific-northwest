This was one FUN event. I love it that Mystery Gatherings have made it
from the East Coast to our neck of the woods. Kudos to Sam Hill for
putting this together. I especially liked the way folks who made their
way there took it upon themselves to hide letterboxes, filling the
area with goodies to hunt, and covering the table with clues. No one
was asked to do this. It just happened...That there were visitors from
as far afield as Texas and Arizona added to the festivities.

I am wondering how many of the boxes that were there are permanent
placements? I didn't have time to find all of them and have my fingers
crossed that some will have been left behind. I go that way on one of
my yearly business trips and would love to stop for a letterboxing
jaunt. Thanks again, Seior Hill for a good time. Funhog
