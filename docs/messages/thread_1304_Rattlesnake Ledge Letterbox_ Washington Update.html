Rattlesnake Ledge Letterbox, Washington Update

From: Danny McMillin (macdanny@mindspring.com) | Date: 2004-04-26 18:35:49 UTC-07:00
Scout the Barn Cat and I went in search of the first known letterbox
planted in the state of Washington. That would be Rattlesnake Ledge
Letterbox.

What a beautiful hike to a wonderful overlook of the Snoqualmie River
Valley. The letterbox was in fine shape. We replaced the zippy bags.
However, the stamp is broken into two pieces. A small chunck is getting
ready to fall off from the center (crumbling). So if you haven't visited
this box, and plan to, you may want to head out soon.

What a great day to get into the woods. The smells were incredible. The
Pacific Northwest Forest Spice smell. I love it.

See you on the trail.
