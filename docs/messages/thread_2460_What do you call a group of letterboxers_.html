Oh ho, funny question. Out of those you've offered, I like "herd" the
best, just because I like the mental image that pops up. I'm offering
"pod" because slugs are gastropods, and I like to go boxing with just
a few friends rather than a whole bunch of people - pod sounds smaller
somehow than herd or swarm. 'Course it won't be an appropriate term
for those who live in areas where they aren't blessed with the
presence of the slimy little critters wherever they go.
