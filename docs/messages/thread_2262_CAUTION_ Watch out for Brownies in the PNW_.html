CAUTION! Watch out for Brownies in the PNW!

From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-30 02:18:00 UTC
I cross-posted this on the Hitchhiker Board, but it is specific to
the PNW.

Just a note of warning, I have found a couple of Hitchhikers in the
past few days in letterboxes where the hitchhiker log was put in the
ziplock with the letterbox log and the hitchhiker stamp was put in a
ziplock with the letterbox stamp. Luckily, the letterbox and
hitchhiker were nothing alike (and had been stamped into their
original logs) and I could figure out who went where.

The last folks to stamp into both logs were a Brownie Troop. Troop
927.

The One Hitchhiker even had it's own Ziplock with Hitchhiker written
all over it (at least this was still in the letterbox)

Now to give the Brownies credit, all 12 of them signed into ONE PAGE!
Hurrah!!!

But the logs and stamps could very easily get confused. So if you are
a letterboxer and find this situation, please take extra care to make
sure that the correct Log and Stamp stay with the letterbox and the
correct Log and Stamp that are a Hitchhiker leave with you. This is
especially trying when you have a Honey Bee HH hidden in a Honey Bee
Letterbox! :-)

-Amanda from Seattle
