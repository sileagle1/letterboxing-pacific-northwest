OR box update - Fanno Creek - out to dry - 3 others a-ok

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-26 10:43:30 UTC-07:00
We hunted Fanno Creek in March and could not get to it due to high water, we finally went back for it last evening, - it was there and it was muddy and soaked. It looked like Wild Woman and Wild Child signed in 4-5 times her stamp ink went thru several pages. Not sure we can help the log. We will put out a replacement as we are able.

Burntwood Park, Cooper Park, and Hiteon Park boxes are all alive and well. Really love the 6 trees! Isn't nature wonderful !!!!! Great placement, but there were kids catching tadpoles so it was tricky getting the box back to it's hidey hole.

Of course we had Molly with us so it was abit tricky for her to log in at Burntwood. I love the log and stamp by A. Rose, great little log and very nice stamp!
