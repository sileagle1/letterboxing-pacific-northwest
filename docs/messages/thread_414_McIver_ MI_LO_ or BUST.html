McIver: MI(LO) or BUST

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-07-11 20:15:32 UTC


This letterbox was originally created for the Upper Great Lakes
Gathering in Michigan during the spring of 2003. After its short stay
there, it made its way back to Oregon and was set free here during the
Rubber Stamp Campout 2003.

Park at the Milo McIver Viewpoint parking lot. Enjoy the view and
greet Milo. While learning of his good works you will notice several
dates. Take note of ALL the dates on the plaque, large and small. Add
them together. From the resulting sum, eliminate the first two digits
and add the last two. Later, you will need the resulting number to
count your steps to the letterbox.

Facing Milo's visage, follow the rock wall to the left until it ends
and then turn right onto a dirt trail. Follow this path as it skirts
the meadow and borders the woods. When you meet another dirt trail,
turn left and head downhill. At signs of equus keep to the right.
When you meet a stump standing beside the trail, walk twice the number
of steps you determined at the beginning. Around the bend you will
meet Holly standing before a nursemaid with a ruddy complexion. The
letterbox hides behind the larger of the two.
