I've been letterboxing for about 6 months now and finally took the
plunge to place 2 boxes in my favorite wetlands in FW, Hylebos
Wetlands State Park. I grew up in FW and have seen this place evolve
over the years from an untended private access wetlands to now a
state park (complete with 5$ fee...:o!)

Anyhoo, drug my hubby out there on a date, and placed my first 2
boxes-Deep Sink and Weasel in the park. The clues are posted on the
LBNA site. My apologies for the stamps-my first ever carvings (our
personal one is store-bought!).
