Fate of Childhood Series, Adventure #1

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2004-07-06 23:34:00 UTC
It's been about a year since my hubby & I have been lb'ing -- we got
busy.

In fact, at the age of 43, I graduated from Portland State University
with an undergrad degree in June! (43--Finally!)

In fact, (off the subject), I did an altered book reflecting on how 4
women in my family affected me for a "Women, Creativity & Healing"
class. If you want to view the book, go to www.kuku.org.

Anyway, back to lb'ing! My hubby & I went and had lunch at the zoo
cafe, then went off & found the four boxes in Fate of Childhood
Series, Adventure #1.

We had so much fun! Michael, my husband, has hiked through and
around there a lot, but we ended up on a couple of trails he had
never discovered, which is one of the joys of letterboxing!

I saw in the logs Smiley, Camp Fire Woman, Sunshine, Charlie Trail
Duster & others I can't remember now!

Thanks for so much fun, Pandora. I can't wait to start another
adventure!

Susan Johnson susan@kuku.org

Just remember that your body is your temple.
But it's also your dance hall and your bowling alley.

---Dharma, Dharma & Greg
