From: 4Dtoes (jfleshman@bendcable.com) | Date: 2006-10-30 15:24:44 UTC
Hi All,

Went out and checked on several of our boxes yesterday, in preparation for winter and
found several boxes exposed and some not even closed properly. Just a reminder
especially for you newbies, ( the rest of you have heard it before), please treat others
boxes with respect and replace the box better than you found it. If you don't the boxes
will disappear and our little hobby will also. Remember to hide them so they can NOT be
seen by passer-by's. I checked 4 boxes yeserday and 3 of them could be seen by just
walking by. Just my 2 cents worth.

Thanks,

John of the 4Dtoes
