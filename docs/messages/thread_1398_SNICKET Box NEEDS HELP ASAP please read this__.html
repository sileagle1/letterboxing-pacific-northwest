SNICKET Box NEEDS HELP ASAP please read this!!

From: pandora Taleon (hispandora@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-06-21 18:33:44 UTC
Iron`Bear and I went to retrieve the Snicket box today, and ran into
a problem. We were stamping in early this morning over by the
concrete shelter that the box is hidden at when someone happened
upon us. She said hello, we said hello, and didn't worry much about
it. We waited for them to leave and then re-hid the box and went our
merry little way.

There has recently be another box hidden in the same area so we
went to nab that box as well and we were stamping into that box when
she happened about us again. Only this time we were a bit more
concerned, so we stuff the box into our bag quickly, and decided to
chit chat with her a bit. She informed us that we were not supposed
to be at this location, that it was coast guard property. We were a
little concerned also with her actions; there were not very eco-
friendly as she was trampling down anything in her way at this
point. She also told us that she worked for Cape Disappointment at
the Interpretive Center. But she left again and headed back down the
overgrown road we took to get up there. So Iron`Bear went to hide
the 2nd letterbox again and suddenly out of no where she appeared
again, right behind him. We are uncertain or not if she actually
saw him hiding the box, but for sure she knew we were up to
something back there. We are worried that since the clues to
Maiden's box Correspond to the Clues to the Snicket Box, that they
both may have ended up compromised. Both boxes were re-hiding well,
and hind sight 20/20 and after conferring with Ryan Carpenter I wish
I had pulled the boxes to maintain their safety and returned them in
a month after the dust settled, but I was worried about pulling
someone else's letterbox and upsetting them. I didn't really know
what to do though, it was the first time I have ever been in a
situation like this, were I felt that boxes were compromised. We
did everything we could and waited a long time for her to go away,
but damn that woman just kept showing back up.

I feel terrible but I can't get back out there to pull the boxes
which I think is the best thing to do at this point. I have written
Mr. Snicket and await hearing back from him, but if anyone is close
enough to this area that they can go pull these boxes TODAY ASAP
Time is of the essence before perhaps the Park finds them, then
please go do so and contact me to let me know.

My most sincere apologies to anyone that might miss this box due to
my unfortunate accident,
pandora{I`B}
