Flat water canoes would not be good.This section of the Rogue has 1
class 4 rapid at Blossom Bar,a portage Rainey Falls at and a
multitude of class 3 rapids. There are some rental places around
Grants Pass where self bailing inflatable kayaks can be found along
with PFDs, drybags and other supplies. Before you consider renting
or figuring out what to get down the river in, you should try to
figure out a put on date, and a take out date. Those that are
interested (no more than 10 folks) should apply for a permit. If 10
folks send in for a permit then you have 10 shots at the lottery
process. Now you have to figure in at least two support boats(gear
boats)and that will take up 4 of the 10 spots. I believe the web
site for the permit processing is www.umpcoos.com/Rogue. The phone
number is either 541 479 3735, or 672 4168.
