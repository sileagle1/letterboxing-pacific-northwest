From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2003-08-08 21:44:38 UTC-04:00
Well, we were planning to try for Doyle Owl--again!--but Rocky Butte lured us
in instead. It's embarrassing to admit that neither Heather or I had ever
been there before. We said very nice things about Funhog and her choices of
venues as we climbed the steps (to make up for the bad things we said while
following the wrong rings of time in pursuit of Doyle Owl! ;o). In fact, Wild
Child was so thrilled with what she called "a castle," she suggested she'd like to
have the Hawg for a mother (so much for my years of diaper changes, sickbed
ministrations & help with homework)! And the kids also loved the entrance to
the trail--those steps closed in by stone & vegetation that could lead into any
adventure! I'm happy to report that the box is still alive & well & now
bolstered with our signature stamps. It's getting toward the end of the logbook,
tho there are still some pages for more stamps.

After stamping in, we returned to the "castle" to watch the sun set--the kids
shouted and turned cartwheels, while young lovers smooched near various
lamposts (cynical Heather & I muttering under our breath that the smoochers should
check out our group--if they keep at it, WE ARE THEIR FUTURE!). All in all,
a lovely evening--fun clues to a fun place--thanks to a Funhog for the trip!

--Wild Woman
