From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-01-21 01:00:26 UTC
Today, I was in Renton and visited the "Ouch" letterbox and
the "Don't Feed the Ducks" letterbox. Both are doing fine and are in
need of more visitors!

The Don't Feed the Ducks location is a lovely lakeside walk and I
highly recommend it, plus the stamp is super cute.

The Ouch letterbox is one of the many of the Outlaw Tracey Letterbox
Series and is worth a find just to walk over the old trestle bridge!

I also did maintenance on the Kubota Gardens Letterbox today, so it
has a new, dry box.
