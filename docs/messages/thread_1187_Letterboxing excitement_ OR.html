Wow! I've finally made a stamp exchange on the trail... while traipsing around
Whitaker Ponds, marveling at the number of Brown Creepers cruising up the
cottonwoods, I spied a fellow with tell-tale sheets of white paper in his hands and a
knapsack on his back. Shy thing that I am, I naturally asked him if he was looking at
clues. He nodded and I identified myself. I had made the acquaintance of Tony and
his son Edan! While Edan watched the avian show with my binocular, Papa and I
swapped signature stamps.... a red letter day for the Funhog for sure.
