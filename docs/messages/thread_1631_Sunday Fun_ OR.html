From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-09-21 00:37:00 UTC
Phew! I finally got the opportunity to get to the Family
Forest Series in Oregon City. I've only had it on my
must do list for a year now. What a wonderful place!
It's far from any major roads, very quiet and there were
absolutely no other people on the trails. There were
mushroom gardens, newts and bunnies but nothing
that makes much noise other than a busy little creek.

The next stop wasn't quite so idyllic but the beautiful
stamps made up for any lack of solitude. These were
Pandora's lovely, whimsical Peter Pan images in West
Linn. In spite of encountering a sizeable quantity of
fairy dust (squishy mud) there wasn't much rain until
the final lap of our journey.

In our travels, Nutria, Oregon Trail, It's a Bird-It's a
Plane and I'd Lasso it for you all saw the light of day.
Thanks to all who contributed to a grand day of
letterboxing. Merci Beaucoup, Funhog
