Re: carving help?

From: CampFireLady (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2010-06-10 13:51:55 UTC
Hello Jessi!

I would say the best thing to do is to practice, practice, practice. There are also many tools you can find that will help you to get the details that you want out of your siggy stamp such as carving material, carving tools, using a magnifying lamp, etc.

Are you signed up for this event?:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1560

There will be a workshop on carving that may be helpful. You will have the chance to use some of the latest carving tools and equipment that many of letterboxers are using now to get the detail in their stamps. All of the workshops will be help on Saturday so if you come for the day, you will be able to attend them.

Also, you can check out my profile page at AtlasQuest.com. I am now selling the tools and carving material if you are interested.

Camp Fire Lady
