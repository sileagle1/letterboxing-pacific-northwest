It's funny you mentioned that, because we did have a bit of trouble
finding ONE of those boxes. =) (We didn't find the others at all.)
We knew that at least one of the clues in the book weren't very good,
but none of us could remember which one it was. So we tried all the
clues and finally figured, after much debate on the proper direction
to head, that must have been the problem clue. We wandered around for
quite awhile anyhow--I was plenty ready just to give up at that
point--and Jennifer says something like, "Well, this box is impossible
to find." And right on clue, Silent Doug, scrounging around ahead,
pipes up and says, "I found it!"

We were shocked. After getting our bearing with that one box, we
continued on to the next box and thought we were doing pretty well
until we started beating bushes and trees and nothing fell out. By
then it was starting to get dark and we really did call it quits.

Overall, though, lots of fun. =)
