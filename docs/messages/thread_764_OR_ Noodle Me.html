with the kids. We both love Oriental food and were anxious to find
this box. Followed the clues to the Parent Tree, but there was no
obvious greenery to look under. We were carefully looking around
when I stumbled upon a ground wasp nest. As I backed away from that
area Wild Woman exclaimed, "Wait a minute! That is Poison Oak!!!"

surrounded by the devils. Luckily 3 of the children were running
around on the paved path and did not follow us into the trees. My
son Josh froze and carefully followd instructions to get out of the
plants safely with us. Luckily we did not just start foraging
through the underbrush is search of the box. We believe the box to
be missing. Could the owner of the box verify very carefully if it
is gone and then delete the clues so no other innocent fools like me
fall into the poison oak trap.
