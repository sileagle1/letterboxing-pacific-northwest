Hi all!

I've been lurking for a few months now and started letterboxing a few
months ago. I still haven't carved my stamp and that's all that is
keeping me from going hogwild. I have hunted around and found a few
boxes and I even placed my own box last month.

I finally got around to writing up the clues and posting it on the
site today. My first box! How exciting! I have to say that the stamp
isn't the most attractive but hey! It's my first stamp. I know it'll
get better with time. And I carved it at 11 o'clock at night by
headlamp so we could place it and leave the island before the wind
increased. Yes, you have to travel by boat to get to this box. I'm
hoping that it will be the first of the Cascadia Marine Trail Series.
For now, it's on it's own.

Enjoy!
