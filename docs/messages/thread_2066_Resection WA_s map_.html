Resection WA's map?

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-27 02:34:47 UTC
Hey fellow Washingtonian letterboxers,

Would there be any interest out there in 'resectioning' WA's map on
the letterboxing.org (LBNA) page? I've currently been working on
amending the Puget Sound map bit to more accurately reflect where
Seattle, Tacoma and Olympia are located and to clean up the
region 'borders' as they don't properly line up at the moment.

What I've noticed in the process is that there are a couple hundred
boxes in Region 2 (WA's LB megalopolis--Whatcom, Skagit, Snohomish,
Island, King & Pierce Counties) known as "North West/Puget Sound
Letterboxes" and Region 5 (Vancouver area) and then just a couple
dozen or a handful scattered about the other Regions throughout the
state.

Would anybody object to separating out at least Pierce & King
Counties from Region 2--perhaps call it (Puget Sound Region) and
then merge Snohomish, Island, Skagit & Whatcom Counties into a new
Region 8 (Northwest WA)?

The rationale to pull these two counties out of the fold would be to
reduce the size of the current list of boxes when you upload Region
2.

Another idea would be to list Pierce and King Counties as two
individual distinct regions (Puget Sound South & Puget Sound North).

I wanted to throw the ideas out there to see if there was any
interest in it. I'd be willing to work on it over the next couple
of months. I may even create a poll to see where folks want to go
with it.

Please chime in. I foresee that pulling out these two 'LB populous'
regions may affect some of the mystery boxes as they're currently
listed--so it might cause a little 'maintenance' effort for a few
folks who have mystery boxes out there. Hopefully, though the
effort will make Region 2 a bit more manageable to deal with--
especially for folks just 'discovering' letterboxing these days.

dvn2r ckr

Re: Resection WA's map?

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-05-27 17:10:35 UTC
Here's the deal: we've adjusted the map to accurately reflect Puget
Sound and we've added a couple more 'major cities' on the map. It
looks much better now. Although we won't upload it to the website
until it's completely finished. We've added Olympia, Aberdeen and
Raymond as towns--modified City of the Dalles (OR) to The Dalles.

*************************

My idea with the regions, at the moment, is this:

I'd leave 1, 3-7 alone (unless there are some major problems folks
more familiar with those areas would like to see resolved)--then
split Region 2--into:

King County (maybe Region 2a?--Puget Sound North)
Pierce County (maybe Region 2b?--Puget Sound South?)

and then merge Snohomish, Island, Skagit, Whatcom counties into
their own new Region 8 (Northwest WA)

The dilemma then would be what to do with Kitsap County? We could
merge it into the Olympic Peninsula Region--and actually give that
region some 'activity' since most of the area is LB prohibited (NPS
lands).

Or, merge Kitsap in with Pierce as you can reach it by land via
Tacoma Narrows? The mental 'leap' of having to cross the Sound to
reach it may make it naturally fit with the Olympic Peninsula
region. Let me know your thoughts on this.

The deal is that the boxes are listed by regions based on their
counties--so that would have to be how the regions are delineated.
It's funny--Pierce County continues across the Sound into parts
adjacent to Kitsap so there will always be a tad bit of confusion
for the Gig Harbor area boxes, as well.

Thanks,

dvn2r ckr

I'll post a picture of the map as it stands right now to the photo or
file section.

***********************************

This is where we're leaning on revamping the WA map regions:

** Create new individual regions out of these counties (as they have
the potential to 'explode' exponentially at any moment):

Thurston
Pierce
King

** Merge the remaining NW WA counties (Snohomish, Island, San Juan,
Skagit and Whatcom) into their own region

** Merge Kitsap County with the Olympic Peninsula region.




Questions for discussion:

Should Snohomish merge with King County, individually or with the other
NW WA counties? Snohomish doesn't currently have many boxes but it
does have the landmass to potentially support a plethora of them.

Does anybody from the Vancouver/PDX area see a need to separate Clark
County (since it too has gobs of boxes) into its own individual region?

Should we break the counties out individually--Thurston, Pierce and
King? Thurston's not so big--Pierce is big but half the land is non-
boxable due to NPS lands, glaciers or military installations--so
merging the two is fine with me. King's large and quite populated and
much of it is potentially 'boxable'. Snohomish is large but not as
populated as King. I don't mind merging King & Snohomish or just
keeping Snohomish with the other NW counties.
