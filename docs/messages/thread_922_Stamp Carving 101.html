There have been "tutoring sessions" at events and even if there's not
a designated one, it's generally pretty easy to find someone at an
event that will give you some pointers.

I have a webpage for an online tutorial on carving at
http://www.geocities.com/rscarpen/Lb/Docs/Carving that you might find
useful. There's also links from it to other websites on how to carve
stamps as well that are very useful.

But I'd also like to mention, there's not necessarily one "right" way
to carve a stamp. What works for one person doesn't always work for
another. A little experience, practice and developing your own style
will go a long way into carving your own fantastic stamps. =)

Carpe diem!
