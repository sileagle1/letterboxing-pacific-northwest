Fallen Feather

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-09-04 02:50:49 UTC
Having learned that this letterbox had gone missing I carved a
replacement and headed out this afternoon to put it in its home. I was
horrified to see the damage done to the area where the letterbox was
hidden. My hope is that it was not the work of frustrated letterboxers
but that of some pinhead miscreants. With great trepidation, I put the
box in its cubbyhole, cleaned up the area and scattered needles and
leaves to try to make the area look natural once again. Hopefully,
future visitors will do their best to tread lightly, erase their
tracks and avoid further disruption to the area. Funhog
