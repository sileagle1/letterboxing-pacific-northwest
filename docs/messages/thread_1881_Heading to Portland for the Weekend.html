From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2005-01-26 17:50:40 UTC

> Does anyone have suggestions for any letterboxes near there? We will
> be on foot and using the local public transportation.

If you want to do do some hiking (easy hiking, but still hiking!), I'd
recommend looking for boxes in Washington Park and hiking out as far
as Pittock Mansion. There are several boxes you can find along the
way, though it's been quite a while since I've boxed in that area and
can't really give you a full list of all the boxes you'd pass. Others
might be able to do so, however. Take the MAX to the zoo stop. (In
fact, there's even at least one letterbox that, last I heard, is still
at the zoo!)

For even easier hiking, there are some boxes at the Tualitin Hills
Nature Park, though some of the trails often seem to be closed down in
the winter. I'm not sure if that's the case at the moment. The stop
at Elmonica (I *think*--I'm running this off at the top of my head and
like I said, it's been awhile since I've been out here so my memory is
rusty) on the MAX will drop you off right at the park.

Happy trails!

-- Ryan
