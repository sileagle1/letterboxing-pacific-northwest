Hello fellow Letterboxers!! I am Erfellie from Grants Pass, Oregon
(Celtic horse stamp). I know as winter comes it can often be too
chilly or snowy to make all those long hikes to find our precious
LB's so I thought we could do a Letterbox gathering which I am
personally inviting you too and any of you LB friends and family.
Unfortunately some of our best trail buddies, the four legged
furries, won't be able to attend since it will be held indoors at
a restaurant. The gathering will be held on Saturday December 4th,
2004 at Wild River Brewing and Pizza Company 595 Northeast "E" Street
Grants Pass, OR 97526 Phone: (541) 471-7487 at 3:00pm. Please
respect that it IS a non-smoking facility. Average meal price (not
included in event) is about $10 and they do offer some vegetarian
food. If you would like to know more about this restaurant their
website is http://wildriverbrewing.com/. Email me if you need
directions, its very easy to find from I-5. I do ask that you must
RSVP with me so that I can reserve the space at the restaurant;
please do so by November25, 2004. To RSVP please go to
www.letterboxing.org, go to the map of Oregon, Click on Area 6, and
click on "Heart of Grants Pass Letterbox" (or any of my other
LB'S) and use the "Contact Placer" option. The earlier the RSVP the
better, and please include your trail name (and real name if you
like) and email address. I promise it will be a fun winter gathering
you won't want to miss! For far travelers, visit
http://www.grantspass.com/ for information on hotels, maps, or
anything else you might need to make your trip easier. Thank you so
much and I hope to see you there!!! In the meantime, Happy Hunting!
Keep warm!
Erfellie
