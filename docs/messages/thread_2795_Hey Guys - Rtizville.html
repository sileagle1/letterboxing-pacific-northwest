FYI - Wood-aug has gotten several reports that the Ritzville box is missing.
:-( Too bad as it's a nice break spot in the trip. Maybe he'll replace it
this summer??

Sorry we'll miss you as we'll be out of town the day you are in the Portland
area.

As far as drive-bys - Wood-aug has a
drive-by (nothing special location) just out of Kennewick called Horse Heaven
Hills. If you're ready to eat when you get to The Dalles, OR there's a box called
Cousin's there. It's fun place, good for kids they have real milk shakes, and the food is home-cooked
style. We like the Rooster Rock box in the gorge. There's the ever popular Jay's Toolbox (Troutdale, OR). I like the stamp in Monte's Suds in Kennewick (short stroll in a nice park). Sorry that many of these are our boxes . . . it's what comes to mind.

Enjoy your trip!
Kurious Jo & Wood-aug
