From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-04-09 06:35:00 UTC
This board seems like a genuine success, and I'd just like to thank
all of you for making it that way! =) When someone on the main
board said they'd like to see a Pacific Northwest board (I don't even
remember who that was anymore--sorry!), I figured I'd try creating
one myself although I had doubts that it would really take off.

Besides the fact that none of the other letterboxing boards really
took off, I wasn't convinced that enough people from the Pacific
Northwest were around to reach the critical mass necessary to keep
thte board going, but you've proved me wrong! =)

And also, I just love having all your websites so accessible in the
Links section. I don't know of anywhere that has a more complete
listing of websites of Pacific Northwest letterboxers. Even though I
know there is still at least one missing (Bill!), I find it a very
useful resource! And a couple of you that have websites--I didn't
even know about them until you asked for me to post the links.
Thanks to you all!

Now I've had a few people asking me to set up a Letterboxing
California board. *rolling eyes* My work is never done.... =) But
I think that will have to wait until after I return my my epic
adventure and have more time to dedicate to its creation.

So you see--some of those Californians are having regional
letterboxing board envy!!!!! =)

-- You Humble Servent, Ryan
