From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-30 06:04:21 UTC
Hey there,

It's been ages since I was up that way & I haven't kept tabs on new
boxes up there but here's some ideas as I noticed nobody else had yet
responded to your question and you're probably rushed for time before
you head out boxing tomorrow. The boxes probably closest to where
you'll be are either in Mukilteo or the North Seattle. A pretty park
with some intense hills and good views of Puget Sound would be Carkeek
Park--which contains a handful of letterboxes at least. Another short,
intense hike and mid-North Seattle is Ravenna--but I'm not sure if the
clues to that box are still listed. You could also try the Twin Ponds
LB or the Pied Piper LB--in/around Shoreline (I think).

Other boxes relatively close to mid-Seattle--Calkins Pt (Mercer Island)-
-nice walk, beautiful park relatively easy to find from I-5-->I-90.
Several West Seattle boxes might be a good choice, too. I've also got
one in Seattle 'West Point Mule' that's about 5-6 miles--depending on
what you're idea of a short, intense hike is--it's got awesome views
and nice hiking along the way. Traffic will likely be a bear with
Seafair starting up so I recommend a good map recon before you venture
out to see exactly where you need to drive to get to the various
parks. You'd probably prefer to hike/explore over sitting in
traffic. ;)

Enjoy your visit!

dvn2r ckr
