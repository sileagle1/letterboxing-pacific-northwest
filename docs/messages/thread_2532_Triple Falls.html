Triple Falls

From: tamaialden (tamaialden@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-05-29 17:20:19 UTC
Kitty and I hiked to Triple Falls yesterday (a *much* easier trek than last
weeks's Wahkeena Spring). We found the Triple Falls box (part of the
Original Gorge Waterfalls series), and were surprised to see how few people
had stamped in since the log book was replaced a couple of years ago. It's
such a nice stamp (and piece of PNW letterboxing history, being so old), and
the hike really isn't too bad, and it wasn't terribly hard to find We wanted to
encourage anyone who hasn't got it to head up there. The hiding place is the
same as in the clues, but the covering is slightly different (we didn't find it on
first attempt. I had to keep at it for a few minutes.)

We also planted the first of a new series in the same general vicinity, in an
area just crying for a box. We're calling the series Edible Forest, and this box
is devoted to Wood Sorrel.

And rain or not, this is such a glorious time to go hiking in the Gorge. The
wildflowers are in full bloom, and the sights and smells are heavenly. (And
when you've under the heavy forest canopy, very little rain makes it to the
floor!)

-Logos (and Miss Kitty)
