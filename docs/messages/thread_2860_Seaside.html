Wonderful trip to the coast last weekend. We updated the clues for the
Sunset Boulevard box, which -- being in the forest near the ocean --
is volatile in terms of trees blowing down and undergrowth going
crazy. The box is in the same spot -- and found 23 times so far -- but
the clues have been tweaked to reflect changes in the trail.

We planted two new boxes, Twilight of the Sea Gods and The Pines & the
Sea, in two treasured spots near Seaside.

Good luck and take home a pocketful of sand for us!
