New box in Vancouver BC - Pole to Pole

From: dbltall42 (dbltall@speakeasy.net) | Date: 2004-10-13 04:29:41 UTC

We saw that there were no boxes listed for Stanley Park in Vancouver,
and decided to remedy the situation. At the gathering, we were warned
by locals that a box in Stanley Park is likely to be short-lived (too
much traffic) so I hope at least one person gets it before it
disappears.

I won't have time to get it onto the website for a bit, so I'm posting
the clues here for any intrepid early finders.

Pole to Pole

Stanley Park, Vancouver, B.C.

Start at the famous Stanley Park totem poles. Go along the path to
your left and follow it to the street. Look to your right and see 3
poles of another kind, near a lighthouse. Standing in front of these
poles, go straight ahead south. Cross the street carefully and go up
the path straight in front of you, to a mossy rock in front of a
multi-trunked maple tree. Standing in front of the rock, you will see
another maple tree at about 130 degrees. Nestled under the fern
growing at the side of this tree is the pole that is your goal.
