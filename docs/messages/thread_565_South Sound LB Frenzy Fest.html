Next time I provide the burgers, I'll do one better than just frozen patties, they'll be the secret family recipe. As far as the potato salad goes, it's pretty simple really, though most of the time, RayvenMom cooks by instinct rather than recipe...

Peel and boil potatoes to the point that they are still firm and not mushy, remember to add salt to the water so you don't have to add it later to
the salad
Add mayo enough to give it a creamy but not dry texture (some people like the Kraft salad dressing mayo... we prefer the deep south mayo)
Add sweet relish to taste
Add yellow mustard to taste
And this is the kicker.... RayvenMom adds sugar to counter the slight bite that the mustard tends to offer...

Other than that, the potato salad is pretty straight forward to make!
