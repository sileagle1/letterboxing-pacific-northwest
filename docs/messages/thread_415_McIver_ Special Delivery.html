McIver: Special Delivery

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-07-11 20:17:07 UTC


This stamp was created by Brett to commemorate the imminent birth of
his and Penny's second child. Not knowing if Grace's new sibling would
insist on arriving before, during or after the Rubberstamp Campout, he
entrusted me with his stamp and the honor of creating this very
special letterbox.

Head east from camp until you might be inclined to spike and serve.
Standing in the middle between two upright citizens, set your compass
for 125 degrees magnetic and walk 108 steps to a shady character.
Reset that compass for 80 degrees and walk until you reach the open
arms of a verdant friend. There's a place in its heart for this little
bundle of joy.

Congratulations! Funhog
