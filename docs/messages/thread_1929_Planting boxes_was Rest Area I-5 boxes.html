Hmmm.... That's an interesting theory. =) My planted count would
certainly go down if that was the case. None of my boxes in Africa or
Central America would count. But then, they aren't really in my

I could probably count on one hand all the boxes I've planted in the
states that haven't been found yet. Even the most remote,
inaccessible crags where I've planted boxes have had someone brave and
daring enough to go after them. One of them even required a 20+ mile
backpacking trip to reach, and I was absolutely floored when I got a
report that it was found!

So make this a lesson: No matter how inaccessible or crazy your boxes
are, there's always someone out there crazier than you are and will go
out to look for it. =)
