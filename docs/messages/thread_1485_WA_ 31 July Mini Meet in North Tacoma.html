Brown's Point Lighthouse Mini Meet

Hi--for anybody out there who is interested in joining us, a few
letterboxers from the region will get together from 2-4 pm (may last
longer if the kids want to play for a while) on 31 July 04 at
Brown's Point Park (Lighthouse), WAY-WAY North Tacoma (practically
Federal Way at this point) to meet with Spacy Holly and her
letterboxing family from Southern California for exchanges.

We plan on meeting at the flag pole in front of (West) of the
lighthouse keepers cottage. The park offers expansive and
incredible views of the Olympic Mountains, Puget Sound, Vashon &
Maury Islands, and depending on the tides lots of opportunity for
beachcombing and boulder scrambling. There's even
enough 'greenspace' to throw a football or frisbee. We plan on
bringing some picnic items, too.

Let us know if you plan on joining us then.

Perhaps we'll see you there?

The kids really had a blast beachcombing, boulder scrambling,
octopus sprinkler splashing and playing in and around the lighthouse
keeper's quarters. Spacy Holly's relatives were kind enough to
welcome us to their big reunion and even led us on tours of the
beautiful & historic lighthouse keeper's quarters and the adjacent
outbuildings. What a treasure trove of history & local lore that
proved to be! It turned out to be a great opportunity to get to
know other letterboxers--not terribly steeped in finding lots of new
letterboxes but, then again, for a park that's only about 2 acres
big you can only hide so many there according to the Green Turtle's
rules of letterbox concentration anyway! ;) The million dollar
view & the great budding friendships more than made up for the lack
of letterboxes. Oh, we really enjoyed the wedding, too! See new
photo under the photos section.

Thanks for warmly welcoming Spacy Holly back to the region. For
folks who were unable to make the event but would still like to link
up with Holly for an exchange or five [ :) ], she'll be in the
region for a few more weeks. Just coordinate with her directly.

See you back on the letterboxing trails in a few months (it's back
to medical limbo, work & non-letterboxing overseas travel for me for
a while),

dvn2r ckr
