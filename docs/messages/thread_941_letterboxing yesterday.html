letterboxing yesterday

From: Rinda (sleepywhippet@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-11-18 02:12:41 UTC
Bookworm, Princess Claire and myself set off on a very wet trip to
find boxes yesterday. We began our search at Battleground Lake. We
found Der Mad Stampers stamp in great condition. BG Lake #2 stamp we
couldn't find - we seemed to be in the right spot - found the rock
and subsequent wood pieces - just no box so I am hoping we were in
the wrong place rather than the box was gone. From there we went
over to catch Camp Fire Lady's Happy Postman's Series. Wow alot of
thought and time went into each of these stamps and boxes. Thank-you
Camp Fire Lady for the terrific hunt. Although I do have to mention
that when I went to put the last box back that I smashed my finger
between the log and the rock as I was yeowlling (as only a whippet
can!) Bookworm proceeded to show me the scar on her shin from a
protruding stick further down on the log that nabbed her when she
first looked up the series a few weeks back. So just goes to show
what can happen when you are more excited about the stamp hiding
process than your own body parts! All in all we had a great day and
can't wait to go again!
Cheers,
Sleepy Whippet
(still nursing a very bruised digit)
