Hi Folks,

I'm running a Letterboxing Haiku Contest on the LBMA group and I need some judges.

I had originally planned for some Noxers to judge the haiku, but the ones I have chosen
don't appreciate the subtle beauties of walking in the woods looking for Tupperware. So I
need some disinterested parties to pick our winners.

After looking all over the other regional groups on Yahoo, only LbPNW seems to have
members with the joie de vivre of the LBMA group. So with your group owner's
permission, I'd like to invite members of LbPNW to participate as judges in the contest.

Deadline for entries is Thanksgiving. So, if you are interested in judging the contest, you
can view the entries at:

http://www.members.aol.com/nattybumppolbna/haiku.html

Please select your favorite 3 and send your vote in an email by Sunday, December 5th to:

nattybumppolbna@yahoo.com

Only 1 vote per person, please.

Each haiku (or poem or limerick in some cases) has a Roman numeral. Please use that to
indicate your selections. All poetry posted on the web site is eligible despite the
humorous (?) "Judges comments."

Your help would be greatly appreciated.
