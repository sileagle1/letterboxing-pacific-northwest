Update: Doyle Owl and Crystal Springs

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-04-14 00:40:29 UTC

I just walked through the door, returning
from checking on these two oldies. They
are doing just fine and awaiting your visit!
Granted, the gardeners made the Crystal Springs
box WAY more difficult to find by covering up
its loose stone camoflage but I've unearthed
it. On the other hand, the sneaky owl was even
a wee bit exposed. I could see the box even
before I removed any covering material.

The rhododendron gardens are absolutely stunning
right now, well worth a visit. This place has
got to be the only place I've been where you can
get within five feet of a redwing blackbird or a
wood duck and the canopies of flowers defy
description. Funhog
