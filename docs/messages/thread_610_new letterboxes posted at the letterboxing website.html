RE: [LbPNW] new letterboxes posted at the letterboxing website?

From: Wizbang (wizbang@softhome.net) | Date: 2003-08-18 15:46:18 UTC-07:00
I post the clues on a separate website, but enter the box in the
www.letterboxing.org catalogue so that they can be easily found.

My reasoning for posting the clue separately is related mostly to
formatting-I want to be able to mention details that aren't technically a
clue, like "this box is good for youngsters," or a bit of history on the
box. The main index doesn't allow for this, and while I could just stick
them in with the clue I'm too nitpicky to do so.

Wizbang
