What a delightful little series, we have been playing in this park
for about 3 years now, I have always wondered about placing boxes
there and always worried about them getting found or vandalized....
It's a very busy place. Never seemed to find a good hiding place
there, well, obviously I lacked in immagination, cause these are
really hidden quite well, and very fun!

We had almost as much fun sitting around waiting to replace them
talking to strangers passing by, as we did finding them.... Funny, we
brought our new digital camera, took tons of photos, a nutria was
right up on the grass and not bothered by us at all. I got 3 feet
away and took some awesome detailed photos of it, well, at least I
thought I did until I turned the camera over to replay and discovered
the whole time there had not been a CF card in it! (it was still at
home in the card reader on our PC) Argh! How embarrasing! Ak, having
to learn a new camera all over again! Sheesh, some photographer I
AM! (really embarrased now)

Anyway we had a very nice walk, on a fall day! Looks like "A-Rock"
beat us to em just a couple of days before! Thanks mudstumpers!
