Don't feel too bad. Letters are by far the most difficult thing to
carve on a stamp! The problem with letters being that everyone knows
what a letter is supposed to look like, so any mistake--no matter how
small!--will be obvious. And they can be quite complicated as well,
making mistakes that much easier to do.

Letters are always the toughest and most time-consuming thing to carve
on a stamp! =) (Might be why you don't see many stamp out there with
letters on them, in fact. I know I got tired of carving them!)
