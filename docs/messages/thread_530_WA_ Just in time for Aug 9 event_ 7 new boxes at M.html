WA: Just in time for Aug 9 event, 7 new boxes at Millersylvania State Park

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-08-06 19:10:29 UTC
As promised, the remaining 7 boxes in the Chuck's Tacklebox Series
has been placed at Millersylvania State Park just south of Tumwater,
Wa. Clues have been posted to the new letterboxing.org list at this
address http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?
boxnum=5107&boxname=Chuck's_Tacklebox_Series_(6-12)

I will be bringing copies of the clues to the gathering on Saturday
at Tumwater Historical Park. I wont arrive until around noon so I
thought it best to post the clues beforehand to allow visitors
traveling from the south a chance to pick them up on the way to the
gathering.

Have fun,

Nailhead
