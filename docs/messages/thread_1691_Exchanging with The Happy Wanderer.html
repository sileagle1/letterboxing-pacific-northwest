Exchanging with The Happy Wanderer

From: Suzanne (quillow@inkandfeather.com) | Date: 2004-10-12 19:12:21 UTC

Recently a gentleman named Andy Wilkes aka The Happy Wanderer posted
on the forums about exchanging signature stamps. He's located in
Bristol, England. I emailed him back as apparently a TON of people
did and today got an envelope with 15 images in it all in a nice
little plastic envelope to protect them and a sheet with each
image's name, box number if it's registered, owner's name and
location - all in England and some in Dartmoor. This was very
cool!!!

If you didn't sign up with him you might want to - this is defintely
a special occasion.
