We Live and Breathe Letterboxing - PNW Hospitality!!

From: Der Mad Stamper (letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 2008-01-21 18:39:09 UTC
As you know by now, the Pacific Northwest is going to be hosting the
big celebration for the 10th anniversary of American letterboxing. It
will likely be one of the largest letterboxing events of the
decade... not to mention one of the most fun!

This is a great chance for us to show off our special brand of
Hospitality! There are going to be lots of out-of-towners who want to
come a few days early or stay a few days after the event and check
out some of the beautiful sites to see in the Northwest. But we all
know how expensive hotel rooms can be!

If any of you have a spare bed or couch that you'd be willing to
consider letting someone crash on for a few days, there is a special
Yahoo! group where you can post such an invitation and work out the
details.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/NeedaBedGottaBed

I know the event isn't until September, but registration begins in
February! Lots of people are trying to make decisions right now about
whether or not they can afford to attend this event. So, please, open
up your heart and home... make a new friend or two, and help out the
letterboxing community at the same time! Let's show everyone why the
PNW is such a great place for a celebration of this magnitude!

Thanks!
Mitch, Der Mad Stamper
