Hey look at what the cat dragged in!?

Wow I really let letterboxingmischief.com fall behind. It all
started with house buying/selling last spring and then a surgery

Well spring has sprung, and I have myself a little letterboxing list
of To Do's.

Now that the site is up to date, I'm going to start checking on my
letterboxes next, for upkeep/repair.

Redmond Elementals
Down for repairs (2 missing stamps). Expect it back by Memorial Day

Snoqualmie Fall Series
I believe #3 is missing (again) and I've already carved a
replacement stamp. I'm also planning on adding a bonus stamp. Stay
tuned.

Langley
Got a replacement stamp ready. It's just a little bit of a hassle
to get out there. I expect this will be the *last time* I replace
this box. Stay tuned.

I'll be checking on all my others too this spring. Then I can get
to the business of planting *new* boxes. Woo! =D

Oh ya and I get to find new boxes too!

Wheeee
