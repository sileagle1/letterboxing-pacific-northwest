WA: Thurston and Lewis County Easter Tour

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-04-21 03:25:39 UTC
The 7 members of the Nail Family, including the elusive Corn
Doggie Dog took a tour of boxes in South Thurston County and
North Lewis County today. We successfully found Eagle Eye at
Tenino Park ( a great hike to this one); Lost Locomotive at
Shaeffer Park in Centralia; Find Letterbox at Fort Borst Park,
Centralia; Jacksons Cabin near Mary's Corner; and the Outlaw
Tracy Catch Me Series box- The Duel in Napavine. All boxes in
great shape and ready for more visitors. Experienced a "Fare"
amount of "Sunshine" as "Well" (HH, you may have to put those
in the correct order). It was still shining when we left one of the
boxes. Added several new stamps to our books and finally
broke 100 (adding F's, HH's, and E's)!! Had planned to see the
Rainbow and meet Kenny at the Dell but those will have to wait
for another day.

All in all it was a great way to spend an Easter afternoon with the
family. ---- Letterboxing, what a great family thing.
