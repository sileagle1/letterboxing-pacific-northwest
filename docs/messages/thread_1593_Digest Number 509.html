Good afternoon

Team Chaos and Team Rayvenhaus have begun planning the second annual FF2:
Booooo-gie in the Park - Echoes of Times Past Gathering for Oct 23rd 2004,
which is the Saturday before Halloween, from 10:00am till 4:00 pm (or later,
the park doesn't close till 1/2 hour after sunset), at Millersylvania State
Park, just ten miles south of Olympia and 3 miles from the I5 freeway. There
will be at least 2 series newly planted in the park, along with the existing
boxes that are already there.

What we would like to also offer is for anyone who can not make it to this
gathering but would still like to participate in some way, to go ahead and
carve up a themed stamp, make a box and ship it to us and we would be happy
to plant and maintain it, listing you as the creator of that box. If you'd
like to take us up on this, contact RayvenMom at rayvenmom 'at' lbworld.org
and she'll give you the snail mail address to send it to.

We invite everyone that can make it to come and join us, meet some of the
people behind the posts. There will be a potluck so please bring a covered
dish, a salad, something to eat, enough for your group and share with your
friends. Bring your own sodas or other assorted drinks, (minus alcohol of
course, this is a dry park). Although this is a Halloween themed gathering,
this is not a costume party of any sort.

Looking forward to seeing everyone there!
