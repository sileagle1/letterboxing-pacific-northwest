From: erfellie (hemenekawan@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-03-15 17:15:15 UTC

So yesterday I set out to collect several of my LB's close to home
for some maintanence, They will be re-hidden today in very nice
looking camoflauged rubber-maid containers, I dont know why I didnt
think of the camo sooner considering how often I find LBs hiding
spots and its like "Oh gee there it is, out in the open, bright blue
lid, Im suprised no one has taken it yet" because I am sure in the
wind and rain and such our leaves and bark and debris we put on top
of the boxes gets removed, but anyways, that wasnt my point haha I
grabbed my "endangered wildlife" LB, which was the first I ever
planted, and now that I look at it after 2 years of letterboxing,
gee.. I am kind of embarassed to put it back out there in the wild.
Its just sooo..uumm...beginner level crappy stamp haha and the log
book is atrocious. I guess as I have learned to carve better and do
better logbooks, this letterbox just looks ridiculously kindergarten
style to me now haha :o) but dont let that stop all you treasure
seekers out there from locating it :o) just dont think "Erfellie must
be really lame" when you find it haha :o)
