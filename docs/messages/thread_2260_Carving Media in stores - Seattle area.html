Hi Everyone,

For those of you in the Seattle area, one of my favorite places for
boxing supplies is the UW Bookstore. You can park in their parking
lot (I think it's on 15th Ave and 43rd St?). They will validate
parking - so I think you can get up to an hour free, even if you're
just browsing, and up to 2 hours free if you buy something.

If you go downstairs, they have the "cuts like butter" Staedtler
Mastercarve (my favorite) in lots of different sizes (I like
4x4x0.5), Speedball carving tools, lots of cool little notebooks
(lined & unlined), ink pads of all sizes & colors, etc. I'm not sure
this is the cheapest place to buy everything, but most things are
competitively priced - and they have a large selection compared to
most Michael's I've been to.

Check it out if you're in the area. I understand there's also at
least one box within easy walking distance, too! =)
