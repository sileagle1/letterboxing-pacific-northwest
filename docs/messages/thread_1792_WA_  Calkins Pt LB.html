You'll never guess this but...yet, another 'confirmed missing' (&
killed off) letterbox has reappeared on the scene--unharmed and
highly visited. Hmmmm.....???? Yes, Quilting Noni, rnrB & trkr went
to hang out near a beach today to look for Bald Eagles and decided
to check the Calkins Point LB hiding spot on a whim. Imagine their
surprise when they found the 'long lost' letterbox there safe &
sound!!! Last time I checked on it (several months ago), we were
there at about 0730 and had the place to ourselves which guaranteed
total privacy as we tried to retrieve the box. I reached into the
hiding spot as far as my 'longer than Quilting Noni's' arm could
reach and found nothing but mud, trash and multiple glass beer
bottles. I tried again with a long stick in my hand--no luck. The
letterbox was HISTORY!!! I even 'killed' the box on the clue site
thinking the spot was compromised.

Well, as of today, this letterbox is back on the scene, alive & well
again.

Another great ending to an odd mystery...
