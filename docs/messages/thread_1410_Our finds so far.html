Our finds so far

From: nikanderfamily (blancoandyou@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-29 03:33:51 UTC
Hello all,
Well we have been officially bit by the letterboxing bug this year.
We have been successful at finding the Springwater trail #1 and # 2.
These were fun and very kid friendly. We just had to watch for all
the people going back and forth.
Next we figured out the Bugle Mystery Letterbox. This one was fun
because it was our first harder one that we have done.
Then one a weekend adventure we headed to Seaside where we found the
Seaside Carousel Letterbox, This one definetly took patience. We then
headed out to Cannon Beach and found the Ecola Letterbox. We also
found a geocache so that was intresting to see what that was about.
The geocache was so plain in site that we hid it a little better.
Then we headed home but even though it was getting dark we had to
stop and get the Picea Sitchensis Letterbox. We tried to get this one
last year but it was when it was missing. We had to use a flashlight
but we just couldn't live with ourselves if we didn't get it this
trip and we were rewarded with a great stamp. Right now we are in
pursuit of the Mother May I series which is very hard. We have been
successful with the first stamp so far but we are determined to get
them all. Thankfully we live just a 15 minute walk away. We are
excited about all the new letterboxes planted out in our area now.
There is so much to keep us busy. We are getting closer to wanting to
plant some of our own but just ahven't had the time to do that yet
with our 2 little babies in tow. Maybe soon we will plant some. I am
really liking carving stamps. Well happy hunting all.
The Nikander Family
