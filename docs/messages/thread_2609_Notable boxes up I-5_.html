From: Jeanne (heronafoot@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-18 17:33:39 UTC
If you go thru Elma during business hours there are two letterboxes at
the Elma Library.

"Elma Timberland Library" and "The Ocean Voyage" both by "Donna"

They are listed in the Elma Library's card catalogue which is online
and you can look them up on your computer before you leave.

If you go to Hoquiam their library also has a box and you follow clues
throughout the library to find the box.
It is called "Hungry For Wood" by Margie2005.

Have a fun trip, H-A-F
