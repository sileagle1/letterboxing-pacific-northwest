Found another little gem of a park in Lacey just begging for a
box. The Homann Park Letterbox by Nailhead is planted and
clues are posted to the new LBNA site.

This box is my first attempt at a micro box using one of those tiny
little plastic breath strips containers. Dont know how well it will
hold up to the weather but could not resist trying such a small
container.

Enjoy
