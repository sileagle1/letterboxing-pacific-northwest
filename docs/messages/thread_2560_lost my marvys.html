lost my marvys

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-07-03 02:13:51 UTC
I think I left a set of marvy le plume II markers on a bench of one of
the picnic tables at the group camp covered area Sunday afternoon just
before we left the gathering. If anyone happened to see them and
picked them up I'd sure like to know and would send you postage to
mail them to me. I'll contact the campground but am hoping that
someone in our group saw them sitting there and knew they had been
left behind. They were brand new, and I'm really peeved at myself for
leaving them behind. For identification purposes, one of the little
round snap-shut dealy bobs on the top of the plastic case had a small
rip in it, although the case did still snap shut last time I had it.
These were the primary color pens with brush tip on one end and
fine-tip marker on the other, 12 of them. Thanks a bunch to anyone
who picked them up recognizing them as probably left behind.
