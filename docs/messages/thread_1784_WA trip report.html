guest gathering! (And thanks again to dvn2rckr for organizing the event!)

At 12:01 PM 11/29/2004, you wrote:
>How in the heck did you guys find #2?
>And how in the heck did you not find #1?

Ryan posted a summary of our attempt; we knew that there were problems with
some of the clues, but we didn't know which ones were affected, so we
didn't agonize too much over them. But I led us astray at #1 and we just
never found the nurse tree or the tree "taller than the rest" after
crossing the road and being confused about which path was light and which
was dark. Then Amanda picked up the path and led us to the general area of
#2. There, the group paused when we couldn't pick up the next part of the
clue, and while the rest of the gang regrouped, I forged ahead a bit
farther and found the "redder tree" and the box just as Amanda exclaimed
that we weren't going to find any of the boxes (one of those classic,
perfectly-timed moments). We then followed the path without problem until
we reached the bench at #3, but we couldn't find that box and our time ran
out and we had to head back.

Still, it was a terrifically fun day, and we had worked up quite an
appetite by the time we sat down to margaritas and Mexican food back in
Centralia.
