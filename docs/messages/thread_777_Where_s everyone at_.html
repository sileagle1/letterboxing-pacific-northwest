Come on friends, lets get together there and give me some feedback on the site, let me know what you want there, get active in the forums, enter your boxes into the Database, tell me what you, the box placers, ant it to do, what features you want. Speaking of staff members, there is a need for Moderators on the forums, if you are interested, contact either myself or Rayvenmom for details.

I look forward to seeing you there!

Thank you for the kind words, RayvenMom is home and is doing much better.

On the other things, yes, we have a bunch of places. The idea for this site
came from the repeated postings of people complaining that there was to much
traffic in the LBNa list. That's all. The goal of the site is to provide
one common place for Letterboxing. That's all. Not to replace what we
currently have but to add to it in a beneficial way. Have I done so? Time
will tell.

As far as the 'committee' notion, no way. This site is created, designed,
developed and maintained by me. Moderators are people that have a little
bit of extra abilities in the Forums so that they can move messages, answer
questions, in general, help the new people along.

I'll beg to differ on one point, we don't have a forum, we have an
advertising subsidized mailing list. Nit picking, I know, but it's my
thoughts on the Yahoo Groups concept. Don't mean any disrespect by it
either.

As far as interesting experiences posted to the mailing lists, since that is
a bit of a touchy subject, resulting in a mixed bag of emotional responses
from people on said list, so, I'll mind my P's and Q's in that area, thank
you very much and NOT post my reports (Under whatever name).
