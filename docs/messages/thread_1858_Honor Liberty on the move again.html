Honor Liberty on the move again

From: wingnut_lb (wingnut_lb@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-01-16 22:04:27 UTC

In a lazy moment, I thought it best to reply on the Hog's previous
post and add to it. This wonderful HH (almost a letterboxing in it's
self) has moved on yet again.... It can now be found resting very
nearby Funhog's Ridgefield NWR "Carty" box. Please note there are
new clues for this wonderful box as the terrain has changed, Funhog
has updated the clues so if you have an old copy, go reprint it!
This is such a wonderful location I am driven to go back as the
seasons change and enjoy it over and over again..... Another
thought, after relocating the HH, I realized I "forgot" to stamp the
HH into the carty box log book and vice versa. When you retrive it,
please do so for me before you move it along to it's next location!
Wingnut
