From: SloMo (SloMo86@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-06 20:57:36 UTC-07:00
Hey, was that a fun gathering or what?!?! Thank you Pungent Bob! Thank
you everyone that attended or contributed in some way! The kids were
delightful, the adults warm and welcoming. And now we have people attached
to those familiar personal stamps - we'll think of you every time we see
your stamp!

We decided to stay at the Park an extra day and were rewarded with a
crowdless evening to go down to the gorgeous falls and look for boxes
without putting them at risk. Very cool.

Pungent Bob - now I wish we'd peeked at the LTMB stamp. Maybe next time!

Funhog - Thank you so much for the Trekin' Llama box in the gorge. After
you left I noticed that it's the first box that we found (a yr ago). . . .
we came down that hill with poison oak {snicker} . . . so I'm not sure how
or why we continued on to other boxes. LOL

David from Bvrtn - Your CRV has nicer rims than mine . . . are they stock?

Maiden - Love the idea of a DVD! We'd gladly reimburse you for expenses.
Wood-aug took almost 200 shots, mostly of scenery (He takes great pictures I
just can't get the guy to snap pics of people - LOL). Anyway, would you care
for any of those?

Camp Fire Lady, dvn2rckr, and others that couldn't make it - we missed you.
Hope to see you at Float Your Boat or on the trail!

Today felt SO weird . . . something missing . . . . no letterboxes on the
agenda! I found myself driving around CLUELESS!

CU
SloMo

It certainly sounds like you all had a wonderful time. Thanks for the
kind note. I was 'overcome by events' and will continue to be out of

had to nix the event and a subsequent mini-vacation following it, at
the last minute--as I'd been looking forward to it for MONTHS. A
project that I'm 'losing my summer to' has grown quite unwieldy and has
required more time than I predicted (it's a 3 year requirement
condensed into 3 months--yuck). Take pleasure in knowing that I missed
the event not for 'pleasurable reasons' but rather to sit home and pore
over 600 pages of technical reading each day--been doing that for
almost three weeks straight! :( Hopefully, I'll reign this project in
prior to the Liberty Lake Gathering and can join in on the fun.
Believe me, by then, I'll definitely need it!!! Times a thousand!!!

Have a great summer everybody! I'll live vicariously through your
comments, so post often. :)
