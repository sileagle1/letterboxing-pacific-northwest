Greetings all! It's been so dang quiet around here of late that I'm
worried that everyone's retired from letterboxing and there are no new
players on the scene. In the hopes of getting things spiced up, I
propose that we start a 'topic of the week' discussion.

For the first one, my suggestion is the question, "What things would
you like to see as a PNW Topic of the Week?" I know that some of the
other regional lists do this as a regular feature and it has resulted
in lots of boxers getting to know the other locals. Let's give it a
try. Any suggestions? Funhog
