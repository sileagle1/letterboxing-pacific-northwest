Hello Bellevue. . .

From: soggy2pair (soggy2pair@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-01 02:55:52 UTC
I might get by with an excuse that I'm just returning to LBing since
early summer but heck if I didn't have a curious outing today.

For those familiar with a couple Bellevue boxes...

Mischief/Sunshine/Twilight place the Kelsey Creek Series of 3 boxes.
1. Is great
2. Yep, doing very well - although needs another tablet and I didn't
have one with to add in.
3. Nope. Found where it should have been but did not find the LB.
Unless it is not actually at or around the base of the cedar tree.
There was a lot of company, track teams running so I left without
much searching.

There is also -Little Happiness.
Heck, I tried about three time to follow the clues and start from
other areas to see if I happen to be on the wrong trail. Needless to
say I never found Little Happiness. It did put my brain in
overdrive when I found the 'crocodile box' that is mentioned.
However this was quite a few boardwalks beyond the clues.

Overall it was a great afternoon out, FINALLY. If anyone has more
info on Mischief's Kelsey Creek #3 or Little Happiness I'd love to
hear about it.

Happy Adventures
soggy2pair
