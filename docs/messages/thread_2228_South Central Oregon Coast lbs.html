Doing my part to fill in where I can, I've placed four boxes in this
area near the beautiful Oregon Dunes, where there seemed to be a huge
void: Umpqua Lighthouse and India Series-1,2&3. I have a fourth India
box yet to be placed. These stamps were carved for a summer art camp,
where I teach. Each year the focus is on a different culture, based
upon stories & folktales from published children's books. It's such a
great experience. I enjoy the experience of witnesssing children
learn about a culture different than ours, hopefully making the world a
more tolerant place. I also always learn alot.

Go get 'em!
