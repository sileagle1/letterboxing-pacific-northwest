From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-12-24 01:56:46 UTC
Never in my life did I ever think there was such a thing as too many letterboxes!
However, I revised my opinion yesterday when I headed to Mount Hood CC to track
down this darling bevy of lost pups. Halfway through the series, I totally wimped out!

The hike was so easy and boxes so close together, that I couldn't warm up in between
them. The east wind was howling and branches were tumbling down all around me. I
felt like I was inside a gigantic swamp cooler. When I captured the pooch named Ice, I
realized his name described perfectly the condition of my fingers. This realization
also clued me in to the fact that the essential ingredient for a Funhog...fun...was
eluding me. I headed home knowing that a cup of hot chocolate was in my future.

This afternoon, I headed back and rounded up the remaining four truant canines. The
winds had calmed; the temperature had risen a couple of degrees; and fun was in the
air. Being Winter break, this is the perfect time to search for these little doggies. The
campus is totally empty and I never saw another human being. Just remember to
bring that thermos of hot chocolate. Thanks, Sleepy Whippet, for a cute series in a
quiet spot. Funhog
