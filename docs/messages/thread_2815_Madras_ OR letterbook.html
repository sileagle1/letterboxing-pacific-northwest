I just arrived back in PDX from Central OR and have to say that Jane
Ahern, AKA Jade Heron, rules! She works at the Jefferson County
libraries in the youth programs and has created a wonderful letterbook
hidden (in plain sight) in the Madras library.

The clue is clever and encourages kids to read and to learn to use the
catalogue system. It's a fun find for adults too. Her original stamp
was machine made but with a little prodding, she made a nice hand
carved one to put in its place. I guaranteed her that she would have
visitors from far and wide. Make me honest... visit this letterbox,
named Get a Clue. Funhog
