The heavy rain last night reminded me that I've been neglectful in
removing my Movie Mystery box for the winter. I went out today to
find that the box was dry but the trail to it not. Rather than risk
it becoming a litterbox, I doffed socks and shoes and went over
about twenty feet of very cold, six inch deep water to rescue the
box. It will return sometime next year after the flooding danger has
subsided (and I manage to get around to it).
