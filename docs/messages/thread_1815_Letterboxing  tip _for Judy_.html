Hi :o) Unfortunately my digital camera died in San Francisco :o( so I
am unable to post a photo. I am not too good at giving directions so
here is my pre-school version, Picture an elephants head. with big
ears. The ears are the pencil pouches and the head would be the piece
of wood. I used an 8'X8' square and drilled 2 holes in each side of
it to line up with the holes in the pouches, however you may want to
cut out a rectangle of wood that would be large enough to line all
all 3 holes (I used strong ribbon knotted sever times to attach).
When your all done, you can fold it up like sandwhich, one pouch in
front, wood in the middle, and the other pouch on the back. :o) Good
luck, hope this helps.
