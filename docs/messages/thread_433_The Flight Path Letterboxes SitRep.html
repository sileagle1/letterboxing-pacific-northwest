The Flight Path Letterboxes SitRep

From: Kerjin (kerjin@myndworx.com) | Date: 2003-07-15 19:41:23 UTC-07:00


We went this past Saturday to the Flight Path 6... What a wonderful hidden trail! Here's the status:

US Airways - Alive and well, found and stamped!
Delta - Missing in action! The location had recent teenage party remanants and I fear the box was discovered and, well, lost.
Northwest - Missing in action we think. We found the trail, but could not locate a tree with a holly bush by it within the distance expected.
American Airlines - Alive and well, found and stamped!
United Airlines - Alive and well, found and stamped!
Alaska Airlines - Missing in action. We found the log at the side of the road, right were it should be, but no box anywhere in a 50 foot radius. (shucks!!!)

Overall, we had such a blast in this trail, it was awesome!

Rayvenhaus (RayvenDad, RayvenMom and the RayvenKids)

At least that was true a month or so ago when we went hunting for them!

Here is what the Northwest Clues say:
If you were on Northwest Flight #7 to Tokyo, you would be looking
down and see me go on past a couple of sewer man-holes then taking a
right on a dirt path up a slight hill. You would see me stay to the
left when I reached an open grassy field and reentering the woods on
the SW corner of the field to a multi-trunked tree with a holly bush
beside it. You would see me carefully reaching into the southern side
of the tree between it and the holly bush. Opps you are gonewinging
your way to the Orient.

The Tree with the Small Holly bush next to it is between the grassy
field area and the edge of the "cliff" that looks back down at the
paved path. The Holly Bush is on the side of the tree that is on the
side of the paved path (away from the grassy field area)
--Amanda from Seattle
