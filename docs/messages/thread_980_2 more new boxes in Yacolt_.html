From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2003-11-30 19:25:51 UTC
Hey everyone,
Now you can take advantage of 2 more new letterboxes in Yacolt, WA.
for a total of 4. Moulton Falls letterbox and Chelatchie Prairie
Railroad Letterbox. These boxes boast some of the prettiest scenery
in the area with waterfalls and rivers, clean trails and a historical
open train ride to Moulton Falls. Check out
www.chelatchieprairierr.org for fares and schedules for the train.
The clues for these boxes are easy with relatively flat trails that
include restrooms and picnic areas. Pack a lunch to take on the train
and make a day of it with the kids. The train also offers Theme Rides
such as a reenacted train robbery, Headless Horseman ride, Rides to
the tree farm for you're Christmas tree and volunteer historians to
tell you all about the railroad. At the moment the Chelatchie Prairie
Railroad Letterbox is in a temporary location due to the Swing Bridge
being closed off but will be moved in the Spring so it can be
accessed from the train. There are even a few surprises
for "Firsties"!
Happy Hunting and don't forget to stop by Lewisville Park on the way
to find my Happy Postman Series and Soggy2pair's Lewisville Scramble.
