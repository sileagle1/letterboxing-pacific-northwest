There are boxes at Dash Point and at Hylebos SP in Federal Way as
well as Federation Forest near Greewater. I am not sure of the snow
status for the Federation Forest right now.

If you head west on Hwy 18 from downtown Auburn where it passes under
I-5 it turns into 348th st. About 1 mile more, on the left is the
driveway to Hylebos, look for the log cabin. This is one of my
favorite treasure spots. About 5 miles from downtown Auburn. Dash
point is a bit further, right on the county line between Tacoma and
Federal Way. Total distance from Hylebos, perhaps 8 miles. You
should do a map quest for that venue, as I cannot remember the name
of the connector road. Even though I lived there for many years, I
just didn't pay attention. 8-o

Here is a link for Trial and Errors boxes at dash point and
federation forest.

http://folks.harbornet.com/bburk/letterbox/Clues.htm
