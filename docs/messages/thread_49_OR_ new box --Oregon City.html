LbNA region page URL

Difficulty: easy

Directions: Frog park is the affectionate name my kids and I have
given Hillendale Park, 19260 Clairmont Way, Oregon City.
During the spring we have had lots of fun catching the giant
tadpoles of bullfrogs. In the fairness to our naturalist friend Wild
Woman I must inform you bullfrogs are not native to Oregon. They are
destroying native species of Oregon frogs and they are bad for
Oregon. (But we still think they are fun to catch!)

Clues: Find the pile of boulders. Walk down the path around the play
structure to the bridge. Wander through the trees seeking a home
for night friends. Directly across the water count 3 trees to the
left. In the heart is the creature you seek.
