A private river trip on the Rogue wild & scenic section requires a
permit, and sometimes the permit is difficult to get. It is set up
in a lottery draw. If anyone is interested in sending away for the
river permit we might be able to figure out how we could manage to
get some folks down river in inflatable Kayaks. We wouldn't want to
do less than a 4 or 5 day trip. I would just have to find some other
guides to help with support gear boats. The river itself is a fairly
easy river with only one class 4 at Blossom Bar, that is if you
portage Rainey Falls. If someone needs the permit address I can look
it up and post it here later. If anyone wanted to do a commercial
trip, a stop at Paradise is usually in the schedule.

You can also raft the rogue without having your own personal permit
by hiring river guides. Rogue Wilderness Inc. located in Merlin, OR
specializes in rafting trips of the wild and scenic regions of the
Rogue but I think it can be upwards of a 100$ a day so it can get
awfully expensive to hire a guide for a 4 or 5 day trip but for those
who have the money just laying around waiting to be spent (ha!) it
might be an idea.
