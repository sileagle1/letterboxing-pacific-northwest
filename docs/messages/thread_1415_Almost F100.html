Almost F100

From: Smiley (dwharris@ipns.com) | Date: 2004-06-30 07:58:38 UTC
Well, I was counting my F stamps in my personal log book this
morning and thought what a great idea to try to get F100 in my first
month...but then I looked at the date of my first
stamp...funhogs "Go Fish"...it was the 29th...that was today...well
technically yesterday since it's now 12:42 am on Wed.., but I
haven't gone to bed yet so for me it's still Tues. night. This
morning (tues)I had 80 stamps. I hit pupps new ones in Ridgefield,
WA...my first firsties...at 6:30 am, before work..that's when the
gates open in the wildlife refuge, I actually had to wait about 10
min for the gate to automatically open thinking if it didn't open by
then I was going to have to leave or i'd be late for work. After
work I picked up 2 more of pupps and then my friend "Sunshine" and I
took off for Pandora and Iron Bear's Childhood series up by the zoo.
We found the last box at 10:55 pm, but alas I was only able to get
to F97...I had to go home, it was past my bed time. I'm hoping to
pick up 3 tomorrow...today...Wednesday. Maybe that can count for
F100 in a month...I did have 4 HH and a PLB, but I don't think those
actually count as true finds...or do they? If they do then I did get
to 100!! This is just crazy...but the hunt is so much fun.
I guess I should work on placing some more boxes now...since I have
filled up my personal log now. I have also had an almost exchange. I
ran into Wild Woman on the hunt, but she wasn't really planning on
boxing that day, so she didn't have her stamp to exchange, but I've
left room for her to stamp the next time I see her.

I want to thank those of you who have posted such positive feedback
on my Fantasy Forest series...placing was a bit harder than I
thought it was going to be and I was a bit nervous.
I really must go to bed now...it's past my bedtime...
"Smiley"
