t-shirt tale

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-12-22 23:14:27 UTC
This was too funny... I had a rendezvous planned this morning with a
California letterboxer who has family here in Portland. We planned to
meet at a coffee shop near my home. I had never met this person before
but I figured it wouldn't be too hard to pick her out. I arrived right
on time, perused the people sitting at tables and spotted a likely
suspect. I walked over to her table and spotted her canvas tote bag.
Emblazoned on it was the slogan, "Not all who wander are lost."
Eureka! I'd found my letterboxing compatriot.

Well, I repeated the phrase to her expecting immediate recognition.
When she gave me a blank stare, I asked if she was the boxer I was
meeting. Another blank look clued me in that this wasn't a letterboxer
at all, just another fan of The Lord of the Rings. She cracked up when
I pulled off my jacket, turned around and showed her my slug
ornamented t-shirt with the matching words.

Moments later, I spotted the correct person with a pack on her back. I
was no longer wandering around looking lost. Funhog
