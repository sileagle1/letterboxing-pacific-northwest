Our no boxes trip to Mississippi, and box updates...

From: gryandrowansmum (gryandrowansmum@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-17 04:02:15 UTC
Well, our big move to Mississippi was a disaster from start to
finish! Major car repairs, really bad weather, nearly lost our cat in
Utah, and NO LETTERBOXING!! Wahhhh!!! I had this huge clue book of
every box we might encounter in a 100 mile radius of our route, and I
hit nary a one. And now we're in Land of No Letterboxes (or
mountains, or beaches, or Starbucks) so I'm just kicking my self for
not doing more letterboxing in Washington!

Thank you SOOOOO much Funhog for repairing my box!! There was a foot
of hard frozen snow on the ground and a very impatient family waiting
in the car, so I hid that puppy in about 3 minutes. Obviously, not
very well. I'll go change the clues ASAP. I really liked that stamp
and am so glad a kind thoughtful letterboxer found it.

I did plant a few more boxes (better, I hope!) in Missouri, and hope
to get to New Orleans soon. I'm so jealous about the Leap day
gathering you are all having. I'll probably lurk here and letterbox
in beautiful PNW vistas vicariously through all you lucky souls.

Cami the stargazer momma (now the 4 inch flying cockroach gazing
momma....)
