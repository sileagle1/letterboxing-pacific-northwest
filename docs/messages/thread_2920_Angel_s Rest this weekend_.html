Sad to say, I won't be able to join you but I do have a heads up for
all who may go. I recently received email from a trail crew volunteer
that worked on recent rerouting of this trail. Unbeknownst to me, he
had been watching one of my boxes along this trail for years and
removed it when the trail work was being done. He didn't want it to be
thrown away by some well meaning workers.

Some of the other boxes on this trail may or may not be accessible. My
box will be relocated at a later date. If you happen not to go there
this weekend, I'd love to join you next Saturday to see where the
trail goes now. Have fun if you do go this week! Funhog

Re: Angel's Rest this weekend?

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2008-07-26 04:21:29 UTC
Your boxes are all just dandy. I was up there a couple of weeks back with another boxer
who found them all with no problem whatsoever. The entire section of trail where my box
was hidden is no more. The area is blocked and a new trail has been cut nowhere near the
old hiding place. The log is gone and the landmark boulder cannot be seen at all from the
new trail. Your boxes are on sections that have been tended to but not rerouted. The trail
maintenance is wonderful.

I had the pleasure of meeting my guardian angel (who incidentally is of the tatooed,
pierced variety and goes by the moniker Zombiedoom) who has been boxing as long as I
have! This particular Zombie has a box hidden way up on Cooper Spur that I've been
gunning for for years. I just haven't made the time. Perhaps this summer will be the time.

Funhog
