Camp Fire Lady is correct - the `We Live and Breathe Letterboxing'
event is not set up as a typical gathering! It is not a potluck at a
park or a campout in a campground. This is completely different
more of a summer overnight camp for letterboxers, or a letterboxing
retreat.

It will be impossible to `just drop in' or pay at the door, even for
the locals. Every attendee MUST be registered before the deadline to
satisfy our contractual agreement with Camp Adams, our insurance
policies, and the kitchen counts for meals.

Also, there are a couple of people who are no longer able to attend
and may be interested in selling their registration. If you want
more info, let me know.

The absolute DEADLINE TO REGISTER IS WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3rd
midnight PST, as either a DayTripper or for the full weekend. The
registration form is located at our website at
WeLiveandBreathe.com. You are not considered registered until we
have received both your form and payment. If you have any questions,
please feel free to contact me.

Don't delay, sign up today!
