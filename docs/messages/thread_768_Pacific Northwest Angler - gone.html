Pacific Northwest Angler - gone

From: Rinda (sleepywhippet@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-10-05 00:52:21 UTC
It is with a heavy heart I found PNW Angler missing today. :( I was
at the correct spot as I got firsties on this cool cool box, as well
as my first hitch hiker, and since I was in the area and know
Wingnut hasn't been there I thought I would check on it. Gone!! I
called Wingnut from the spot to confirm I was in the right spot
after I scoured the bushes and surounding area. While talking to
her on the cell phone I looked in the very stinky garbage and found
the lid to the box. Confirmed gone :( Not sure what Wingnut plans
to do but I know she'll keep us posted.

I did find Royal Angler alive and well and very well hidden.

The Salmon are coming in the little river and up to the fish
hatchery. If you haven't been at McIver lately It is so worth the
trip. There are gigantic Salmon in the ladder and in the locks.
Very cool!
