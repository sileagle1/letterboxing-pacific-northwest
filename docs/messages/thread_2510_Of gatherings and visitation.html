From: rscarpen (letterboxing@atlasquest.com) | Date: 2006-05-09 16:31:40 UTC
> P.S. - does anybody have any recommendations about whether the drive
> is prettier/better up the Washington side or the Oregon side?

Oh, gosh, that's a tough question. They're both astonishingly
beautiful, but in very different ways. The Oregon side tends to be
clouded in shade most of the time, while the Washington side basks in
the warm sun. The Oregon side has a heck of a lot more interesting
waterfalls, but the Washington side seems more of a 'stop to smell the
flowers' feel since it's not on an Interstate.

It's a tough call. I often make a loop out of it and drive up one side
then come back on the other, but if you're heading all the way out to
Montana, that's probably not an option for you.

You can hit both sides by switching sides at Cascade Locks or a bridge
somewhere around The Dalles. If you do drive the Oregon side, I'd
definitely suggest using the Old Columbia River Scenic Highway (did I
get the name right?) instead of the Interstate as far as you can,
though. Much nicer than the Interstate. *nodding*

As a whole, I also think there are more letterboxes on the Oregon side
to choose from. =)
