Well, you see, it was like this; Pandora, Punkin (I mean Iron Bear)
Cat's Meow and I were boxing in the Beaverton area on Saturday and
thought we would drop on by Tualatin Nature Park to see if we could
collect any exchanges and we see this poor scrawning-looking guy
sitting all alone carving something and minding his own business. He
looked so lonely that we thought we would drag him along to make him
show us where the boxes for this series were. We scored with 5 out of
8 and if it weren't for the scrawny guy we wouldn't have gotten them.
We then felt so sorry for him that we took him to lunch and made him
show us where Pooch Place at Willow Creek was. After threatening him
that we would hang him by his heels in the stream if he didn't tell
us where the box was he complied and to our horror the box was
missing. We cheered him up by letting him romp around the soccer
field with Cat's Meow and listened to his boring stories about his
travels. It was getting very dark and we still couldn't get rid of
him so I just left him with poor Pandora and Punkin (I mean Iron
Bear) and headed home. No one has seen hide nor hair of the scrawny
guy since. Hmmm. Maybe the guy in the black mask and wig knows where
he ended up.
