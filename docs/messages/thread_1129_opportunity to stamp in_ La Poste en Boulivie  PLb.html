From: sailing_bindlestiff (sailing_bindlestiff@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-11 10:43:36 UTC
I have permission from La Chola to bring her La Poste en Boulivie
from Mich to Portland with me next week!

Camp Fire Lady, this means you get to stamp in "out of turn" right
along with me!

Anyone else who wants to stamp in, is welcome to as well. Even if
you don't stamp in, you should see this stamp! It's an amazing work
of art. Even after two days, I'm still stunned by the skill it took
to carve.

BTW: I promised to send it back to Mark & Sue Peppe ASAP so they'll
have it in time for their event on Leap Day. So I'm sending it out
via Priority mail to them on Sat (2/21) morning ... if that doesn't
work, I'll upgrade to FedEx.

Bindle Bum and I are going to check on our Kent Trails box this
weekend. Last time we looked there was a hitch hiker in there. If
it's still there, I'm planning to bring that with me as well.

Looking forward to seeing all of you soon!

Enjoy!
---PJ
aka Bindle Babe
