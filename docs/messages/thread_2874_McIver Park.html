Another trip to McIver Park, on New Years Day. This park is a
popular place for horseback riding and I ran into my share of
equine. Also found some boxes and a hitchhiker.

Coop's Heron in fine condition and well-hidden.
2nd Grade Ancient China, likewise.
2nd Grade Digestive System, also good.

I especially appreciated Coop's Heron since the image is similar to
the cover of my new logbook.

Having been a newbie to this section of the park (if you read the
different clues for boxes in McIver, many them say "find the gravel
parking lot"), I was at first a smidge unsure I had the correct
starting place... because there has been a change that makes these
clues a bit inaccurate, for a while anyway. No more hints. If I
can figure it out, so can you.

What is everyone else out there finding?
