Wow, what a fun and well attended event! Lots of good company and
good food and bunches of new stamps. Even the weather was tolerable,
tad bit on the cool side maybe but mostly dry. Only got to find two of
the four new boxes but it included a nice walk with Camp Fire Lady up
the "high trail" all the way to someone's back yard (that
was so special!). Have they really found more than half the
letterboxes in the world? What a treat to meet Wanda and Pete from
Rhode Island. And Dvn2rckr, no wonder everyone treasures you...
you're such a sweetheart. I really enjoyed meeting and visiting
with you and your dad and sister. Thanks for putting this awesome
event together, I know everyone appreciates your hard work. Looking
forward to the next one Charlie Trail Duster
