Hi--we ran through Ft S*teilacoom Park today on a mission to
repair/replace some boxes. We found these boxes today & yesterday
(those not placed by me):

Pirate's Treasure: alive & well
Fledgling: alive & well
Keche: alive & well
Happy Patriot: alive & well
Waughop: alive & well
Clown #4: alive & well

We ran out of time to check any of the others. I can, however,
confirm that the following are indeed & unfortunately missing:

Dear Aunt Beth--hiding spot has been decimated
Haunted House--I think the box pirate got this one because it was
alive & well a couple of months ago although it wasn't in its
correct hiding spot when I found it. Unfortunately, I couldn't

fate as some of the others in the region.

I'm fairly certain the first in the Nails' Series is missing, too.

pretty sure it was no longer there. Didn't get a chance to check on
#3 in their series. Perhaps on another trip.

I did replant a modern Heath Farm LB--so ours are almost all back in
place. One more left to replace and then our whole series will be
up & running.

Also, finally--I pulled the Big Tree Inn Letterbox at the end of the
gathering on the 29th of May. It will be transplanted to a spot
closer to the actual site of the unique venue. I'll post a note
when that happens.

While checking on boxes at Ft S*teilacoom Park, I ran into a new
family of letterboxers. Seems letterboxing was mentioned in an area
Homeschooling Organization Newsletter this past week. Also, got
news that there was an article on it in another area paper. I'll
try to track that down & post the link to it.

See you on the trails...
