Had a great hike Thursday with Funhog to the end of Sauvie Island. It
was wonderful. I have lived here 9 years and never took time to sit by
the mighty Columbia. The sun broke out just as we sat down for lunch
next to the lighthouse and we enjoyed the many Blue Herons and ospray
flying overhead. Even saw several little garden snakes on the trail.
(They must have been enjoying the sun too). The grass through the
fields were shoulder high and we really felt like we were exploring
the island for the first time. Thanks DMS! Another great adventure
brought to me because of Letterboxing.
