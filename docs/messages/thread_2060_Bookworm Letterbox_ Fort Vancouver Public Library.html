I recently received a message from the Fort Vancouver Public Library
concerning my Bookworm Letterbox that is hidden there. It seems that
when visitors cannot find this box, they are asking the library staff
for the location. Fortunately, the staff has welcomed this letterbox
into their library and has informed me that a geocache is also located
there. I was also informed that the library reshelves their books
periodically and that my clues for this box may not pertain to it's
hidding place much longer. The staff was gracious enough to show me
some areas that will be more suitable for this little worm so check the
clues before heading out to find him since the clues have changed.
Happy Hunting!
