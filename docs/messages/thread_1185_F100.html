From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-03-13 18:05:13 UTC-08:00
Today I found my 100th box. I found it nestled under a stump on the Upper
Macleay trail. It was an adorable stamp, and a perfect day for the climb.

We started out today with 93 boxes and finished off the day with 101.. an
excellent day if I do say so myself. We found all 4 of the Upper Macay Park
series boxes in fine shape (two were a little damp, but otherwise in
excellent health). The walk down there is beautiful. I had never even been
up to that part of town, I will DEFINATELY have to go back. We planted a
box down there the headed back to the car for lunch. After lunch I headed up
the hill after the Goofy Pub Names series. I found all but number 4, which
it seems others have had troubles with as well. I left a box on the hill as
well. I'm going to post the clues for the two boxes we planted now!

Thank you to everyone that has taken the time and effort to plant the
fabulous boxes I've found so far this year. 100 boxes in 22 weeks.. what a
wonderful way to get outside and enjoy the beautiful Oregon countryside.
I've gone places I never knew existed and learned a whole new craft!

Thank you again.

Melanie
