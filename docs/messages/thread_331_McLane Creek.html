McLane Creek

From: Fudrick (fudrick@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-06-15 16:12:51 UTC-07:00
Had the opportunity to get out to McLane Creek Nature Trail yesterday. A beautiful day, a beautiful park, not too many people, lots of birds.

I had been there before, got dvn2r ckr's Tree in Stump (still there, good condition) and Beaver (not there, hiding place devastated). Looked for her Frog, but could not find it. The clues put it by the old Beaver location, but no luck in finding it.

Trial 'n Error's Beaver and Woodpecker are great stamps, but typical T'nE clues. Read the signs to find the Fish Box, but it wasn't until I was heading back to the trailhead that the aha! arrived and I knew where to look.

NailHead's Place of Solitude most certainly is! Behind the fence where noone will join you, the creek rushing by and the bird's songs is very calming. Unfortunately, had to get back to reality :-(

Head on over for a great afternoon of boxing!

Fudrick
