OR: Central Oregon Good Times

From: The Fleshmans (jfleshman@bendcable.com) | Date: 2003-10-06 02:22:10 UTC
Big LBing weekend in Central Oregon. We covered a lot of ground.
If you have not visited our area, you should consider it.These boxes
brought us to some of the most spectacular views in Oregon. We
got Phoebe Pond, Croooked River Bridge, Crooked River Ranch 1,
Smith Rock-The Golden Eagle and Redmond Dry Canyon South,
and a Bonus Box. Much thanks goes to The Lennens 3, Birder and
Funhog for making this weekend one to remember!

The Fleshmans
P.S. A new box has been placed and clues will be posted soon.
This new box is a joint effort of The Fleshmans & The Lennens 3

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-10-06 03:56:42 UTC
There I was, walking down the dusty road, minding my own business and this van
pulls over. Then the window comes down and the occupants start asking if I'm
Funhog? It was the Lennens3 and the Fleshman clan on the prowl for letterboxes at
Crooked River Ranch. WooHoo! we even got to do an official stamp exchange. It's
almost like Connecticut out there in the sagebrush. You can't even take a little walk
without running into other letterboxers. Funhog


Re: OR: Central Oregon Good Times

From: The Lennens3 (cckylesmomi@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-10-06 15:14:56 UTC
AMAZING, WONDERFUL, FUN, WOW..... words describing our weekend!!!
We had a wonderful time!! Besides the great boxes and great hides,
we also saw some wonderful wildlife!!!!! Deer, Bison, Reindeer
(hehehe), Coyotes, and.....while walking back from the Quail Ale box
at Eagle Crest, we see 4 count them 4, Otters in the river. What a
sight. We have never seen otters in the wild. It was
incredible!!!! My brother also took me for my first time doing some
4 wheeling in the dirt and mud, it was fun but that was enough for
me!!! All in all we found every box we looked for, however, on the
way home my family stopped to get Waterfowl Highway, and were
disappointed at a no find. I know it is there, but it was getting
late around dusk, and we started to hear rustle in the bushes and
got a bit scared!!!!
I did rustle up a family of Quail however!! What a weekend!!!!
Thank you for the wonderful weekend to the Fleshmans and to
Funhog, it was a real treat!!!!!
Just a bit of advice, Everyone needs to plan a trip to Central
Oregon soon, There is a great new stamp carver who lives there and
he is planting great boxes!!!!!! These are stamps that you will
treasure in your journals, they are beautiful!!!!! ( ok yes I am
just a proud sister of a wonderful artist, and no I am not trying to
score points here, just proud that is all!!!)
Well I am late for work now, I overslept this morning from
having so much fun!!!!!
Be on the lookout for some new clues soon, the joint effort
box is a cool one!!!!!!
Talk soon
Carie
The Lennen's 3
