New series at Willamette University!

From: Caitlin Letts (windairen@softhome.net) | Date: 2003-04-29 00:41:31 UTC-07:00
Wizbang and Windairen's Willamette Series

We're working together on a series in Salem, OR. It will be an
"in-progress" series, and we will be adding to it until May 2006 or so
(when we graduate). There are a few things about this series that make
it very different from other boxes:

1. This is an "in-progress" series, so we'll be adding to it as we
go. Hopefully there will be at least four boxes in the series by May
14th, when we go home for the summer.
2. Each box must be found to even get the clue for the next. This
means that we will be maintaining the boxes very carefully, so no dead
boxes!
3. The clues are insanely hard-so you get to meet us when you need
a hint.

The series is posted on the new LBNA site and at Wizbang's letterboxing
site: http://www.willamette.edu/~alorente/letterboxing/

Happy boxing!

Windairen and Wizbang
