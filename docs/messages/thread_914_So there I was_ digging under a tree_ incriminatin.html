So there I was, digging under a tree, incriminating lb'ing paraphenalia all

From: gryandrowansmum (gryandrowansmum@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-10 07:39:02 UTC
around me, at a little bitty park in Portland, with my good old Army
buddy Kris and her grouchy Korean dog. Under the guise of visiting
Kris, I've come to Portland to letterbox. Kris reluctantly comes with
me to Lacamas Creek Park, where she swears everyone there is a
letterboxer. I assure her that in my long months (since April 2003!)
I've never met anyother letterboxer while letterboxing, and all these
people will look at her like she's insane if she asks them what they
are doing. Now we are out at a park I'm sure houses the "Lost
Letterbox", despite not having a proper street map, as the clues
recommend.

So anyway, I'm looking under a tree, on hands and knees, my logbook
and baggie o stamps next to me, when Kris insists that a couple who
just entered the (formerly deserted) park are letterboxers. I assure
her they are not. Then a veritable horde of people come towards me,
and I scramble to come up with a logical reason to be rooting under
trees, when I notice that the couple is none other than Pandora and
Iron Bear! And the "horde" is Camp Fire Lady and Willow with
attendant children! Kris got a great introduction to lb'ing, gathered
all sorts of stamps, and I got an event stamp for this little
serendipitous meeting in the Portland wilds! Good thing too, as under
the trees was completely the WRONG place to be looking!!

So I just had to come out of self imposed lurkdom to share-this hobby
is the best!

Cami the Stargazermomma
