Day at Discovery Park

From: laura woodcock (laurajw@concentric.net) | Date: 2004-03-01 20:04:01 UTC-08:00
Nory & I went out to enjoy the *gorgeous* day at Discovery
Park on Sunday. We started with the dvn2r ckr gang's West
Point Mule. What a fun way to start our afternoon in the
sun. He's a very cute mule who seems somewhat lonely - he
hasn't had a visitor since may of last year! Go and do this
box!

We then head over to the Wolf Tree Trail to do Candlelight &
lp's boxes. Unfortunately, it was a sad sad trip. I looked
for the Blizard Boxes before and found two of them missing.
Nory wasn't with me that trip but together we found that
four of them are gone. Very very sad. And then, to make
things worse, we went looking for the Funny Little Man. He
seems to be gone too. What is up with the Wolf Tree Trail?
It's such a great trail but all of the gorgeous trees seem
to be taking all of the letterboxes!
