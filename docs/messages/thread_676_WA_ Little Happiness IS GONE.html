WA: Little Happiness IS GONE

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-09-06 21:23:20 UTC
I went back yet again ( second time) to the Little Happiness LB in
Kelsey Creek, Bellevue WA.

This box has been reported lost and found several times.

Lizard and Catbird placed this box early last year and have since
moved. I offered to adopt the box.

Somewhere along the line, perhaps spring of 2002 a second box showed
up at the spot. This box is the Green Crock, a crocodile stamp in
green and I believe placed by a local school teacher.

I have searched the tree up to my elbows. There is a cavity with a
drop off far back and to the right of the cavity that holds the box.
It falls away to nothing and is not accessible from any other hole in
the tree. It is possible that the smaller Little Happiness fell into
the pit never to be recovered. Lizard and Catbird did NOT pull the
box before they moved, as I had assumed.

I looked at the log book of the Green Crock Box and noted a comment
by Team Rayvenhaus about "happiness being..."

I am assuming that the people reporting finding the Little Happiness
( the image is that of a dim sum; chopstix, take away carton and
dumplings) are really finding the green crock.

I will leave the clues in place which allows folks to find the GC and
enjoy the hike. Right now I cannot edit the clues sheet. I will
tackle that next.
