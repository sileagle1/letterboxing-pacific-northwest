From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2003-05-27 00:35:00 UTC
Yes, I too "found" that Council Crest was missing.

Council Crest is one of those spots where a bunch of people hang out,
drink, climb trees, etc. and the box, according to the clues, was too
much part of the public area not to have been found by non-boxers.

This was the the first one that my husband & I tried for -- we walked
up and over Council Crest from Hillsdale to downtown, and checked for
it.

I thought maybe someone was trying to hide a stash and found lb and
tossed or kept it. My husband said that would be a baaad place for a
stash, since a website had directions to it!

However, yesterday, my husband & I found our first box! I'm still
flying high! Just joined this group today -- thanks Funhog!

We found the Japanese Garden one. (I recognized Funhog on this list
from the LB Notebook there!) Afterwards, we relaxed IN Japanese
Garden (I'm a member), then had gourmet pizza at Vista Ridge Caf
to celebrate!

Yippee!! I am now F1!
