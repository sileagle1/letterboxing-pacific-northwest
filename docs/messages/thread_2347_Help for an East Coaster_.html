Hi all,
I'll be travelling to Portland for work a few times over the next year.
My first trip will be this month and I'm hoping to snag a few boxes
during my trip. I know absolutely nothing about the city, including
geography, parks, or the transportation system.

I'll be staying near the Lloyd Center in what I guess is the downtown
area, and will be working close to the Rock Creek Campus of Portland
Community College. Der Mad Stamper's RCC boxes are already on my list
of boxes to get.

Anyone have suggestions for others to get? I won't have access to a
car, so public transport will be necessary to use. Any tips would be
appreciated, as I feel like I'm travelling blindly - not knowing
anything about what I've heard is a beautiful city!

Thanks,
Fleur de Lis

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-11 03:27:33 UTC
If you have the time, you can get to Mt. Tabor from Lloyd Center and
find two really cool boxes - one is Der Mad Stamper's box "Mt. Tabor"
and the other is a three-stamp box carved by a Central Oregon boxer,
4dtoes, which I adopted and recently placed up there again. Here's a
link to the buses you would have to take - this would take you some
time, mind you, but could be done if you have some free time. It's
also a very beautiful park even without the boxes.
http://www.trimet.org/go/cgi-bin/itin.pl
