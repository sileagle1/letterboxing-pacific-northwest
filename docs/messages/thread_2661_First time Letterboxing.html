Hi there,
DH & I tried our luck today on A Galloping Gander in Victoria, BC.
Our very first letterbox search and we had a ball. We never did
find the letterbox, I had no idea how many 'large, distinctive'
trees that are here and how many 'clusters of roughly seven trees to
the right and slightly downward' are within close proximity to the
aforementioned 'large, distinctive'trees. All we found was a dirty,
empty, and leaf covered Grocery bag. I don't think that was IT.
But we did have a good time and there are a bunch more letterboxes
planted in Victoria. Never give up, Never surrender.

TTFN

Sounds like you DID have success on your first letterboxing outing! So many people forget that the point is not just the stamp, but the whole outing. Don't get discouraged, of course, and maybe if you let it set for a while, you can come back to Gander and it'll suddenly make sense to you. Everybody's got their story of a big "AH HA!" moment and boxes they've given two, three, or more tries before they found it. It just makes it that much better when you DO find it!

Happy 'boxing!

ClioMouse
