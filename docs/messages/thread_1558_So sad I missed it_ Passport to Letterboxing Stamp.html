gone.

Snickerdoodle --

We are really sorry you couldn't make it - we were a bit concerned about you
and yours on Friday night when you didn't show up and were relieved a bit to
find out that it was just a case of someone being sick and not something too
terribly serious when Charlie informed us on Saturday. I am terribly sorry
to hear about your husband's adventures in ER this weekend and hope all
turns out well when he sees the cardiologist tomorrow. -- I think you are
right though, sometimes things happen for a reason and it ended up being a
good thing that you stayed home and was able to be there for him. I felt the
same way when our car broke the night before going to California -- it
delayed us, made us miss a lot of boxes we would have gotten on the way
down, but saved us from being stranded when the car would have broke down on
the way there 100s of miles from home.

Please keep us informed and we will look forward to seeing you at the next
event!

Pandora
