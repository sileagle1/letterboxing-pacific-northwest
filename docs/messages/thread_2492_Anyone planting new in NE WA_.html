My Girl Scout troop will be Attending the Hands Across the Border event
at Peace Arch Park (in Blaine, WA) on June 11th. I would LOVE for us to
find a letter box or even better a series of boxes while we are there
for the weekend. This weekend will be our Mother/Daughter event. I will
be introducing them to Letterboxing as we find a couple of So. Hill
Henderson's boxes that are close by. Then I hope to letterox that
weekend with them.

We will be close to Bellingham. I see there are a couple of boxes in
the area, but they don't sound like they would be easy enough for us to
get to. What I would like is something that will take us an hour or so
or longer for a series.

plant our first box AT Peace Arch Park while we are there. to my
