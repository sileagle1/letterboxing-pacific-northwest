From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2007-05-16 16:01:02 UTC
LadyB, her son Monkeyboy and I found this last summer.

It was originally in a metal case (similar to a mints case) and it
had been crushed under the huge mounds of rocks at its hiding place.
Though the logbook was in a bag, the deterioration of the box and the
nearby water had allowed moisture in. The logbook was not only wet,
it was a gooey mess of filth and mud and who knows what else.

LadyB and Monkeyboy had a spare box for planting that they ues to
replace the case. I have a spiral logbook, so we ripped out a few
pages for a temporary logbook and pulled out some spare ziplocks.

I think LadyB tried to contact the planter about the icky logbook
(beyond redemption) and I don't think she got a response.

Sounds like this box or the location just are not going to work,
especially with needles nearby! (shudder)

Thanks for salvaging what you could and letting us know.

Please let us know if you hear from the placer.

KuKu
