The Traveling Circus Unites

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-01-04 01:10:04 UTC

In the last couple of years, the TC has had its
members peel off and head for far flung
places, British Columbia, Texas, Thailand,
Salem... Yesterday, a quorum (plus one guest)
managed to meet for a box lunch. Letterbox lunch,
that is! The rendezvous began at the fabulous
Temple of Gluttony mentioned in The Green
Hornet's fine Crime and Punishment #3
clue. After stamping into the overflowing
logbook and leaving a new scroll for future
visitors we moved on to the eating part of
the day. Beer, burgers and a quickie
carving demo from one of the gang were
the order of the day.

Since one of the crew has a very uncooperative
knee that was just attacked by a surgeon, no
Green Tortuga hikes were planned for the
afternoon. After a little puzzling and map
searching we found ourselves stamping into
the Everything That Isn't logbook. This is yet
another wonderful stamp by Pandora, one that
I love having in my collection.

The next stop was a sobering one at the
Holocaust Memorial in Washington Park.
After a brief bit of quiet contemplation,
we found ourselves adding the image from
pupp's lonely letterbox into our logs. This
one is poignantly appropriate for the recently
completed holiday season. After six months in
the wild, we were the first in the log... unless
this was a replacement, of course.

All in all, this was a most enjoyable way to ring
in yet another year. Make new friends, but keep
the old...Funhog
