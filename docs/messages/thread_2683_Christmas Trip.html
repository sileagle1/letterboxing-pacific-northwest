I really like the "Rooster Rock" box.
There's "Cousins" which was tailored for just such a trip. As was Horse
Heaven Hills . . . although you may already have that one if you live in
Kennewick.
If you can go off 84, "Fruit Loops" Tour is pretty neat. Especially the
Panorama box.
And my guess is that you already heard about the famed "Jay's Tool Box".
Hope this helps - it's so hard to decide what boxes to look for!
Have a wonderful trip!
