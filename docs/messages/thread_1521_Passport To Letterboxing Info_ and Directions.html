Wouldn't you know it. There is construction going on in the area of
the directions to Battle Ground Lake found on the link so I will give
you the path you need to travel to get to the Passport To
Letterboxing Stamp Camp at Battle Ground Lake. Please take note of
this message before you head out to the gathering.

I-5 freeway, off at exit 9/179th St.,
if you are coming from the north, pass under the freeway and make a
left on NE 10th Ave./SR 502.
if you are coming from the south, go straight through the light at
the end of the off ramp onto NE 10th Ave./SR 502.
Travel 2 miles to NE 219th St/SR 502 and turn right, travel approx.
10 miles (NE 219th will turn into Main St. when you get into Battle
Ground) and turn left on Grace Ave., right at NE 229th St./Weisson
Rd., right onto Palmer Rd. to Battle Ground Lake State Park and turn
left into the park.
There will be red signs with red, blue and green balloons directing
you from the freeway to the park so keep an eye out for them. See you
there!

PASSPORT TO LETTERBOXING STAMP CAMP

When: August 27-29, 2004
Where: Battle Ground Lake State Park, Battle Ground, WA.

Come out and spend a weekend camping or come for the day on
Saturday. There will be workshops on Letterboxing 101, Stamp Carving,
Book Binding, Jr. Ranger Program and Camp Crafts for the kids as well
as many letterboxes in the area to find! Visit
http://www.parks.wa.gov for information on campsites, directions to
the park and other things that the park has to offer while you are
there.
Each person attending will be given a Passport that will include
pages for your personal stamp and information, a map of the park,
clues to letterboxes in Battle Ground Lake Park only (you will need
to bring the clues for letterboxes at Lewisville Park and Yacolt if
you plan on searching for them while at the Stamp Camp), a complete
schedule of events and pages for the images of the MANY event stamps
and letterboxes that you will find at the park. This passport will
also make a great keepsake after the event.
There will be some awesome letterbox items raffled off at the
event. Raffle tickets are $1.00 each or 6 for $5.00. Kid's raffle
will be free.
When you arrive, everyone including the kids will need to "check
in" at the Passport to Letterboxing Stamp Camp check in table located
at campsite # 14 to receive your passport and information about the
event. If you are attending on Saturday only please arrive any time
between 6:30 am and 9:00 am to check in and be there in time for the
first workshop.
Please bring your carving tools if you have them, starter kits
will be available for $5.00 each if you would like to purchase them
at the event, a hitch hiker if you would like to participate in the
Hitch Hiker Hostel hidden in the park, your own firewood if you will
be camping (the park does not provide firewood), your own meals since
this will not be a potluck event, your letterboxing supplies and
plenty of cash for the raffles and the day use fee of $5.00 if you
are not camping out. Please do not bring extra letterboxes or hitch
hikers to leave on the tables.
Your hosts for this event will be Camp Fire Lady, Iron Bear and
Pandora, Maiden and Bookworm.
Look for the red, blue and green balloons that will direct you
into the park from the I-5 freeway and we will see you there at the
Passport To Letterboxing Stamp Camp at Battle Ground Lake State Park,
Battle Ground, WA.
Please R.S.V.P. to Camp Fire Lady at tinarie at msn dot com if you
plan on attending this event so that we can make sure that we have
enough supplies for everyone.

Camp Fire Lady
