WA: Boxing at Pt. Defiance today

From: minibullymom (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-02-07 03:48:09 UTC
Hmm...methinks that the Forest Gnomes are infested with
Poltergeists!! We spent two hours this afternoon looking for "The
Forest Gnome" and "Jarl the Wood Gnome". When the sky was rent apart
with hail we decided that those two gnomes were sending a signal that
today would not be the day to find them.
I will admit that we went back and forth on the trail counting and
recounting all the markers and still were unable to find a small
hemlock tree evenly spaced between two cedars which would be the home
of the first gnome. Even with all the possible combinations of the
triangles (forwards, backwards or facing both directions)and looking
only at cedar and hemlock combinations (I do know my trees - I used
to teach plants and trees at Camp Seymour) we were zero for zero.
So..we tried to find "Jarl" we found three curved stones on the trail
that fit into the clues but none of them revealed "Jarl" at the cedar
that was at 50*.
SO...soaking wet and cold from hail...we backtracked and went after
the Pt. Defiance #1 Box! PAYDIRT! Quite literally...the box hasn't
been visited since last summer and has only had about a dozen visits
since it was planted in 2001. With that summer being the banner year!
Due to the lateness of the day (2:30pm and our growling tummies we
decided to forgo Box #2 of that series until next weekend or the
weekend of the Leap from Fantasies to Frontiers event.
We have decided to sit and carve several stamps and get boxes ready
to plant next weekend at the park in anticipation of the Leap Day
event!

Til later,
MiniBullyMom and MomNoTime
