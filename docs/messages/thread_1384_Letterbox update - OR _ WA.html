From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-06-08 05:14:50 UTC
Hey Charlie and anyone else who might be vaguely interested,

There will be a letterboxing class at the end of the month in Longview and the first
session will include carving instruction. I'm no Legerdemaine but I can give you a lot
of the pointers I've picked up along the way and you can run with them. The
information is available online at: http://www.ci.longview.wa.us/parks/
Just click on the family activities link to see the brochure.

One of the cool things about this class is that the last session will be a letterbox hunt
for the initiates. I have gotten permission from the parks folks to open this activity up
to the general public. We will all be able to welcome the newest of newbies into our
ranks on that day. Clues for the special letterboxes hidden for the event will be
available at the venue. The class participants (crossing fingers that some will sign up)
will get first dibs but at 2:30pm the clues will be available to all comers.

The details of time and place will be posted here. I am hoping that some of you will
be able to show up. Funhog
