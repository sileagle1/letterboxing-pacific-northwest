1 year Anniversary

From: bcostley (bobbyeubanks@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-04-18 22:18:53 UTC
We made it to our first year anniversary of letterboxing this month.

In honor of the event I counted up our stats - something I don't
ordinarily keep track of - and found we are F70P23X11.

I think our ratio of about 1 box planted for every three found is
pretty good!

Note to Amanda - I checked your list of your 501 finds and saw that
other than one hitchhiker you haven't found ANY of our boxes!

Of course a lot of the Portland folks need to get out of the big
city sometime and look for our boxes. Of course we do have at least
4 boxes in Forest Park too (which have been a bit lonely). Meanwhile
we need to get into the big city to find a lot of the new ones that
have been springing up!

One other note: to our knowledge five of our boxes have never been
found: Nehalem Bay, Alaska Souvinier, Wedding Mystery, Hummingbird
Bonus, and Chief Kno-Tah.

A list of our clues can be found here:

http://home.agalis.net/bcostley/Letterbox/
