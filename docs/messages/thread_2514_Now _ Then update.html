I apologize for not responding immediately--we've been in SE Asia
for the past 10 days and upon returning to Korea I had 700+ emails
to sort through (that includes purging about 246 of them while
sitting in an internet cafe in Bangkok on day 2 of our trip and
purging another 375 of them in an internet cafe in Siem Reap on day
8) of which about 20 included questions about letterboxes. The sad
part is that most of these hundreds of emails were spam that somehow
bypassed the filters we've set up!

The two "Now & Then" boxes were alive & well when I repaired them on
1 Apr. They should still be there. The "Now" container blends in
well with its hiding spot--no need to dig--there should only be a
piece of bark with a bit of moss and leaves scattered about it that
would need moving in order to find it. The box is lying flat on the
ground next to the stump. I doubt it's gone missing unless mushroom
hunters got pretty aggressive this year. ;) Hope this helps,
daelphinus
