I remember way back when...Rattlesnake Ledge was the ONLY letterbox up
in Northwestern Washington. (I sound so old, don't I?) So, remember,
the supposedly "Good Old Days" ain't always so good compared to now when
you have lots of great letterboxes up there to choose from.

Trial and Error and Evil Glenn got the ball rolling up there. No matter
what folks may think of them, I'm thankful they came along and got
things underway in the Tacoma area. Then Amanda from Seattle came on the
scene and things really picked up. And all who have come along since.
Thanks so much for making Washington such an excellent letterboxing
state just a few years later.

Best regards,
