New box in Seaside

From: seatroute (seatroute@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-08-18 18:31:17 UTC
I wanted to let everyone know about our newest box -- this one is
Seaside -- because it is in such a cool place! I can't wait for the
first person to find it.

I lived in Seaside for many years and wanted to place a box somewhere
little known by visitors, but special. Most of Seaside's boxes are in
town or near the Prom. This one is on the edge of town, in the woods
but with a spectacular view of the sea. And the best thing about it --
for some -- is you don't have to hike an hour to find it.

So check out letterboxing.org for "Sunset Boulevard". It would be
better to find the box before the mud season arrives if you don't want
to get dirty!

Good luck.
