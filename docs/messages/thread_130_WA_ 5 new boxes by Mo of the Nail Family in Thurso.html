WA: 5 new boxes by Mo of the Nail Family in Thurson Cty

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-04-27 22:38:50 UTC
Mo is 9 and has been working hard on carving lots of new
stamps.
The Nail Family went out today and planted 5 of his new boxes
around the Lacey area.
Look for Jo the Alien at Lois Lake Park, as well as a new 2 box
series at Lois Lake Nature Trail, and 2 new boxes at
Wonderwood Park. All the clues have been posted to the new
LBNA site.

Please email or reply to this message when you visit, Mo wants
to know how everyone likes his boxes.

Nailhead

From: Fudrick (fudrick@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-04-29 20:04:27 UTC-07:00
This morning, just before I left home, I got a call from the office that the
power was out, all over the neighborhood. If I wanted, I could come in
later. So I did!

Went to Lois Lake Park and Nature Trial, was first at Jo the Alien, Fresh
Fruit, and the Carrot.

Still some time left, so went over to Wonderwood to get the Pencil and Bill
the Upside Down Robot (at least that is how he is in my logbook :-(

Very nice stamps, great locations. Recommend them highly to everyone.

Good job, Mo! Looking forward to more of your good work.

Was first in Lois Lake, but was behind Bill and Glenn at Wonderwood Park.
Nothing to be ashamed of following such expert 'boxers.

Fudrick
