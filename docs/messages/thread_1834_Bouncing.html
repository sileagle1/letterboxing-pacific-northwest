ADMIN: Please check your memberships


Hi Folks,
The last couple of days a lot of folks have started bouncing their
memberships. What does that mean? That means if you aren't getting
list mail. It seems that many ISP's decided to make some changes and either
add spamcop (that seems the most frequent one I run across) or change the
ISP's on their blacklist.

I'm posting this to the group in the hope that some of you that
stopped
getting mail will see this post and contact your ISP (or if you
installed the
software on your computer make changes) and have them whitelist Yahoo
Groups.

We're trying to reactivate many of the accounts (which is all I can do
at
this point) but until this gets corrected the accounts will continue
to bounce. Right now every time I log on I'm getting about a dozen new
accounts bouncing.


Now some are probably saying but I'm set up to No Mail so that
shouldn't affect me. Well it won't if you are No Mail on ALL of your Yahoo
Groups but if even one of your groups starts to bounce e-mails, ALL of your
groups will start to bounce whether your are on No Mail or not.

I'm sure many of you know where to go to check your bounce history but
for
those who don't here are the steps.
Log into Yahoo Groups at http://groups.yahoo.com
Sign in with your Yahoo ID and password
Click on My Groups link
Click on the E-Mail Preferences link
Click on the Bounce History link

At the bottom of the page it will list the reason you are bouncing.
This
should help you or your ISP fix the problem.

Thank you for your help in making our jobs a little easier.

And before I close, if you are going to be away from your computer for
a
long period of time please make sure you set ALL of your groups to No
Mail. I
know some groups don't allow it (in that case set to digest) but we
aren't one
of them.

Thank you all for your help in this. You'll really help us get off
this
computer and back to stamping!
Hugs,
Paula McElroy
List Mom/Owner
