which we may be able to meet with our mutual friend in letterboxing--

******************

Would anybody be able to attend one or either of the following
events?

--Monday, 10 Nov 03--evening/dinner event in the Olympia area
(thinking along the lines of the Oyster House on the marina--upscale

or some other similar environs)

--Saturday, 15 Nov 03--0900-1030 quick exchange event at Wapato Park
in Tacoma (with all exchanges ending precisely at 1030 so one Silver
Eagle can catch his return flight home shortly thereafter)

******************

Please let me know if you would be able to attend such an event
(either post to the board or write me directly)! Also, include the
number of folks in your party who would also be able to attend. I
need to know the details about the Monday night event by tomorrow
afternoon at the latest. As for the Saturday event, we have a bit
more coordination time.

Thank you in advance,

From: Nail Family (the7nails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-08 20:57:11 UTC
The Nail Family, sadly, will not be able to attend. We will burying
Nailhead's grandmother at the Claquato Cemetery that morning and a
family gathering will take precedence over letterbox activities that
evening. We will miss the fun.

Please, everyone, have lots of fun and eat some oysters in
rememberence of Marian Santee, Adna and Chehalis resident of more
than 70 years who adored the succulent Olympia Oyster. Marian died
at the age of 87 and had a long and wonderful life and witnessed a
world of changes in Western Washington during her lifetime. She is
being laid to rest among her family and the pioneers of the area.
She will be missed.

And if you get the chance, dont forget to check out the letterboxes
at the Claquato Cemetery (Pioneer Fir Tree) and Claquato Church near
Chehalis commemorating those pioneers.

The Nail Family
