From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-24 19:58:50 UTC
Had a wonderful evening yesterday exchanging letterbox hunting stories
and shooting the Salem breeze with Kernville's own Don & Gwen!!! What
a delightful couple! It was so neat to finally meet them in person
after all these years. These two incredible folks have led us on some
really neat adventures around the West Coast! We look forward to
meeting up with them again in the future.

Letterboxing is such a cool hobby and it's mostly because of the
awesome people who make it happen! Don & Gwen epitomize that with
their great letterboxes--awesome stamps, exquisite locations and unique
clues!!! Thank you for the adventures!!!

beyond our control)
