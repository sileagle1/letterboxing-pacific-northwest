Those of you who showed up, I want to thank you! It was a great time
for John and me. I'm glad I got to meet so many people who have gotten
involved in the sport within the past year. I see their stamps and now
I see their faces!

It was great having Rangers Kyle and Elisa there! I'm going to try to
get together with Elisa soon. She says they have a box of many of my
Rick's Farm series there, still. Perhaps with their informed guidance I
can replace some or all of these letterboxes in the Nature Park again!
And maybe those who are interested can get together and we can do some
trail maintenance for the park sometime in the future (like in the
spring or something). It was great that they are wanting to reach out
to our letterboxing community and get us involved in a way that works
well for the park system and letterboxers alike.

Great job to Funhog for getting this together. What a huge hit this one
was! The idea of our signature stamps on the name tags was perfect!
This was just the type of winter gathering I was looking forward to.
Ryan and the beautiful Amanda from Seattle did arrive fashionably late,
but at least we got to see and visit with them briefly. I would have
like to have hung around longer and BS'd more when we had to move
downstairs, but a certain gorgeous man of mine was ready to go.

All the great folks involved in this sport make me eager to go out and
seek their letterboxes. For sure Pandora and Team Chaos are must haves,
though with their clues I'm afraid I may actually have to put my brain
to use. DANG!!! Come on, guys! I'm not that smart. I like the easy
gimme type clues. But I just might make and effort for those stamps.

Willow, Daisy, Heartsey, Campfire Lady, Cat's Meow, Bookworm, Sleepy
Whippet, Wingnut...who am I missing...YIKES! It was great to meet you
for the first time, too and I will have to go out and seek any boxes
that you have placed. You are a delightful and creative bunch FOR SURE!

Great event stamp carvings by Brett! It was terrific seeing you, Penny,
Grace and the ever-adorable new sweet pea, Nicholas! I was so glad to
see you!

Mark and Beth you have got to place more LB's. Your carving skills are
outstanding! That new Halloween series you placed is an all time

see you last night.

Windairen and Wizbang came to this event in such a way that I was afraid
