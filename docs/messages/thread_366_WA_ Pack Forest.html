WA: Pack Forest

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-06-29 21:18:32 UTC
After consultation with the site manager (twice) and a visit, I have
decided NOT to place boxes here.

It is a lovely venue with over 4000 acres of potential. However
there is ongoing logging and timber work, hunting in season and road
and trail closure as these activities require.

It is far enough away from the mainstream of boxland in the region
that I thought it unfair to send someone out to this venue only to be
greeted by closed roads and / or trails that would require much
longer hikes, ( if at all) or totally closed access. I know I would
not appreciate spending the time and gas to go so far only to be
turned back.

I now have three boxes ready to go ( humble I know, don't snicker
Bethany). Perhaps this coming weekend there will be a likely spot.
