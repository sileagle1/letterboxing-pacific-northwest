Clues to a new letterbox in honor of Olympia's 28th Arts Walk have
been posted today. Arts Walk is a celebration of the local arts
community by artists, businesses, and the City of Olympia Arts
Commission. Arts Walk kicks off from 5 til 10 tomorrow night and
continues Saturday from 2 til 9 -- including the 10th Annual
Procession of the Species at 5:30. It's a free event which includes
nearly 100 exhibits as well as street performers, music, dance,
theater, and family hands-on art activities throughout the city of
Olympia. For information call Olympia's Parks, Arts, and Recreation
Department at 360.709.2678. Or check out the Procession web page at
http://www.procession.org/. The Procession has been referred to as
the Mardi Gras of the NW. Do I get paid in any way for any of this?
No. Like many of you, I am always looking for something new and fun
to do and the Spring Arts Walk and Procession is always a good time.
