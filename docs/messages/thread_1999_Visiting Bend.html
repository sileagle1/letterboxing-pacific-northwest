From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-04-07 23:31:11 UTC

Smith Rock is stunning but a little one who doesn't like letterboxing might be
overwhelmed by some of the hikes. Shoshoni Sun and Smith Rock Golden Eagle are
the two easiest ones there. Tumalo State Park is quite close to Bend and has quite
and array of boxes, all easy hikes. There is also a kids' play area close to the
campground, complete with swings. Most of the others in Bend, with the exception
of Pilot Butte, are easy hikes with wonderful stamps, beautiful scenery and
interesting wildlife. The boxes at Newberry Volcanic Monument will take you to see
some VERY interesting areas. Honestly, you can't go wrong with any of the
adventures offered by the boxes in this part of our fair state. Enjoy! Funhog
