Egyptian Series Letterboxes

From: bookwormbox (bookwormlb@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-06-29 02:43:49 UTC
Hi all,

I went to check up on my Egyptian Series in Orchards Park this
morning. I'm sad to say that box #1 & #2 have gone missing. #3 &
#5 are both safe and sound. When I checked up on box #4 I found a
little baggie on the trail in front of the hiding place, and was all
set to find it gone as well. It was there, but sitting out in the
open and was all moldy and gross inside, so it has been pulled for
maintenance. I will re-carve and re-hide them all soon. For now,
I've changed the clues on the letterboxing.org site, so toss out any
old ones you may have hanging around.

For those of you who have box #1 & #2 you may want to go back again
when I replant them. These were my very first stamps I ever carved,
and I've gotten a little better since then. I even have a few new
ones to hide...with some special treats inside. Coming soon to a
stump or rock near you soon!

Hope you are all out there having fun finding boxes!

Bookworm
