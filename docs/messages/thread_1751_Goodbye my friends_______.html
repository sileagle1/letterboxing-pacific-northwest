I'd like to personally thank each and every person that came to wish me
off to Alaska at Izzys yesterday. The stamp was awesome, the food was
great and the friendship was overwhelming. It meant more than you'll
ever know to heave you guys throw something like that for me. Each one
of you is special to me and will not be forgotten. To those that
couldn't make it, but sent their best wishes, I can't thank you enough
either.

single sheet of paper 7x5 and stamp your signature stamp on it, (In
landscape mode not portrait mode) and just a few thoughts on it and mail
it to RayvenMom (Getting the snail mail address by writing to her at
rayvenmom at lbworld dot org) and she'll return mail you the stamp image
made by Team Chaos.

I'll get pictures of the area uploaded as soon as I can when I get there
tomorrow.
