The earthquake, naturally, was quite exciting. My grandmother and
aunt in Paso Robles got an even better ride out of the deal, although
from what I heard it made a class A mess of their place knocking
darned near everything to the floor. But they're okay and their
homes are okay. We traditionally head to Paso Robles each Christmas
to celebrate at my grandmother's house, and I'm hoping for a couple
of good aftershocks while I'm there. Later we plan to go into
downtown and check out the damage firsthand. I drove passed it on my
way down to San Luis today, but never got out of the car to check it
out close up. Parking is tough! It seems like everyone in the
county was out there taking pictures. *shaking head* Sick, twisted,
people that they are. =)
