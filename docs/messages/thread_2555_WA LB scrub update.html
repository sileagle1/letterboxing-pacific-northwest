Hey there,

We're rapidly running out of time to 'repair' many of our LBs before
we depart the area. I have found some revised clues for one of
our 'clueless' boxes of late, though. So, I posted the amended
clues to the Zug-WA Monster II letterbox in Tumwater, WA. The
original LB of this repeatedly went missing in a longterm 'turf
battle' with area geocachers. Eventually I surrendered the 'battle'
as it just wasn't worth the mental anguish and wasted gasoline to
the area anymore. I decided to replant a 'new' letterbox in a
totally different area of the park--so that explains the two
different LBs listed on AQ. I just posted the revised clue to
this 'new' (although it's almost been there 2 years) location.

In about a week or so I'll officially 'abandon' most of our WA/OR/ID
letterboxes--in the event anybody in the area may be interested in
adopting them. I tried to scrub the clues to our remaining LBs on
the sites to ensure they were as accurate as I can possibly tell
(without compromising the intended mystery/intrigue incorporated in
them) at the moment without laying my eyes on them in the process.
Hopefully this will help prevent a lot of confusion for
future 'seekers' of these boxes. But--as in life, you just never
know until that time comes. If you're interested in adopting any
specific LBs of our's out there, please contact me off-line. Thanks
& have a great week,
