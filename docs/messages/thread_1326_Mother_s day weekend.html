From: eric jaroch (e_jaroch@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-10 14:06:29 UTC-07:00
We went camping at Milo McIver SP Sat night and Sun
night. Had a great time camping/boxing. We didn't
break the record 21 boxes set last week, but found a
respectable fifteen, including our 100th. That
memorable one was the first box of the Jurassic Park
Series. We managed to find all but a few that we were
looking for, 14/19 plus a HH(our second)! So not a
bad weekend of boxing.

Seeing all of the stamps from the gathering last
summer, we can't wait for an opportunity to attend
one.

We also came up with a great idea for a series, we
just need to put it together and find a place to hide
it.
