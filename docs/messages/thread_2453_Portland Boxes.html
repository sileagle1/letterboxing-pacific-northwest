The workshop is in Johnson City, I think. Off 205, and they say Oxbow is
about 15 minutes away from there. Does that sound right? I have absolutely
no skill at getting an idea of distance from Google maps. It's at a
friend's house (it's been in Waldport in years past), and I'm going on pure
memory. I've never been there, and can't find the message where she told me
where she lives.

I'll be going one direction via Seattle to/from my home on Whidbey Island
and the other via the Gorge to or from Montana. And I knew there were boxes
in Oxbow, but wasn't sure if I should focus there, or catch some
not-to-be-missed beauty. I'm not sure yet if my best time opportunities
will be near to my camp or near to the activites, where I could bail for a
break. It gets kind of tiring standing over paper vats for hours, as much
fun as it is. Thanks, Maiden, for the recommendations.

And I would SO be into a mini-gathering. I don't know what the workshop
schedule is like, but based on years past, I'd be able to do something in
the evenings, or else the Sunday that I leave, since we'll be clearing out
early and I'm in no rush to get back. It's the first Friday-Sunday in
August.

I'm really looking forward to taking in some of your treasures. Y'all are
so friendly and cool.
