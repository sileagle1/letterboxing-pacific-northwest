Our 10yr anniversary at the beach.......

From: nikanderfamily (blancoandyou@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-29 16:59:36 UTC
My dh and I headed to Lincoln City without the kids this weekend. So
I carved a new sign in stamp for just us and headed out with high
hopes of finding many boxes.
We started with Devils Lake but were unable to locate it. We
thought we were in the right place but we could have been wrong.
We then headed to We Love Lincoln City which was probably our
easiest find since we started letterboxing and being 8 months
pregnant I needed that about now. Very hard to be discreet though but
we did a good job. When we found it we could see it even before we
got to close so we hide it better from the crowds by placing it it in
a little farther.
On we went to Boiler Bay. Again a success. We are liking these
cause of the ease of the find but this one was even harder to be
discreet. We were happy that the kids weren't with us since they make
it almost impossible to be very discreet.
Then off we went to Devils Punch Bowl and were finally rewarded
with Paisley Orca's creation. It's alive and well.
We then went to Beneath the Blue # 2 at Beverely Beach. We looked
and looked and finally gave up. We later remembered some extra clues
we had gotten and went back later and had success. This makes 2 out
of the 4 of this series for us. Thank you Funhog.
We kept going and ended up at the Yaquina Bay Lighthouse. We
searched and search and could not find this on. We are almost certain
we were in the right spot on this on.
We then decided to call it a day cause I was starting to feel a lot
of baby activity and I didn't want the baby at the beach.
Then on the way home we decided to see if we could find the Van
Duzer Corridor one. Unfortunatly we came up empty handed on that one
too. We again are pretty sure we were in the right spot. Paisley Orca
if you would like to know if this one and the lighthouse one might
just have been missed by us or if they are gone please e-mail me
blancoandyou@yahoo.com and we will describe were we thought it was. I
hope they aren't cause we like your stamps.
Sorry it got lengthy....I love hearing how people have done and I
hope there are those of you that do too. We are so happy we found
this sport and are always excited to hear the news of how it is going
with everyone. Thanks again for all those that place boxes. We hope
to do that next summer.
