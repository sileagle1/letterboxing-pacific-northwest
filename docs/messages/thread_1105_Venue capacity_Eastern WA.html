I, for one, prefer boxes placed at least ten minutes apart. I'm always trying to use
letterboxing as an excuse for excercise. In the cold weather finding boxes placed
close together can be downright unpleasant since there is no time to warm up
walking from one to the next. However, I do know that some folks would rather find a
bunch than walk a couple of miles. To each his own...Funhog
