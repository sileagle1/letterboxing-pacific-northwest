From: Jen Wintersgill (jenwin@shaw.ca) | Date: 2007-01-24 11:43:19 UTC-08:00
Red Soup here in British Columbia Canada. We have been boxing since November.

When we are out and about, the Red Soup team consists of me Jen, my fianc Mitch and sometimes my daughters Katie and Emily.

Online, Red Soup is almost 100% me (Jen). I'm the one that does the recording and finding of clues, the others come along for the hunt. I'm also the one who does the postals.

I think the girls will pick there own names this summer when we can box outside again properly.

I have several letterboxing dreams, I would love to take a road trip throughout the PNW States and go boxing, I would love to go to Stone Mountain, and I would really LOVE to go to Dartmoor Park in England.

Happy Boxing,

Red Soup
