From: Sam (singing_wolf@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-01-25 08:10:12 UTC-08:00
Hi everyone!

I'm back! (What? You didn't know I was gone? Hey!)

After a semester in beautiful (unless you're freezing your butt off)
Boston, I'm finally home in Portland. If you ever get out that way, I
highly recommend it, and not just for the letterboxing. Though the
letterboxing is outstanding! There are some wonderful indoor boxes in
Boston proper, perfect for winter or for tourists, since they're in
touristy places. Also places with much security, so it's an adventure
not getting arrested (as I almost did), or seriously questioned about
terrorist activity (as I did, by a very scary woman - use caution with
letterboxes hidden inside buildings where you have to pass through metal
detectors and past armed guards before skulking around in dark stairwells).

My one regret is that I didn't get a chance to hide a letterbox. I
didn't get my carving tools sent out to me until the last few weeks, and
sadly, finals took precedence. They also sucked every waking minute.
In that vein, if you've sent me a private email in the last month, I'm
not ignoring you. It's in my inbox, along with the hundreds of other
emails I'm currently sorting through. If you don't feel like waiting
patiently, feel free to email me again, I check new ones first, before
going back to the beginning, just in case.

I missed you all, and am looking forward to getting back to soggy boxing
here! If anyone wants to go with me, I'd love it, I've had enough of
solo boxing for now. :)

Singing Wolf
