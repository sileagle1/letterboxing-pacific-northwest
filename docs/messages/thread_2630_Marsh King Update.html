Marsh King Update

From: Lisa Harkins (lisah@whidbey.com) | Date: 2006-09-04 23:03:51 UTC-07:00
Hi everyone,

This is to let you all know that I have retired my first letterbox, the
Marsh King (Spokane, WA) so if you are holding clues, please discard them.

I have had a few missing reports, so we checked on it this weekend. It was
right where it should be. We originally planted it off a small side trail
in Liberty Lake County Park. Since then, it appears the park has placed
some logs and brush in an effort to prevent use of the side trail.
Therefore, I have pulled the box so that I don't contribute to the further
damage they are apparently trying to prevent. I may or may not find a new
home for it sometime in the future.

Thanks!

Thunderbird
