From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-26 03:51:26 UTC
Yep, I figured it was about time for something in the Puget Sound
region again. Personally, I've been swamped with LIFE these past 18
months (especially) so I haven't been able to put anything together.
I'm still swamped with LIFE (in many facets) but we recently learned
that we'll be moving in a few months and I wanted an opportunity to
meet folks from the area before we depart and while folks are still in
the post-summer letterboxing mode. ;) We'll try to schedule another
event before we pack up and drive away (kicking & screaming the entire
way!!!) but that'll happen most likely during the 'rainy' season so I
wanted to do something before the 'weather' sets in and it's still
enjoyable to go out and actually hunt letterboxes.

My work schedule is absolutely MAD leading up to and immediately after
this get-together so I apologize in advance that I won't be able to
go 'all out' for this. There are plenty of boxes in the park
(potentially a few more if folks want to plant some in advance of the
event) and the region and I figure folks would be focused on meeting
some of the other area boxers and enjoying the opportunity to hunt for
a handful of boxes. If there's any interest in a quick carving class,
compass/orienteering class or possibly a journal making class, pipe up
and we can try to coordinate something. Otherwise we'll focus on fun,
folk, food and boxing. ;)

dvn2r ckr
