From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-03-31 04:58:52 UTC
Well the formerly mentioned box because of the nifty area it takes
you through.

The Carkeek Park boxes because they have a variety of fun stamps. I
think 8 all together.

A mysterious, if not fishy, box in a park in the city (you will have
to figure out where), because of the wonderous trees that are there.

Kubota Gardens because of Kubota Gardens.

These 4 venues will take you to two quadrants of the city so the
first two could be done together, the second two ditto.

These are just in the city. I won't brag on the boxes on the East
side ( cough cough)
