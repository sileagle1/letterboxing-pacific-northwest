Re: [LbPNW] holy grail letterbox

From: Clio Mouse (cliomouse@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-09 08:26:43 UTC-07:00
I don't know about one that everybody thinks is the "Holy Grail" of letterboxing, but I know there's been a few that seemed that way to just me. That's usually because of situations surrounding the box, though. For one, my friend and I had hiked miles and miles, even going past the same places several times, trying to find features mentioned in the clues. We finally gave up after an entire day and I e-mailed the placer. It turns out the box was hidden under a rock/tree feature we thought was so cool we sat on it and took pictures of each other. We were actually SITTING on the box and went on to hike another several miles beyond that. By the time we went back to find it again (the third time, by this time) it had gone missing. We actually have fun memories of it, though!

ClioMouse
