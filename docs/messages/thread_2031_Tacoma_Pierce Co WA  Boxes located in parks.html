Hey..heads up!

If you have a box located in a Tacoma/Pierce Co. park you may want to
check on it. Yesterday (Saturday) was the Annual Park's Appreciation
Day and many parks were scoured by volunteers picking up trash, doing
maintenence and landscaping. I would hope that since this is the
third annual one and many boxes have survived in years past that
there would not be an issue but one never knows. I began thinking
about this when I saw that Titlow Park was on the front page of the
local section today with photos of volunteers rebuilding hillside
trails ruined by BMX bikers. I thought..omigosh...letterboxes in
other parks could be found!! AKK!!

This annual event is held every April (generally the third weekend),
during previous years it was limited to a couple parks in the Tacoma
system but they have really expanded it and the County has joined in
on the program too. I know that Pt.Defiance was absolutely crawling
with volunteers doing clean up since it's the 100th birthday of it
this year. In years past (this is the first year we've missed it)
volunteers concentrate on one park and comb it for garbage pick-up,
spread gravel/bark on trails, trim bushes and such. Now it is open
to "groups" who want to focus on a park of their choice so many
churches and organizations "adopt" a park for the day and concentrate
on all kinds of mainentance.

So..it's a nice day in Tacoma/Pierce Co if you have a box you may
want to go check on it! Especially if it's located in
Pt.Defiance..I'm going to go check on some of dvn2r ckr's today since
she is on the other side of the ocean.
