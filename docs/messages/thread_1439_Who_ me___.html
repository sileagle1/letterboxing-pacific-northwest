As much as I love getting all the compliments, I have to say that the day at Wake
Robin would never been half as much fun without all the people who were there. I'm
sure the newbies had a blast and your presence is what made it extra special.

When I first heard that Campfire Lady and Pandora had enrolled in this class I was
afraid that I would have little to offer them. Both are already skilled at stamp carving.
Pandora has been producing amazing works of stamp artistry in recent weeks.

feedback on my class. Fortunately for me, neither had made any stitched books
before. So I was able to introduce then to something entirely new. Phew!!!

So, many thanks for the kudos and thanks for help send the newbies off with a great
impression of our strange little world of letterboxing. Funhog
