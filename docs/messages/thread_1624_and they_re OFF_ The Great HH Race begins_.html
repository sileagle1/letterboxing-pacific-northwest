Yesterday, in Philadelphia, I picked up 9 hitchhikers bound for
Seattle and the start of the GREAT HITCHHIKER RACE! Today, Sunday
September 19th the hitchhikers "caught" rides with 9 letterboxes in
the Seattle area. None of these are mystery boxes, and I think the
longest hike is about 3 miles round-trip (if you do
the whole series). They are all on the Western side of Lake
Washington and they range from the Shoreline neighborhood in the
North to Des Moines in the South. All of these hitchhikers are in a
RACE to reach the East Coast, specifically the South Mountain
Hitchhiker Hostel, in the South Mountain Nature Preserve in Allentown
PA.

First out of the gate was LANCE ARMSTRONG! at 8:32am
PST

Second was BY LEAPS AND BOUNDS at 9:38am PST

Third in the lineup TURBO SLUG at 10:05am PST

Fourth was CAPTAIN TERROR 10:29am PST

Fifth and following closely was SPEED RACER 10:32am PST

Sixth was DALE EARNHARDT 11:24am PST

Seventh, BON JOVI 11:57am PST

Eighth and breathing hard was ON THE RUN FROM MASSACHUSETTS 12:36pm
PST


and bringing up the rear in

Ninth place, WWI FLYING ACE at 1:11pm PST

If and when you find any of these Hitchhikers PLEASE contact the
placer in the log book, notify the Yahoo groups for letterbox
travellers OR you can contact me, Amanda from Seattle.

We'll be tracking the progress of the Hitchhikers across the country
at:

http://greathhrace.blogspot.com

ALSO VERY IMPORTANT! Do not put these Hitchhikers into Postal
Letterboxes! We also frown upon mailing the hitchhikers to your
friends. They should be carried bodily from place to place.
Thanks so much. These hitchhikers represent some really great
carvings from East Coast letterboxers!

-Amanda from Seattle
