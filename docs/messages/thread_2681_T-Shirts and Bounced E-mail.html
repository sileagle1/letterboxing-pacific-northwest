The saying is a good one. So . . . I guess you guys decided that
advertising letterboxing is ok? I've had several people on the trail ask
about the LbNA logo on my pack. There's been a few times where I felt it
was in the best interest of the hobby (like when we're in the vicinity of a
letterbox) to tell them it stands for "Little Birds of North America".
Ha-Ha! afterwards, Wood-aug pulls my leg - asking "where's your
binoculars?" Guess answering it with "Little Birds of the Pacific NW" is
out with the word "letterboxing" on the back, huh? Have to get more
creative . . . or maybe wear a shirt over it.

Sounds like things are getting firmed up! I'm anxious to see what they come
out looking like.
Thank you so much for the kind generosity of your time and effort for this
cool project.

Hope your Thanksgiving is filled with blessings!
Kurious Jo
