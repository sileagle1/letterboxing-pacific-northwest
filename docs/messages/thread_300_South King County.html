South King County

From: Fudrick (fudrick@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-06-07 22:26:27 UTC-07:00
Coming home from a meeting, stopped by Dash and Brown Point. The weather was hot, the sun was out, as was the tide. At Dash Point, people were way out there, digging up interesting items, pypo boarding, and enjoying the sun. The Beach Box is right where it should be, away from prying eyes.

Brown Point is in good shape, especially if you are not compass challenged. Another beautiful place to watch the boats go by, very relaxing.

Previously had gotted the Campfire at Dash, was in good shape, with the Lost Hiker. The Anniversary Box at Saltwater was there earlier this year, but was Feeling Sluggish.

If you are heading up or down the I-5 corridor, this is a very rewarding detour.
