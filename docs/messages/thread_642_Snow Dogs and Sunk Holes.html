Snow Dogs and Sunk Holes

From: Fudrick (fudrick@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-08-25 23:01:28 UTC-07:00
Was in Seattle last evening, stopped on the way to pet the last two Snow Dogs, all was well.

Stopped by a place that I would never have guessed existed, Hylebos SP. Fantastic place, very peaceful and unique. Found the 'W'easel, great location. The Sink Hole was not to be found. Ready to claim I had dropped my pen thru the crack on the platform, layed down and felt and looked under the appropriate plank, and the others that were open to looking, but nothing. Must have fell in the Sink Hole (didn't see it floating there :-(

Even so, a enjoyable time in two great parks, Thanks!

Fudrick
