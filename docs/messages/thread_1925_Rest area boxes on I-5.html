Hey, we tried to find these four rest-stop letterboxes the other day
and only were successfull with two of them. The one at Valley of
Rogue was nowhere to be found unless a burrowing animal took it to
parts unknown. Moon Goddess and Dig Dog were there and we were
completely lost on Baseball Goddess as we never found the 'log' over
which to climb--we tried to look for a box underneath EVERY bent
tree within that stretch of woodland we could find but to no avail.
We found a spot that looked 'applicable' but no box beneath the
bend. Lots of 'used' TP piles all around. Then, along the 3 hour
subsequent drive home we wondered if perhaps the clues were intended
for the rest stop on the Southbound side of I-5? Not sure but that
may be the case? Otherwise, there was a bunch of 'forestry' work
that had occurred on the Northbound side and we're not sure if that
totally changed the environment to the point that the clues no
longer work. The stamps we did find were awesome--as usual with
Rambopupp's work!!!
