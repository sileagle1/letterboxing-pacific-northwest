Near Cougar Mountain, in Issaquah there is a Qdoba Mexican place that
Ryan and I stop at often after hiking in the North Bend/Issaquah area.
It is in the shopping center off East Lake Sammamish Parkway. Take
that exit off of I-90.

If you are driving around 405 towards Renton and the Airport, you can
get off at exit #5 Sunset Blvd, turn west onto E. Park Drive, turn
right onto Lk. Washington Blvd N., turn left at the next intersection
into Gene Coulon Memorial Beach Park in Renton. There are a couple of
letterboxes at this park and there is also an Ivar's for seafood and a
Kidd Valley for burgers Both are Pacific Northwest chains, unique to
this area! And the Kidd Valley milkshakes are terrific!!

Bai Tong Thai on International Drive (hwy 99) near the airport is a
good place for Thai.
