I have a sad, funny story for you about this ethereal 'place'. Back in
Aug/Sep 2000, we rerouted a cross-country trip about 1,000 miles of our
way just to go to this spot--TRKEE's idea as it's right up his Star
Trek fanatic ally. We show up to the area early one morning and the
place is closed for 'maintenance' for 2 days--something they said that
occurs once every decade or so. Talk about a big bummer. Ever since I
saw these and Green Tortuga's clues explode into space I knew we just
had to return to the spot. It's just not all that easy to get to--and
after our first 'frustrating attempt' I fear that we'll pick the
next '2 day maintenance' period they choose for the day we reattempt a
visit. ;) They sound like wonderful boxes! Can't wait to seek them
out!
