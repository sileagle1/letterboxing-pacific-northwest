Them are ring neck ducks. There should be Wood Duck in this
environment with the nest boxes all around the marsh.

Why they were not named ring billed ducks is one of those cruel
things done by Ornithologists.

I visited your three new boxes at Mc Lean Creek and Nail Heads Bonus
box.

Bravo the two of you. It was a perfectly wonderful day. Warm and
balmy. The woods alive with birds, including Common Yellowthroat
warblers just in from the south.

The "Fish" box is a classic Trail and Error box and the minute I saw
the spot I knew it had to be it.

I feel mighty pleased over that.
