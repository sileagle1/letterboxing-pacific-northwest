From: cdagse (canadiangirl@email.com) | Date: 2004-10-17 23:02:04 UTC

So I headed out today to grab 4 of the 8 boxes in the Spokane
area:)....All are alive and well:

Snow Days (great spot, nice view)

Pine Rock (still there, hope the neighbours were not watching)

100 Years at Montrose (great clues, nice view)

Forever in Bloom (good clues, quick grab)

Note I found the "E" hitch hiker in the Forever in Bloom box however
folks appeared to stop indicating where it was found and hid in
June/July.....so I am not sure how it got to Spokane WA from the
Western WA area....it has gone as far as Shasta though...so the Nail
family it has been travelling! Just a reminder to make sure to
document where hitch hikers are found and dropped off....and by whom:)

Also Mouse in the House is alive and well.

Canada Goose
