From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2003-07-22 14:23:00 UTC-04:00
Der Mad Stamper appears to be the ultimate mystery box. No one has been able
to locate him for a long time. I think the general consensus is that we
should divide up and maintain his boxes until such time as he (hopefully!)
reappears.

I had assumed you could use "Search for Boxes" to find his boxes (for placer,
choosing DerMadStamper) --but I just tried it--and the only boxes that showed
up are those already adopted (in WA). All his unclaimed boxes are currently
listed under the placer "Abandonedbox." If you are familiar with the name of
a Stamper box, you could find it thru Abandonedbox--otherwise, you'll have to
go the longer route Susan described in her e-mail (titled What happened to Der
Mad Stamper?).

--Wild Woman

From: Mark (nomad_pnw@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-07-22 22:14:33 UTC
The last time I saw Mitch (Der Mad Stamper) was at the 1st Portland
Letterboxers get together a couple years ago. At that time he was
just finishing up his degree at the Rock Creek campus, and I had
heard he was offered and had accepted a web developer position at one
of the east side colleges. I believe he had plans to move his family
to that side of the city, Gresham, if memory serves. I can't
remember which college it was, maybe Mt. Hood Community
College, ...maybe not.

At any rate, I'm pretty good at using the Internet to find people so
let me give it a whirl and see what I can find. If I find him, I'll
ask him to make a guest appearance or send an email stating his
wishes with his boxes. If I can't find him or he doesn't respond, I
say we divvy the boxes up among many to keep them alive at least.

One suggestion when this whole subject of Abandoned Boxes came up,
was to credit the creator of the box(es) and also state our contact
info. for the maintainer. I like that approach and it gives credit
where due.

What exactly is the process to adopt another's box? I've sent a
couple emails to the folks managing the Yahoo Group and LbNa Web
Site, but have received no responses.
