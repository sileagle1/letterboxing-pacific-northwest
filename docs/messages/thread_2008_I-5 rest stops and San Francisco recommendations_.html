I just came from the San Francisco area, after going on a road trip to
visit family and friends. I think it's probably further out of your
way than you were wanting to go, but I placed a couple of boxes while
I was there. One is in Berkeley, at Tilden Park east of the
university, and two are in the same place on Hwy 101 just north of the
Russian River near Hopland. (Those two are really easy to get from the
highway...it's just a turnout off the road!) I'm hoping some people
will find these soon so I know if my directions actually lead you to
the boxes, or if I made some big mistake!
