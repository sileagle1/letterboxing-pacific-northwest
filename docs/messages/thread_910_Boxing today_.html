Well, Campfire lady and my clan decided to do some boxing today with Sleepy
Whippet, Pandora, Iron Bear and Wingnut. They brought along Bookworm,
Transplanted Cowboys and See Shore. It was a beautiful day the the Pittock Mansion.
Great stamps there for the Haunted Series. Everyone got a bit frustrated
looking for Sleight. We gave up on that one. We then decided to go with Pandora
and Iron Bear to find the Lost Letterbox and who do we come across, but
Stargazer Woman and her friend Dopey (a first timer). This was our first time at
meeting someone on the trail. Then I find my 1st Hitchhiker. While on the way
home following Pandora and Iron Bear, a woman in a huge car, and I drive a huge
van decided to side swipe me. But this old Willow with her quick driving
skills swerved, as I did this she saw what she was about to do and she swerved,
and went to the right very quickly and across the street she goes into a pole
and almost a wall. What a sight. It got my heart pumping. Luckily everyone
was alright. But she has a huge dent to fix. We always seem to have something
interesting happen to us while letterboxing. Willow.
