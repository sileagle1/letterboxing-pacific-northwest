Hi everyone,
My kids and I traveled to Idaho Falls, ID last week and had alot of
LD fun along the way. I am happy to report "Let'er Buck" is Alive and
Well. It had to go to lunch with us though. Some people decided to
sit right next to the hiding spot after we finished stamping in. I
think it enjoied the company though because we were only the 2nd to
stamp in. Great place to stop and go for a little stroll and work out
the sore behind from the drive.
Second box was "Welcome to Idaho" It is also alive and well. We
were the first to find that one. One of the cleanest rest stops I
have ever been to.
Third stop was the "birds of prey" LB in Boise. It too was alive
and well. We arrived to late to see the birds though. That was enough
for one day so we headed to the motel to swim and get pizza.
Bright and early the next morning we traveled to Star, ID to visit
a fun litte hometown waterhole on the river. The kids had great fun
exploring under the bridge. Then we were on our way to Idaho Falls.
We stopped on the way at Craters of the Moon to explore the lava
tube. It was a GRAND ADVENTURE for all of us. We also left a few LB
friends behind. (look for clues in the near future)
After a busy weekend at my sister-in -laws wedding we were off
hunting again today. Found "the Spirit of the West" alive and well.
Several geocachers had logged in also. We also hid the "Idaho Falls
LB" near by.(clues to be posted soon)
Well now I must go to bed. Getting up early and heading to Jackson
Hole and will search for several more boxes. I am taking my in-laws
with and hope to get them interested.

Heather
