From: IronBear (ironbear@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-08-30 20:56:51 UTC-07:00
Good evening fellow boxers.

As Der Mad Stamper alluded to already today - the Passport to Letterboxing
event had is fair share of surprises. One of those surprises being some very
interesting and romantic notes in a few boxes that were already established
on websites before the event. Upon returning home Sunday pandora and I got
an itching for mystery clues because this note in particular was truly
something to behold. Therefore we decided to crunch down and work out a list
of mystery boxes and set out on a journey today. However we had no idea
where that journey would take us. I am happy to report that Funhogs Ace of
Fun is alive and well. Also Maidens Horsin around Clackamas box -though not
a mystery box - is alive and well though the logbook needs a little TLC.
Unfortunately what we experienced before that was something most unexpected.
The mysterious Cloakedletterboxer provided us with a wonderful journey -
shrouded in mystery and romance of a day long past it seems. So upon finding
the first box it become overly apparent that our journey into the
Cloakedletterboxers world is far from over so we spent most of the day doing
our best to help her find her long lost love. We returned home jubilant and
yet sorrowful. There is no question that we long to return to her next
mystery very soon.

If you have not attempted any mystery boxes I certainly encourage you to do
so - it is one of the more enjoyable aspects of this Hobby.

I bid you all well.

Iron`Bear
