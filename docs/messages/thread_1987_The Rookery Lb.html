Howdy all, I do hope everyone has gotten a chance
to enjoy the warm, dry weather of late. Just this
afternoon, we headed to Vancouver, WA to have a
look at the heron rookery by Vancouver Lake. If

time to take a jaunt over there. The nests are filled
with birds, and the trees don't quite have their
leaves yet. In a few more days, after the trees leaf
out, it'll be pretty difficult to see all the avian activity.
