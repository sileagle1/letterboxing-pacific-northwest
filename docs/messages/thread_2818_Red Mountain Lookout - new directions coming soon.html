Red Mountain Lookout - new directions coming soon

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-07-29 05:35:54 UTC
Those of you who have met Heyokah Man know he has been bitten really
REALLY bad the by the LB bug - he was thwarted last weekend by the
setting sun and a gated road to my Red Mountain Lookout box, so he
goes up there this weekend and hikes all the way around onto the
Pacific Crest Trail and up there that way. He kindly updated me on
the condition of the road and the tower, took photos, and has agreed
to provide hiking directions. The road is gated now because of safety
concerns since mother nature took the roof off the tower, and although
one could hike up the road beyond the gate, it will be a much more
beautiful hike via the PCT. So watch for new hiking directions soon -
I will post them alongside the original driving directions. He says
he thinks I am now the owner of my very first (and likely only)
extreme letterbox, but it is still there safe and sound - woo-hoo!
Thanks HM, you are a true friend.

From: (KuriousJo@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-08-01 00:57:34 UTC
Just a comment. In June when we went up there it was gated but not locked. The gate was only closed because a tree had landed on the hinged part of the gate in such a way that the gravity kept the gate closed. Maybe they've locked it since then?
On the other hand we wouldn't recommend driving up that road unless you have a true 4 wheel drive, are into mud and scratched paint (I imagine the snow is melted by now (?)), and have a satelite phone to get out if you get stuck. Cell phone service was non-exixtent. We were able to drive within a mile of the top but I simply didn't have the stamina to hike it.

Funhog - if you're going to hike to this from the OR PCT allow extra time to get from OR to WA. Tee hee.

BTW - there was a Don and Gwen sighting this weekend! Very fun!!

Taa-taa,
Kurious Jo
