LadyB, I think that is a great idea! I love Enchanted Forest and go
as often as I can -- taking my nephew every year is my excuse right
now!

Just suggestions (maybe you already know where you're planting): I
don't know how well you know EF, but I would suggest planting
somewhere along the first part (the old part) where there are plaster
displays of fairy tales. Kids don't linger over those, but it would
be easy for LBer's to linger pretending to look at them as they wait
for people to clear out.

Planting by the Old Women's Shoe would be bad, as that is very
popular.
