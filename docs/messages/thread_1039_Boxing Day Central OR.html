Boxing Day Central OR

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-12-29 01:20:06 UTC
This year, Boxing Day was spent doing what else?... I was lucky enough
to be in Central OR and met up with the Lennens 3, the Fleshmans plus
Mom and Dad to form an intrepid troop of nine letterboxers! We went
for Birder's Phoebe Pond series and were successful on all but one.
We're hoping that our crawling and poking was just in the wrong spot
for the elusive one and the box is still awaiting discovery. Not only
were we rewarded with finding the other letterboxes but scat and
skulls figured prominently in the finds of the day.

On Saturday, I set out alone to track down the Fleshman's Tetherow
Crossing letterbox. I now have yet another of John's charming stamps
in my collection. As snowflakes drifted down, I left a new letterbox
to commemorate the historic Tetherow House. I was also lucky enough to
receive a private invitation to visit a limited edition Christmas 2003
letterbox while I was in that part of the state. What an honor!

Here's hoping everyone else had a wonderful Boxing Day. Funhog
