Ryan and I decided to get outside on this dreary, rainy day. We hiked
up to the Bullitt Fireplace on Squak Mountain and I left a letterbox
up there. It is about 5 miles round-trip. But the elevation gain is
only 900 ft or so and it is a very nice gradual climb up. (took us
about 2 1/2 hours for the whole trip) The Central Peak trail is
supposed to have views of Seattle (on a clear day). Practically no
parking at the trailhead, which explains why these trails don't get as
much use as the ones next door at Cougar Mountain. Ryan and I saw 2
other hikers on this day. On a sunny weekend, I can see that it might
be much busier!

The Bullitt Fireplace Slug letterbox clue is available at Atlas Quest.
