From: Pungent Bob (PungentBob@HotPOP.com) | Date: 2005-08-04 22:32:18 UTC
I managed to get out a couple of times this week working on secret
missions and other stuff. I fought with gorillas, braved the heat,
evaded a nutria with a gun, took on blackberries, and ran directly
into a tree. Running into the tree wasn't as bad of a thing as you
might expect, as my dog was with me and apparently he sniffed out a
letterbox while I was dazed. Once I came to my senses I saw that (and
this is the cool part) Rex toted it (cause that dog is usually pretty
worthless) right over to a nice place to sit and stamp in. Oh yeah,
that $100 bill is still out there awaiting some clever boxer.
