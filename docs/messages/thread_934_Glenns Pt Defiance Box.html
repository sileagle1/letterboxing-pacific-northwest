Glenn, I had to go back and review my log book. I remember that box
precisely based on my notes. Amazing that I could recall it once I
read my book. I didnt remember the stamp as much as the Bald Eagle
that distracted my proper navigation. Darn birds.

I remember that long ago 4th of July weekend where I snagged 30+
boxes including my first Glenn and T&E experience at Pt Defiance. I
was a Green as Grass newbie and learned a lot from those four daze of
boxing.

For you new folks, the Pt Defiance boxes are a great weekend or two
of boxing. A grand venue with some most excellent adventures set up
primarily by Glenn and T&E ( visit their web site linked in Glenns
post for clues). The best forest you will find in the Puget Lowlands
some of these trees are massive.

It is an education in cluesmanship. You will learn all is not as it
seems, that you think outside of the box and that correct spelling is
not as important as paying attention as you go from here and there.
Some are simply simple others, well if they were all easy it would
not be fun.

Put on your thinking caps. Enjoy the venue.
