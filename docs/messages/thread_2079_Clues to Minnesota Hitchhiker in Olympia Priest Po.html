Hello, all,

I am only posting the clues to this hitchhiker on this site. This
hitchiker was picked up in MN when I was there for my brother's
funeral. It is ironically called the "Good Luck Hitchhiker"...Sigh.
(Bet you're all wondering how it was I came to find a hitchhiker in MN
while there for such a hard time. It was in a box I planted just a
skip from my sister's back door. I took a little stroll for some
breathing time and checked on it.) The hitchhiker itself is not inside
a letterbox because I wanted a seasoned letterboxer to find it.
However, the starting point is one of the boxes in the park-

Good Luck Hitchhiker:
Head West from the Swamp Gnome. When your footsteps crunch (but before
they slurp) turn north. Find the breaker wall. There is a fallen tree
that crosses it about 6-8 feet from where you encounter the wall. The
tree is covered in ivy. Approach the tree from the south, step up and
reach around in the ivy on the top of the trunk until you find the
hitchhiker. It is double bagged. When you find it, would you mind
letting dropping me a line? That way I won't wonder if I need to check
on it over time.

Regards~ Kristi
