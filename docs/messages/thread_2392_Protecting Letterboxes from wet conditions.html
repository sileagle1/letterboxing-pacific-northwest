We have found something that we hope will work well when we try it. We
bought some small wide mouth water bottles that we have covered with
black duct tape to camoflouge them and will be placing our stamps in
these when we get back to boxing again (in better weather). They sell
them in the $1 area at Target and we bought a bunch when they
clearanced them off for .25 a piece a few months ago. I noticed the
other day that they have them for sale again (although they're at a $1
a piece right now, which still isn't a bad price for a good container).
