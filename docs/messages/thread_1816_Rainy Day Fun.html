From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-12-22 01:37:41 UTC

Just got home from the perfect wet weather
letterbox! We headed out to find Jay's Toolbox
and I have to say that this is one of the most
clever letterboxes I have found. We took the
entire box back to our car and stamped in
while keeping everything perfectly dry. With
all the goodies that have been added to this
box, the process is pretty time-consuming
but the collection created by so many carvers
is a treasure. If you haven't found this one
yet, you're in for a treat! Funhog
