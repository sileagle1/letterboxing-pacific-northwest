> Spinach Artichoke Dip
>
> 1 (14oz) can of Artichoke Hearts, drained & chopped
> (I used the packed in water artichokes not marinated in olive oil--
> but you can use either type)
> 3/4 cup mayonnaise (I used Best Foods/Hellman's type)
> 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (I used parmesan reggiano from
Costco-
> -I grated it right before I cooked it)
> 1 teaspoon lemon juice
> dash garlic powder (I used garlic salt--would've used fresh garlic
> if I had some on hand--that's life after being on vacation for
> several weeks!)
> about 6 dashes of Tabasco sauce
> about 1-1/2 cup of chopped fresh spinach (about 1/2 inch square
> pieces)
>
> Preheat oven to 350*. Mix all of the ingredients (you can mash the
> artichoke hearts with a fork if you like) and spread in a baking
> dish. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Serve
> hot or cold with crackers, vegetable dippers or bread.
>
> **You can also microwave it--prepare it the same way but cook it in
> the microwave on medium for 4 to 6 minutes until it's thoroughly
> heated, stirring once halfway through cooking.**
>
>
> Enjoy!
