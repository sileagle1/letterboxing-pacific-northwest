Last weekend was a springlike mixture in Central Oregon: drizzle, snow, grey skies and blazing sun. The desert
wildflowers are just beginning; Yellow Bells and Sand Lilies abound. On Sunday, it was perfect weather for going
over Misery Ridge at Smith Rock State Park, still hard work but no killer heat to make it really unpleasant. All
this is encouragement to get thee over the big hill and letterboxing in Central Oregon.

I checked on four of my boxes while I was there: Crooked River Ranch #1, #2 and #4 as well as the Mariposa
Lily box at Smith Rock. All are alive and have been visited at least once but are positively begging for
attention. It seem that one of these boxes it mighty popular with geocachers but their signatures are not
nearly as exciting as a rubber stamp. Please don't make me grovel.... Funhog
