Since those two weekends will get the most people out, how about two
gatherings? They both don't have to involve extravagant feasts. When
Patrick Delavariensis was visiting, there was a spurofthemoment Super Box
Sunday gathering. Thirteen boxers came out to meet Patrick, and a fun time
was had. A camping weekend on the 12th and 13th, with all the trimmings,
and then on the 23rd or 24th a designated time and place to gather without
the trimmings. That way those of us who can not make the big one can still
meet the people behind the stamps. It's not as if letterboxing was
something we don't enjoy doing :-)
