Thanks to everyone who gave me ideas on boxes to-and-from Diamond
Lake. Because of the snow, most were unavailable. But I did find one
and gave it some TLC, was full of water. I've posted 3 pictures from
the trip - what a beautiful place. We visited Crater Lake too. Not
sure how many feet of snow there, but there was a lot. Wizard Island
is amazing.

I planted a couple boxes at Diamond Lake (where the snow was only a
foot or so in some places). I'll get them posted as soon as I can get
the clues written.

Diamond Lake is a must-see place that everyone should visit, sooner or
later.
