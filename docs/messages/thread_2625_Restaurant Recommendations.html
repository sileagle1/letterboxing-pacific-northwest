Here's some from Wisconsin for Washington. I enjoy going to "I Love
Pho" on Mercer Island--tiny place, but I like eating outside. It is
fun to drive around the island and there are some boxes there, too.
In Bellingham, I had my first experience with tapas at "Flats Tapas
Bar". Two thumbs up from Mike and I who have widely divergent taste
in food.
