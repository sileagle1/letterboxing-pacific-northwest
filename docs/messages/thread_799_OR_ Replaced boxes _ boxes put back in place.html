The Department of Redundancy Dept. LB is back by popular demand! It seems
appropriate that this LB in particular had to be replaced, does it not? Anyway,
it is now in a new, improved location, and I thank all who suggested I
replace it. Believe me, I would not have tackled all those letters again w/o your
urging. (If you've already gotten the first stamp, it's not really worth your
while to go back for this one. Having run out of creativity, I simply carved
a copy.) I'll hope it doesn't go missing again, and also wish that it stays
in place.

Also, the "Mouth" box of the "Making Faces" series has been replaced. Many
thanks to Heather who sat and carved the replacement mouth stamps right there
near the trident tree, while I sought a better hiding place and ran around
keeping track of various wandering children. Happily, we'd received "Protection"
before going after the box (making us 3 for 3 on Sleepywhippet's new boxes).

Happy boxing to all!
