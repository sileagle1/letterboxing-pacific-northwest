A boxing we will do this weekend!!

From: Cherie Mortazavi (cheriescorner@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-02-22 02:52:38 UTC
Well it all started out at the Spagettti Factory in Vancouver where
we met Bindle Babe from Michigan. We were joined by Maiden, Tooth
Fairy, Jay Jeopardy, Dad, Bookworm, Sleepy Whippet, Campfire Lady
and Cat's Meow. It was a great night. I got razzed because I did
not make an event stamp. So of course this caused me to wake up in
the night several times. I thought about what stamp I could
scramble and do the next morning before we set out again with the
same bunch and a few more boxers. I have only carved 2 stamps so
far. I decided on a "Circle of friends" event stamp. And I have to
say I was proud of myself. Campfire Lady came to pick us up and I
asked her to do my writing on it because she has way better hand
writting than poor old Willlow. I will have to get the event stamp
to Bookworm and Sleepy Whippet who did not join us Saturday. We then
went to meet most of the same bunch plus MomNoTime, Mini Bully Mom,
Pupp and Jenn at West Linn to do all of the boxes that were there.
We had a great time and found all that there was to be found. 2
boxes in the Mom, Mexico & Me series were MIA. But talk about some
great carved stamps by these wonderful boxers. It was so nice to
meet a few new friends and box with some old ones. It was a
beautiful day for this hobby and I hope many of you were out as we
were. Willow,Daisey and Heartsey.
