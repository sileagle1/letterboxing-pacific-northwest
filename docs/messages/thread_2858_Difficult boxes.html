Re: Difficult boxes

From: Pungent Bob (PungentBob@HotPOP.com) | Date: 2007-10-22 21:26:30 UTC
Here are a few I thought of off the top of my head that don't
require any strenuous hiking:

Council Crest but only because the tree grew such that getting the
box out was nearly impossible! I think this one has since been moved.

Hidden Dragon. This one is notorious for stumping finders. It took
me three tries.

Spirit of Dartmoor. A classic. It took me forever to figure out the
clues.

As for boxes that are hard due to the hike, there are many but what
really stands out to me are the Dog Mountain boxes. There are so
many on the dog now that it makes the journey well worth it. Of
course what really makes the journey worthwhile is the wildflower
display in the spring. This is a trip that should be done by
everyone who is capable.
