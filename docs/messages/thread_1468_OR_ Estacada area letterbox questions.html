OR: Estacada area letterbox questions

From: browjaso62 (browjaso62@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-27 05:05:35 UTC

Today my wife and I want letterboxing in the Estacada area. We
found a bunch but were stumped by a few of them. I was wondering if
anyone has found the following boxes recently and if so do they have
any advice to help us.

At McIver State Park

Special Delivery
Sunburst
Mini Cooper

And at Timber Park

Whippet Litter - Whippet
Bistro


We also couldn't find the Icebox at Feyrer park but I think it was
because were too tired to figure out the clue.

We did find the Jurasic Park boxes and the county park boxes at
Carver, Barton, and Metzger. Eagle Fern park is for the next couple
of days.

Jason



RE: [LbPNW] OR: Estacada area letterbox questions

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-07-27 17:35:08 UTC-07:00
I was in Estacada this weekend camping and also tried for Whipper Litter and
Bistro. I can guarantee that Bistro is gone, we found the box and I'm
certain I was at the right place for Whippet Litter and the location had
been thoroughly trashed before we got there.

On the upside, we left 14 new boxes in Estacada. All of which are in
fantastic locations. I highly recommend people take a day trip to the
Estacada area!

Maiden
