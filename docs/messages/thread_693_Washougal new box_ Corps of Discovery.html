From: the4boxingbees (the4boxingbees@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-13 05:27:42 UTC
Can it be called a new box if it was out for less than 24 hours and
it was stolen? We put this box out in what we thought was the perfect
spot (if you go there and get to the post look behind you at the knot
in the tree) and we must have been spotted. It was placed Sat evening
and we checked on it Sunday morning. When we checked on it Sun.
evening (newbies do that sorta thing, seperation anxiety) it was
gone! Our first box and it was already gone. That doesn't make you
too confident in your judgement. The place seemed deserted when we
checked on it the first time. When discovered it missing there was
one family, and only one, milling around the park. As we were leaving
rather stunned and angry my wife noticed their teenage daughter
putting our letterbox in the back of their truck. Pretty good (I say
lucky) spotting at 50 yards. I shout "Is that our letterbox?" She
seemed pretty amiable and we do believe that it was her intention to
return it but that she was to shy, but maybe the "DO NOT REMOVE, THIS
IS A LETTERBOX" should have been a clue that we intended it to be
left there. But then they didn't seem the literate sort either. Hope
you can find it. Hope somebody actually looks for it. I hope we are
not a bunch of losers and you guys laugh us off the net. Going back
to that anxiety thing again. Jerry of The 4 B's
