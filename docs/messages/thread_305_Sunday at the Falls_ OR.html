Sunday at the Falls: OR

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-06-09 01:39:21 UTC
Everyone and their grandma decided to take a trip to Silver Falls on
this perfect spring day. It was an amazingly international crowd, too.
We saw Japanese camera-toting tourists, a woman hiking in a sari, a
Russian gal in a dress and there were so many Hispanic folks I took to
saying Hola instead of Hello! The hordes made it lousy for
letterboxing but the trail was as wonderful as ever. Both my boxes
there, Frenchie Falls and Buddah are just fine.

I am still missing Ryan's elusive Twin Falls stamp in my book. I think
I went to the correct stump this time and I am pretty sure that the
box has gone missing. I didn't have the clues to the rest of his boxes
with me and I didn't remember their exact hiding places. So, I am
unsure about the status of his others.

If you intend to letterbox, a midweek trip to this spectacular area is
highly recommended. I don't know if I have ever been to this park on a
sunny, warm day and I have to say that I was overwhelmed by the number
of people out enjoying the falls. Au Revoir, Funhog
