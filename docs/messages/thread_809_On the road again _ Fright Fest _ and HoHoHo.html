Taken directly from the website:

Come one come all! Newbies and veterans alike! Team Rayvenhaus invites you
to the first annual Fall Letterbox Gathering, Fright Fest 2003!

Rayvenhaus is providing the Burgers and the buns (Hamburger buns! Sheesh!
This is a family venue here folks! (Grin!)) and the fixings to go with that,
Ketchup, mustard, mayo, tomatoes and lettuce and my mother is bring her
famous Watergate salad!

All you folks have to do is show up, bring a covered dish and provide your
own refreshments and we'll have a frightfully wonderful day of friendship,
food and letterboxing!

Yes, we are carving a event stamp. (WooHoo!)

Now for the who, what, when, where and why!!!

Who: All letterboxers that wish to attend!

What: The First Annual PNWLb Fright Fest `03!

When: Saturday, Oct 25th, 2003

Where: Wapato Park in Tacoma, Wa.

Why: Why???? Because we love you!!!!!

The address of the park is:

Wapato Park
6500 S. Sheridan
Tacoma, 98408

Coming from North (Seattle, et. al.) travel south on I-5 to exit #129 (72nd
Ave) and proceed to the 72nd Ave crossover, not the 74th. 72nd will take you
under the bridge and back around to you are facing north. Turn to the right,
heading south, so you cross over I-5. Proceed east on 72nd, passing a sign
for Wapato park on the left, to Sheridan Ave. Turn left, North, on Sheridan
and proceed approx 1/2 mile, to the park entrance on the left. Enter the
park and at the roundabout, head right at the start, rounding the roundabout
to the left so you end up heading towards the parking lot to the left of the
entrance. Find a parking spot!

Coming from the South (Portland, et. al.) travel north to exit #129 (72nd
Ave). At the intersection, turn right, heading east. Proceed east on 72nd,
passing a sign for Wapato park on the left, to Sheridan Ave. Turn left,
North, on Sheridan and proceed approx 1/2 mile, to the park entrance on the
left. Enter the park and at the roundabout, head right at the start,
rounding the roundabout to the left so you end up heading towards the
parking lot to the left of the entrance. Find a parking spot!

We're in the main covered area in front of the lake.
