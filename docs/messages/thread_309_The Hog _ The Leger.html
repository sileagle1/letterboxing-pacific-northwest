Crooked River Ranch #1. Like all the 'Hog's boxes, it's located in a
purty, out of the way spot that you'd never find were it not for her
leading you there. Like so many carvers, the silly thing's
embarrassed about her first effort, which is rather crude compared
with her current work. I'd have to call it my favorite find of the
trip, and I found some good boxes out there. History is the creation
of anecdotes and artifacts, and it was a thrill to have the 'Hog's
first effort in my hand. Consider the path that she, and all of us,
have been on since that first bit of innocent dabbling...
