From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-07-30 23:05:15 UTC
If you go over Mt. Hood instead of down I-5 there are
quite a few along the way. I know there are a lot
around Eugene but I have done little boxing
in that area and don't know much about what's down
there. If you cross over at Roseburg, there are several
boxes on that route as well.

Since Crater Lake is a National Park, letterboxes are
verboten. If there are any other boxes in that park,
they are likely to be ones that have only word of mouth
clues. I am surprised that the Phantom Ship is still listed
publicly. Down towards Klamath Falls on Highway 97
there used to be a box at Collier State Park but I don't
know about its current status.

South Central Oregon has a distinct shortage of letterboxes.
Perhaps this will be more of a planting opportunity than a
finding adventure. I suppose this is just about no help at all
but good luck and have fun! Funhog
