LB in Eagle Fern Park

From: shebearof3 (thyost@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-02-17 20:19:30 UTC

Tuesday I had a wonderful frosty morning hiking through Eagle Fern
Park with a fun swine. The shadows were frosty with ice sicles, but
the sunny spots were fresh and beautiful. We had the entire park to
ourselves. We found "Triple trouble" without any trouble. Then found
all the "Farm friends" warm and dry.{We didn't even slip in the
frosy mud.) Even saw a "Cotton tail" snuggled comfortably under its
log. If you want a beautiful hike with lots of fun creatures to
find, I suggest you wander down to Estacada.(with a pair of boots
with good traction)

Heather

I'll second that! Wood-aug & I went to Eagle Fern last summer and had a
great time. The boxes that I thought we'd never find (such as "cotton
tail"), we found. I don't know if they're still there but Buffalo Baby and
Morning Ritual are pretty neat too. Triple trouble was very fun... although

more Estacada parks - so neat.

Happy boxin' all!
