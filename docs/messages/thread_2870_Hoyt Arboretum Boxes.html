Hoyt Arboretum Boxes

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-12-23 01:53:23 UTC
Hi friends - went out today (just could not stand one single more day
inside) to check on the three boxes I put up at the Hoyt Arboretum a
while back, and also to see if I could find the Fall Harvest series.
All four of the harvest boxes are fine, dry, and have delightful
stamps in them. The hike is a wonderful trek through the arboretum
area, mostly on the "west" side of the main road up from the zoo. On
the easterly side of the road the three boxes I placed (all carved by
others) are also doing just fine thanks to some wonderfully kind
boxers who have replaced a couple of full books. I had no idea these
would have so many visitors. I thanked these people personally when
they told me they had put new log books in but want to say again here
that it's so nice to have people do that when a book gets full. So if
anyone wants seven boxes and can hike maybe 3 miles or so total, these
are all available. Hoyt Arboretum Series is listed at LBNA and AQ,
but you need to look at AQ for the Fall Harvest At Hoyt Arboretum
series, only listed there. Wonderful stamps by Der Mad Stamper, Aunt
Teri, Raqs Enigma, and 2 Hats and a Dog.
