Fort Steilacoom SitRep

From: Kerjin (kerjin@myndworx.com) | Date: 2003-07-15 19:30:29 UTC-07:00


Rayvenhaus (Comprised of RayvenDad, RavenMom and the RayvenKids, who which, were not on this trip. (The RayvenKids that is.)) went in search of the Fort Steilacoom Six trail.

Letterbox #1: Washington Territorial Insane Asylum @ Steilacoom
Located and stamped!
Letterbox #2: Heath Farm
Located and stamped!
Letterbox #3: RIP Letterbox
Located and stamped!
Letterbox #4: Purple Pumpkin
Located and stamped!
Letterbox #5: Model Boat
MIA
Letterbox #6: Leschi Gallows
MIA

This was an awesome little trek! We had a blast. Found #1 right off the bat but almost missed #2, and then, on the way to #3, we stumbled across #2!!!!! Located #4 with out a hitch, it was a wonderful hick up the trail, then couldn't find #5 to save our lives, it appears theres been much damages to the trees there, and even though we are sure we found the right burned stump 68 paces down the trial, we found not located a box anywhere in or around or under anything with an hour of searching.

All in all though, we had a BLAST!!!!

Rayvenhaus
