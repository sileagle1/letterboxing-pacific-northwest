Looking for Sunkids

From: ivanaletterbox (ivanaletterbox@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-20 00:38:15 UTC
Hello everyone!
I'm brand new to letterboxing as I chanced upon a box a few weeks
back. I didn't look through the box, only picked up the flyer
regarding letterboxing. After reading through it and going online to
learn more...I became hooked without even starting yet. I looked for
clues to the box I had found on the national site and didn't find
any. I'm wondering if this wonderful box is known by others?

I went back to the letterbox I'd found and made my mark, picked up
one of the postcards from the box to send out to the Sunkids to
inform them of the condition of the box. "Still really good by the
way!!!" What I did notice is that the postcards had 33 cent stamps on
them. That tells me that the box is about 2-3 years old. Are the
Sunkids still around? Do others know about this box? It's in
Washington State in the Gifford Pinchot National Forest near a
trailhead within a Federal campground.

I can't wait to do more LBing.
