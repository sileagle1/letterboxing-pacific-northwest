FF2: Boooooogie in the Park

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@lbworld.org) | Date: 2004-10-23 21:04:21 UTC-07:00
We've just returned from FF2: Boooooogie in the Park and I want to thank
everyone that showed up. We had such a wonderful time. The kids had
their own letterbox "Harry's Halloween at Hogwart's" to find with
special clues written just for kids. All in all approx 20 kids were
able to complete and successfully locate that letterbox. Now, Harry's
Halloween at Hogwart's has become Hogwarts Hitchhiker Hostel,
permanently placed at Millersylvania State Park.

There are pictures taken at the event that have been posted to this
URL:
http://www.lbworld.org/index.php?set_albumName=album14&option=com_gallery&Itemid=27&include=view_album.php


I'd like to thank everyone that came and made this a wonderful time for
all. To all of the new people that showed up, and there were a lot of
you folks, I'm thrilled to see such enthusiastic people, that's what
this hobby needs more of! Thanks you for choosing this event as your
first one to attend and we look forward to seeing you on the trails in
the future.
