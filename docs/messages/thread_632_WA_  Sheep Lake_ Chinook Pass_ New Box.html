This box was placed for all to enjoy, particularly "The Northerner"
who is currently doing a through hike on the Pacific Crest Trail. He
should be in the area in about 2 or 3 weeks, last I head from him.


The Meadow
Placed by Hoopoe
Place: Sheep Lake, Chinook Pass, Yakima County WA
Nearest town: Greenwater PIERCE county. This box is right on the
county line.
Distance, about 3 - 4 miles round trip. Allow plenty of time to
stop, look and enjoy the sights.

Hike: A fairly kind, steady hike with moderate gain. In some places
the trail requires careful steps. Small children can make this trip
but should be well supervised due to steep edges on parts of the
trail and the wilderness nature of the whole area.

Take water and a lunch. Prepare for stunning vistas, wildflowers (
late July is best time), busy chipmunks, birds, bugs and
butterflies. Wonderful warm woodland smells.

This area is subject ot heavy snowfall and the box will only be
accessible during the thaw, a variable mid-May through early
October. Check on current road access reports for snow closure and
unexpected closures, such as the forest fire that closed the road
south from the pass the day I placed this box.

www.wsdot.wa.gov

Near by boxes at Sun Top, Federation Forest and Packwood.
