From: cariekins (cckylesmomi@hotmail.com) | Date: 2003-04-28 04:56:26 UTC
We also found this one today. We had a great time in Salem today.
After a family birthday party, we decided to go get some letterboxes
in the area. What a great thing!!!!! FIrst we found the riverfront
one, and then headed down the road. My dh is from Salem so as we
were driving I asked if we were anywhere near the state capitol???
He laughed and said yeah it is right there, so out I whip the clues
and we were off again!!!! Found the LB, and opened it, and got all
the stamping done. Putting it all back in, I noticed an extra bag,
and said" what is this??? OHHHHH a hitchiker!!!!" this is my first
expereience with a hitchiker, and really feel like I got an extra
bonus!!!!! It is really cool to see familiar names in these books
too like Funhog, Brett, etc, etc!!!!!! Hitchiker Goerge is well and
safe and will be placed again soon!!!! Loving this new
adventure!!! Going out with Wingnut in the morning!!!!!! Gotta find
more!!!!
Thanks
Cariekins!!!!
