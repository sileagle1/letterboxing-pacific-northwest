Letterboxing dreams

From: erfellie (hemenekawan@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-03 16:21:01 UTC

When ever my mind gets on the letterboxing track I tend to dream
about it too. I looked up a new LB in my area called "Ghost town
series" read the clues, went to bed, next thing I know I am in the
setting of some old western ghost town standing on the porch of a
mercantile. As I stand at the top of the stairs I think to
myself, "the treasure you seek is where the spiders do crawl" and so
I reached under the stairs and grabbed the letterbox. Sandi keeps
telling me to put it back and its not what I think it is (then what
is it?). *Remember back in science class the glass jars filled with
formaldahyde and fetuses of different animals?* Well this letterbox
was a jar also, and floating in the liquid was a living alien fetus
staring at me. it was the ugliest thing in the world and just looked
pure evil like it wanted to kill everyone in horrible alien ways, but
me, being the sappy motherly kind, couldnt just abandon this alien
fetus even though Sandi was telling me to put it back under the
stairs. Well we went and found a few more letterboxes and every
single one of them was lying out in the open on the trail, not hidden
at all, and it was making me very annoyed because they were my
letterboxes and i didnt want anyone to steal them or vandalize them.
Well someone was chasing us down the trail, trying to steal the alien
fetus from me, and I tripped and fell over a tree root on the trail
and the jar went flying and broke when it hit the ground and the
fetus came out of it all grey and looking like an eel, all wrinkly
and had a huge gash in its head from the fall and it stared at me
with its big evil eyes, extremely angry at me for dropping it, and
then it was just about to do something very horrible to me as it was
slowly unfolding its body from the fetal position and I just got up
and ran like crazy!!!! Then I was back in the ghost town in a big
wooden barn and everyone was hunting for easter eggs (havent done
that since I was a kid... and I never won them or found the prize
egg) and all the sudden there I was with my (alien-free) basket
finding all the easter eggs that no one else was finding and then i
found the prize egg and won the easter hunt (for the first time in my
life!) Then I went and found more letterboxes.

Dreams can be so entertaing!
