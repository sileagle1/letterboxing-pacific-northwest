From: melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-07-07 17:10:16 UTC-07:00
There are several boxes. I have one in the same park that Go Fish are in..
called Family Camp. A little closer to Estacada (about a mile) you would
find Fishing for Fun. About 5 miles past Promontory park you will find 9
boxes called "The More The Merrier".

At Estacada, Sleep Whippet has many boxes in the Timber Park. At least some
of them were there last summer when we went camping.
