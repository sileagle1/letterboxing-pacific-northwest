I'm happy to report that the Happy Postman Series is alive and well. I finally dragged my hubby and the dogs out to Lewisville Park this afternoon for a bit of letterboxing. What a wonderful park! I never would have gone here if not for the Happy Postman Series. We will definitely be back to this park again, and not just to find the Lewisville Mini-Scramble box (didn't have the clues with me!).

Camp Fire Lady, these stamps are wonderful! Thank you for a most enjoyable hunt. The mail was fun to read and I enjoyed the story aspect of the clues. All your boxes were in good shape. A bit damp on the outside on a couple but nice and dry on the inside where it counts. All were well hidden.

If ya'll haven't made it out to hunt for this new series, I highly encourage it. While the path has some hilly areas, it would be a good one for families, even if one has a stroller. Lots of people using the paths there today, but no letterboxers that I saw.
