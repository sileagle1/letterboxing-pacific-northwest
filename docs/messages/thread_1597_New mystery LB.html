can find on www.lbworld.org -- some clues you can find on www.atlasquest.com

From: IronBear (ironbear@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-09-08 22:51:46 UTC-07:00
Good evening lezlie

I thought that I would mention actually that this list does have its purpose
as well. Just as many other Letterboxing chat lists and forums. Of course it
is not very helpful in finding clues - but as you can see it has helped you
get pointed in the right direction. It is helpful because you can ask
questions about anything Letterboxing related - find out about up coming
events - get some status reports from time to time on boxes that may be in
your area - meet fellow letterboxers and make friends are just to name a
few. So while this wasn't was your were looking to find - I think you will
find it to be very useful.

Welcome to the hobby.

Iron`Bear
