From: Liza (dots4you@sinclair.net) | Date: 2006-06-20 10:08:30 UTC-07:00
We tell people we are searching for buried treasure. We have a map and we have to be careful not to let other people see us searching for treasure or they will steal it and it won't be there for the next group of people to find. (this sounds better coming from my 7 year old...)

And if they are interested, I hand them a sheet with info where to go on the internet to find out more.
