Hi, all! I just ventured south-ward for a little bit of letterboxing yesterday afternoon. Hopefully I'm not spoiling anything here, but if you're worried I might, don't read on! On the agenda were...

Fallen Rainbow (dvn2rckr) (http://www.leecomputers.com/letterboxing/cgi-bin/boards/ikonboard.cgi?act=ST&f=17&t=2)
I think the three boulders might be moved or missing, but we did find the box. Unfortunately, the book was rather covered in mold and I didn't venture to even take it out of the bag. Cute stamp, though!!

Railroad Bridge Letterbox (The Nail Family) (http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=5400&boxname=Railroad_Bridge_Letterbox)
Got right to where the box should be, we think, but my boyfriend wouldn't let me do the climbing, so I have to trust his word that the box wasn't there.

The Duel (dvn2rckr) (http://www.leecomputers.com/letterboxing/cgi-bin/boards/ikonboard.cgi?act=ST&f=17&t=11)
We think we found the place, but we weren't certain of which post was actually Northernmost. We checked behind a bunch of them, and where we think it should be may no longer be a good place. They've decorated for Christmas, and some extention cord excess is coiled up there, so they may well have found the box and taken it.

"Egg"ceptional Mystery (dvn2rckr) (http://www.leecomputers.com/letterboxing/cgi-bin/boards/ikonboard.cgi?act=ST&f=17&t=10)
Found the egg (yay us! apparently, I went there when I was little, but I don't remember it!) and the park and even the turquoise 4, but we had some trouble past there. Some discrepancies regarding the 27 steps, and we looked by a lot of roots, but didn't find the microbox. It was getting a little darker by then, though, so visibility was low.

1 out of 4; not great odds, but at least I worked off some Thanksgiving eets! Thanks for the fun!

ClioMouse
