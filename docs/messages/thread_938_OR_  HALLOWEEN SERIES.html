OR: HALLOWEEN SERIES

From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-11-16 03:20:33 UTC-08:00
Carie of Lennens 3 and I finally had a chance to get together! We went
over to Pittock Mansion and got Mark and Beth's Halloween Series.
AWESOME STAMPS and just an excellent fun time letterboxing. Just my
type of series! Great clues and great art. This would definitely be an
excellent series for families with children.

When we were finished with that, we decided to go find Sleight, which
I've found twice before, but she never had...and she got it!!! It's
definitely there, folks! Just where its always been. That stamp is a
real treasure.

By the time we were finished it was long past time to meet up with
anyone at the Nature Park and we were hungry so we high-tailed it to
Gresham for conveyor-belt sushi.

It was a wonderful fall day for letterboxing. I hope everyone else who
made it to the various gatherings around the region had a grand time.
We didn't make it to any of those, unfortunately, but we had a grand
time by our own lone selves!

Best regards,
