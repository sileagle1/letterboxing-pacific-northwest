From: doubletreepfx (greenthummm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-07-11 02:08:30 UTC
I had the best time this last weekend -- neice Braidy and I went to
Bend to visit family, and we picked up 4 boxes, 1 Friday, 3 Sunday.
All 3 on Sunday had hitch hikers in them. What fun! One appears to
be carved in linoleum, which I've read about but never seen before.

So there will be an influx of HH's in the general Portland area over
the next few days. One of these little buggers came all the way
from Connecticut with Mark & Sue and Letterboxing Ham & Lazy
Letterboxer a few weeks ago...

The following boxes are all in fine condition, well-hidden and very
neat carvings:

Waterfowl Highway
Hollinshead Park
Duck Soup
and Cline Falls

Also in good condition, I've recently found:

Blue Lake Willow
Blue Lake Park
and (3rd times a charm, how blind am I) See Spot Run

My thanks to all the owners!
