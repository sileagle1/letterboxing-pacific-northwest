From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-08-12 02:33:12 UTC
Long, long ago I used to put little glass piggies in my boxes.
Then, I realized that the same folks were always getting them
and gave it up. I also came to realize that I didn't want to
make hunting for my boxes any kind of competition. So, I
gave up on the idea "prizes" altogether. I also
didn't want to tread that line between geocaching and
letterboxing by leaving trinkets in my letterboxes. I know
some folks love to be the first at a box but, personally,
I'd rather be way down the line. I love reading previous
visitors' log entries. Funhog

From: 4Dtoes (jfleshman@bendcable.com) | Date: 2005-08-12 16:19:37 UTC
I'm so glad I got one of those piggies before they became extinct! I too have
left first finder prizes, but haven't done so for a long time. It's fun to do but I
would only do it if it were a special box and I had something that was just the
right fit for the theme of the box. If it is done too much then it's expected and
the excitment wears off.
