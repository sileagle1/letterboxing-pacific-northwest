From: csb_bc (csb_bc@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-11 04:48:00 UTC

If you're ever in the Fraser Valley area on a quest for letterboxes,
(what other quest would you be on?) check out the "Westminster Abbey
Series" in Mission. Placed by Zara, these boxes are definitely worth
the trip. Follow the advice in the clues, and visit the area on Sunday
between 4:00 and 4:30. It's at this time that the Tower Bells are
rung; and when you hear them while walking through the woods, it's
almost as though you've gone back in time.

But watch out for the wildlife. While investigating a particularly
promising hiding spot, I had a squirrel dart out at me. I had no idea
these little creatures could be so frightening!

It's a quick jaunt... maybe 45 minutes, (including stamping time). It
took me longer because I went in to see the chapel, or at least what
they call the "chapel". It's very large with lots of stained glass and
carvings. It's also very, very quiet, cool and subdued. It's very
spiritual. I'm not Catholic, but even I felt compelled to confess!
