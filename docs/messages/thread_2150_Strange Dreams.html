Strange Dreams

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-07-15 22:37:58 UTC-07:00
Ok, my husband says I'm officially obsessed.

Last night I dreamed that a crazed letterboxer got upset because no one was
visiting their boxes. So they planted a bomb and carved the location of the
bomb into a stamp and left it in "a box somewhere in Oregon". They issued an
email to the LbPNW list and gave the letterboxing community 24 hours to find
the stamp that told the location of the bomb so it could be disarmed. I am
happy to say that the fast acting PNW community all jumped right on it,
organized themselves and managed to stamp into every box in Oregon in just
under 14 hours. Pretty cool huh?

Anyone else have strange letterboxing dreams?
