Wauna Viewpoint / The Gorge

From: doubletreepfx (greenthummm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-08-19 02:14:07 UTC
I checked on Wauna Viewpoint box today. It's lonely.... No visitors
since late 2005. Box in great shape though. It's a fairly short
hike, about 3 miles round trip, and affords great views of the Gorge,
including Hamilton Mountain, Beacon Rock, the Bridge of the Gods,
Bonneville Dam, Mt. Adams, etc. Today was cloudy but cool, great
conditions for hiking uphill in the Gorge. From Portland, head east
on I-84 and take the Eagle Creek exit, just past Bonneville Dam (about
25 minutes). There is a NW Forest Pass requirement. Park where ever,
walk across the wooden bridge and follow the signs. In a few more
weeks, the salmon will be returning to the creek, that's always an
amazing sight.
