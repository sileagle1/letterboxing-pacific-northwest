I was thinking something more along the lines of a Memories HH LB
where people would stamp in and, if they like, they could add a
blurb honoring somebody whom they think should be memorialized.
Sort of like a mini history LB but more to remember those dearly
departed folks who impacted you or somebody/something in a great
way. Your Iwo Jima image would be great for this--in fact, the
person who I'd like to honor in such a way fought the great fight in
the Pacific theater during the same timeframe. Contributions to the
LB though wouldn't have to be devoted to 'military' service just
remembering folks from all walks of life whose lives touched us in
some thoughtful capacity while they lived among us.
