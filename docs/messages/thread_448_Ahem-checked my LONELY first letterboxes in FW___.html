Ahem-checked my LONELY first letterboxes in FW...

From: gryandrowansmum (gryandrowansmum@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-07-21 04:58:05 UTC
Had a glorious and mostly free (always a highlight for us!) date with
my hubby on Saturday. We went to Snoqualmie Falls, hiked to the
bottom amid hordes of tourists, found Amanda's 2 boxes-really
beautiful stamps!-and one more placed by her #2 box. I am assuming #3
is still not back, I didn't have the clues at any rate. We wanted to
hit Rattlesnake Ledge but ran out of time.

We took a quick detour through Federal Way to check on my very first
placed letterboxes, they are sooooo lonely! Not a stamp to be
found..sniff,sniff. It's only been a few months but we are moving to
Mississippi in January so they will be gone soon! Get them while you
can-hint, hint.

Today we all hit the Tolmie State park boxes, but missed the 7 nails
huckleberry box due to a tired 5 year old! Got the other 2, though.

Cami and Family
