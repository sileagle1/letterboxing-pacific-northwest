Letterboxing event at Lewisville Park

From: Cherie Mortazavi (cheriescorner@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-05-17 02:51:04 UTC
We had a great day today at Lewisville Park for our little
gathering. We met some new fellow letterboxers. Purple Iris came
from CA to visit her daughter Campfire lady. And we met Charlie
Trail Duster from Oregon. The highlight, besides meeting new faces,
was when we did our last box at Chelatchie Prarie. Charlie told us
that we were right next to a Geocache. He showed us how to use his
machine and the kids along with Charlie found the cache. It was
pretty neat to see what that hobby is all about. The sun stayed out
for us and it was a great time had by all. See you on the trails.
Willow.

I too was at the gathering and had a great time. I got to meet 3 new people,
stamp a FABULOUS event stamp, and then traveled around Battle Ground Lake to
see Camp Fire Lady's Spring Bouquet series in the wild.

A couple of weekends ago our group stumbled upon a geocache at Eagle Fern
Park. It was interesting to see for sure.

I hope to see more people at the next gathering! It's always fun to meet new
people, get new ideas, and share some laughs.
