Here is the address for the boxes that are in your area. You may
wish to look in the "whats new" section too. There will be boxes

I am not sure if yu have any boxing buddies in your area, You may
be a pioneer.

Use whatever stamp you want . One will do,you can carve yourself or
pick a stamp from the store that best represents you. I carved my
first stamp using a kit from Michaels that came with "the pink
stuff" I currently use PZ Kut which you can find on line. It really
holds the carving tool firmly, but is not hard to cut. You might
like a slightly softer product to start.

When you see others stamps you might get an idea for carving one
yourself or perhaps planting boxes in the CB area for others.
