From: wingnut_lb (wingnut_lb@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-04 06:13:08 UTC
So it was a busy Sunday for us. We headed out to Lincoln City,
here's what we found:

*Devils Lake, alive and well
*We Love Lincoln City, yes, it REALLY is there, I have to admit, I
did cheat after seraching and called Carie on her cell and had her
walk me thru it! Duh, just like the clues said, but under some
blackberry brambles
*Boiler Bay.... doing fine
*Spruce Goose! What a cool little park!
*Devils Punch Bowl, woo hoo, firsties!
*then 3 out of 4 at Tresures of the Sea at Fogerty Creek State Park,
for the life of me, I cannot believe that it could be missing so
fast, but #4 was no where to be found by us!
*and last but not least, we struck out at Beneath the Blue #2. We
were getting tired, and dd was trying to sleep on us, and dh had had
it, so I think maybe we didn't get far enough, but gave it up for
another day. We did however escape a near epic proportions
catastrophe, when looking around a tree stump, I noticed movement,
before it noticed us, and revealed we were inches away from a
swarming hornets nest.... My 2 year olds stroller the closest to it,
and me having about 5 minutes to get my shot before I go into
convulsions if I get stung.... All I could say is go, go, go and
point. Luckily dh didn't stick around to question me until we had
cleared the area.... Whew! After the bees, we decided to call it a
day. And what a great doy it was.... OH

and

Somewhere along our travels, I turned loose "hitch hiker costal
conch" OK, so I am not good with names, but it is a cool stamp I
think.....

Hope you all had as much fun this weekend! I am still waiting to
hear from anyone who has made it up to the new series at Trillium
Lake on Mt Hood. Anyone, yet?
