From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-10-02 15:16:54 UTC
Greetngs and welcome to the wacky world of letterboxing. You'll find
that geocaching and letterboxing are similar in that they both get you
out of the house and often on the trails. The difference is the lack
of GPS cooridinates in a letterbox clue. The letterbox contains no
trinkets only a logbook and a stamp to be left in the box.

The FAQs at letterboxing.org and tutorials at Atlaquest.com can be
very helpful for newbies. Most of your questions will be answered
there. I'm sure your family will have some memorable letterboxing
adventures... Funhog
