From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2004-04-03 22:20:02 UTC-05:00
I discovered the HOPE HH in a box near Seattle on February 22 of this year
and planted it in the 'Duck in the Park' box in Yuba City, CA 2 days later, It
traveled to the 'Bridgeport' box in Penn Valley, CA then back to the Yuba City
area, the 'Sutter Buttes' box, and when Old Blue went to get that box today (I
with him, having gotten it months ago) we found it again! That's the second
time we've gotten the same HH twice!!

We had a great boxious day today adding 13 to our count, and have planted
this HH in a box within a 50 mile range of the Sutter Buttes box, in a box
originally planted in the winter of 2001!! There's a big hint for anyone who wants
an HH to add to their collection!
