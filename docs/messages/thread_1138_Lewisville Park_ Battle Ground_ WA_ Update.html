Lewisville Park, Battle Ground, WA. Update

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-02-16 16:54:34 UTC
While taking a quick break from selling Camp Fire candy on Sunday,
Cat's Meow and I ran up to Lewisville Park in Battle Ground to find
Melanie's new letterbox and check on my series. Thank you Melanie for
connecting your letterbox to my series. Three Blind Mice is alive and
well and we were Firsties to this box. Cute stamp. All of my Happy
Postman Series is alive and well and while we were checking on it,
Cat's Meow and I ran into a couple of letterboxers on the trail for a
quick exchange. Good to see you again Goofy, Sparkle and MS. While at
the park we checked on Soggy2pair's Lewisville Scramble and I am
sorry to say that it looks like that box is MIA. The Lewis River has
risen and possibly claimed it's second victim. Just a note to anyone
planning on planting boxes near the Lewis River: Plant on higher
ground. This river doesn't look all that intimidating until it snows.

P.S. my Yacolt boxes are patiently awaiting visitors with some really
cool surprises!

Camp Fire Lady
