Cycle Oregon is visiting Vernonia in June and we are doing four
letterboxes as an optional activity. The letterbox clues will be
available to the letterboxing public after the event. I'm planning to
provide some little sig stamps for the non-letterboxers. I've carved
some truly awful little butterflies, cars, and ladybugs with some of
my scraps. If anyone wants to volunteer to carve and donate some
stamps, let me know and I'll let you know where to send them. I'll
need them before June 22.

Thanks!
