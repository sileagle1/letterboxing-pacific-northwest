I have a stamp carved for a Space Needle box, and a great hiding spot
I found a few months ago. I also have been toying with the idea of
planting a series of boxes in honor of my grandparents, who grew up
in the Ballard area.

My conundrum is that I will be moving to Mississippi in January, and
I hate to knowingly abandon boxes to be the future flotsam of some
rainstorm, or broken into and strewn about as litter. I'm not so
attached to my boxes as to be crushed if they are destroyed, but I
don't want to just leave them to be someone elses trash to have to
pick up!

Is there anyone in the Seattle area willing to be the caretaker/owner
of these boxes when I move?

And it totally stinks to be moving to a state with only FOUR
letterboxes in it! Guess I'll have to place some there, to
visit...myself?

Cami
