It's official!!! Currently, have 12 confirmed folks--made
reservations for 20 (although that can always be increased)--please
join us for the:

*****************
Silver Eagle Oyster Exchange

Olympia, WA
Mon, 10 Nov 03
7pm--until you have to leave

Oyster House Restaurant
320 4th Ave West
(near the marina/Bayview Thriftway/dancing waters fountain)
Kid friendly, sports bar atmosphere establishment...


We will carve the event stamp during the event!!!
Get ready to shuck some of the sweetest oysters in the world...

Please email me directly or post to the list if you plan on
attending, include # of folks in your party who will attend. If you
can't stay for a meal and just want to drop by for a quick exchange,
that would work as well.

Thanks,

dvn2r ckr
(Adventure Seeker)
DuPont, WA


Directions to the restaurant:

From Interstate 5 in Olympia. Take I-5, to Exit 105.
At exit 105, turn RIGHT onto Ramp 0.2 miles
Keep LEFT to stay on Ramp 0.5 miles
Keep STRAIGHT onto 14th Ave SE 0.3 miles
Turn RIGHT (North) onto Capitol Way S 0.6 miles
*Turn potentially restricted* Turn LEFT (West) onto
5th Ave SW 0.1 miles
Turn RIGHT (North) onto Water St SW 0.0 miles
Arrive 0.0 miles

If you get disoriented for some reason (which is a tad easy in a
city of one way streets), find the Capitol Bldg, head North to the
water, look for the marina and Bayview Thriftway grocery store--the
restaurant is due East of the Thriftway and due South of ALL of the
boats!!!

It's about 50 miles from SeaTac Airport and about 125 miles
from downtown Portland.
