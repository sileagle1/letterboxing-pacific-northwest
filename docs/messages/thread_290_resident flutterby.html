I've revised Hint 2 to read: Its in a clump of rocks, and when on the main
path facing the sign, its on the right side of the clump. Look under a low
out crop of one of the rocks, covered with a bit of debris. Its approximately
the forth rock back on the right side (or alternately, approximately the second
to the farthest back), somewhere in that area. I am vague because I didn't
count which rock it was exactly (How do you count a medley?), though I would
recognize it if I saw it. As Funhog said to me later: "Supposedly that pile of
basalt is a native plant garden by mid summer. I hate to think of folks trampling
it looking for plastic." Good point. I'd hate to see that happen too. You can
reach it from the path. Absolutely no trampling necessary.
