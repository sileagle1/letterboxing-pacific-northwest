> 1. The Waterfalls Letterbox Series
> (I understand PonyTail Falls and Wahkeena Falls are
> both missing - will someone replace them or are they
> forever gone?)

All of the waterfall boxes are alive and well, so far as I know. The
Horsetail Falls box I've heard is a 'challenge' because the 'finger'
in the clue is no longer there, so there's a lot more trial and error
involved to find it than in years past.

I replanted the Ponytail and Wahkeena Falls boxes a couple of years
ago, but due to a change in topography, the clues had to change. The
new clues are at
http://www.geocities.com/rscarpen/Lb/US/OR/ColumbiaRiverFalls/

> 2. The Spirit of Dartmoor

You know, I thought this box could NEVER go missing, but for some
reason I have this nagging feeling in my head that the stamp is
missing or something. Did someone say that once upon a time?

> 3. The Lost Letterbox

I think this one has been found fairly recently.

> 6. Marquam Trail

The one on Council Crest is missing. Not sure about the other one.

> 8. Warrior Rock

Warrior Rock is definitely missing.

> 4. Japanese Gardens
> 5. Powell Butte
> 7. Elk Rock Island

Not sure what the status of these is. It's been at least a year
since I've heard a status report on any of these, though I haven't
heard that any of them are actually missing.

Good luck! =)
