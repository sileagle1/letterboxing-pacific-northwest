From: melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2005-08-21 18:11:48 UTC-07:00
The Brat Pack and family just got home from camping at Promontory. While we
were there we added another 8 boxes to the Clackamas River Trail and checked
on all of the existing boxes there.

All 9 boxes that were planted last year are there and happy. Two of them had
had no visitors, most of the others had 2-3 visitors. The kids were VERY
excited to see people had stamped in.

Family Camp in Promontory Park had been reported missing, but it's happy and
healthy in it's home. It had seen 2 people since last year.

I'll be posting the clues for the new 8 boxes along the Clackamas River
Trail this evening.

Happy Hunting!

Maiden
