How to find "Abandonedbox"

From: (PNWEXPLR@aol.com) | Date: 2003-07-22 12:35:58 UTC-04:00
To check out the list of abandoned boxes: On the LbNA website, go to "Search
for Boxes." Choose the state you prefer. Under placer, choose
"Abandonedbox." Click on the button that says "Search boxes," and your shopping list for
boxes will be revealed. (They're hot! They're hip! They're doomed to
disappear from the LbNA list--so get 'em while you can, folks!)

--Wild Woman
