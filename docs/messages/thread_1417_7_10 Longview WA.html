For anyone who missed Fudrick's post (http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbPNW/
interrupt?st=2&m=1&done=%2Fgroup%2FLbPNW%2Fmessage%2F3704) I'd like to
urge you to pencil in the afternoon of Saturday the 10th to meet and greet the newest
of the new letterboxers from the Longview Parks and Recreation class. So far there
are ten new boxes planned for the venue and there are more to follow. Fudrick has
done the honors and created an event stamp for the occassion. My fingers are
crossed that he heals instantly from the eye surgery he has scheduled next week and
will be there to unveil it in person.

This place is great. The staff there is 100% supportive of our visits and really excited
about hosting these boxes. One of them begged me for clues so she could go
hunting on her lunch break. I made a deal with her: she gets a stamp, she can have
the clues... I will post directions to the park next week. I hope to see many faces old
and new there. In the meantime, let's all send wishes for good health to our
letterboxing buddies. Funhog
