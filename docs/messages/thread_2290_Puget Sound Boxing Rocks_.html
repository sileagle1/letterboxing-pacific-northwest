Puget Sound Boxing Rocks!

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-11-13 05:03:33 UTC
Finally got a rare moment to head out boxing 'on my home turf' today
and what a pleasant experience! Found many incredible letterboxes
around the southern Puget Sound region. There has been a lot of box-
planting activity in this region this past year and boy, do we have
some incredible carving talent out there! SoHillHendersons have been
especially prolific! What incredible venues you took us to today.
After 3+ years of letterboxing in this area we were starting to feel
like we'd 'seen' everything already. We sure were wrong! We saw so
many new things today we had no idea existed--it was definitely an eye-
opener! Who knew???

That's the beauty of letterboxing! It leads you to all these
incredible nooks and crannies throughout the world you otherwise would
have had no idea they even existed.

Thanks for a great day & keep the awesome boxes coming!!!
