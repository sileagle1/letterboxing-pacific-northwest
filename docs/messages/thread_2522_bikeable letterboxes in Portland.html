If you haven't tried it yet, the Advanced Search on Atlas Quest let's
you click an option to only show "bike friendly" letterboxes. I tried
it for all boxes within 25 miles of Portland and it came up with 24
choices. Not all of them are likely to be on bike *trails*, but it
gives you somewhere to narrow down your search. =)
