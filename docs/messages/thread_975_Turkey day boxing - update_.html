Turkey day boxing - update!

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-28 06:45:33 UTC-08:00
WooHoo! Murder in Gabriel Park is solved. New evidence was released and we were able to track the remaining 2 boxes! All 6 accounted are accounted for!

We attempted the Extreme Box, but we quickly found that our climbing equipment was inadequate for this particular climb. We are working on upgrading and are determined to get it.

We then headed for Forest Park for a Dog day afternoon. We found Skip and Spot. Fido escaped our grasp. We'll wait for Rover to return and then go back for both.

While we were out we placed our 1st box of the Makin' Tracks series. The Hiker.

We ran short of daylight and the gang was fixing a big bird and dinner would not wait long for us to get there.

Hope ya'll had a fun filled and happy day as well!
