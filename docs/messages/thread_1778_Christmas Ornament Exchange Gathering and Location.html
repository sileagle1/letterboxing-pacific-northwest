Christmas Ornament Exchange Gathering and Location

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-11-25 03:27:08 UTC

Come join Cat's Meow and I for lunch and a Christmas Ornament
Exchange Gathering at Izzy's restaurant located at 7615 E. Mill Plain
Blvd. in Vancouver,WA., Saturday, December 11 at 1:00
pm. I was so inspired by a recent PLB that I received and thought it
would be a great way to get some of the many letterboxers in the
Vancouver and surrounding areas together to finally meet each other.
In order to participate in the ornament exchange, you must bring an
ornament, hand made, with the theme of letterboxing, and wrapped in
holiday wrapping paper. We will have lunch, exchange signature and
gathering stamps, play some fun games and everyone can then go find
some of the wonderful letterboxes that are planted all over the
Vancouver area. Please R.S.V.P., A.S.A.P. to tinarie at msn dot com
if you would like to come. If you cannot make it at 1:00 for lunch
then please join us at 2:00 for the exchange. See you there!
