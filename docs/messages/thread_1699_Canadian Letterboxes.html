I've just joined this group, so you may see some old news. Re: the
Canandian Gathering. I too, would like to thank the Secret Gardeners
for more than just the gathering. I only started letterboxing in
2002, at a time when they had already started the ball rolling. I
was extremely sorry to hear they were leaving the area.

Our American neighbors were ambassadors in the true sense of the
word. This was my first gathering, and they were all about the
friendliest people I could have hoped to meet. I'm looking forward
to seeing them all again.

Kudos to Ryan for the event stamp. It was very impressive.

We're working on the Whistler and Nanaimo boxes. The Prince George,
Sicamous, Taylor, Tlell, and Atlin boxes will be found as funds
allow. We are only about 2 km from the U.S. border, and so a lot of
those boxes are closer. . . but, the circle is expanding!
