Speaking of the Purple Heart Letterbox

From: minibullymom (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-04-20 04:07:57 UTC
How do you get the opportunity to get it for stamping? Apparently my
run in with stinging nettle this past January with BindleBabe and
MomNoTime while boxing qualified me. At least according to others
when they heard the story at the Leap Day Event.

When placing one of those pups from the Multi Hound Series of
SleepyWhippets back into it's hiding spot under a log I encountered
the stinging nettle. Needles to say it was only an inch or so tall
and looked more like a blackberry sprouting not the nettle it turned
out to be. I had blisters on the back of my hand for two days! But
that was only because I missed some areas when I rubbed the fern
frond all over the back of my hand to counteract the nettle!

So...I have witnesses and sources that say I am probably eligible for
a Letterboxing Purple Heart...don't know if that's a good thing or
not! Certainly not a great war story but none the less..my kids love
teasing me about not identifying the nettle after all the years of
harping about nettle to them.

MiniBullyMom ...who has determined that she won't get a chance to box
for at least three more weeks...ahh!!! BooHooo!!!
