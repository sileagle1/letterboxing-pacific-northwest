Jessa, an exchange is where letterboxers meet up and exchange the
stamps they carry with them all the time. These are called signature
stamps. An exchange can be as simple as running into another
letterboxer on the trail and exchanging stamps, or as involved as what
we usually call an "event" where more than two people are meeting up -
events can be single-day affairs or things that take place over
several days involving camping or staying overnight somewhere - the
possibilities are endless. At an event there is usually an event
stamp that everyone gets to put in their log book as well as the
signature stamps from the other people in attendance. Like Maiden
said, Portland boxers are ALWAYS up for an exchange or and event,
whatever it ends up being called. It's a great way to find out what
the other letterboxers in your area are doing or meet people when you
are from out of town.
