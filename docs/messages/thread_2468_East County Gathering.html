Re: East County Gathering

From: Lisa Harkins (lisah@whidbey.com) | Date: 2006-03-31 09:28:23 UTC-08:00
Wow, how cool is that when you ask a simple question about visiting an area
and strangers want to have a party while you're there. How neat!

My papermaking workshop is the first weekend of August. I plan to arrive at
Oxbow before lunch on the 3rd. We will be setting up our stuff on the
afternoon/evening of the 3rd, making paper on the 4th and 5th, and cleaning
up on the morning of the 6th. So, at the moment, it looks like I'll be
letterboxing, at the very least, the late morning/early to mid afternoon of
the 3rd and the afternoon/evening of the 6th, and probably early in the
morning on the 4th and 5th, as a nice beginning to my day before the pulp
gets slinging. But I'll be kidless and jobless, so I'll probably come
early or stay late by a day or two. I don't know which yet. It depends on
my friend, who needs housesitting/care for her pooches at the end of May.
It would be actually nice to come early, since I'm a little worried that
Oxbow is first come, first served camping, and I want to be sure I get a
place.

If y'all are gathering, I would sure love to come and meet you all.

Thunderbird
