I finished the event stamp for tomorrow. It's difficult to come up
with a stamp theme for an event without a theme!

Can't wait to meet everyone tomorrow. Remember--rain or shine it will
occur!

Shine: Ft Steilacoom Park offers 'uncovered' picnic tables, portapots
and possibly 'running water' toilets off in the distance beyond the
soccer fields. No access to electrical outlets (although there might
be some BBQ grills nearby) as far as I can tell. Handicap accessible.

Rain: Saltar's Point Park offers a covered picnic pavillion with two
large picnic tables, a firepit, BBQ grill, plenty of 'countertop' space
around the 'walls' of the pavillion and a running water toilet/sink
facility and one of the most incredible views of Puget Sound and
surrounding islands. It's basically a rocky/stone beach but the kids
enjoy it for the 'rock skipping' potential. Not handicap accessible as
it requires negotiating a wooden footbridge and about 25 steps down to
the pavillion.

Hope this helps with folks planning to attend.

See you tomorrow!
