Pilot Butte
(I won't type the directions, I'll leave it up to you to find it, in
the middle of Bend city).

Get to the top on a beautiful day
Drive or hike, however you may
Once at the top, be sure not to fall
Look for an aging Juniper with a short rock wall
It's old roots point the way to your treasure
Just a few short steps no need to measure
If you are still having problems finding this box
It may help if you look under some rocks.

Watch for snakes!

Juniper and the box weren't disturbed. Email me tonight if you want
Big Tree, not sure if you are going there.
