N. CA quickies:

Source of the Sacramento
Dunsmuir Shasta Shade
Hedge Creek Falls
Craggy Castle (entrance fee & drive up a steep, narrow road required)

Then a bunch in the Redding area which would require a detour off
299W (or 199W--can't remember right now?) for a few miles--

Dammed Shasta (6 mile drive one way off I-5)
Queen City Shasta
Glory Hole

There are a bunch of others, these are just boxes we've planted.

We also have a box with somewhat of a hike (3 miles roundtrip)
that's quite close to I-5 near Shasta Lake (Packers Bay Overlook).
It's a great spot to stretch your legs before the long stretch to
Sacramento.

Enjoy your trip!

dvn2r ckr
