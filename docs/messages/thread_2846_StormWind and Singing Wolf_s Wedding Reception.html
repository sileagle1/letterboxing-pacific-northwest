Hey everyone!

I just realized tonight that I hadn't posted anything about this
mini-event on this list, and that not everyone will have seen it on
Atlas Quest. Sorry about that!

Stormwind and I were married on July 7 of this year, and would like to
invite our letterboxing friends to celebrate this event with us!

We'll be holding the event this Saturday, September 15, in Cedar Hills
Park in Beaverton. It's located in the 2400 block of SW Cedar Hills
Blvd. After a short walk through the park, we realized there are not
a lot of areas for permanent letterboxes. (I know some of you make
take this as a challenge, though! :) Go for it!) Day boxes and plain
sight boxes are more than welcome!

We're doing a casual potluck, so please let us know if you can bring a
dish to share, and what. I'll put up a list here as people rsvp.

This is just an excuse to celebrate our happiness with our friends. No
gifts, please.

Other note: We are a same sex couple. If you don't approve of our
marriage or our relationship, that's your right. But we're here to
celebrate with our friends who are happy for us, so those of you who
are not may wish to avoid this particular event. Seems silly that I
have to even include this statement, but I'm sure we'd all like to
avoid any unpleasantness.

Sign up is on AQ at
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=462

Hope to see you Saturday!

Singing Wolf
