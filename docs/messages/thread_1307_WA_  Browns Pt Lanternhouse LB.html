Reports of this Tacoma area letterbox's demise have been greatly
exaggerated. It was there today alive & well with a full log--a
microletterbox with 31 visitors's stamps and possibly more
signatures, too!

a slightly modified location. It was an absolutely awesome day to
visit this spot--great weather, incredible views & the garden was
absolutely gorgeous! Sure beat the tornados, hail, falling trees
and gale force winds we experienced yesterday!

Enjoy!
