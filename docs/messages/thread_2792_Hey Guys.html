Re: [LbPNW] Hey Guys

From: colleen (colleendurham@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-06-12 00:32:39 UTC-07:00
There's an abundance of excellent boxes along I90, the trouble is you'll never get them all. In Montana you can't go wrong looking for any of Judy's boxes. Don & Gwen have also planted not too far off the freeway, their box "On the Trail" will take you back to the Corp of Discovery days. In northern Idaho Sondog's boxes are not to be missed. We especially like the "Mullan Road" letterbox and it is not far off the freeway. As far as Spokane goes, I'd head to an area with several boxes near by. Finch Arboretum on the west side of town has several by Storm Crow and 2 placed by Pied Piper, all are excellent boxes. The Flying Squirrel Squad has one not too far off the freeway, "The One that Got Away" and it is a nice walk besides. My plant, "Riblet's Goddess", won't take too long to get to and the view is cool. When you get to Cheney you could check out the several boxes at Turnbull Wildlife Refuge. All Excellent. There's not alot until you get to Ritzville, there's a pretty cute drive-by by Wood Aug & Kurious Jo once you get there. Have a great trip!
