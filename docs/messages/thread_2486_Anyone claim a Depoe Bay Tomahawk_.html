Anyone claim a Depoe Bay Tomahawk?

From: Der Mad Stamper (letterboxr@aol.com) | Date: 2006-04-16 19:59:05 UTC
Someone contacted me recently through my stamp-carving tutorial site. I
don't think the person who wrote the message knows anything about
letterboxing. Here is what they wrote:

"hay i was egg hunting at or park in depoe bay oregon and i found a
stamp called the tomahawk and its a rubber one just like yours so maybe
this is yours get back to me asap plez"

It sounds to me like this person might have stumbled onto a letterbox,
but I can't seem to locate a letterbox or hitchhiker on LbNA or AQ that
meets this description.

If I could determine who this "tomahawk" belongs to, I would like to
put them in direct contact with the person who sent the message.

Otherwise, I suppose I will just try to suggest that they put it back
where they found it.
