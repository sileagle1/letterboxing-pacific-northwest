Letterboxing Record

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-05-03 15:41:13 UTC
Sunday proved to be a wonderful day to set a new personal
letterboxing record with 21 finds in one day and boy am I feeling it
this morning!
Cat's Meow and I picked up Maiden and Tooth Fairy and headed out to
Eagle Fren Park in Estacada. While waiting for others to arrive, we
picked up Morning Ritual. As we headed back to the car we were met by
Pandora, Iron Bear, Bookworm and Sleepy Whippet. We located Buffalo
Baby and sat down to do some stamping into a few Postal Letterboxes
and Hitch Hikers. After passing out some much needed trail mix, we
started our power boxing journey. We found all 6 of the Farm Friends
Series and were firsties to these boxes. Next, we went for the Triple
Trouble 3 and found only 2 and caught Whippet Litter- Fishing Whippet
as we were leaving the park to meet up with The Ravenhaus clan at
Milo McIver Park. After lunch and finding out that they were not
going to show, we picked up Protection and headed out for the
Jurassic Park Series of 5 and were successful in finding them all.
Next on to the Making Faces Series. After many encounters with
Stinging Nettle and being pretty worn out by now, we were only
successful in finding 3 out of 5. Wild Woman, I beleive the "eyes"
have gone missing and the "nose" was pretty elusive. Lastly we picked
up Clackamas River to conclude our power boxing trip with some sore
feet and muscles but all in all a great day for boxing and meeting up
with good friends. I can't wait to do it all again!
Camp Fire Lady
