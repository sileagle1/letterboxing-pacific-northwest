From: (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2006-01-30 14:23:42 UTC-05:00
One of the ways we make sure a box in a potentially snowy or wet location
has a chance of staying dry is to dig a little hole and line it with stones or
a rock the proper size so when we place the box on top of it (them), the top
of the box is even with the original ground level. Then we place a rock or
other items native to the location on top of the box. This way, it would
probably not ever be sitting in a pool of water or wetness.

Of course, 'digging the hole' needs to be done with care so no harm will be
done and little will be disturbed.
