OR: Tryon Creek Success

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2004-09-01 22:09:01 UTC
Yay!!! good new to report. As many of you may recall,
we had a note posted here last spring from the powers
that be at Tryon Creek SP telling us that letterboxing
was forbidden in the park. I put on my PR hat, made an
appointment and met with Ranger Karen Trombley there
hoping to shift her stance. In the interim, Mudstumpers
removed all known letterboxes there.

I just heard back from the park, after all this time. Word
is that they are welcoming our boxes with a couple of
small caveats: they don't want us to go off trail and
they want this policy posted for boxers to see. The off
trail exclusion presents a small problem for a couple
of the boxes but for the most part all should pass muster.
Hopefully, they will be back in action soon.

Ranger Karen is also the person who is in charge at Milo
McIver Park. Her approval extends to both places. So, I
consider this doubly successful. Go Boxers! Funhog
