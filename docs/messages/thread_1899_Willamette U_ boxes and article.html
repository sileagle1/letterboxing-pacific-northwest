From: windairen (windairen@softhome.net) | Date: 2005-02-04 07:12:52 UTC

Hey,

I've been kind of out of the loop for a while, since I'm actually
doing schoolwork and stuff. However, I thought I'd point you to this
article that appeared in the Willamette University paper yesterday:

http://wucollegian.collegepublisher.com/news/2005/
02/02/News/A.ModernDay.Treasure.Hunt-849076.shtml

The article itself is fine, but the WU box that has had it's picture
taken and printed is already missing. As a result, Wizbang and I have
pulled our other boxes from around campus, except the Little
Frustration box, which is presumed safe because anyone that takes the
time to solve that clue is probably a serious letterboxer. As far as
I know, there aren't any more surviving boxes on campus.

We plan to rehide the boxes and rewrite the clues, and I will be
writing an editorial to the paper to complain and hopefully do a
little bit of explaining about letterboxing.

Just thought I'd give a heads up to anyone in the Salem area.

Windairen
