From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2005-01-10 17:43:30 UTC-08:00
This would be wonderful if we could get the Group Camp like we did at
Milo McIver! That worked out wonderfully for everyone. Folks just
showed up as they could and camped for free (at least it was free the
way Funhog and I hosted the event. I refused to take anyone's money for
camping and hanging out with us).

It may be different this time depending on who is hosting and how they
want to go about paying for it. Not everyone is willing to blow big
bucks to host their letterboxing pals at a state park. Anyway, that's
putting the cart before the horse since a date has not firmed up yet.

Who's taking charge of this event this go 'round? And by the way,
hosting an event is really not too tough. At least it wasn't for me.
Our event was pretty loose and free. People have different styles of
hosting. Some folks like to have more control and have everyone
register. That is fine. I thought the way we did it worked out fine,
but I have a less rigid style. Everyone always seems to enjoy
themselves no matter who hosts or how it is done.

Best regards,
