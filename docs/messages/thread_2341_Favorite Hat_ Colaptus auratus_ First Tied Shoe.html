(blush) Thank you for the lovely welcome! My kiddos are 8.5, 5.5, and 3.5.
Dh stumbled across the LBNA website sometime last year, and forwarded it to
me. I actually got around to looking at it at the end of the summer, and was
hooked! We did our first round of LBing at Olallie Lake in September (we're
homeschoolers, and spent a week up there with almost no neighbors!).

Since then we've done a good amount of hiking and hunting in the gorge,
although we've been sticking to town since the rain and potential for
landslides has increased. We are in the Gresham area, so we're hoping to
get to know you folks out here.

We planted our first box there when we were up there. It's very primitive,
being spur-of-the-moment. I hadn't planned to plant any boxes, since we had
never even hunted before that trip. But we were so inspired by the several
great boxes we found (including yours, I believe!) that I scrounged around in
our school supplies and was able to put one together. I imagine it's buried
under several feet of snow right now!

I 'm hoping we can spend another week up there next Sept. The cabins are
SO cheap during Sep/Oct.
