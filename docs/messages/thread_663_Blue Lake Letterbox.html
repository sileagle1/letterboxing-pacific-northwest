Blue Lake Letterbox

From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2003-09-01 13:26:51 UTC-07:00
I, too, was part of that letterboxing Dream Team that included Funhog,
Ryan, Amanda from Seattle and some of the members of the travelling
circus. The clues, of course, you have to sort of decipher beforehand
so, as you can guess, I just tagged along. I did sort of help seek it
out once we were there, though. We all sort of fanned out and paced it.
Eventually there was success, but it's not a quick grab, that's for
sure.

I re-carved the original stamp that went missing many years ago, but
never put it in the new letterbox. I figured that the stamp he put in
the new box was a replica of the old one...NOT EVEN. Maybe I should do
that. It is Der Mad Stamper's design. BUT...can I find it again???
That's the question. Yeah, it's a tough one. Not too many people have
the original stamp in their logbooks. Or maybe I should place a new
letterbox in the park with the old Der Mad Stamper Design but with
Paisley Orca pathetically easy clues. What do y'all think?

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
