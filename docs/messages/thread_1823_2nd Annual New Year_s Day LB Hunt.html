2nd Annual New Year's Day LB Hunt

From: minibullymom (bedotcalm@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-01-02 19:57:30 UTC

Well... MomNoTime and myself enjoyed another New Year's Day of
letterboxing sans kids! We headed out to the Millers Place and
decided to check out the fishing equipment that has been hidden in
the area surrounding the lake. We found lots of interesting lures
and lore. Only one lure couldn't be found but we let Chuck know that
#8 was no where to be found or else we were completly off base.

Heard rumors of ghastly beings lurking in the woods around there but
we ran out of time to track any down as proof. So we will go back
another day in search of wee beasties. We did check out a nearby
home for hitchhikers that hasn't been visited in months. We are now
helping a couple of stranded "out-of-state visitors" along their
journey! We will drop them off later this week at a tourist trap
somewhere in Pierce County.

All in all...it was wierd to not spend the day mucking about in snow
like we did last year. I missed my fourlegged companion dearly as
she enjoyed our letterboxing foray in the snow with us last year. We
are back on the letterboxing trail and ready to recarve a couple
stamps for a couple of our boxes that have came up missing. Most of
all we have figured out which boxes are going to be added to our
found list this next month!!

See you all on the trail sooner or later,
MiniBullyMom & MomNoTime
