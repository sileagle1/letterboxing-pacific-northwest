From: D. Huffman (omniteista@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-02-07 13:58:16 UTC-08:00
Ooo, I know this one! Along the top of the homepage is a series of choices;
pick "Letterboxes" and select "Advanced search" on the menu that pops up. On
that page, way over to the right, there's a drop-down menu box labeled "search
type." "Trip planner" is the third in the list; select that and hit "go." The
new search boxes you get will be route and endpoints, plus the usual
search-narrowing tools.
