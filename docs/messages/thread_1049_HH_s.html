At 12:56 PM 1/2/2004, you wrote:

>So now all the talk about various hitchhikers on the NA list has me
>curious.. I haven't heard a thing about the hitchhiker I released a month
>or so ago.. is it standard practice to let the owner know when you move it
>or just move it on? I moved one hitchhiker and let Wanda and Pete know so
>it could be logged on the tracking page but didn't let the owner know..
>What is the standard?

Many owners of hitchhikers love to hear reports of their travels, but
relatively few letterboxers ever report them (I'd be surprised if one in
ten finders ever reported back to me about the travels of my hitchhikers).
If the owner of the hitchhiker asks for reports in the hitchhiker log, then
it's common courtesy to provide them; if the owner doesn't provide any
means for reports (like an email address) or doesn't ask for reports, then
you're off the hook.
