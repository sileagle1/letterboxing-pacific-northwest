I usually keep my opinions to myself, but could not pass this one
up. Lets just say I think becoming experienced is more a "mind set"
than an actual "time frame". I have only been boxing for two years,
but compared to alot of other people in this area, that might be a
long time. There are several people in this area that have not been
boxing as long as I have, but I would consider them much more
experienced than myself. If you catch letterboxing fever (you go
letterboxing at least once a week, or maybe more) you will be
experienced very quickly.
