Okay I think the thing that is happening here is that my copy-cat remarks is
being taken in a negative context. Perhaps I wrote it that way, if so I
apologize. It was not meant that way. I was referring to the sociological
definition of copy-cat any time something becomes a popular way of doing
things and people jump on the band wagon it is called a copy-cat thing, be
that smoking, suicide or in this case, using a "pen name". I wasn't putting
anyone down; I was just trying to say that I too would like it if these
mysterious people making these wonderful boxes would come out from behind
their masks and plant the boxes as themselves. What I have seen from these
caped crusaders has been by far some of the best letterboxes I have ever
seen. Lemony Snicket, the Cloaked Letterboxer, Miller and Hutch, they are
all some of the most wonderful letterboxers ever. But I would be even more
impressed if I knew who they were. Like if I knew oh maybe that Amanda was
Lemony Snicket, or that Der Mad Stamper was the Cloaked Letterboxer. By all
means please all of you keep putting out these wonderful boxes, mysterious
and inventive and creative, put what ever slant you want on your boxes, be
as creative as your imagination will allow, but don't always do it behind a
mask is all I was trying to say. Perhaps as Amanda said, it came out wrong
at 4am.

Pandora
