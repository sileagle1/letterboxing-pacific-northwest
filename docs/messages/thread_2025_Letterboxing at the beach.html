Letterboxing at the beach

From: erfellie (hemenekawan@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-21 01:17:30 UTC

I went to Brookings, OR yesterday and what a beautiful day! we located
the "Indian Sands" LB which only had 3 other entries in it. This is an
awesome stamp!!! get out there and get this one!!!! Anyways I dragged
along 3 non-letterboxers and evidently this was "cruel and unusual
punishment" these wonderful friends of mine are not used to hiking and
this is a very short one, maybe 1/4 of a mile, but it is a steep trail.
They were huffin and puffin and hurtin and there I was trekking along
like a kid after candy hehehe :) Had a great time, my friends enjoyed
it once we got back home and I treated them to Caramel Caribou icecream
and then all in the world was well. Get out there and get this
letterbox!!!!! its an amazing stamp, very detailed and authentic :)
