Tacoma area--both letterboxes are back in business. Finally got the
wandering soul of a stamp put back in its proper place after work

folks had found the second stamp in the series... Can't imagine

Enjoy your glimpse into an historic little block of Tacoma. On a
clear day, you'll get a great view of Mt Rainier, too!
