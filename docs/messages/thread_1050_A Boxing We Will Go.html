A Boxing We Will Go

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-01-02 16:43:40 UTC-08:00
We just had the most wonderful time! The snow has stopped and most had melted around our house so I loaded up the kids and hubby and went in search of Paisley Orca's Beaver Creek box. I was thrilled to find it safe and sound exactly where the clues said it would be. The hike down was a bit treacherous due to the area being pretty heavy with snow and ice still, but it was a definite adventure. My 6 year old and 4 year old made it with no problem and we got stamped in. It was fun to see that the last person to log in was a park ranger with very nice comments.

If you haven't gotten this box yet, I highly recommend it.

Melanie
