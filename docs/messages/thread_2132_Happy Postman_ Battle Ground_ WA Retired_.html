Happy Postman, Battle Ground, WA Retired!

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2005-07-07 03:54:20 UTC
I am sad to say that the Happy Postman Series at Lewisville Park in
Battle Ground, WA is retired but happy to report that a new series of 5
boxes is in it's place with new clues, hiding places, and 5 new finds
to add to your count. It is called Fractured Fables. If you have the
images of the Happy Postman, they are now a collectors series of
stamps. If not, then I apologize but there are new ones to be had. I am
calling this trail in Lewisville Park "Storytime Lane". Every series
that I place on this trail will have a story to it so it is very kid
friendly and adults are welcome to laugh along with the story as well.
Come on down to Lewisville Park and enjoy this series, pack a picnic,
and swim in the Lewis River for an afternoon of great fun! I will
change this series every couple of years to keep the trail exciting and
the experience at the park enjoyable everytime you come out. Look for
many other boxes placed throughout the park while there. This is a
beautiful park to come back to year after year!
Camp Fire Lady
