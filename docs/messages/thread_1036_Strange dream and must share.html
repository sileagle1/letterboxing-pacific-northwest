This was too strange and too vivid not to share.

Letterboxing dream this morning. Was in the woods seeking a series
of boxes. DVN2SKR was there and we were seeking someone elses boxes.

I was way off the line ( tisk)and stumbled upon what at first looked
like a collection of kids toys. Little things like Legos and Weeble
people. ( hummm Trial and Errors Gnomes???)

Then I see there is a shoe with all these little plastic things glued
on. ( hummm I think that is RayvenDads new ploy but not with plastic
toys.)

Perhaps it is a nod to Geocaching but I digress.

I pick up the shoe and I hear DVN2 behind me saying "check out the
bottom"

Sure enough the stamp is carved into the bottom of the shoe. It is a
goodly sized shoe at that!

The image was far and away too strange but did involve a military and
baseball (?) theme.

I inked up but never stamped in. Woke up instead.

I think I need a vacation.
