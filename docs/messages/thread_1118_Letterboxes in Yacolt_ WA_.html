Letterboxes in Yacolt, WA.

From: Tina (tinarie@msn.com) | Date: 2004-02-03 02:42:36 UTC
Cat's Meow and I decided to take a break from our annual Candy Sale
on Saturday and took a cruise up to Yacolt to check on our boxes.
Alas, I have my first casualty and it wasn't even found yet. The "Luv
Ya" Letterbox has washed away somewhere down the Lewis River and it
still had it's "Firsties" prize. Oh well, I will be recarving the
stamp and replacing the box on higher ground soon so in the meantime
this box is MIA. Lucia Falls, Chelatchie Prairie Railroad and Moulton
Falls are all alive and well and dry and waiting for visitors. Each
one has some magnificent views of waterfalls in the area with water
roaring by. Well worth the drive out there. The view of Mt. St.
Helens is spectacular off of SR 503! All three trails are kid and
stroller friendly so bundle up the kiddies and head out to Yacolt!
