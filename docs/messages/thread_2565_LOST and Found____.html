Hi Star gazers,
I am missing a couple items. A compass which I think I left at Maryhill
Museum on one of the tables in the peacock area. It's small (2" sq) and
black. I'm hoping someone in the group found it. There were several people
up there hunting when we left.

The other item is a . . . well for lack of a better term a bottle fob. On
one end is a loop for going over the neck of a regular store bought water
bottle the other end has a carabiner for hooking to your pack. It's only
about 4" long. I'm not sure but I may have left in your van, Maiden. ?????
I forgot my water bottle in there - not sure if the fob was on it.

Please let me know if you run across either of these items. Both are hard to
replace. I'll pay postage or meet you somewhere - whatever is convenient.

We had a great time at the gathering. Really enjoyed everyone.

Oh, and neener-neener-neener - we found Paisley Orca's Lb at the museum.
FunGal has a good eye - she gets all the credit.

Thanks for the fun!
Kurious Jo (previously SloMo)
