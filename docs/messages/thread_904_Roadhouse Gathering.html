Roadhouse Gathering

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-12-01 04:25:59 UTC
I hope everybody has a big red X on their calendar for next Saturday,
December 6th. That's the day that we'll be meeting at McMenamin's
Cornelius Pass Roadhouse, 4045 Cornelius Pass Road, Hillsboro, Oregon
(just south of Highway 26) from 4:00 to 6:00 PM. We will be in Imbrie
Hall, just behind the big, white house.

Kyle Spinks from the Tualatin Hills Parks and Recreation Division will
be the star attraction. He wants to meet members of the local
letterboxing community and let us know about boxing opportunities in
his parks. Let's hope he brings a stamp to exchange with all of us!
See you there. Funhog
