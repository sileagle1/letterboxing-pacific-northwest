Tacoma, WA - Snake Lake Nature Center Update

From: Laura B. (bedotcalm@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-10-20 18:01:40 UTC
Okay everyone,

Snake Lake is gearing up for a major remodel/overhaul/Hwy 16 cleanup
project. The letterbox/geocache friendly staff has let me know that
all trails markers will be replaced and renumbered this winter. The
two large footbridges will be completely reconstructed. Recovery
projects as a result of the unavoidable impact to the preserve which
has occured over the course of the last two years due to Highway 16
widening are starting now. The staff believes that all the
letterboxes and geocaches could be impacted or compromised over the
next year as a result of the entire remodel/construction project.

I have confirmed that the entire Snaky Lake series which was placed
in the preserve as part of their letterboxing class (in partnership
with Adventure Seeker) was destroyed by vandals. They don't
officially track any other letterboxes or caches in the park so they
cannot confirm the status of them individually. They did recommend
that box/cache owners follow-up frequently to ensure that their
boxes/caches aren't impacted by the parks improvements and that they
are not negatively impacting the area in which they are located.

If someone has a specific box they want to check on and can't get
there let me know offlist. I drop by Foss every Friday morning (my
daughter attends school there and I'm the PTA President) and can
followup on boxes/caches if I have exact directions to them.
