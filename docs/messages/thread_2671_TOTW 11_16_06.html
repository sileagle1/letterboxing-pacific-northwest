It's gotten quiet again. How about telling us about a letterboxing
experience where you got really dirty...

My personal favorite was the time I decided to "shortcut" across a
tidal flat in Olympia to get back to my car. I had a morning flight to
catch in Seattle for a business trip and decided to go a day early to
do a little letterboxing.

After finding the Lost Loons, it was getting dark and I just knew I
could get back to my car much faster if I went across what I thought
was the beach instead of walking the trail back. Well, faster it was
but tidy is wasn't. The beach consisted of squishy black muck and I
was soon covered covered up to the middle of my shins. Plus, the aroma
of the tides was with me. The clothes I was wearing were meant to be
part of my travel wardrobe. Fat chance! I spent that evening in a
laundromat, carving a stamp and drying my shoes over and over. I guess
this just proves that pigs like to wallow in the mire. Funhog
