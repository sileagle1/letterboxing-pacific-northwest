It's been a good week for the Pig's journal. I've added three new
Wildhair stamps to my collection! I tracked down the box that appeared
as a National Mystery Day plant the other day. Today, we headed east
for fishing, birdwatching, hiking and letterboxing at the confluence
of the Deschutes and Columbia rivers.

It was a glorious eastern Oregon day, wispy white clouds in a clear
blue sky. The fish were elusive, birds plentiful, trails excellent and
the letterboxes very nice. Anyone looking for a grand place to spend a
day on the sunny side of the state need look no further. Funhog
