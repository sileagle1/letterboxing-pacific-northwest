From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-05-23 16:39:26 UTC

April Fools is being checked on - seems to be missing in action

Department of Redundancy Department - very clever, well chosen
location and lots of fun, And large big stamp. Come prepared with
lots of ink and a big ink pad!

Perseus is wonderful well placed, alive and well.

Duck Pond Friends Series
#1 Turtle Island - great stamp and location!

#2 Duck Pond - stamp is missing thanks to one of the mudstumpers
(me). I dropped it off a nearby bridge (not into water but into
bushes and of course there are blackberry vines there too). 45
minutes later darkness descended and alas no stamp. We didn't even
get to properly stamp in. The box owner has been notified. We will
if need be continue to search for it, however we cannot get back over
there until next week.

We are thrilled to be letterboxers and excited about all the new
places to visit. CCC Environmental Learning Center is fabulous!

Great locations, great stamps, and great thanks to all box placers.

PS. we do not intend to be problems - such as opening boxes on a
bridge, I promise not to do that again!

thanks again
mudstumpers
