Hello!

My parents and I will be venturing down to Eugene to pick up a new puppy (!!!) and visit my sister, and I (OF COURSE) have to try and cram in as much letterboxing as I can while down there. Re: Visiting Eugene and looking for recommendations!

From: Jeanne (heronafoot@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-17 04:36:20 UTC
Hi Clio - I reccommend anything by Team Springamajack especially the
more recent ones. Lani's carvings have really become works of art.
Quiet Place is an incredible stamp. There are quite a few at Alton
Baker Park - Team Springamajack's "Poe Classics" a series of six, "Out
of this World" by Fizzy and Poe and "Tom Sawyer" by Jess and I think a
couple remaining old boxes planted by Barry but adopted by Jess.
Amanda has a cute one at/near Valley River Center but you need to
watch the video first and answer a few questions to be prepared.
There is a easy but nice library box by Camp Violet. There is also at
least one box (possibly 2 up on Skinner's Butte).

Hope that will get you started. My son lives in Junction City. I have
a couple of jobs in the mornings and evenings but could meet you mid
day and escort if you aren't too familiar with Eugene.

Have a great time in new territory, Heron-A-Foot
