From: rscarpen (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-11-03 17:54:06 UTC
On more than one occasion I've had people request making the member
list public since it's a useful tool to find a specific letterboxer
or insure you don't make private e-mails into very public posts.

I turned it off under the assumption that some people would like the
additional privacy and perhaps those that lurk would prefer not
having anybody know they were here. But then again, maybe I'm wrong?

So I'm taking an informal poll: Who'd like to have the list of
members available (like it is on the main talk list) and is there
anyone that would prefer it to stay unavailable. You can e-mail me
personally if you want to stay anonymous on the matter.

Thanks for your input!

-- Ryan
