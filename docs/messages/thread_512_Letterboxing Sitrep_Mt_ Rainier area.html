Letterboxing Sitrep/Mt. Rainier area

From: gryandrowansmum (gryandrowansmum@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-08-04 05:50:04 UTC
We headed off to Mt. Rainier with a relative from Illinois in tow,
and managed to hit 2 letterboxes despite the relative not being very
fond of a sport that requires the great outdoors. I have found this
to be true of most of my DH's relatives in the midwest-we have tried
to get them outside, to no avail. It reached the point of the
ludicrous when a whole host of inlaws came with us to Pacific Beach,
WA and a few never even made it to the beach! And some more declined
Lake Quinalt in favor of renting movies in the room! AAAAHHHHHHH!

Anyway, thats a subject for my therapist, we visited "Dammed Alder",
right where it should be, cute little box and us only the 3rd to
stamp in! Found some blackberries just down the hill from it,too-an
added bonus!

Though I brought the clues to Comet Falls, we didn't get it due to
the above mentioned relative. The views from Paradise were gorgeous
though, and the wildflowers are in bloom. Get up there if you can.

We hit the "Elbe Train" on the way home, though the relative was not
pleased with overgrowth on the trail! Great stamp as I have a 2 year
old train addict. It got stamped all over her body!

A nice day, and letterboxing to boot!

Cami the strgzrmomma and family
