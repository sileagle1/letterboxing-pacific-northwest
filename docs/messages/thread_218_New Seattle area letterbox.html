Snoopy Come Home Letterbox
Snoopy was looking for a place that would allow dogs and he found one
such place here in the Seattle area. You just have to go to the Park
that has a trail where you could find Snoopy. Trail maps are
available online. Once on that trail look for the chain link fence.
Could the Daisy Hill Puppy Farm be beyond that fence? Snoopy is
sitting at the base of a 2-in-1 tree waiting for you to find him!
