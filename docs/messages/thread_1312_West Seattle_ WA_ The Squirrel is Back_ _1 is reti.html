West Seattle, WA: The Squirrel is Back, #1 is retired.

From: samanark (samanark@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-05-02 23:42:15 UTC
I have done some updating to my Schmitz Park clues. The Squirrel is
back with a new and improved stamp. Unfortunately, when I went to
replace the squirrel, I found that the Schmitz Park #1 box was also
missing. I had replaced this box 3 times already, so I have gone
ahead and retired it after a nearly 3 year run (It was originally
planted in June of 2001). Thanks to everyone who visited and enjoyed
the Original Schmitz Park 3 letterboxes. Schmitz Park is now home to
two letterboxes and I have revised the clues to reflect this change.
(I have also shortened the walk a bit)

So if you are coming over to West Seattle to find some of the new
boxes over here, you might want to drop by Schmitz Park and stamp
into the NEW and IMPROVED squirrel! I also saw some hitchhikers in
the area!

Also be aware that there is a geocache in the park that has multiple
waypoints. One of those waypoints is close to the Schmitz Park
Squirrel, so please please please rehide the box very well and be
discreet when you are stamping in. Thanks so much!

-Amanda from Seattle
