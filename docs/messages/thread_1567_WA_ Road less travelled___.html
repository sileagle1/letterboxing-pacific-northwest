WA: Road less travelled...

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-01 15:56:53 UTC
Okay, for a quick update on some boxes we visited along the way home:

Along the oft travelled route:
Happy Postman--(5 of 6: Big Bad Wolf appears to be missing)
Cherokee Flag
AIDS awareness
Don't pass go...
Lewisville Gathering
Lucia Falls
Luv Ya
Moultan Falls
--we weren't so luck with Chelatchie Railroad--not sure if we were
stumped or if it was the stump that stumped us.

THEN, we took the path less travelled:

We spelunked with a member of the pongidae family, then we soared
with an eagle near the cliffs. Next, it was off to a beautiful
majestic sunrise, capped with an incredible view of The Mountain and
our adventures ended with a great hike to an alpine lake adjacent a
gorgeous cascade Meadow. The lake was named for sheep but that's not
what we found in it--instead we found a man swimming in his birthday
suit! Talk about an adventure!!! Not exactly what the Green Turtle
experienced on his descent from denny creek a few years back but
definitely something fairly close to that. Only this man wasn't
wearing hiking boots, either!!! ;) We snuck in a couple of other
incredibly memorable hikes around these adventures but these were
the ones that resulted in 'lasting' impressions. ;)

All in all, a great drive home to cap an extraordinary Stamp Camp
weekend! If you're ever looking for a way to get out and see
nature's gems (minus the hoards of tourists) this route is
definitely THE way to go!!!

dvn2r ckr
