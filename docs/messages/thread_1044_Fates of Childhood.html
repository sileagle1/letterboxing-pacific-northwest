Fates of Childhood

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2003-12-30 14:59:21 UTC-08:00
We took the kids to the Childrens Museum today. We were flabbergasted to find the Museum, Zoo, and all surrounding areas buried under 4 inches of ice and snow. The kids had a great time at the museum and I couldn't let the chance to find a box go by. So I grabbed my bag out of the car and followed the clues to look for Puff the Magic Dragon. After a sudden inspiration last night, the clues led right to the poor cold dragon. I warmed him up between my numb hands (silly dragon wouldnt't even warm them up for me) before returning him to his VERY icy hiding space.

It was cold enough that I wasn't going to attempt the rest of the series but was very happy to find one box!

Melanie of the frozen fingers
