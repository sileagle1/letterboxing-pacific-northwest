Psalm series across US -- whaddeyathink?

From: Susan Johnson (susan@kuku.org) | Date: 2003-07-15 18:19:57 UTC
I had this idea for a series of letterboxes on the psalms.

There are 150 psalms -- could be even more than 150, as Psalm 119 has
22 parts to it and is very long!

I was thinking that I could organize it and people across the country
could volunteer to do a box for a psalm where they live, hopefully
tying in the theme of the psalm -- mountains mentioned, box near
mountains; desert, near desert-type landscape (like Eastern Oregon,
for example.)

After every psalm was taken, people would create boxes & clues, then
I would have a listing on my own website, with one link on
letterboxing.org to my website. This would help the webmasters'
problems.

People who were interested or travel alot could even have a "psalm
book" -- a special logbook for the psalm boxes.

Since there are so many psalms, I see it encompassing the entire
country.

Even if someone wasn't Jewish or Christian, they still might want to
place and/or find in this series.

I am asking the LbPNW list first to find out your thoughts on this
before I start the organization and planning prior to posting on LbNA
list.

Please let me know if you think it is even feasible, sounds like a
good idea, etc.

Thanks so much for your thoughts and time!

Susan Johnson susan@kuku.org
