Garlic Breath revisited 5/16

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2007-04-05 17:05:58 UTC
My garlic emissions rating has finally declined from yesterday. In
case anyone wants to up their rating, there will be another fine
opportunity on May 16th. The famed retired LbNA webmaster, Wes
Garrison, will be in town for a conference and will be able to tear
himself away from his busy schedule for a meet and greet that night.

For those of you who don't know, in 2003 Wes was responsible for a
major LbNA website redesign that made it possible for all of us to
enter our clues by ourselves. He kept the pleasing Old World look of
the pages (designed by our own Der Mad Stamper) but made them user
friendly. Prior to that time we were at the mercy of volunteer
webmasters who didn't always have the time to enter new data on the
site. Wes' innovation was a giant leap forward.

Let's thank him in PNW style at the Laurelwood Pub, 1728 NE 40th,
Portland, at 6 PM, May 16th. (Sorry this couldn't be planned for a
weekend due to conflicting conference events) This is a family
friendly spot with good food and brew. Who's in???
