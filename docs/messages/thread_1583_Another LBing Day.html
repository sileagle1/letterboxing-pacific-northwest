Another LBing Day

From: Wood Fmily (letterboxing2004@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-06 20:16:43 UTC-07:00
Today TeamWARE headed up to Mt. Hood for some fun,
sun, and letterboxing. Around 10:30a we hit the Camp
Fire box but much to our surprise....the sites were
full and there was no way around to get to the box
without crossing every single piece of rented space.
ARGH!

We decided to forego the box and head to Timberline to
ride the Mile High Chair Lift to the top! WOOHOO!!
We are sunburned but had a BLAST and took a ton of
pictures! We ate lunch at home base then planted a
box (clues forthcoming). This has been a spot I've
desired to visit since we moved here two years ago.

Anyway, after some hiking we headed over to Ski Bowl
for a ride down the Alpine Slide! The price is
highway robbery but it was SO MUCH FUN!!!!

I figured the kiddo's could handle a trip around
Trillium Lake for a series of 6 of the same name.
Needless to say the disappointment of this hike was
great. Of the six, we found two. One was confirmed
lost before we started but that's not bad out of a
series of 6. However, the clues were a bit muddied
for box #1 and that was not a great way to start.
Anyway, we grabbed box #2 (which was found left wide
open to be found by anyone and everyone) and used the
last available pages in the log book, #3 is confirmed
missing by us and also logged in as missing when we
hit box #4 which is still there and had a full log
book. #5 was confirmed missing prior to our arrival
but what we found at box #6 was disturbing. After
searching three places that fit the clues we came upon
what was a perfect match but there was no box.
Hmmmm....what are all these little papers ripped up
and laying on the ground. Oh, and the lid to a letter
box. Oh, and the bottom to a letter box. Oh, and the
cover to the log book. Yes, you guessed it -
VANDALIZED......how sad and disappointing.

After that trip I was able to photograph Trillium Lake
with a mirror image of Mt. Hood in the water! It's
postcard picture perfect and I'm hanging it proudly on
my wall!!!

Well, it was late so we headed back to Rhododendron to
pick up Camp Fire. Luckily MouseAttack has the eye of
a letterboxer and found it lickity split! It was
great to pick up a stamp from a boxer we had never
found a box from before.

So anyway, we're back home after 12 hours of being
gone. We planted, we stamped in, rode high in the
sky, came down the hill fast, and even played in the
snow on September 6th! What an AWESOME last day of
summer vacation!

LILY
and TEAM WARE
