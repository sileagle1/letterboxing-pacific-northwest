From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-07-10 05:43:55 UTC
Have you seen Jay's post on the main Talk List? Does this mean we are
about to lose the clues to Der Mad Stamper's 50+ boxes??? (not to
mention his others in other states) I'm horrified at the possibility.
He hasn't been active for the longest time, may well be totally
unaware of the new website.

A few of his boxes are gone, a couple have been replaced by Ryan but
the majority of his boxes are so well hidden that they are still up
and running. Last year, tracking down his North/Central Kansas boxes
gave me a unique tour of his home state. He and Tom Cheney are the
pioneers of Oregon letterboxing. Having his clues disappear would be
the worst! Funhog
