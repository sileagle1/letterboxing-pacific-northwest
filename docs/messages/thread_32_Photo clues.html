Photo clues

From: hoopoe4me (scribenbirder@msn.com) | Date: 2003-04-01 04:56:19 UTC
The national Orienteering magazine has an annual contest that is a
photo clues one.

For those not familiar with orienteering, the maps are highly
detailed with features found in the environment. Using the photos
you found the spot on the map. Usually a series of about 15 photos
made up a complete course. I could rarely get more than 5 or 6 spots
identified, though I do not think I ever really TRIED hard enough.

I like the idea of a photo challenge. If somehow thumbnail size
photos could be used, perhaps 4 per page.

Or photos get you to a location then you have a written clue then a
photo etc.

It could be very challenging in an environment where the plants and
trees change with the seasons.

Hint - o - the- day. A large ziplock bag is a great rain cover for
clues.
