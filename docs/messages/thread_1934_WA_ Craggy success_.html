WA: Craggy success!

From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2005-02-21 07:01:49 UTC

Speaking of which... I guess Der Mad Stamper and I both
qualify as brave/silly since we nabbed elusive African
game this very afternoon on one of the Tortuga's forced
marches. Up, up, up we went to the summit of Hamilton
Mountain on a gorgeous winter afternoon. We were
rewarded with a stamp image in our logs that has only
graced the pages of a scant few other boxers' journals.
This gem has been in place nearly three years and has
only seen the light of day three times! It actually took an
entire year before the first intrepid souls proved that this
was a truly a 'placed' letterbox. Funhog
