Salmon Creek Greenway Trail Closing for Repairs Starting October 1



Clark County Public Works will close the Salmon Creek Greenway Trail for
four days starting this week. The trail will be closed between Northwest
36th Avenue in Felida and the pond just west of the Vancouver Girls Softball
Association fields.



The closures are necessary so park maintenance crews can repair sections of
the asphalt trail damaged by tree roots.



The trail will be closed from approximately 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. on each of the
following days:



Wednesday, October 1

Thursday, October 2

Tuesday, October 7

Wednesday, October 8



The trail will be open to the public outside these dates and times. However,
the pavement will be in various stages of removal and resurfacing and
walkers and riders should use caution.



The pavement repair work is dependent on a number of factors, including
weather, and may be rescheduled if necessary.



For more information about the closure or the repair work, please contact
Clark County Public Works Maintenance and Operations at (360) 397-2446.
