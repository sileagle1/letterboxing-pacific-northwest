Thanks for the update on the boxes, Maiden. It feels much better to come
home from Lbing with something to show for it than skunked... especially
when it's cold out!

Here's the last several weeks adventures in Vancouver. Ladybug, Wood-aug and
I went out several times. We replaced some plastic bags and cleaned up some
boxes but now I can't remember what was done to which boxes.

Found:
- Report Card - clever!
- Fort Vancouver - great hide!
- Bookworm
- Live to Ride, ride to Live
- Paradise
- Corp of Discovery - stopped by to get a color rendering of this neat
stamp. Found the lid had a hole in it which turned it into a beetle trap,
complete with live beetles. We didn't have a box with us (never thought of
carrying an extra). Thankfully the oh-so-appropriate log book is dry and in
good shape.

Attempts:
- Maiden's Birthday Series box 1 (is it there? ... maybe they took out the
tree ...?)
- NW Wildlife Series *Bear* - spent lots of energy on this one... only found
poplars, not aspen trees. Is it there?
- Trees where important too.

For those that write directly please note my new email addy. We finally
have cable... Woo Hoo, now we're part of the new century!

Hope to hear about the status of more boxes. ... and hear if the boxes we
attempted are there.

Cheers!
SloMo
