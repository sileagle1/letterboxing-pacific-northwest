patches for LbPNW?

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@letterboxing.info) | Date: 2005-09-14 09:15:10 UTC-04:00
The Great Lakes and Texas just had some cool patches made with their
group logos, and Mid-Atlantic and Northeast put out patches earlier
this year, so I think it's time that all the regional groups should
have patches made. They're usually less than $2 each, and my pack
looks great with patches from all the areas where I've letterboxed,
along with the LbNA patch. And they do work -- I did meet
letterboxers on a trail once who introduced themselves after seeing
the letterboxing patch on my pack!

Has anyone in this group given any thought to making a cool logo and
patch for this group? Clueless and I have boxed in the PNW, so we'd
certainly be interested in a couple of them. If there's some creative
and industrious person out there who would like to take on the
project -- I'll be happy to put you in touch with some other boxers
who have had patches made so you can quickly research providers. The
LbMA and LbNE groups let members submit ideas for logos, and then
members voted on them before they created their patches.
