Greetings all! Let it be known that the Kelso Longview
Parks and Recreation class "Letterboxing A to Z" is
being offered once again, taught by yours truly. This

boxers. There are three sessions that cover carving,

Longview, WA.

If you have an interest in this call Karry Williquette at
360.442.5409. There is a charge and the price includes
tools, carving media and bookbinding supplies. I hope
to see some of you there. Funhog
