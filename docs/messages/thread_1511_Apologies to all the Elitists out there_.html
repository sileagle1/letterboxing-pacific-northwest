Apologies to all the Elitists out there!

From: pandora{I`B} (hispandora@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-08-09 23:51:23 UTC-07:00
Okay I've had it - I give in - I understand now - I surrender. - So long
ago I spoke out against the elitist attitude, I felt we should embrace
newbies, I thought the secretiveness of letterboxing was over rated. I give
now, I wave my white flag - I was wrong.



In a matter of weeks I have heard of the following



People moving the debris of the boxes as a favor to the placer

People altering the image in a box

People relocating the boxes



8 of 12 of my California boxes have now been found within a week, out in the
open, boxes open, stamps gone



Tonight I just got an email from a geocacher informing me they took my stamp
as a trinket and left toys in my box..



I get it - I understand - Its no wonder old time boxers were annoyed with a
new generation -

It's getting worse and worse, more ridiculous and more ridiculous.



I apologize to everyone I ever called an elitist and told to chill out - I
get it now. I would say you should have given me a clue - but I guess you
all did, I was just too busy following the ones for boxes!



Pandora
