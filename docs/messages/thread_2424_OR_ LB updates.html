Enjoying time in Oregon's Wine Country for a few days and had the
pleasure of finding a couple wonderful letterboxes that really merit
more attention than they're receiving. I hope Funhog doesn't mind
me posting this about them. If you want to enjoy some great views--
check out the great letterboxes at Bald Peak. Not sure where the
clue to one of them is anymore (I stumbled upon it in a book of
clues I pre-printed about 3-1/2 years ago) but they're probably
retrievable if you search through the letterbox-usa archive from
back in 2002 sometime.
::
If you're into birding or gorgeous views of the river or hills,
check out Funhog's Bluebird Trail letterbox in Shampooey. ;) We
didn't see any bluebirds at that park but we were extremely
fortunate to get to see about a dozen of them while boxing atop Bald
Peak--so work the two outings together if you must go during 'off'
bluebird season. It was odd that there weren't more visitors to
this box--given its close proximity to the metro area and its
gorgeous venue with great visitor center. I faintly recall a note
about somebody not being able to find it years ago and have a
sneaking suspicion that this 'false missing' report wound its way
into the hearts and minds of area boxers--thus leading to the mere
trickle of visitors over the years. Take joy, it is alive &
well!!! The clues, venues & stamps of these letterboxes are SO
WORTH the effort to track them down! Enjoy your adventure!!!
