*This message was transferred with a trial version of CommuniGate(tm) Pro* Hi fellow Puget Sound letterboxers out there (and any other
letterboxers who might be interested)!

Are any other Puget Sound letterboxers interested in attending a
South Sound Letterboxing Fest o/a the morning of 9 Aug 03?

***SOUTH SOUND LETTERBOXING FEST 9 AUG 03***

I was thinking of a quick get-together somewhere in the South Sound
area (Olympia/Tacoma area) with a focus of exchanging signature
stamps/hitchhikers/event letterboxes you might have hanging around
your house, hitchhikers you want to launch to the 4 winds, etc.
Wouldn't need to plant any boxes specific to the event--just an
opportunity to meet with fellow letterboxers and exchange stamps,
carving ideas, clue writing hints, etc for a couple of hours.

Let me know if there is any interest out there--I've heard from a
couple of folks off-line already--just want to know if there are
others out there. The primary 'gathering' location I was thinking
of was Tumwater's Historical Park just off of I-5 in the
Olympia/Tumwater area. It offers a great picnic/playground area
with running water & flushing toilets!!! ;) If there is enough
interest & you want to extend the length of the event, we could
stretch it into a potluck style picnic. This park is quite
centrally located to MANY, MANY, MANY letterboxes in the Olympia
area.

***TUMWATER HISTORICAL PARK, TUMWATER, WA***

Another possible meeting location would be Pt Defiance Park in
Tacoma but it is often QUITE busy on the weekends with only one spot
with a playground (near the back entrance to the zoo).

Please let me know (or, better, yet, post your comments on the board
so others get the feel for the number of interested letterboxers out
there):

1. If you are interested in attending.
2. Whether you are able to attend on 9 Aug 03 (approx 0900-1000
start time and ending whenever), if not--another more feasible
date/time for your group.
3. # in your party

This is also a great time to invite your friends who might be
interested in letterboxing. What a great time to start the hobby
with a logbook full of exchanges!!!

Hope to here from many of you out there soon.

dvn2r ckr

Re: WA: South Sound LB Fest 9 Aug anyone???

From: Fudrick (fudrick@comcast.net) | Date: 2003-08-01 20:29:13 UTC-07:00
Stopped by the Tumwater Historical Park on the way home this evening. A
great place to meet!

There are 7 picnic tables by the playground, should be enough to hold all of
us. There are 5 or so tables behind the rest rooms (with flush toilets!!!)
from the playground if the first ones are already full. They are
first-come, first-eat tables, so those early risers can save the tables for
the rest. There is a covered area, but it requires a reservation and a fee
:-(

I will be in that area again Sunday, will verify the best way to get there
from both North and South bound I-5, will post Sunday evening to this fine
chat group, so you all won't miss all the fun (unless all the Fun is Hogged
by the time you get there :-) It is an easy 2 hours from Portland (104
miles of freeway from the Columbia River), a long hour from Seattle (65
miles from downtown), so don't let the distance keep you from coming.

The Potluck should probably start a bit after Noon, to accommodate those who
get a late start, and so we can enjoy NailHeads company.

Check out the many boxes in the Thurston County area. Decide on which ones
you are interested in, I will have a map, can show you the 'best' way to
get there and on to the next. Guide services may be available :-0

Plan on attending and seeing the strange people behind the stamps you keep
finding!

Fudrick
