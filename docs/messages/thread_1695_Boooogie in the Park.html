From: RayvenDad (Team Rayvenhaus) (rayvenhaus@lbworld.org) | Date: 2004-10-13 09:22:56 UTC-07:00
Thanks for asking. It's never wrong to ask questions in my book.
Basically, you bring one item that you'd like to share and make enough to
feed a group of people. Since the event is scheduled through lunch time,
we're having the potluck. In the past Team Rayvenhaus has supplied
burgers and dogs for all to feast on, however, this year times are very,
very lean and we just can't afford it this time.

It's not really a balanced table at all at a potluck kinda thing, it's
more the hit or miss kinda thing really. (smiles)

There's no wrong way to do it really. Some bring enough just for them if
they have special dietary requirements. Other bring enough of something to
feed an army. Others bring barely enough to share and, of course, that's
what everyone wants. (lol) You put what you bring on the table and
everyone shares. I think we're going to bring the (in)Famous Rayvenhaus
Baked Beans and maybe rolls.

I'll say this much... It'll all work out and the important thing is that
you attend. Getting to meet the wonderful people in this area are what's
important. ;o)
