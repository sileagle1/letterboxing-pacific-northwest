From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-02-16 20:54:40 UTC-08:00
We too had a wonderful weekend of boxing. After hiding 3 boxes on Sunday,
we traveled up to Olympia today and tried for two boxes in the pourding
rain. The patriot box was not there. I found the right location but no
box. We then headed over for Hedwige's Journal. My 4 year old son and I
had a great time playing James. Bond. Senate was in session so there were
State Troopers crawling all over the capital. A set in full riot gear were
going in the exact path to the box. We stood around looking at statues
trying to look inconspicous while we waited for them to leave so we could
get down there. We did in fact get it without being spotted andn got it
returned with no problem. Very cute stamp. We made a few other stops and
dropped off a Candid Camera in one of them. That thing sure has a tricky
button.. it goes off at the most inopprotune moments ;)

Melanie
