Just a note for anyone who might venture off to Longview, Washington,
for the Book Keeper letterbox:

The clues for the Count of Monte Cristo box (the clues are inside the
Book Keeper box) are a special version. They will not be reprinted
like they are currently. We (Ladybug and I) wanted to do something
special, but it was rather time consuming and a bit painful, truth be
told. So I guess you could say these clues are limited edition, and
quite nifty if I say so myself. :)

Just thought I'd let people know.

Jill aka Lillbit
