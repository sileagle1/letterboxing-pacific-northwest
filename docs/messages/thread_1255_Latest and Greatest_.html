Latest and Greatest!

From: Rayvenhaus (rayvenhaus@myndworx.com) | Date: 2004-04-04 12:03:25 UTC-07:00
Just a quick note to let every know that the following states have been
added to and are now accessible by, the Database at the NLC website:

Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, Ohio and Wisconsin (Region 7)
California, Hawaii and Nevada (Region 2)

(I expect to have Oklahoma and Texas completed within a week and online as
well.)

We've optimized the search routines and added in a function to control the
number of results displayed per page. Also, a listing of the total number
of boxes per placer as been recoded and optimized.

Currently, the search by State/County/City is offline while we recode it to
make it easier and faster loading.

And lastly, the country of Canada is nearing completion and will online soon
and open for people, who wish to, to add boxes located within it's
boundaries.
