The very first letterbox I found was in pieces- about a dozen of them.
There were supposed to be several stamps in it, but all I found was the
letterbox lid, the shards of the box, and one stamp all laying on the
ground in the mud. I collected the pieces and took them home with me, and
emailed the box placer.

It's been a few weeks now and I've heard no response. I'd like to make it
known that it's missing so people don't look for it, but I'm not sure what
the generally accepted protocol is on that.

Thoughts?
