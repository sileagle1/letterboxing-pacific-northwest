Just got home from checking on my boxes in the park. Sad to say that
the Amalgam Series has been decimated. Only one of the four boxes is
still there. Funny thing is that that was probably the second most
vulnerable box in the group. The third and fourth which were in my
opinion the hardest to get are totally missing...no trace. The first
box's hiding spot has been totally dug out all the way underneath
the tree. I don't know if an animal dug it out or a human did.

I have reconitierred the trail and have came up with some alternate
locations. Hopefully I can get the boxes replaced this coming
weekend.

We checked on the Lost Mariner and he is not lost. There are also
two types of "Bears" hidden in the park. The Polar one is missing
again and the other one can't be reached via the route given in the
clues due to fencing that has recently been installed. I will send a
message to the placer and let him know.

Oh yeah, on the bright side we picked up the Moaning Myrtle HH at
the Pt. Pa goda box.

We checked the first of the Bowl boxes and it is confirmed missing.
Of course it didn't help matters this afternoon when just as we were
coming away from it's hiding spot we looked up and there was a parks
person looking at us from his truck. He really gave us a dirty look
since we actually had the dogs offleash (yes we know it is a no-no)
and if he had followed us he would of found out that we were parked
illegally too. Both CLEB and myself (and the dogs) ran back to the
truck as fast as we could.
