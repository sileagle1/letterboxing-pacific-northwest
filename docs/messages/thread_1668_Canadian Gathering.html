You know, for the longest time I didn't believe it ever rained in
Washington! We'd been letterboxing for several months before we
ever had to don rain gear on the trail and that first hit us while
boxing down around Portland/Vernonia/Banks area. Then, it still
didn't rain on us on the trail in WA for quite some time. However,
we did get hit by massive deluges letterboxing in DC/VA/MD/NJ/NY in
Oct 02. Those outings not only required raincoats but UMBRELLAS, as
well! How dorky! But--the nice thing about boxing in the rain is
that you don't have to be as discrete along the way, because NOBODY
is watching you!!! ;)

From: Secret Garden B&B (secretgardenbnb@shaw.ca) | Date: 2004-10-15 07:35:00 UTC-07:00


Hello everyone and thank you for all the kind words following what we all
consider was, "the first Canadian Gathering".
Chris and I had a lot of fun planning - or should that be planting, prior to
the event and apologize for the weather, but what can we say.

Shortly after we confirmed hosting the event was when we got word that we
would be taking an extended vacation and suddenly our lives - or at least
mine did - became a bit muddled in the question of how to fit all of our
"stuff" into a 20 foot container !@# What to take what to leave, who would
take the koi, the placostomas, cockatiel - even my mother!!!!!

Thus books, stamps and clues were set at the back of the burner. However,
hope you all understood and I promise that when we host the first Barbados
Gathering, the books and stamps will be top notch!!!! :0) Perhaps even as
good as Ryans!!!! Hey Ryan, better start working on that event stamp now -
and - saving your $ so you can come.

We also enjoyed seeing everyone again and meeting some new people, we'll
keep in touch and hope you all will too.

Till we meet again on the boxing trail - happy hunting !
Jane and Chris
The Secret Gardeners.
