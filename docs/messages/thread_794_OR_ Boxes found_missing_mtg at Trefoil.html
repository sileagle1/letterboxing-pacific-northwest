Recently, Heather, kids, & I had a lovely time saving children's songbook
characters by following Pandora's clues. Unfortunately, we are quite sure that
Puff has taken a powder. (Pandy, I will e-mail you off-list with specifics as
to where we looked.) But the remaining 3 in the #1 Adventure are in place,
and we also just had time to squeeze in the first box from #2 Adventure before
we were forced by time constraints to call a halt. Can't wait to get back and
follow this series--such clever clues and GREAT stamps. A big and wonderful
effort by Pandy that moms & kids alike appreciated!

On Friday, we'd gone after the Trefoil LB and were annoyed to see that some
people had stopped on the trail, right near the log where we were sure the box
was waiting for us. Some bird watchers or a class identifying plants...or
some people with stamps and baggies--hey! Our first exchange in the field! It
was the Bishop Clan (from out of town) and the Dixon Clan (who planted the
Browns Ferry LB in a wonderful little park I'd never knew existed--take your
binocs when you go!).

Between our trail exchange and the recent Fleshmens-Lennins/Funhog meeting,
it's getting kinda Connecticutty out here!
