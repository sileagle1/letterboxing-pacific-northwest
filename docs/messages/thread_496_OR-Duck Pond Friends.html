Hi Carie & Hoopoe,

A few days ago I chatted with one of the people who works there & she said
the ponds naturally drain this time of year (one pond has a small spring, so
that one continues to be at least mucky thru out the year). When the ELC was
privately managed, they used to pump in city water--so the ponds were full year
round. Now they intend to let the one non-artisian-supplied pond become an
emergent wetland. Some ducks are still there--others probably took off for a
wetter place. Now there are 4 chickens as well-- some yahoo let them loose
there. If anybody wants 'em, the staff would be happy to see them go!
