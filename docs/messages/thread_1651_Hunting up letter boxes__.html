Hi All,
We, the RHOG Stamper and I, had a great Friday of letterboxing. We left
Teri-Anne the Packdog home, the RHOG stamper has a new car and doesn't want
dog hair in it, imagine that? I don't think that I have ever bought a car
that wasn't bought for the comfort of my dogs... but I digress...
First we fed the ducks while looking for the Quackerbox series in Albany,
this is where I was chased by a flock of geese... very exciting!!! Then we
were off to find the Bookworm letterbox also in Albany, which by the way is
really cool and worth finding!!!
Then it was off to Eugene for Bucky the Beaver, The Eugene Skinner box , the
Sisters (which we couldn't find) and then back to Peoria for the Snab Boat
bend (replacement). All in all it was a great letterboxing day!!! We had
very nice weather and the sights at Skinners Butte, although kind of smoggy
was nice.
I hope everyone else had as nice a weekend and I.
Happy Hunting
Scottish Amy
