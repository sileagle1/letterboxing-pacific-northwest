Olympia Tour, was Batty's Back

From: Nail Family (c.nail@attbi.com) | Date: 2003-09-22 03:46:20 UTC
Well, Bethany, we must have missed you by mere hours! Stopped by to
view a new addition to the wet menagerie late this afternoon. Not at
all surprised to find your stamps still wet in the bonus logbook.
Had I known that there was a replacement hippo, I would have nabbed
that too. Seems I am following your footsteps all around Olympia
this weekend. Got out to the capitol and got all 4 of the new boxes
there. Looks like #2 of the moon tree eluded you. We got some
funny looks ourselves by the state patrolman driving around the
area. We think we spotted another letterboxer who spotted us and
decided to leave the area and come back later. Too bad we didnt get
a chance to ask about exchanges.

We also finally solved an Orca Mystery. Had it figured out the
first time I read the clues but havent had a chance to get to that
location until today. Nice stamp. Great location. Tough to be
secret there but kids always help to run diversions.

All in all 4 out of 7 Nails got 6 new finds today! A great day for
inky fingers!

Nailhead
