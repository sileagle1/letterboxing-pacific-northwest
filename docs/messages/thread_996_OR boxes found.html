Thursday evening I headed across the Cascades to call on customers in
Central Oregon. Friday's schedule was absolutely grueling: pastry at
the Sisters Bakery, The A Day in Sisters Letterbox plus a HH, a
customer, The Cold Springs Letterbox, another customer, The Calliope
Crossing Letterbox, and then...another customer. Now that's my idea of
a business trip: Changing from shoes to boots every hour or two. I
especially like the clever clue for the Cold Springs box and the
lettering on the Sisters stamp. Many thanks to Birder and the
Fleshmans for contributing to a very successful day.

Saturday entailed a trip back over Mt. Hood to attend the Roadhouse
Gathering. I second everything said by Paisley Orca. I had a grand
time meeting everyone and was only sorry that it wasn't long enough to
really sit down and talk to everyone.

After the Gathering died down, I dragged the famed Ryan and Amanda
back to my house for a head-chopping game of guillotine. This morning,
we solved a letterboxing Conundrum and visited some old friends to
release some HHs. Funhog
