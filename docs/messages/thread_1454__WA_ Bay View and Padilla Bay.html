We checked our Bay View and Padilla Bay boxes, on our annual camping
trip to that
beautiful area. They are both in fine shape and exactly where we
placed them. Clearly,
Skagit County attracts a high caliber class of letterboxer.

We removed and gave to our kids plastic trinkets which had been left
by friendly
geocachers, who thankfully did NOT take anything in return and had
signed in politely (a
high class of geocacher, too). We were pleased to see that our little
boxes have been
visited by folks from as far away as Massachusetts and Colorado, as
well as several locals.

We also set the "Farewell Daylight" hitchhiker on its way somewhere
in the area.

Mariette, wishing she could have stayed longer by the bay
