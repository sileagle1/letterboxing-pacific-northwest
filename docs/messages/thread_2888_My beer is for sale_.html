My beer is for sale!

From: Pungent Bob (PungentBob@HotPOP.com) | Date: 2008-02-15 18:11:59 UTC
Last summer a local restaurant asked me to brew beer for them. After
they jumped through all the licensing hoops, my beer went on sale last
November and was on sale for just a couple of days before the flood
hit Vernonia and wiped out the restaurant. They finished the repairs
and reopened last night!

For those interested, the restaurant is the Blue House Cafe which is
located a few miles north of Vernonia, OR on highway 47. It serves
very good Mediterranean food and is worth visiting just for that.
Beers on sale now are an oatmeal porter and a pale ale. In waiting are
a Scottish ale and a cherry vanilla wheat beer.
