From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2004-04-18 08:38:42 UTC-07:00
Hey All!

It is with great sadness that I inform you that reports of Van Duzer
Corridor and Devil's Punchbowl Letterboxes missing are true. We checked
both out on Friday afternoon and they are gone, gone, gone.

It really breaks my heart, especially Devil's Punchbowl, because I think
it is my very favorite of letterboxes that I've ever placed. I had so
much fun carving the stamp. Now he's probably back in hell and won't be
bothering anyone with his cup of brew any more.

Clues will be removed from the main site when I can get to a real
computer. Hopefully sometime next week.

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
