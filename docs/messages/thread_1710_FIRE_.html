Hi all,
We went up to Fall Creek Res to plant a box for our Campin with JR
Series,(clues will be up shortly). Any ways there was a storm through the area
on Saturday night and when we got up there on Sunday morning we found a
tree that had fallen and was leaning on some power lines and the tree was on
fire! We called 911 and they sent the Fire Department up. It was pretty wild,
we have never prevented a forest fire before! There were several fires in the
area and it prevented us from getting where we wanted to place our box, but
we found a good place and all is well.
