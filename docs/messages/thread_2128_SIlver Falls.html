From: Colleen (colleendurham@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-07-04 21:52:08 UTC-07:00
Hey Silver Falls Letterboxers--

Thank you all, for a delightful weekend. DB and I finally rolled into our Spokane driveway about 8:00PM. We especially enjoyed putting faces to some of our favorite box planters and posters. A special 'thankyou' to DMS and Funhog for the impromptu carving tutorial, although I must say, waiting in the woods for DB to re-create it in all it's 4 color wonder did try my patience! :) The doughnuts were great, perhaps the best we'd ever had!

We were struck as we drove back, at the spectacular beauty of Silver Falls, thanks Brett, for picking such a great spot, the generous enthusiasm that welcomed us, good food and of course, great company! I also think I've uncovered another layer of letterboxing...watch out Spokane, the game just gets better and better!
colls
