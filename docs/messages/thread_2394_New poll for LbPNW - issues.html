Hi Everyone,

I love the idea of a LbPNW Patch and also the idea of a poll to
determine the favorite. Thanks to everyone for submitting their
really cool designs.

I do feel compelled to point out however that a simple 1-person, 1-
vote poll when there are 12 different entries is probably not the
best way to determine an overall consensus. For example if the poll
ended today, the winner would have only 27% of the vote -- meaning
that three-quarters of people voted for something else.

In these kind of cases, there are much better ways of getting a true
sense of what the group desires. In no particular order:

1. We could use a weighted vote in which people could vote for
multiple entries; for example, someone could vote for their favorite
(3 points), their 2nd favorite (2 points), and their 3rd favorite (1
point). You get a much better idea this way of which designs have
broad appeal.

2. We could use "approval voting", in which everyone can have as
many votes as they want; for example, for EACH entry, 1 through 12,
you can vote whether it is acceptable or not. If you like them ALL,
then each gets a vote. If you think 4 of them are acceptable, only
vote for those 4. If you like only 1, then just vote for it.

3. We could use a run-off system. This might be the easiest system
if we want to use Yahoo's automated polling feature. For example,
after this poll closes, we take the top 5 vote-getters (or all
entries that capture say, at least 10% of the total vote) and vote
again. If no single entry gets more than half of the vote, then more
choices are eliminated and then we vote again until we have a
majority winner.

If there is a stalemate, it goes to the House of Representatives and
they decide.

Just kidding. I don't mean to stir up trouble or take this whole
voting thing too seriously, but I do strongly believe that simple 1-
vote elections with a huge number of entries are highly
undemocratic.

There are also a few other issues like the fact that Thunderbird's
patch 2 and patch 3 are VERY similar to one another. Each one
separately is currently in 6th or 7th place in the voting, but if you
added the votes together, it would be almost tied for 2nd.

Just my 2 cents....


Green Guillemot
