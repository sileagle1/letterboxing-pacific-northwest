From: (RiskyNil@pocketmail.com) | Date: 2003-05-29 14:10:53 UTC-07:00
If my letterboxing memory holds up correctly, the Beneath the Blue that I found at the state park--I forget which number was assigned to it--was rather a challenge to find.

First is figuring out exactly the right spot. Several places along the way you think, 'Ah, this is it!', but it wasn't. Keep going....

Parts of the trail were rather waterlogged as well, which made the hike itself more difficult than anticipated.

And even when I did get to the right place, I kept thinking that I must have done something wrong, because Funhog NEVER places a letterbox so far off the trail. But she did.... =)

And FINALLY, when I did find the box, it was half burried by eroded setiments and I actually had to dig it out to extract it from it's spot. I would NOT be surprised if it's completely burried by now. Bring a good sharp stick and poke around and see if you strike burried treasure.

I sincerely doubt the box was missing--it was so covered by sediment that I had trouble finding it even when I was looking for it. I can't imagine anyone found it by accident!

-- Ryan
