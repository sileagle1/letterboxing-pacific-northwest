The missing Mt. Tabor stamp has been replaced!

From: Sunshine (letterboxing8675309@hotmail.com) | Date: 2004-07-12 11:41:59 UTC-07:00
I went up Mt. Tabor a couple of weeks ago in search of Der Mad Stamper's
classic letterbox, but unfortunately the stamp was MISSING! I was unable to
contact DMS even with multiple email addresses, but after careful
consideration and good advice from Ryan and Funhog, we decided that it would
be best to make a reproduction of the original stamp. The log book was
almost full, so that has also been replaced. (If anyone knows how I can get
in touch with Der Mad Stamper, please let me know--I would like to send the
log book to him.)

The Mt. Tabor box gets a lot of visitors, so I am very happy to report that
it is again alive and well! And would you believe that this letterbox just
turned five years old on July 8? Even if you've been there before, maybe
the new stamp and five-year anniversary are good excuses to go visit an old
letterbox again. It's one of my favorite hiding spots and stamp designs so
far (Thanks, Mad Stamper!) so I hope you take the time to stop by and enjoy
it!

Sunshine
