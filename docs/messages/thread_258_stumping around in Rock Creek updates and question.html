stumping around in Rock Creek updates and questions

From: mudstumpers (mudstumpers@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-06-01 05:10:35 UTC
We went to PCC Rock Creek and found only #1 and #3. #1's log was
removed for drying in March 2003. #2 is truely missing! The remains
were chewed thru and nothing to be reached within. #3 was in fine
shape. Anyone found #4 recently? We may have been way off but we
know we were on the right path - Does one have to blaze their own
trail or is there now a very nice swimming pool on top of the cellar?

We then headed to the boxes at Bethany Lake. Only 1 remains - per the
series owner. #1 was retired, we got #2 where there was a note left
explaining that #3 and #4 are now gone also.

3 out of 8 not so good.

We moved on to the Rock Creek Park and found the Troll stamp quite
damp and dried it off. No one had been here since March of 2002! We
were again disapointed to see notes that the Dragon was missing and
went to see for ourselves. It looked like some locals had used the
branches to make a hideout in the suspected area where the clue took
us.

We decided to call it a day ending with a depressing 4 out of 10. We
would appreciate any insite on any of the above to ease our misery.
