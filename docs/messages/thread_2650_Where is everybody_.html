From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-10-20 16:18:15 UTC
Welcome to the wacky world of letterboxing. You may find that winter
boxing is just fine. Torrential downpours and frigid days aren't all
that common. I always keep a big sheet of plastic folded up in my
backpack in order to have a dry place to sit when stamping in. A
cheapo umbrella from the Dollar store is always there,too.

An alternative, relatively dry, place to box is on the east side of
the mountains. There are lots of great letterboxes in Central Oregon,
and lots of blue sky. It can be chilly December and January but most
of the other months aren't too bad. Enjoy! Funhog
