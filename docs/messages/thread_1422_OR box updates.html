From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-07-05 10:32:59 UTC-07:00
It in the last couple of weeks I've had the good luck to pick up several
boxes. Thank you to all the placers for another fun set of boxes.

Yaquina Head Lighthouse - Beautiful view and well visited box. I saw stamps
in the book from 12 different states. That was to much fun for words.

What's For Dinner - Box 2 log is almost full, box 3 log is full. Very cute
series. I'm dying to know what the missing box 4 was.

Anna's Hummingbirds - Very cute series, nice location. Slight mix up in
directions but I already talked to Pandora and she was getting right on
fixing those!

Care Bear Stare - Wonderful series for kids... both of mine were tickled
pink.

I'd Lasso It For You - Fun box, cute stamp, great drive by!

And I do believe that's it for now.

I also hid a box, "Can't See The Forest For The Trees". Clues to come today.
It has the first of my new special firsties in it, I hope whomever picks it
up will let me know what they think of it.

Maiden
