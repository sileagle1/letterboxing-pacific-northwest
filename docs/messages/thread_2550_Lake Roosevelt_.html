From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-22 14:41:15 UTC
Welcome to letterboxing!

I'm not familiar with any boxes in the Lake Roosevelt Natl Rec Area
either. If you do decide to plant something in that region, though, I
highly recommend that you do NOT plant anything around (remotely even
close to) the Grand Coulee Dam as it could and more likely would be
considered a security threat and treated as such.

I think there are a few boxes in/around Wenatchee (relatively close by
if it's along your route--ok so it's in a neighboring grid square on
the map but it's closer than where most folks live in the state) and a
few more (if they're still active) in Lake Chelan further north.
Otherwise, the letterbox choices in the area are slim pickins...
