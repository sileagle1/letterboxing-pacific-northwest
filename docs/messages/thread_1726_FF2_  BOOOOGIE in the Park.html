Well my group if finally rested up and almost all awake after our adventures
yesterday at FF2: Boogie in the park. I just wanted to take the time to
thank everyone that attended and made the event something to remember. It
was even better than last year!

I hope everyone is dried out after the rain and hail they went through
yesterday! It just goes to show you that some letter boxers have the
devotion of the US Postal Service. Neither Rain nor sleet nor hail shall
stop them.

Iron`Bear and I will be posting our clues soon.

A special thank you to Team Rayvenhaus for co-hosting such a wonderful event
- I can't wait until next year guys!

Just as an added note, I noticed that the event got referred to by the
copyrighted name of Fright Fest and just wanted to remind everyone that this
event was not named Fright Fest, it was named FF2: Boogie in the Park -
Echos of Times Past. FF2 stands for Family Fun 2 and next year will be
Family Fun 3. After all when you get a group 50 people together and 20 or
more of them are kids it is definitely a family affair. and guess what. they
weren't all the nail family kids, contrary to what I thought they didn't
have THAT many :) Thought the Head Nail did tell me they are cheaper by the
dozen. (laughing)

Sorry some of you couldn't make it, we really hope to see you next year.
