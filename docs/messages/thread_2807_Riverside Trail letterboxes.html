From: doubletreepfx (greenthummm@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-07-09 04:21:57 UTC
I'd recently been alerted that box #2 on Riverside Trail near
Ripplebrook, was missing. I checked on them this last Tuesday, and
sure enough, it was. There were also numerous downed trees hiding the
side-trail to #2. So I've removed it from the clues, and you now have
the option of making the hike somewhat less than the original 8.2
miles, since you need not go all the way to Riverside Campground for
that box. Now the hike is around a mere 6.5 miles around trip! Easy
cheesy. Thank you Dogglesnoffer for the heads-up.

Double Tree

Indeed it is an incredibly beautiful trail!
There's some big ol' trees out there! Lot's of trillium (purple!) and orchid's in may / june as

those weary muscles.
