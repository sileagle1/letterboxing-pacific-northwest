Carie of Lennens3 and I had a day of small portions of letterboxing and
sushi today! We went over to Whitaker Ponds (a place I'm very famililar
with since it is the home to an organization I have volunteered for
religiously for the past 4-1/2 years). We got one of Maiden's boxes,
called Garden of Love, I think. We didn't find any of the others.

After that we headed elsewhere for a wonderful box by the elusive
twosome, Miller & Hutch. SUCCESS!!! We have decided that this
enigmatic pair is much better at carving than singing, but we appreciate
the innovative clues!!! Not only that, but we absolutely love their
wonderful stamps.

I wish I could get more of their boxes, but how to access their clues is
as much a mystery to me as the placers themselves are. In any case,
thank you, Miller & Hutch for a fun hunt and another work of art in my
logbook!
