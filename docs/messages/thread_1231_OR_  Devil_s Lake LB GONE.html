From: (paisleyorca@webtv.net) | Date: 2004-03-28 02:59:35 UTC-08:00
Hi Everyone!

Yesterday John and I were down in Lincoln City for SOLV's bi-annual
beach clean up. While there I thought I would check on the Devil's Lake
Letterbox. I've had reports that it had gone missing. Each time I
checked on it previously, those reports were just rumors and the box was
safely tucked in where it was placed.

However, now the rumors have become true. The Devil's Lake State Park
Letterbox is truly gone. BOO HOO. I lament the demise of this
letterbox. I will remove the clues when I am able to get on an actual
computer.

Thanks to those of you who made me aware of its current state. I'm
sorry you went looking for a box that wasn't there. I hate to
disappoint people. I always liked that stamp. I placed it in my early
years of letterboxing when I was very eager and excited about the sport.
Perhaps sometime I will replace it, but for now it remains only as an
image in some of our logbooks. Thanks to those who have sought it out.

Best regards,

Amanda Briles
The Paisley Orca
