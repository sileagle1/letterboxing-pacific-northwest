Yes, at long last, I have struck again! But rather than torture your
poor feet anymore, I hid this letterbox in what may be the easiest
location yet to reach. Even easier than my famed Pirate's Treasure
letterbox--and you all know how easy THAT box is to hike to. Well,
this one is even EASIER! Yes, EASIER!

Of course, as everyone knows, the difficulty of a hike must always be
inversely proportional to the difficulty of the clue. Therefore, I
have made what might be--for some, at least--the most difficult clue
EVER created! Mapsurfer, you've met your match! Legerdemaine, your
days are numbered. I, the Green Turtle, have trumped you all!
MUHAHAHaHahahaha!!!!!

This afternoon, while out on a stroll and driving the wonderfully
deserted freeways of Seattle, I found myself goofing around in
Redmond and planted a letterbox. The clue? There isn't one. Not
even the title of the letterbox will help you out this time. But it
is findable!

I still created a webpage for it--even a letterbox without a clue
still needs to be recognized for its existance, I always say, and the
webpage is at http://www.geocities.com/rscarpen/Lb/US/WA/Clueless

But then, I don't know why you'd bother to look at it, since it is a
clueless letterbox and you won't see a clue there. =)

Enjoy!

-- Ryan
