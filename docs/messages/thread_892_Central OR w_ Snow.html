From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2003-11-02 23:52:37 UTC
I am sooooo jealous! If only I could have been at Steelhead Falls
today... We woke up to falling snow this morning and knew that it
wouldn't be prudent to hang around 'til the afternoon to leave for
Portland. As it was, the east side of Mt Hood required chains and we
saw an upsidedown SUV down the embankment and a loaded pickup nose
down in the creek along the highway. (We stopped for both, but help
was already on its way.)

I tracked down the Fleshman's wonderful Lower Bridge letterbox Friday.
I feel as though I've won the Lb lottery. Not only are there new boxes
appearing regularly in Central OR but there are great stamps and
challenging clues to accompany them. This one is terrific.

Yesterday was my day to leave a new box in the area. Most of the
stamps I have hidden there are my first attempts at carving and tend
to rank pretty high on the funk scale. Hopefully, my skills have
improved over the intervening months. Watch for the Odin Falls Angler
clue. Funhog
