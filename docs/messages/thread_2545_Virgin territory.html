We were excited to plant a new box over Memorial Day Weekend because it's in a really cool area AND there are no letterboxes around (that we could find).

A region dubbed "Oregon's Outback" in the southern central section of the state, is amazingly dramatic -- with lava rings, salt lakes, dramatic volcanic scarpes and wide open vistas.

The new box borders Lake Abert, an 'inland sea' and is called "Heaven and Earth". Check it out on www.letterboxing.org.
