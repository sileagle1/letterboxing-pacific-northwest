Re: Letterboxing supplies in Eugene

From: Jeanne (heronafoot@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-11 04:14:05 UTC
Coyote - That is a sad loss. I get my carving tools at Oregon Art Supply.

http://www.oregonartsupply.com/

I know for sure that they have the individual Speedball Cutters in the
different sizes and the caligraphy type handles. They may also have
the rounded wood handles, some kits and or carving medium but I am not
positive about those.

Also try the U of O bookstore

http://literary.uoduckstore.com/StoreAbout.aspx

Michaels, located in Springfield near Gateway Mall, is the last
resort. The cutters that they carry are really large and not what you
would want to carve a stamp with. I think they carry Mastercarve but
again I am not sure about the "Pink Stuff"

We have a little scrapbooking shop here in Florence and I think she
carries 1 or 2 beginner kits with basic carving set and pink stuff.

Hope you can find what you are looking for and get back in the game soon.

Heron-A-Foot
