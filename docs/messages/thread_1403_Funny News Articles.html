Late Breaking News: The Wizard of Oz originally debuted in 1939.
Just recently in honor of the 65th anniversary of the Independence
of Munchkins, these darling little people were presented with the
deed to their name sake park in somewhere in Oregon. In honor of

bridge. The Mayor of Munchkin City, who was also present, but evaded

Photographs of the Coroner and the deed were taken near a munchkin
sized Christmas tree and will be preserved for historical purposes.
