Hi there everyone, I sneaked (snuck?) our of work early today, and
between rain showers, checked on my Picnic Basket letterbox at Oxbow
Park, today.

Box is in great condition, thanks to a Lock N Lock container. For
those of you who have visited it and remember how close it was to
the river... think rushing water maybe 5 feet away. The Sandy River
has risen that much. Amazing sight, if ya'll get the chance, take a
drive by a local river and see how much it has risen from the recent
rains.

Anyway, the box is perfectly safe and dry, and several people have
been kind enough to contribute Picnic-themed stamps to it. Thanks
to Lady B, Calli-K, Lily, Teata95, Hayler Sister #1, and several
others that left stamps but apparently didn't say so anywhere that I
could find. There are a total of 16 stamps in the box, so plenty to
enjoy, and a picnic table just a short ways away for stamping in
(gotta get your heart rate up though, hee hee!)
