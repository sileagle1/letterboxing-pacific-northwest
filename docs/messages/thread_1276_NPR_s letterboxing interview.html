For those who missed this the other day, NPR's Linda Wertheimer
recently conducted an interactive letterboxing interview with Mr
Thomas Johnston--whom I 'guest'imate undoubtfully personifies a
letterboxing enthusiast extraordinaire of the scarably variety--in
the District of Columbia. It's an interesting account of
letterboxing as together they seek out the Radio Days LB. Listen to
it while you can--not sure how long this link will remain active.

http://www.npr.org/features/feature.php?wfId=1832303
