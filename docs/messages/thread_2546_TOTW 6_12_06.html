I usually do whatever repairs on site that I can. Baggies and
logbooks are easy...I almost always have those on hand (and if there
are no baggies handy, a poop bag works too! A clean, empty one, of
course!). I've been known to replace a soggy logbook with a fresh one
and then contact the placer for further instructions. I have pulled a
few boxes that belong to people I know if the box is in really bad
shape.

I don't usually carry boxes around with me, but I was boxing with a
group of people once and we found a broken and soggy box. Between all
of us in the group we had everything needed to replace a box in our
bags. Luckily, the stamp was still salvageable, but I'm sure we could
have replaced that too if needed. :-)
