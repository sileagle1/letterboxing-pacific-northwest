From: FLASH (jfleshman@bendcable.com) | Date: 2003-12-09 22:35:17 UTC
Letterboxing you are given clues to a specific location and you stamp in.
Geocaching you are given and GPS coordinate and you locate it with a GPS
device. You sign in and usually a small token is exchanged.
