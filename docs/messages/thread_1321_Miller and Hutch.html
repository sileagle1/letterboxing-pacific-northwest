We were out for a wonderful Mother's Day and picked up several boxes.. one
of which was Miller and Hutch's 5th box. If you know who they are, or know
how to get hold of them, or if you ARE them, they need to know that their
5th box is now the home to an ant colony. Thank goodness for rubber gloves
in letterboxing bags. I had to dig the box out of the ant colony, empty the
millions of ants out of the box, out of all three bags, and then try to
stamp in without being to grossed out. We cleaned out as much as we could
and double bagged everything.

We also picked up three other boxes while out driving around and Tooth Fairy
got her 100th box by resucing Puff the Magic Dragon.
