I just wanted to send a big thank you to Maiden and Camp Fire Lady -
We met up at my house this morning to get together to plan the Camp
Stamp Event coming up in August, I really encourage anyone that can
make it that weekend to come on out, the three of us have some great
things planned out and I am just positive there will be some things
you all don't want to miss. I must say that I am very honored to be
hosting this event with these wonderful Ladies.

After our meeting we said good-bye to a hurried home for a birthday
party Maiden, and Camp Fire Lady and I headed out in search of a
certain mystery box but came up empty handed. I grabbed another
Mystery Box and another addition to Mom, Mexico and Me that I hadn't
found out my first two hikes through the park.

Then we were off to Lake Oswego to hit the Bon Apetie (sp) Series.
We found 2 of 3 and ended up getting ourselves a little turned
around in the park, alot hungry, pretty tired, overly thirsty and my
suggest of cutting through and heading back on the surface streets
ended up to be a really bad idea and a rather long detour! So back
up the hill yet again, back through the park and I must say, I'd
never been so happy to see Camp Fire Lady's car before!!

But a great fun day, Thank you Camp Fire Lady for driving me around
today in Iron`Bear's absence!
