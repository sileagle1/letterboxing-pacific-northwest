I will be placing our first order for PNW Letterboxing T-shirts
hopefully within the next week. They will be Gildan 6.1 oz long-sleeve
in colors forest green, maroon, or royal blue. You can view the blank
shirts and colors on Amazon at:
http://www.amazon.com/sta6244-GILDAN-Cotton-Sleeve-5X-Large/dp/B000FVJYGA/sr=1-2/qid=1163966616/ref=sr_1_2/102-0861345-6482517?ie=UTF8&s=apparel
(I know, that's a huge address.)

The front will have a pocket-sized rain-drop-shaped logo with Lb PNW
in it and the back will have the logo "Not all who wander are lost...
some of us are letterboxing". After some discussion on the Oregon list
and the Yahoo PNW Letterboxing list these seemed to be the preferred
designs.

I should have the shirts in plenty of time to ship for holidays. And,
yes, you are allowed to buy one for yourself, put it under the tree
and say it is from Santa :)

The cost is going to be 13.67/ea (sizes S-XL) plus 3.00 for shipping
in the U.S. The 2XL shirts are a dollar more. At this point, I'm
taking pre-orders in colors and sizes. This is not a commitment to
buy, but it will give me a general idea of how many, what sizes, and
what colors to order. So if you are pretty sure you will want one,
send me a note including your size, color choice, or any questions.

-tyrarachele@comboard.com
