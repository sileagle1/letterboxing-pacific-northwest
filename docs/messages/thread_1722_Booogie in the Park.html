Re: Booogie in the Park

From: Nail Family (the7nails@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-24 03:58:54 UTC

It was great to meet all of you folks today. The Nail Family had a
great time just chatting with everyone and enjoying the plentiful food
provided by our hosts and other attendees. We plan to get back out
there on Monday or Tuesday and find all those boxes!

It was especially great to meet Der Mad Stamper, Camp Fire Lady,
Maiden, Team Nashoba, Secret Agent and Never Enough, Big Dogs, and
others I cant recall names of without looking at my log book. We had
lots of fun chatting with Pandora and Team Ravenhaus as well as old
friends dvnrckr (and kids and dad too!), Ryan and Amanda, and Fudrick
(even though he showed up late). But who was that mysterious fellow in
the shadows? I didnt catch his name. The kid's activities were
awesome, the cooties were cool, and the fire was just what we needed
to keep warm! We had an awesome time and will look forward to next
year too!

A quick note to those who didnt attend and anyone who did but didnt
get to find the Moondance box: We will be pulling the Moondance box
at the end of the month so if you have not gotten to it yet you still
have till the end of October before it will be pulled and shelved
until next October. Its a marvelous night for a Moondance!

Thanks again to Kris, Steve, Pandora, Iron Bear, Maiden, and Camp Fire
Lady for a great event. You guys really know how to throw a party!

Nailhead of the Nail Family
