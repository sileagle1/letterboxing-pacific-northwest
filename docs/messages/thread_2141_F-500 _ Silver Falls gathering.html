F-500 & Silver Falls gathering

From: Diane (Doublesaj@aol.com) | Date: 2005-07-11 05:19:14 UTC
In an attempt to go from F447 to F-500 in 8 days, Old Blue and I
decided Portland, Oregon was the place to go for our vacation this
year. With over 100 boxes listed in the city alone, we thought we'd
have a good shot. We hiked about 30 miles in all, we figure, one day in
pouring rain--BUT we were able to get some much sought after boxes.

Lucky for us, as a bonus to our trip, there happened to be a gathering
in the area (Silver Falls) and we added 32 exchanges to our logbooks
too! What a wonderful group of people we met and boxed with. I'll tell
you, we aren't much the camping types but we really regret not planning
for at least one overnight with this bunch! Der Mad Stamper, Wingnut,
Funhog, Maiden, J-Rock Family, Paisley Orca, Wild Woman, Double Tree,
Heather and all the kid-stampers, Lil Bug, Button, Emily, Paw and Nose
in a Book and all the rest of you, it was such a pleasure to meet and
box with you you!

We're positively HOOKED on Der Mad Stamper's clues and got Mad Mallard,
Der Saintly Stamper Series, Evil Genius, Lost Letterbox and #1 of the
Cedar Hills Twins and Spirit of Dartmoor. We found Funhog's 45th
Paralell and Beneath the Blue #2 and 3, Paisley Orca's Who Let The Dogs
Out series and Fort Canby series, and many, many more.

Thanks to all you wonderful carvers and hiders for making this such a
memorable and exciting vacation!

~~Doublesaj & Old Blue~~
