West Seattle Beach Letterboxes Update

From: Danny McMillin (macdanny@mindspring.com) | Date: 2006-02-04 10:51:39 UTC-08:00
February 4, 2006 10 am

I just took a walk along Beach Drive Southwest in West Seattle. I
checked in on my letterboxes Constellation Park Marine Reserve,
Cormorant Cove and Weatherwatch Park. All are ok. The winds and waves
are pounding the beach. I estimate the winds at a steady 35 mph with
gusts over 45 mph. It's raining buckets sideways.

Constellation Park Marine Reserve letterbox is certainly under
assault from the powers of mother nature. I was only able to visibly
check that the letterbox is still above the waterline. Whew!

All three letterboxes are going to be quite salty.

Hope you all are well where ever you live in the PNW.

Keegan the Airedale Terrier
aka
Danny
