From: Strand (strandnet@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-11-17 08:55:22 UTC-08:00
OK, ok, ok, ok, ok! [squinting from the glare] Time to check in -

I was just at the beach checking for flood damage and happened to check on Seaside Happiness. It's alive and well (and dry!) despite nearby flooding.

Lucky Lenard is a fun, local series, despite my wandering up a wrong trail. Great stamps. Found it myself in September then took my kids out last week so they could enjoy it too.

By the name, one would assume Lost Letterbox had been found at one point. Well, I never did. ~ Though the neighbors are very familiar with my face.

I've been introducing myself to carving with something more advanced than an exacto. All the frustrations of transfering image have been mine, though I think I've finally figured that out with the help of several sites. Many missteps later and I might finally have a stamp to plant!

stratus
