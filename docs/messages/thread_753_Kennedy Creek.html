Kennedy Creek

From: W.K. Burk (bburk@harbornet.com) | Date: 2003-09-29 19:35:52 UTC-07:00
Made a visit to Kennedy Creek on Sunday.
Visited all the boxes, all are well and in good shape.
The exception being that I could not find Bethany's 'Scout' box, I know it *was* there, I have the stamp in my book.
But I could not locate it this time around.

The stream is very low and there was no evidence that salmon have started to enter. I think we'll have to wait for some steady rain before that happens.

It also seems that 4 wheel ATV riders have been using the paths as a race course, there were three riders leaving as I entered, like to run me over!
The trails show the scars that riders have been using the trails for awhile.

This collection of boxes was placed by South Sound L Boxers for the enjoyment of a local scout den, but is open to all.
These boxes should not be missed by those new to the hobby.
