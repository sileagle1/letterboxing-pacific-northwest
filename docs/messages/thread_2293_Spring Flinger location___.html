Hey everyone!
Thanks so much for the input. Yes, I do want it in a more southern
place to enable some of the great boxers from Oregon to come. Priest
Point is about half the size of Millersylvania, plus Mill. has camping
and RV spots. The place is flipping huge. There is also a cute
lakeside cottage that they rent out there.
Wildwood is a good place to letterbox, but my vote wouldn't be for a
gathering. On the weekends it can get real busy...hard to box when
you have to wait for an all clear. Additionally, the parking can be a
hassle. Also, it can get busy during the week around lunch, as
several daycares BUS kids there for an outing. I loved when they were
repaving the road, because we would park on the road and walk down,
and have the place to ourselves, HE HE!
I will devote more brainspace to it after our Thanksgiving trip, and
I'll keep ya'll posted!
Thanks again! Elisabeth/Buppsters
