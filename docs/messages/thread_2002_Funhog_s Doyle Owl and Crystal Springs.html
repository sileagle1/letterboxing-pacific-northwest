Hello,

I'm from Portland, OR and we are just starting out. We have already
found the Mt Tabor letterbox and I can't be more excited.

But today was frustrating. We went searching for these two boxes and
could not find them!

The Doyle Owl: This one is probably our error. We got as far as the
second to last clue and got stuck! We had a great time and ran in to
some interesting people with dogs. Maybe we just need practice. Has
anyone found this one recently?

Crystal Springs: This one seemed to be nowhere in sight. (or out of
sight). We looked throughout that whole area where the box could have
been. In every little spot. (I don't want to give away too much.) This
one in my mind has to be missing. If anyone has been there before or
has seen it recently, let me know. This trip was also wonderful; we
even found a geo-cash while we were looking! I am assuming the
geo-cash was defintaly different than the box, right? Any thoughts on
this?

I am determined to find these. So if anyone has and tips on finding
the right spots (in general, not for these particular clues), or has
seen either one of these recently, let me know! We want to go back out
there soon to check it out again.


Thanks!
Becca and Ryan
