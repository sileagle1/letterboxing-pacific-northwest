From: Pungent Bob (PungentBob@HotPOP.com) | Date: 2006-04-19 02:38:05 UTC
On such a glorious spring day I just had to play hooky from work and
head out for a hike. I meet a pig in the parking lot and proceeded to
voluntarily load 30 pounds of water, rocks, trail books, and a laptop
computer onto my back. We headed down Leif Erickson Drive (this is a
trail, not a road) in Forest Park to admire the abundance of trilliums
in bloom. Along the way we stopped by to find the latest additions to
the Musician Series by Camp Fire Lady and Maiden. These are boxes that
should not be missed by any local boxer. After nabbing the boxes we
continued down the trail, up Hardesty, back on the muddy Wildwood
trail, down Water Line and then back on Leif Erickson to the cars for
about a six mile loop, making for a nice training run for this
summer's backpacking (something I'm planning for part of my sabbatical
July/August this year).

I encourage all to get out there to get these boxes: with the older
boxes there are now ten boxes in the series. Note that you are allowed
to hunt these without a 30 pound pack and if you just go out and back
on Leif Erickson Drive it is an flat easy stroll of less than 4 miles.
