Sadly, this letterbox has gone missing. Some misguided soul took the stamp and
handmade logbook, leaving only the empty container. This was the first letterbox that
I hid in collaboration with the enigmatic Legerdemaine, the only one of his opi to have
a permanent home in Portland.

I just received word that this box will not be replaced. So, those of you who have the
beautiful impression in your logs, enjoy your collectors' piece...sigh... and may the
perpetrators have permanent inkstains on their fingers. Funhog


RE: [LbPNW] OR: Sleight

From: pandora{I`B} (hispandora@phlsystems.com) | Date: 2004-09-02 18:22:54 UTC-07:00
This is very very sad news indeed. Sleight is one of the letterboxes that
left a long standing impression on both Iron`Bear and myself. First the
carving inside from Legerdemaine, was nothing but beautiful. Secondly, the
hiding spot was ingenious. Iron`Bear and I had to look for this box 7 times
before finding it. Somehow it eluded us each time we were there looking for
it, it finally took us meeting up with Funhog, and having her stand there
for the brick to suddenly fall on my head and make me look in the correct
location. I was so proud to recover this box. Because the stamp was so
special and the hiding place so clever, many times my friends asked for help
finding it and all I would say was what Funhog told me. You are in the right
place, keep looking it's there. Finally after Camp Fire Lady looked for it
several times, we stopped there last week to see if she could find it with a
little help. Alas, the box was gone. :( We were so sad to find it missing.
I am sorry to hear that this box will not be replaced, but thank you to both
Funhog and Legerdemaine for the ever lasting impression, inspiration and
memory this box left with us!

Pandora
