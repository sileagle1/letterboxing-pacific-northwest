On another topic - sort of last weeks - Miniles and I met yesterday to exchange
some postals and somehow got on the topic:

when boxing in the wild sometimes we wonder if the box is missing or maybe
someone else just has the box for signing in.

When you've gone a long way and doubt you'll be back to the area or it was a
long hike you'd hate to give up / leave only to find out that someone logged in
that day and you left because you thought it was MIA.

Miniles mentioned that she'd heard someone say they have a laminated card
that they leave in the place of the box that says they are signing in and it will
be back shortly.

I think it is a wonderful idea and wanted to spread it to as many as possible!

I think it would be great to leave one if you take the box for maintenance too -
that way we can just go on our merry way looking for the next box instead of
stalling or wasting our precious letterboxing daylight/time coming back to
recheck.

I hope it doesn't sound like I'm negative or complaining - I don't mean to be - I
just wanted to pass on this idea because I hadn't heard of it or thought of it in
the 7 mos. I've been boxing and I think this would be really nice/helpful for all
of us if everyone adds these 2 little cards to their important things to carry list!
:-)
Happy Hunting!
