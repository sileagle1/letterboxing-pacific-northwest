Wonderful Boxng Day

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2004-01-17 19:30:03 UTC-08:00
We headed out this morning to pick up a few boxes during a rare study break.
I oncen again went for Crystal Springs, only to go home empty handed once
again. Yes that makes 5 trips down there and 5 failures. I've almost given
up for good on that one.

Then we headed for the Oaks Bottom Wildlife Refuge. We found the Oaks
Bottom box in good shape. Hike the rest of the way around the pond and then
headed for Wallace Books. What a fantastic place and a wonderful box!

Overall, 2 new stamps in my box and a wonderful hike. What more could a
person ask for?

Melanie
