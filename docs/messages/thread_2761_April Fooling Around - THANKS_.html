April Fooling Around - THANKS!

From: HER (fauxsum@hotmail.com) | Date: 2007-04-06 04:16:27 UTC
Ok so first of all...I think Yahoo Groups was on Crack the last time
I posted. April Fooling Around aside, I did not! deliberately post
twelvety-hundred-times on purpose. Holy Garlic Breath!

I just wanted to say a Huge Mile High Thanks to EVERYONE for coming
out to Laurelwood on Tuesday. I was truly and completely
overwhelmed by the mondo-sized welcome mat ya'll put out! I'm
rarely at a loss for words, but well...words fail me once in a
while.. and this is one of those times.

Extra special thanks go to marmalade for carving some very special
April Foolish stamps for us all to enjoy - which personally I'll
treasure forever since I collect all things related to Fools Day, to
funhog for placing a 'box for the occaison for us all to find and
for catching an earlier flight to join us, to GT and Amanda for
wandering by on their way north from "other" Foolishness - I'm sorry
I didn't get to chat with you more, to the Cootie Crew for working
overtime (I'd only ever "met" one cootie before and well..good lawd
those things are like bunny rabbits!) and to ALL of you for being
there, sharing your stories, stamps and your WONDERFUL amazing
selves for the evening!

The meet was the 'boxing highlight of the trip for me. There were
many on the family front, but one I thought I'd share with you:

The next morning after our meet I was awakened by the Party Penguin
who says "oink, oink, oink" and puts his hands under his arms while
wandering around the house rocking happily back and forth...a bit
like one of the ways to spot a letterboxer! I've been "into"
penguins long before the recent movie stuff. The Party Penguin who
says "oink, oink, oink" in the form of my adorable 3-year old nephew
made sure I wouldn't miss my flight.

I smiled happy thoughts as I waddled my way to the Max station the
next morning with my pack on my back and my explorer hat on my head
saying "oink, oink, oink" thinking to myself: Yep, Portland was the
perfect! place to spend April Fools week 2007. And Fools really
don't care how "that" might look to "other" people...

Left 3 PREsents behind for ya'll to find (a few already have found
one) to show my apPREciation for a terrifically memorable week.
Two 'boxes have clues up already with one more set of clues I still
need to type up.

Risas y sonrisas!

preboxed
Denver
who next year will ...once again.. become
The Boxing Fool
