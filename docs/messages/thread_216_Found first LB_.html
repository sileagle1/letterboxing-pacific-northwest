From: Emily (emilyplays@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-05-21 13:41:05 UTC-07:00
After frolicking at the beach, swinging & climbing, my wee one was finally tired enough to be loaded
into her stroller and off we went up the trail. There it was, our first find....out of sight in
plain sight! We parked ourselves on a bench, I handed over a much coveted teething cookie and
unpacked my stamp. Sigh, my inkpad had been hauled out of the bag by a pixie who shall go
nameless! Best laid plans of mice & mommies......
But, we shall return for the lovely Vashon Ferry LB! If I work my timing a bit better, we may be
able to find the one on the northend of the trail also! I'm hooked!

cheers,
Emily
