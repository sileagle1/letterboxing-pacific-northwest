Boy, this is something we just lived through...

We just renewed passports for the kids. If your ducks are in a row
then at most it takes 3 weeks provided you use the 'rush' service. If
there are extenuating circumstances (birth abroad without the original
birth certificate but only a previous passport and a certificate of
birth abroad issued by the consulate) then it takes about 5 weeks
provided you get the documents submitted in an expedited fashion.

For a parent who plans to bring a child over the border while the
other parent doesn't accompany you, plan on either bringing a power of
attorney and/or a document signed/notarized by the non-traveling
parent that authorizes the other parent to take the child across the
border alone. This is thanks to all of the intl kidnappings that seem
to occur in/outside the US these days.
