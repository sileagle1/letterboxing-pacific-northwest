From: kleibrand2002 (bookwormlb@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-01-21 17:57:34 UTC
At last I was able to get out and find a few boxes this weekend. How
I've missed it! Lacamas Creek is alive and well, although it hadn't
had a visitor since early November. Has anyone adopted this box (one
of Der Mad Stampers)? It's in good shape (except for a ton of sap on
the outside of the box) but the log book is full.

Big thanks to Sleepy Whippet for planting some boxes out in East
County that I could get in the dark on my way to work. Sometimes
that's all the boxing I can get in, and I can't wait for the sun to
come up sooner. You Betcha is a terrific stamp and the clues lead
you right to it (love those!). Olive & Stine are great, but just a
hint for those of you who box in pantyhose...watch out for the
blackberry bushes! They are small but mighty stickery (kind of a
Audrey II kind of thing going on there).

Minibullymom, Sleepy Whippet and I met up ringside at the dog show on
Monday for a quick exchange. I just realized that we should have
gotten your dog's footprint while we were there! I hope you found
PDX Equus okay. You'll have to come back to Portland so we can box
together.

Hope everyone else got to get out and find a few boxes.
