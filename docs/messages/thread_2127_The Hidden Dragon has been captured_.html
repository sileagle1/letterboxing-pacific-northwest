From: Forest People (forestpeople@zilknet.net) | Date: 2005-07-03 16:46:53 UTC-07:00
While all the rest of you were at Silver Falls, the Forest People
managed to capture a Hidden Dragon, and after nearly an hour of
searching, it took a six year-old to find it.

Other than a garden maintenance worker, that came across it
accidentially, it appears we were the first to find this legendary
Funhog box after more than three years.

In defense of everyone else who looked for it, the box had been obscured
in such a way that the clues weren't completely accurate. We placed it
back in its lair so that the 'ring' can be found.

David
of the Forest People
