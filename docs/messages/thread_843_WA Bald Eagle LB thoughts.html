WA Bald Eagle LB thoughts

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-10-21 22:23:02 UTC
Ok, so watching the news of late another one of my letterboxes may
now be history since the region in which it was hidden has WELL
EXCEEDED flood levels--although I won't be able to verify that it's
missing anytime soon--

Bald Eagle LB in Skagit County, WA
(placed in honor of the last Bald Eagle Festival in Concrete, WA
this past Spring)

I presume at this moment this little letterbox is bobbing up and
down somewhere in the Pacific Ocean, on its way to the Arctic Ocean
at the moment (unless, of course, its little magnet was able to hold
up to the forces of torrential rushing water!). ;) Fortunately,
it's in a waterproof container so it will be quite interesting to
possibly hear from it again sometime in the future--sort of like a
message in a film canister? Ironic, isn't it, that I carved this
little fellow on a ferry ride so it's only fitting it also died in
the water...

Unfortunately, however, it was one of my favorite micro-stamps to
date. :(

Happy bobbing little Eagle! At least, you'll be much closer to your
food source in the ocean... ;)

dvn2r ckr
