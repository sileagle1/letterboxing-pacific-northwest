From: funhog1 (funhog@pacifier.com) | Date: 2006-03-13 06:47:46 UTC
Can't say as I have an absolute favorite. I have favorite stamps,
favorite hikes, favorite clues... However, there are a few that I
consider "top drawer." In this category, I include Pungent Bob's
wonderfully challenging $100 Bill. This one is amongst the finest
around. This guy is evil, evil, evil and I thank him for it. Then
there are the enigmatic duo, Miller and Hutch. Finding one of their
clues is a real treasure. The wonderful Yellow Rose of Suffrage is a
special gift hidden in a lovely spot, as well. I just can't pick a
favorite but these are three of my local faves. Funhog
