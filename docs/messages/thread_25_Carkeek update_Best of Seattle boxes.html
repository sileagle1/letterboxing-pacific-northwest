Carkeek update/Best of Seattle boxes

From: payntonm (perky@bung.org) | Date: 2003-03-31 18:18:56 UTC
Had a very enjoyable day yesterday picking up Candlelight's Carkeek
series. Great stamps and what a cardio workout! :) One small word
of caution: the nettles are out in full force at this time of year,
and the second box, the Pixie Witch, is basically surrounded. The
location of the third box isn't quite so bad, but the areas where you
might be tempted to sit to box in are pretty infested as well. Keep
an eye on the kiddies or dogs if you go after these this time of year.

As a beginner, all the Carkeek stamps have been a joy to find. I'd
recommend all of them to any beginner and to anyone who wants to
enjoy the beauty of a typical Seattle park. Visitors would probably
especially like Eliza B's series since it has a great view of Puget
Sound on the hike up the hill. And the stamps are a great variety by
some really talented folks. Keep up the good work!

Learning as I go,
Mary
The Gorilla Librarian
