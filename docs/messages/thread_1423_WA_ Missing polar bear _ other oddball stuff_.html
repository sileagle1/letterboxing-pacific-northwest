WA: Missing polar bear & other oddball stuff?

From: dvn2rckr (dvn2rckr@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-06 02:12:01 UTC
Got a fairly credible report today that our Polar Bear LB in Tacoma
is missing. As I'm essentially out of the letterboxing net for most
of the next 4 months I won't be able to personally verify this is
indeed missing anytime soon. Just a heads up for anybody who was
interested in looking for it.

Other boxes of ours that are missing or most likely missing include:

Dolphin Mystery (confirmed missing from its hiding spot--although it
could very likely be embedded in mud all around the area?)

Kite LB (Ilwaco--most likely missing, not verified)
Big, Bare Foot in Burfoot (Boston Harbor, possibly MIA, not verified)
Goldfish (Lacey, possibly MIA, not verified)
West Point Mule (Seattle, possibly MIA, not verified)
Crystal Creek Falls (CA, most likely MIA, not verified)

I'm sure there are others--these are just ones we've heard about
recently.

Ones that have been confirmed recently as still alive & well despite
myriad missing reports include:

Eggceptional Mystery
Ecola (Oregon)
Cold Moon over Puget Sound
Bastion (VA)
Charles' Jazzy STreetcar (New Orleans, LA)
It's Raining Animals (Olympia--all 4 are alive & well)
Mushroom & Fern (both were alive & well)

I'm sure there are a bunch of others in this category, too, but my
memory escapes me at the moment.

Hope to see you on the trails in the Fall, :(

dvn2r ckr

Ohhhh dvn2rckr - I apologize I had meant to write you a long time ago about

down was one of the highlights of that trip!



I'd love to go again and hope you replace the Polar Bear!
