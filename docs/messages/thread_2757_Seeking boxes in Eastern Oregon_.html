From: Dan Clark (flying.monkey@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-04-04 02:41:35 UTC
Greetings all!

I just wanted to provide a couple of updates on some of my boxes in
Eastern Oregon that are still awaiting their first finders! Two are
in the Heppner area and the third is right outside the small town of
Condon.

Molinos - I finally added clues to this box.
Wapiti - No finders on this yet. The roads to this area should be
clear now.
Bull Pairie - I replaced this stamp with a much better one late last
year. This box is very close (a few minutes drive) from the Wapiti
box. Bring your fishing pole!

Clues for all three are in the usual places. Please update me on the
health of these boxes if you are able to uncover them.

FlyingMonkey
