Incredibly Cool Boxes

From: Melanie (maiden1974@verizon.net) | Date: 2006-08-03 09:07:24 UTC-07:00
Our family left for a road trip on Sunday. We have had an amazing trip. We
boxed our way down 101, finding many fabulous boxes. (Thank you Carie, Don
and Gwen, Ryan, South Hill Hendersons, and Legerdermain) Each one just
happened to be right where we needed to stretch our legs. There were two
that eluded us, but Im not sure one of them was there. We ended our 101 run
at the Rogue River. We went up the Rogue on the Jet boats. If you ever get
the chance to do that, DO IT, its amazing. We didnt get to go as far up as
Don and Gwens boxes, but I did leave one for you at our end point. (Clue
coming soon to a web site near you).



Then we went across to the Oregon Caves. That is truly an amazing tour. We
toured the Caves and then went after Erfellies Baby Big Foot. That poor box
has been there over a year now with only one visitor (last week). Its an
amazing box my friends. One of my all time favorites! If youre driving
through that area, STOP and get this box. You will kick yourself for not
having done it sooner.



Maiden
